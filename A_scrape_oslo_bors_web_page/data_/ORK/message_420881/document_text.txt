


On 22 February, in connection with Orkla's former management option programme,
50,000 options in Orkla-shares were exercised at a strike price of NOK 40.03 per
share.

The transactions comprise primary insider Håkon Mageli, Group Director,
Corporate Communications and Corporate Affairs. Mageli exercised 50,000 options
at a strike price of NOK 40.03 per share, of which 50,000 shares were sold at a
price of NOK 75.6794 per share. After this transaction, Mageli and close
associates own 76,001 shares and no options in Orkla.

After this transaction, the total number of options issued in Orkla shares under
the management option programme is 825,000. Orkla owns 1,983,135 treasury
shares.
