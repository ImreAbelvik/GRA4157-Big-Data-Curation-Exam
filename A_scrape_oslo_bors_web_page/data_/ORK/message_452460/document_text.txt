


According to the instructions for the nomination committee of Orkla ASA
("Orkla"), shareholder-elected board members are required to use minimum 1/3 of
their gross board fee to purchase shares in Orkla, until they own shares in
Orkla with a value equivalent to two times their gross board fee. On this basis,
the following board member has purchased shares in Orkla today.

Grace Reksten Skaugen, Deputy Chair of the Board of Orkla, has, through her
wholly owned company Infovidi Board Services Ltd, bought 3,000 shares at a price
of NOK 75.68 per share. Following this transaction, Reksten Skaugen and related
parties own 8,500 shares in Orkla.
