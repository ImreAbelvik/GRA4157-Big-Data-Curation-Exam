


On 25 February, in connection with Orkla's former management option programme,
110,000 options in Orkla-shares were exercised.

85,000 options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 38.88 per share and
25,000 options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 39.86 per share.

The transactions comprise primary insider Atle Vidar Nagel-Johansen, Executive
Vice President and CEO Orkla Foods. Nagel-Johansen exercised 60,000 options at a
strike price of NOK 38.88 per share of which 55,000 shares were sold at a price
of NOK 60.6596 per share. After this transaction, Nagel-Johansen and close
associates own 16,905 shares and 70,000 options in Orkla.

After this transaction, the total number of options issued in Orkla shares under
the management option programme is now 5,608,000. Orkla owns 442,903 treasury
shares.
