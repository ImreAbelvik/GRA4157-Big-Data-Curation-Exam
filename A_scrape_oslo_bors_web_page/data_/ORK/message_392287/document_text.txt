


As  announced in the stock exchange notice of 9 November 2015, Orkla's President
and  CEO Peter A. Ruzicka acquired 576 shares in Orkla in relation to the Shares
for  Employees Programme. Mr  Ruzicka has today  transferred these shares to his
wholly owned company Ventotene Invest AS for a price per share of NOK 69.55.

Mr  Ruzicka and his related parties' ownership interest in Orkla ASA of 683,086
shares remains unaffected by this transaction.

Oslo, 23 December 2015
