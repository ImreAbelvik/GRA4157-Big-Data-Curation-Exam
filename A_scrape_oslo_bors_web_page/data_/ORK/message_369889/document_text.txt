


On 27 January, in connection with Orkla`s former management option programme,
105,000 options in Orkla-shares were exercised at a strike price of NOK 38.88
per share.

After this transaction, the total number of options issued
in Orkla shares under the management option programme
is now 7,033,000. Orkla owns 1,697,903 treasury shares.
