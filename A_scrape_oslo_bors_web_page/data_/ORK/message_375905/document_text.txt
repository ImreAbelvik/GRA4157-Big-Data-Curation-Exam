


On 21 April, in connection with Orkla's former management option programme,
20,000 options in Orkla-shares were exercised at a strike price of NOK 37.36 per
share.

After this transaction, the total number of options issued in Orkla shares under
the management option programme is 4,445,500. Orkla owns 1,325,403 treasury
shares.
