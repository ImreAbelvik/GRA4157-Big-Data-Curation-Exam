


According to the instructions for the nomination committee of Orkla ASA
("Orkla"), shareholder-elected board members are required to use minimum 1/3 of
their gross board fee to purchase shares in Orkla, until they own shares in
Orkla with a value equivalent to two times their gross board fee. On this basis,
the following board members have purchased shares in Orkla on 7 May 2018.

Stein Erik Hagen, Chairman of the Board of Orkla, has personally bought 10.000
shares at a price of NOK 74.20 per share. Following this transaction, Hagen and
related parties own 250,020,000 shares in Orkla.

Nils Selte, Member of the Board of Orkla, has bought 3,000 shares at a price of
NOK 74.20 per share. Following this transaction, Selte and related parties own
27,000 shares in Orkla.

Ingrid Jonasson Blank, Member of the Board of Orkla, has bought 2,500 shares at
a price of NOK 73.96 per share. Following this transaction, Jonasson Blank and
related parties own 6,250 shares in Orkla.

Liselott Kilaas, Member of the Board of Orkla, has bought 2,700 shares at a
price of NOK 73.96 per share. Following this transaction, Kilaas and related
parties own 5,200 shares in Orkla.

Lars Dahlgren, Member of the Board of Orkla, has bought 2,500 shares at a price
of NOK 74.16 per share. Following this transaction, Dahlgren and related parties
own 6,500 shares in Orkla.

Peter Agnefjäll, Member of the Board of Orkla, has bought 10,000 shares at a
price of NOK 73.66 per share. Following this transaction, Agnefjäll and related
parties own 10,000 shares in Orkla.
