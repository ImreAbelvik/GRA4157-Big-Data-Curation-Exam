


On 13 February, in connection with Orkla`s former management option programme,
160,000 options in Orkla-shares were exercised.

30,000 options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 38.88 per share, 90,000
options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 39.86 per share, and 40,000
options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 45.03 per share.

The transactions comprise primary insider Karl Otto Tveter, Chief of Group
Functions and Group Director Legal Affairs. Tveter exercised 50,000 options at a
strike price of NOK 39.86 per share of which 47,000 shares were sold at a price
of NOK 59.2689 per share. After this transaction, Tveter and close associates
own 40,092 shares and 100,000 options in Orkla.

After this transaction, the total number of options issued in Orkla shares under
the management option programme is now 6,158,000. Orkla owns 897,903 treasury
shares.
