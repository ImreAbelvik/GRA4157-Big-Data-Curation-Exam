Jaan Ivar Semlitsch, President & CEO of Orkla, has today, 25 November 2021,
bought 6,500 shares in Orkla ASA at a price of NOK 85.07 per share. The shares
were acquired on the Oslo Stock Exchange.

Following this transaction, Semlitsch and personal related parties own 85,171
shares in Orkla ASA.
