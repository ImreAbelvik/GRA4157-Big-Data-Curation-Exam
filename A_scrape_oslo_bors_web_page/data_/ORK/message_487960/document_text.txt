According to the instructions for the nomination committee of Orkla ASA
("Orkla"), shareholder-elected board members are required to use minimum 1/3 of
their gross board fee to purchase shares in Orkla, until they own shares in
Orkla with a value equivalent to two times their gross board fee. On this basis,
the following board member has purchased shares in Orkla on 22(nd)  October
2019.

Peter Agnefjäll, Member of the Board of Orkla, has bought 4,000 shares at a
price of NOK 85,39 per share. Following this transaction, Agnefjäll and related
parties own 14,000 shares in Orkla.
