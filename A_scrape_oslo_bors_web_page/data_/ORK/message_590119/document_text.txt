Employees in more than 20 countries have been offered the opportunity to buy
Orkla shares at a discount, for the amount of NOK 10 000, NOK 15 000 or NOK
30 000 (included the discount). The purchase price will be set at Orkla's
closing price on 12 May 2023, minus 15% discount.

The offer is valid from 9 - 12 May 2023. This programme was introduced in 1999.
