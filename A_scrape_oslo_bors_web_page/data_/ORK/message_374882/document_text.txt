


On 7 April, in connection with Orkla's former management option programme,
50,000 options in Orkla-shares were exercised.

20,000 options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 38.88 per share, and
30,000 options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 45.03 per share.

After this transaction, the total number of options issued in Orkla shares under
the management option programme is 4,993,000. Orkla owns 1,827,903 treasury
shares.
