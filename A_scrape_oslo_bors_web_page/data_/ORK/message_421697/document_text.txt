


On 2 March, in connection with Orkla's former management option programme,
35,000 options in Orkla-shares were exercised at a strike price of NOK 40.03 per
share.

The transactions comprise primary insider Atle Vidar Nagel Johansen, Executive
Vice President and CEO Orkla Foods. Nagel Johansen exercised 35,000 options at a
strike price of NOK 40.03 per share, of which 30,800 shares were sold at a price
of NOK 75.5246 per share. After this transaction, Nagel Johansen and close
associates own 25,881 shares and no options in Orkla.

After this transaction, the total number of options issued in Orkla shares under
the management option programme is 790,000. Orkla owns 2,548,135 treasury
shares.
