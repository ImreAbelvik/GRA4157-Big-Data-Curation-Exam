On 20 May 2021, Canica AS, a company closely related to Orkla's chairman of the
board Stein Erik Hagen, acquired 5,800,000 shares in Orkla ASA from Stein Erik
Hagen AS, another closely related company to Stein Erik Hagen. The transaction
was made at a share price of NOK 84.30, equal to the closing price on Oslo Stock
Exchange.

Stein Erik Hagen and his closely related parties' ownership interest in Orkla
ASA of 250,386,000 shares, constituting 25.003% of the share capital, remains
unchanged by this transaction.
