


Orkla ASA (''Orkla'') has decided to initiate a share buyback program for up to
1.5 million shares in the market. The buyback program will be conducted in
accordance with the authorization provided by the annual general meeting to the
board of directors on 14 April 2016.

The buyback will be executed according to the market price on the Oslo Stock
Exchange. The program will be initiated today and terminated on 12 April 2017 at
the latest.

The shares purchased under this program will be used for Orkla's stock- and
incentive program for own employees.
