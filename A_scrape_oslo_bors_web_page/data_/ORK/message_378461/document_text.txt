


On 19 May, in connection with Orkla's former management option programme,
288,500 options in Orkla-shares were exercised.

107,500 options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 36.38 per share, 90,000
options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 37.36 per share and 91,000
options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 42.53 per share.

After this transaction, the total number of options issued in Orkla shares under
the management option programme is 3,832,000. Orkla owns 826,903 treasury
shares.
