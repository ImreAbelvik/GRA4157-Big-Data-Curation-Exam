


On 6 February, in connection with Orkla`s former management option programme,
330,000 options in Orkla-shares were exercised.

120,000 options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 38.88 per share,
140,000 options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 39.86 per share, and
70,000 options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 45.03 per share.

The transaction comprises primary insider Håkon Mageli, Group Director,
Corporate Communications and Corporate Affairs. Mageli exercised 50,000 options
at a strike price of NOK 39.86 per share which were sold at a price of NOK
58.5228 per share. After this transaction, Mageli and close associates own
75,425 shares and 100,000 options in Orkla.

After this transaction, the total number of options issued in Orkla shares under
the management option programme is now 6,498,000. Orkla owns 1,237,903 treasury
shares.
