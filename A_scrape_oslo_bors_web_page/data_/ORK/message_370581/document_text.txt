


On 5 February, in connection with Orkla`s former management option programme,
205,000 options in Orkla-shares were exercised.

50,000 options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 38.88 per share, 40,000
options were exercised at a strike price of NOK 39.86 per share, 25,000 options
were exercised at a strike price of NOK 43.85 per share and 90,000 options were
exercised at a strike price of NOK 45.03 per share.

After this transaction, the total number of options issued in Orkla shares under
the management option programme is now 6,828,000. Orkla owns 1,492,903 treasury
shares.
