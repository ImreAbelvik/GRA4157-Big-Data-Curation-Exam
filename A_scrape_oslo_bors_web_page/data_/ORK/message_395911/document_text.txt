


On 24 February, in connection with Orkla's former management option programme,
172,000 options in Orkla-shares were exercised at a strike price of NOK 42.53
per share.

After this transaction, the total number of options issued in Orkla shares under
the management option programme is 2,140,000. Orkla owns 358,135 treasury
shares. The number of treasury shares has been corrected for shares related to
the shares for employees programmes, which mistakenly have not been deducted in
2016.
