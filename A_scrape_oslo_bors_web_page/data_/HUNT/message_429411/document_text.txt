Mr. Martin Lingsten has on 12 June 2017 purchased 1 400 000 
shares in Hunter Group ASA (the "Company") at a price of NOK 
0.35 per share. 

The acquisition was made through Lingtech AS, a company 
wholly-owned by Mr. Lingsten.

After the transaction, Mr. Lingsten owns in total 20 614 196 
shares through entities controlled by him, equal to 
approximately 1.57% of the issued share capital and voting 
rights in the Company.

This notification is made pursuant to Section 4-2 of the 
Norwegian Securities Trading Act. Mr. Lingsten holds the 
position of VP Work Over Rigs in the Company's subsidiary 
Dwellop AS.
