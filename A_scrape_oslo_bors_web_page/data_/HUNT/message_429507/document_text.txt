Ms. Anne Brit Børresen Sterri, Finance Manager, Dwellop AS,  
(through Resonans Invest AS) has on 13 June 2017 purchased 
600 000 shares in Hunter Group ASA (the "Company") at a 
price of NOK 0,35 per share. 

After the transaction, Ms Sterri owns 19 814 196 shares, 
equal to 1,51% of the issued share capital and voting rights 
in the Company.

This notification is made pursuant to Section 4-2 of the 
Norwegian Securities Trading Act. Ms. Anne Brit Børresen 
Sterri holds the position of Finance Manager in the 
Company's subsidiary Dwellop AS.
