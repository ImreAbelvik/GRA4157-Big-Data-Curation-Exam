Hunter Group ASA (the "Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: HUNT) has been notified that
the following Primary Insiders has purchased shares in the Company today, 15
February 2024. Bertel Steen, Board member and primary insider in the Company has
purchased 458 333 shares in the Company at an average price of NOK 2.0 per share
through B.O. Steen Shipping AS and Skarris Kapital AS. After the transaction
companies affiliated with Bertel Steen owns 16 000 000 million shares in the
Company, corresponding to approx. 14.04% of the shares outstanding in the
Company. PDMR notification form attached hereto.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Article 19 of the
Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading
Act.
