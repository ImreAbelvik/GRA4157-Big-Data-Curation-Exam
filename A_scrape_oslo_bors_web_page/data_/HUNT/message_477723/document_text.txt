Apollo Asset Ltd., a company owned by primary insider and Board Member Arne
Fredly has subscribed for and been allocated 63,000,000 New Shares in the
Private Placement. After the transaction, Apollo Asset Ltd., owns 166,500,000
shares which constitutes approximately 29% of the share capital following
registration of the New Shares.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
