Mr. Helge Hustoft, CEO Dwellop AS,  (through Dynamo 
Management AS) has on 12 June 2017 purchased 1 295 000 
shares in Hunter Group ASA (the "Company") at a price of NOK 
0,35 per share. 
 
After the transaction, Mr. Hustoft owns 56 802 583 shares, 
equal to  4.33% of the issued share capital and voting 
rights in the Company.
