Oslo, 20 December 2022

Apollo Asset Limited, a company fully owned by board member and primary insider
Mr. Arne Fredly, has today bought 8,000,000 shares in Hunter Group ASA (the
"Company") at a price of NOK 2.17 per share. After this purchase, Apollo Asset
Limited owns 191,500,000 shares which constitutes 33.28 % of the outstanding
shares shares in the Company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to MAR
article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
