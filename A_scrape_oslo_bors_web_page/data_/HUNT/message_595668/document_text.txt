Oslo, 21 July 2023

Sagittarius Capital Ltd. has today bought 100,000 shares of Hunter Group ASA at
a price of NOK 1.526 per share. Sagittarius Capital Ltd. is 100% owned by CEO
Erik A.S. Frydendal. After the transaction Erik A.S. Frydendal, or companies
controlled by him, owns a total of 2,041,166 shares of Hunter Group ASA,
corresponding to 7.1% of the outstanding shares in the company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to MAR
article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
