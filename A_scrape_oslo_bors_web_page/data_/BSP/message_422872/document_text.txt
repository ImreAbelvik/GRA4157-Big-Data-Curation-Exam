On 16 March 2017, the board members and primary insiders of 
Black Sea Property AS (the "Company") has subscribed for a 
total of 13,634,390 new shares in the rights issue resolved 
on 8 March 2017 (the "Rights Issue"), of which 3,150,353 new 
shares are subscribed for through over-subscription. The 
subscriptions are made at the subscription price in the 
Rights Issue, being NOK 0.70 per share.

Please find enclosed an overview of the subscriptions made 
by the board members or companies controlled by them and 
their maximum holdings of shares in the Company following 
completion of the Rights Issue.
 
The maximum number of shares held by the primary insiders 
above will be subject to change following the board of 
director's allocation of new shares in the Rights Issue. An 
updated overview of the holdings of the primary insiders 
listed above will be published in due time.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to the Continuing Obligations 
for companies admitted to trading at Merkur Market
