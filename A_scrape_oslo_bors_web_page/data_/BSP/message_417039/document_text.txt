Erik Sture Larre, board member and primary insider, 
has on 27 December 2016 sold 1,442,500 shares in Black 
Sea Property AS ("BSP"), through wholly owned company 
E. Larre Holding AS, to the company CL-Holding AS 
(previously named Aheloy Beach Commercial AS) at a 
price of NOK 1.50 per share.

The sale was made in connection with a restructuring 
of Larre's ownership in BSP where the number of shares 
mentioned above was moved to CL-Holding AS. 
Simultaneously with the sale, Erik Sture Larre has 
purchased 50% of the shares in CL-Holding AS. 
Following this, CL-Holding AS is jointly owned with 
50% each by Erik Sture Larre and Hans F. Gulseth (both 
board members in BSP and primary insiders).

Following this transaction, Erik Sture Larre (through 
the companies E. Larre Holding AS and CL-Holding AS) 
owns 4 211 879 shares, corresponding to approximately 
14,04% of the total outstanding number of shares. The 
transaction does not imply any change of total 
ownership for neither Erik Sture Larre nor Hans F. 
Gulseth.

This information is subject of the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to the Continuing Obligations 
for companies admitted to trading at Merkur Market
