Christinedal AS, a shareholder in Black Sea Property 
AS ("Company"), have on the 19th of December 2016 sold 
1 442 500 shares in the Company to Aheloy Beach 
Commercial to a price of NOK 1,50 per share.  
Christinedal AS is owned 100% by board member Hans 
Gulseth and related parties and Aheloy Beach 
Commercial is owned 100% by board member Hans 
Gulseth.  
 
Hans F. Gulseth is regarded as a primary insider in 
the Company and, thus, the trade is notifiable 
pursuant to the Continuing Obligations for companies 
admitted to trading at Merkur Market. 
After this, Christinedal A/S will own no shares in the 
Company, while Aheloy Beach Commercial will own 1 442 
500 shares in the Company, corresponding to 
approximately 4,81% of the Company.
 
This information is subject of the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to the Continuing Obligations 
for companies admitted to trading at Merkur Market.
