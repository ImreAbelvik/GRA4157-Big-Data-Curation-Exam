Rødningen Invest AS, 100% owned by boardmember Kåre 
Rødningen,  a primary insider in Black Sea Property AS, has 
on the, 13. January 2016 sold 700 shares in the company at a 
price of NOK 3 per share.

Following the transaction, Rødningen Invest AS holds 71 800 
shares in the company, corresponding to 0,26% of the total 
shares outstanding.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to the Continuing Obligations for companies 
admitted to trading at Merkur Market.
