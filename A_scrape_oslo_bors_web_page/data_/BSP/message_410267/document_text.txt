MTB Invest AS, a shareholder in Black Sea Property AS 
("Company"), have on the 27th September 2016 
respectively sold 198,330 shares in the Company to 
Egil Melkevik, board member and 162,270 shares to 
Johannes Melkevik, a close related party to Egil 
Melkevik, to a price of NOK 3 per share. MTB Invest AS 
is 55% owned by board member Egil Melkevik and 45% 
owned by Johannes Melkevik and the shares transferred 
according to the ownership distribution in MTB Invest 
AS.
 
MTB Invest AS and Egil Melkevik himself is regarded as 
primary insiders in the Company, the trade is, thus, 
notifiable under the Norwegian Securities Trading Act 
section 4-2.
 
After this, MTB Invest AS will own no shares in the 
Company, while Egil Melkevik personally  will own 
198,330 shares in the Company, corresponding to 
approximately 0.66% of the Company.
 
This information is subject of the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to the Continuing Obligations 
for companies admitted to trading at Merkur Market.
