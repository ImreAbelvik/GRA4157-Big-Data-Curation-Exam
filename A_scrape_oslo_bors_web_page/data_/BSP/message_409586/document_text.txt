Håvard Lindstrøm AS, 100% owned by Chairman of the 
board, Håvard Lindstrøm,  a primary insider in Black 
Sea Property AS, has on the 13. September 2016 sold 
125.000 shares in the company at a price of NOK 3 per 
share.

Following the transaction, Håvard Lindstrøm AS holds 1 
375 000 shares in the company, corresponding to 4,6% 
of the total shares outstanding.


Sace Holding AS, 100% owned by CEO Torbjørn Myhre,  a

at a price of NOK 3 per share.

Following the transaction, Sace Holding AS holds 1 375

This information is subject of the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to the Continuing Obligations
