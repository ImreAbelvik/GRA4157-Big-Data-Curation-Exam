Reference is made to the notice of the ordinary general meeting of Black Sea Property AS (the "Company") to be held on 18 June 2019, specifically to item 7 on the agenda relating to a grant of share options and to item 10 on the agenda relating to a conversion of an outstanding bond loan with principal of NOK 12,410,000 raised in December 2017 and incurred interest (the "Bond Conversion").

The Board of Directors has proposed that the ordinary general meeting grants share options to the members of the Board of Directors as set out below:

-	Egil Melkevik (chairman): 3,000,000 share options;
-	Hans Gulseth: 3,000,000 share options;
-	Kåre Rødningen: 1,500,000 share options; and
-	Erik Sture Larre: 750,000 share options.

The options are granted free of charge, and each option gives the right to require issuance of one new share in the Company. Please refer to the notice of the ordinary general meeting for further information.

Further, certain of the Company's board members will convert their claims to new shares in the Company in connection with the Bond Conversion. Please find below an overview of the new shares to be subscribed by the board members in connection with the Bond Conversion:

-	Hans Gulseth (through Gulseth Holding AS): 5,805,528 new shares;
-	Hans Gulseth (through Christinedal AS): 1,597,728 new shares;
-	Hans Gulseth personally: 1,139,423 new shares;
-	Kåre Rødningen (through Rødningen Invest AS): 1,434,901 new shares;
-	Erik Sture Larre (through E. Larre Holding AS): 14,261,654 new shares; and
-	Erik Sture Larre (through CL-Holding AS): 5,805,528 new shares.

All of the new shares mentioned above will be subscribed at a subscription price of NOK 0.20 per share.

Completion of the share capital increase relating to the Bond Conversion is subject to approval by the Company's ordinary general meeting on 18 June 2019. Shareholders holding approximately 69 % of the shares in the Company has already committed to vote in favor of the proposed share capital increase.

About Black Sea Property AS:

Black Sea Property AS is a real estate company with holdings in a real estate project on the Black Sea coast called Sunrise Gardens. Sunrise Gardens Resort is a partially-completed project and will upon completion comprise approximately 950 apartments as well as significant commercial space. Black Sea Property AS owns indirectly 75% of the project. Black Sea Property AS will work to finalize Sunrise Gardens Resort, to realize the project's added value through the sale of residential units and hotel operations.
