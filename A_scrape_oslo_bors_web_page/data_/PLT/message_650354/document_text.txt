Tønsberg, 30 June 2025 - Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement
by poLight ASA ("poLight" or the "Company") published on 27 June 2025
regarding the final results of the subsequent offering of up to 19,122,933 new
shares, at a subscription price of NOK 2.69 per share (the "Subsequent
Offering"). Reference is further made to the stock exchange announcement
published on 20 June 2025, regarding the exercise of subscription rights by
certain primary insiders and their close associates, including
over-subscription in excess of their allocated subscription rights.

The allocation of shares in the Subsequent Offering was made available to the
subscribers today, including to the primary insiders and their closely
associated companies. Please see the attached forms for further details of the
allocation of shares to the primary insiders and their closely associated
companies following the exercise of subscription rights and subscription of
shares in the Subsequent Offering.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of
the Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12
of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

About poLight ASA
poLight ASA (listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange: PLT) offers a patented,
proprietary tunable optics technology, starting with its first product, TLens®
which replicates "the human eye" experience in autofocus cameras used in
devices such as smartphones, wearables, barcode scanners, machine vision
systems and various medical equipment. poLight's TLens® enables better system
performance and new user experiences due to benefits such as extremely fast
focus, small footprint, no magnetic interference, low power consumption and
constant field of view. poLight is based in Tønsberg, Norway, with employees
in Finland, France, UK, US, China, Taiwan, Japan, and the Philippines. For
more information, please visit https://www.polight.com
(https://www.polight.com/)
