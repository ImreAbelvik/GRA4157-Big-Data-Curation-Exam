primary insiders

September 5, 2022 - Karl Strömberg, primary insider of SmartCraft ASA, sold on
September 2, 2022 45 000 shares in SmartCraft at a price of NOK 17.74 per share.
The share sale was made to cover payable personal tax obligations related to the
IPO of SmartCraft ASA in June 2021.

After the transaction, Mr. Strömberg holds 123 235 shares in SmartCraft ASA.
Additionally he holds 110 000 shares in the SmartCraft long-term investment
program.

Please see the attached form for a detailed notification and public disclosure
of the transactions.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements set out in the Market
Abuse Regulation EU 596/2014 Article 19 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
Section 5-12.

ABOUT SMARTCRAFT ASA

SmartCraft is the leading Nordic provider of mission-critical SaaS solutions to
SME's in the construction sector. The company's business model is highly
scalable, based on 96% recurring revenue and low churn. The construction sector
is among the least digitalized industries and represents a NOK 10bn software
market in the Nordics, growing at a double-digit rate. SmartCraft's solutions
help customers to increase their productivity, margins, and resource efficiency.
