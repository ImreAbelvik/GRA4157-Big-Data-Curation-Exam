March 1, 2024

Timo Makkonen, primary insider of SmartCraft ASA, sold on February 29, 2024, 110
000 shares in SmartCraft at an average price of NOK 25.85 per share. The share
sale was made to invest in property for private use. After the transaction, Mr.
Makkonen holds 437 125 shares in SmartCraft ASA. Additionally he holds 230 000
shares in the SmartCraft long-term investment program.

Please see the attached forms for a detailed notification and public disclosure
of the transactions.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements set out in the Market
Abuse Regulation EU 596/2014 Article 19 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
Section 5-12.

About SmartCraft ASA

SmartCraft is the leading Nordic provider of mission-critical SaaS solutions to
SME's in the construction sector. The company's business model is highly
scalable, based on 97% recurring revenue and low churn. The construction sector
is among the least digitalized industries and represents a NOK 10bn software
market in the Nordics, growing at a double-digit rate. SmartCraft's solutions
help customers to increase their productivity, margins, and resource efficiency.
