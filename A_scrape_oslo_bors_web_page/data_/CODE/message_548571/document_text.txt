Oslo, Norway, 6 December 2021

Anton Lorenz Bondesen, Head of Business Development, and primary insider, has today acquired 100,000 shares in PatientSky (ISIN NO0010895667 / PSKY-ME) at an average price of NOK 3.1 per share. Following the transaction Anton Lorenz Bondesen and related parties hold 7,300,000 shares in the company, corresponding to 3.67% of the outstanding shares.
