Oslo, Norway, May 3, 2023

Codee Holding AS has today entered into an agreement to acquire 14.604.676 shares in PatientSky Group AS at an average price of NOK 0,50 per share. Codee Holding AS is controlled by Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen, Chairman of the Board, and primary insider. Following the transaction Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen and related parties hold 95.538.409 shares in the Company, corresponding to 48,04% of the outstanding shares.
