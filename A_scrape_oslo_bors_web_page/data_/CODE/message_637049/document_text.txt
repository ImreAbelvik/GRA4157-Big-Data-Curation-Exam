Oslo, Norway, January 23, 2025
 
Codee Holding AS has today sold 102.538.117 shares in CodeLab Capital AS in a block sale at NOK 1 krone (equal to an average price of NOK 0,00000001 per share). Codee Holding AS is controlled by Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen, Chairman of the Board, and primary insider. Following the transaction Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen and related parties hold 63.679.792 shares in the Company, corresponding to 18,63% of the outstanding shares.

The buyer is Lorenz AS, controlled by Anton Bondesen, which will own 30,60% of CodeLab Capital AS after the transaction.
