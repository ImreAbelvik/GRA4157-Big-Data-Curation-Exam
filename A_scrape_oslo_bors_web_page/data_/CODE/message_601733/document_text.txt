Oslo, Norway, October 19, 2023

Codee Holding AS has today acquired 1.300.000 shares in CodeLab Capital AS at an average price of NOK 0,6526 per share. Codee Holding AS is controlled by Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen, Chairman of the Board, and primary insider. Following the transaction Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen and related parties hold 122.573.943 shares in the Company, corresponding to 61,64% of the outstanding shares.
