Oslo, 27 January 2024

Codee Holding AS has today acquired 25.821.551 shares in CodeLab Capital AS at NOK 0.0442 per share. Codee Holding AS is controlled by Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen, Chairman of the Board, and primary insider. Following the transaction Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen and related parties hold 89.501.343 shares in the Company, corresponding to 26,19% of the outstanding shares.

La Padre Invest AS, 100% owned by CFO CodeLab Capital Christoffer Mathiesen, has today sold 2,000,000 shares at NOK 0.0442 per share. Following the transaction Christoffer Mathiesen and related parties hold 862,068 shares in the Company, corresponding to 0.25% of the outstanding shares.

KI Holding 2020 ApS, 100% owned by CEO CodeLab Capital Kristian Ikast, has today sold 1,000,000 shares at NOK 0.0442 per share. Following the transaction Kristian Ikast and related parties hold 2.463.945 shares in the Company, corresponding to 0,72% of the outstanding shares.

Nha Invest Holding, 100% owned by Board Member in CodeLab Capital, Nikolaj Helsinghoff Andersen, has today sold 500,000 shares at NOK 0.0442 per share. Following the transaction Nikolaj Helsinghoff Andersen and related parties hold 367.827 shares in the Company, corresponding to 0,11% of the outstanding shares.

This stock announcement has been published pursuant to article 19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
