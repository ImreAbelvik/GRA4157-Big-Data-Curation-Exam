Oslo, Norway, October 11, 2023

Codee Holding AS has today acquired 542.854 shares in CodeLab Capital AS at an average price of NOK 0,7942 per share. Codee Holding AS is controlled by Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen, Chairman of the Board, and primary insider. Following the transaction Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen and related parties hold 121.273.943 shares in the Company, corresponding to 60,98% of the outstanding shares.
