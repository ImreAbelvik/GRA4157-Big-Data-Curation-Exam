CodeLab Capital AS (the "Company") has received notifications from Codee Holding AS, close associate to primary insider and chair of the board of directors, Jesper Melin Ganc-Pedersen, regarding the redelivery of shares by Arctic Securities AS pursuant to a share lending agreement entered into between Codee Holding AS, Arctic Securities AS and the Company.

The new shares were issued following the registration of the share capital increase with the Norwegian Register of Business Enterprises on 31 July 2024, and subsequently, Arctic Securities AS returned 34,193,078 shares to Codee Holding AS.

The loan of shares was to facilitate delivery-versus-payment settlement of tranche 2 of the new shares in the Company allocated in the private placement which was placed on 26 June 2024 (the "Private Placement").

Please see the attached form for further detail about the transaction.

Following the redelivery of the shares, and the delivery of the shares allocated to Codee Holding AS in the Private Placement, and adjusted for the shares sold to Peter Tuborgh Nielsen as announced on 22 July 2024, Codee Holding AS holds 166,217,909 shares, equal to approx. 48.63% of the shares and capital in the Company based on a total of 341,793,722 outstanding shares. The ownership will be 49.65% when adjusting for the shares allocated to Codee Holding AS following the settlement of the guarantee provision.
