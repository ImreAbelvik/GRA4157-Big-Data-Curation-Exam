Oslo, Norway, March 03, 2025

Lorenz AS has today acquirted 1.000.000 shares in CodeLab Capital AS at an average price of NOK 0,0623 per share. Lorenz AS is controlled by Anton Bondesen, Chairman of the Board, and primary insider. Following the transaction Anton Bondesen and related parties hold 105.573.414 shares in the Company, corresponding to 30,89% of the outstanding shares.
