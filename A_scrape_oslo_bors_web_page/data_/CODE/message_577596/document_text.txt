Oslo, Norway, December 7, 2022

Codee Holding AS has today acquired 195.886 shares in PatientSky Group AS at an average price of NOK 1,306 per share. Codee Holding AS is controlled by Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen, Chairman of the Board, and primary insider. Following the transaction Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen and related parties hold 75.517.180 shares in the Company, corresponding to 37,97% of the outstanding shares.
