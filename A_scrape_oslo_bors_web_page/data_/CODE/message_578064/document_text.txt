Oslo, Norway, December 13, 2022

Codee Holding AS has today acquired 5.416.553 shares in PatientSky Group AS at an average price of NOK 1,340 per share. Codee Holding AS is controlled by Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen, Chairman of the Board, and primary insider. Following the transaction Jesper Melin Ganc-Petersen and related parties hold 80.933.733 shares in the Company, corresponding to 40,70% of the outstanding shares.
