Friminutt AS, a company controlled by Gunnar Gullaksen, CFO and Board member of Matvareexpressen AS has 3 October 2023 purchased 210 shares in Matvareexpressen AS at an average price of NOK 75 per share. After this transaction Friminutt AS holds 5600 shares in the Company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the market abuse regulation MAR article 19 and 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
