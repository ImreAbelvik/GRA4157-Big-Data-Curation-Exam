Anooj Unarket, Board member of Otello Corporation ASA, has as of 26 September 2019 purchased, on behalf of certain related parties, 54,523 shares in Otello Corporation ASA at a price of NOK 14.19 per share.
 
The balance of Anooj Unarket and related parties' holdings after the transaction totals 30,546,559 shares.
