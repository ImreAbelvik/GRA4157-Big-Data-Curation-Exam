Anooj Unarket, Board member of Otello Corporation ASA, has on behalf of certain related parties, as of 17th September 2020, acquired interests in 26,981 shares of Otello Corporation ASA at an average price of NOK 17.50 per share.

The balance of Anooj Unarket and related parties' holdings after the transaction totals 38,926,687 shares.
