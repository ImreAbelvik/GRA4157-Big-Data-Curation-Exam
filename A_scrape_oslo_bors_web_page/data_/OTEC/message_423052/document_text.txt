On March 20 2017, DNB has, on behalf of Opera Software, purchased 11,496 shares
for use in the group's employee share purchase plan (ESPP). The shares have been
acquired at a price of NOK 38.0355, based on the price obtained by option
holders March 20th 2017. The discounted share price to the employees is NOK
30.4284 per share.

The following primary insiders has purchased shares:

unexercised options in the Company, prior to contingent options grants at Opera
EGM 4th April 2017. As of today the primary insider holds 5 525 shares in the
Company.

Jørgen Jordet purchased 525 shares at NOK 30.4284. As of today the primary
insider holds 925 shares in the Company and no options, prior to contingent
options grants at Opera EGM 4th April 2017.

Petter Lade purchased 525 shares at NOK 30.4284. Petter Lade holds 40,000
unexercised options in the Company, prior to contingent options grants at Opera
