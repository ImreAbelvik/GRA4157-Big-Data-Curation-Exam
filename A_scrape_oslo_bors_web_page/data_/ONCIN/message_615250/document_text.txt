Sarsia Seed AS, a company closely associated to Sveinung Hole, Member of the
Board of Directors of BerGenBio ASA, has on 4th and 5th April 2024 sold in total
2,940,000 shares in BerGenBio ASA at an average price of NOK 0.1705. Following
these transactions, Sarsia Seed AS holds 5,160,000 shares in the Company and
4,050,000 Warrants.

The transactions are conducted to fund exercise of Warrants received in the
Rights Issue in June 2023 with expiry date 15 April 2024. Please see further
details about the transactions in the attached forms.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
