Sandnes, Norway – Malene Sandøy has on 16 March 2023 purchased 30.000 shares in Horisont Energi AS (EURONEXT: HRGI), at NOK 9.88 per share.  
 
After the transactions Malene Sandøy has a total holding of 30.000 shares and 37.500 share options in Horisont Energi AS.  
 
Malene Sandøy is Chief HSSE Officer in Horisont Energi AS. Details of the transaction attached.  
 
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Regulation EU 596/2014 article 19, no. 3, and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
