On 9 September 2015, Sora AS entered into a forward 
contract to purchase 40,000 shares in NRC Group ASA 
at a price of NOK 33.80 per share. The term of the 
forward contract is approximately six months, with 
expiry on 1 March 2016. At the same time, Sora AS
sold 40,000 shares in NRC Group ASA to the 
counterparty in the forward contract, at a price of 
NOK 33.32 per share.

Sora AS is wholly owned by Alfred Beck, who is the 
managing director of the Rail division in NRC Group 
ASA.

Following the sale, Sora AS owns 360,000 shares in 
NRC Group ASA, representing approximately 1.38% of 
the outstanding shares and votes in the company.
Inclusive of the shares comprised by the forward 
contract, Sora AS' number of shares and votes are 
unchanged.

This notification is made with reference to Sections
4-2 and 4-4 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
