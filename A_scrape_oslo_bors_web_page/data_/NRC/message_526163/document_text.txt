Henning Olsen, CEO of NRC Group ASA, has today purchased 30,000 shares in NRC
Group ASA at an average price of NOK 17.44 per share.

After the purchase, Henning Olsen holds 92,773 shares and 10,000 share options
in NRC Group ASA.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements to section 5-12 of
the Norwegian Securities Trading act.
