NRC Group ASA (the "Company") has completed a limited share program for its new
employees in acquired companies in 2017, such as ALTi Bygg og Anlegg AS, HAG
Anlegg AS and Gravco AS, which was not included in the share program for 2016.
The employees have been offered the opportunity to purchase shares in the
Company at 20% discount for a total amount of either NOK 12,000, NOK 20,000 or
NOK 32,000. The offer period was completed on 4 September 2016. The purchase
price per share before the discount was NOK 54.00, corresponding to the trading
price of the Company's share on the Oslo Stock Exchange on 5 September 2017.

A total of 34 new employees accepted the opportunity to become active owners in
the Company and the majority of the participating employees opted for the
largest package. The Company has today completed transfer of a total of 22,937
of its shares held in treasury to the employees participating in the program.
The Company holds 245,228 shares in the Company in treasury following the
transaction.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
