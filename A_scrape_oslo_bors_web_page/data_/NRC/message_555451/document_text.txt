Henning Olsen, Chief Executive Officer in NRC Group ASA, has on 2 March
2022 purchased 15,000 shares in NRC Group ASA at an average price of NOK 19.17.

After the purchase, Henning Olsen holds 110,025 shares and 30,000 share
options in NRC Group ASA.

Please refer to the attached notification of trading for further details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of
the Regulation. EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of
the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
