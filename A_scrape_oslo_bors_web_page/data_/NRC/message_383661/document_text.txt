As of today Urbex Invest AS has subscribed for 49 
331 shares in the repair offering in NRC Group ASA. 
In addition Urbex Invest AS has acquired 15 980 
shares in NRC Group ASA at NOK 27 per share.

Kristian G. Lundkvist, who is chairman of the board 
of directors of NRC Group ASA, is (indirectly) the 
majority shareholder of Urbex Invest AS, through the 
companies Middelborg AS and Merckx AS.

Given full allocation, Urbex Invest AS will own 5 
571 828 shares in NRC Group ASA, representing 
approximately 21% of the votes in the company after 
completion of the repair issue.

For further information please contact Chairman of 
the board of NRC Group ASA, Kristian Lundkvist, 
(+47) 977 77 177

This notification is made with reference to Sections 
4-2 and 4-4 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
