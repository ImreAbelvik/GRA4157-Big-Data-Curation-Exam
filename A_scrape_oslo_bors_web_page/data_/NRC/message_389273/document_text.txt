On 11 November 2015, Urbex Invest AS has purchased 850,000 
shares in the NRC Group ASA at a price of NOK 24.93 per 
share in accordance with the terms of the forward contract 
entered into on 13 May 2015. Furthermore, Urbex Invest AS 
has entered into a new forward contract to purchase 975,000 
shares in NRC Group ASA at a price of NOK 43.749 per share. 
The term of the forward contract is six and a half months, 
with expiry on 25 May 2013. At the same time, Urbex Invest 
AS sold 975,000 shares in NRC Group ASA to the counterparty 
in the forward contract, at a price of NOK 43 per share.

Kristian G. Lundkvist, who is the chairman of the board of 
directors of NRC Group ASA, is (indirectly) the majority 
shareholder of Urbex Invest AS, through the companies 
Middelborg AS and Merckx AS.

Following the above transactions, Urbex Invest AS owns 
5,446,828 shares in NRC Group ASA, representing 
approximately 19.65% of the total shares and votes in the 
company. After the completion of the private placement 
announced by NRC Group ASA on 9 and 10 November 2015, Urbex 
Invest AS' shareholding will correspond to approximately 
16.97% of the total shares and votes in the company.

Inclusive of the shares comprised by the forward contracts, 
Urbex Invest AS' number of shares and votes are unchanged.

This notification is made with reference to Sections 4-2 and 
4-4 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
