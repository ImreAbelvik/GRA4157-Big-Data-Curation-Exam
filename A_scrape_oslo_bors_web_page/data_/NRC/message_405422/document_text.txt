Urbex Invest AS currently owns 5,446,828 shares in NRC 
Group ASA (not including 975,000 shares covered by a 
forward contract), representing approximately 15.4% of 
the total shares and votes in the company (and 
approximately 18.2%of including the forward contract). 

Kristian G. Lundkvist, who is a member of the board of 
directors of NRC Group ASA, is (indirectly) the 
majority shareholder in Urbex Invest AS (owns 85.8% of 
the shares and votes), through the companies 
Middelborg AS and Merckx AS.
 
On 4 July 2016, the shareholders of Urbex Invest AS 
approved a resolution to split the company, in such a 
way that the minority shareholders' shares in Urbex 
Invest AS will be redeemed in a share capital 
reduction in exchange for a consideration of in total 
1,039,825 shares in NRC Group ASA. 

After completion of the transactions, Middelborg AS 
and Merckx AS will own 100% of the shares and votes in 
Urbex Invest AS, which in turn will own 4,407,003 
shares in NRC Group ASA (excluding the aforementioned 
975,000 shares covered by a forward contract), 
representing approximately 12.5% of the total shares 
and votes (and 15.2% of the total shares and votes 
including the forward contract).

Kristian G. Lundkvist's effective ownership in NRC 
Group ASA will remain the same, as the transactions in 
Urbex Invest AS relate to the minority shareholders 
only.

This notification is made with reference to Sections 
4-2 to 4-4 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act 
(Norwegian: verdipapirhandelloven).
