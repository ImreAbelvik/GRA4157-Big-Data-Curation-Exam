Lars André Gjerdrum, board member of NRC Group ASA 
has today subscribed for a total 3,000 shares in the 
repair offering in NRC Group ASA. Assuming that all 
subscribed shares is allocated, Mr Gjerdrum's 
holdings after completing the repair issue will be 
8,000 shares in NRC Group ASA.
  
The information in this report is mandatory under 
the Securities Act § 5-12.
