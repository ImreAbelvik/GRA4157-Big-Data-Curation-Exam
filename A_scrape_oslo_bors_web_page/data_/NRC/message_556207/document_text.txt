Jodfabrikken AS, a company wholly owned by Ole Anton Gulsvik, Chief Financial
Officer in NRC Group ASA, has on 10 March 2022 purchased 53,996 shares in NRC
Group ASA  at an average price of NOK 20.66 per share.

Following the transaction, Jodfabrikken AS owns 133,996 shares in NRC Group ASA.

Please refer to the attached notification of trading for further details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the
Regulation. EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the
Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

The stock exchange announcement was published by Siri McKellar Nilsen,
Administration, NRC Group ASA
