On 17 November 2016, Sogn Invest AS entered into a 
forward contract to purchase 325,000 shares in NRC 
Group ASA at a price of NOK 44.06 per share. The term 
of the forward contract expires on 19 May 2017. At the 
same time, Sogn Invest AS sold 325,000 shares in NRC 
Group ASA to the counterparty in the forward contract, 
at a price of NOK 43.25 per share.

Sogn Invest AS is wholly owned by Gjermund Sogn, 
business developer for NRC Rail Norge. 

After the sale, Sogn Invest AS owns 1,281,046 shares 
in NRC Group ASA, representing approximately 3.63% of 
the outstanding shares and votes in the company. 
Inclusive of the shares comprised by the forward 
contract, Sogn Invest AS' number of shares and votes 
are unchanged.
