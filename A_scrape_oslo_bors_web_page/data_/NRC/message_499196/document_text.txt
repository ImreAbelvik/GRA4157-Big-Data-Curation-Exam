Robert Röder, MD of NRC Sweden, has on 23 March 2020 purchased 7,000 shares in
NRC Group ASA at a price of NOK 20.46 per share. After the purchase, Robert
Röder holds 20,000 shares and 3,125 share options in NRC Group ASA.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements to section 5-12 of
the Norwegian Securities Trading act.
