On 25 May 2016, Urbex Invest AS has purchased 975,000 
shares in the NRC Group ASA at a price of NOK 43.7490 
per share in accordance with the terms of the forward 
contract entered into on 11 November 2015. 
Furthermore, Urbex Invest AS has entered into a new 
forward contract to purchase 975,000 shares at a price 
of NOK 44.4380 per share. The term of the forward 
contract is six months, with expiry on 21 November 
2016. At the same time, Urbex Invest AS has sold 
975,000 shares to the counterparty in the forward 
contract, at a price of NOK 43.7490 per share.

Kristian G. Lundkvist, who is member of the of the 
board of directors of NRC Group ASA, is (indirectly) 
the majority shareholder of Urbex Invest AS, through 
the companies Middelborg AS and Merckx AS.

Following the above transactions, Urbex Invest AS owns 
5,446,828 shares in NRC Group ASA (not including the 
shares covered by the forward contract), representing 
approximately 15.43% of the total shares and votes in 
the company. 

The above shareholding of Urbex Invest AS includes 
1,039,795 shares in NRC Group ASA that have been lent 
to Lundkvist's co-owners in Urbex Invest AS in 
anticipation of a possible redemption of the co-owners 
in exchange for a distribution from Urbex Invest AS of 
the same number of shares in NRC Group ASA.  If such 
redemption be carried out, Lundkvist's effective 
ownership in NRC Group ASA will remain the same, as 
the possible transactions in Urbex Invest AS will 
relate to the minority shareholders only.

This notification is made with reference to Sections 4-
2 and 4-4 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
