On 8 December 2017, a party related to Geir Nilsen, MD of NRC Norway, has
acquired 2,000 shares in NRC Group ASA at a price of NOK 55.50 per share.

Following this transaction, Geir Nilsen and related parties owns 113,539 shares
in NRC Group ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 5
- 12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
