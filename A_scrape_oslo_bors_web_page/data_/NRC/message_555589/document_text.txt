Outi Henriksson, member of the Board of Directors of NRC Group ASA, has on 3
March 2022 purchased 5,000 shares in NRC Group ASA at an average price of NOK
19.69.

After the purchase, Outi Henriksson holds 5,000 shares in NRC Group ASA.

Please refer to the attached notification of trading for further details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of
the Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of
the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

The stock exchange announcement was published by Siri McKellar Nilsen,
Administration, NRC Group ASA.
