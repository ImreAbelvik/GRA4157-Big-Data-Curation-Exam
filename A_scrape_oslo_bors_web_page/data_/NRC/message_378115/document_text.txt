On 13 May 2015, Urbex Invest AS entered into a 
forward contract to purchase 850,000 shares in Blom 
ASA at a price of NOK 24.93 per share. The term of 
the forward contract is six months, with expiry on 
11 November 2015. At the same time, Urbex Invest AS 
sold 850,000 shares in Blom ASA to the counterparty 
in the forward contract, at a price of NOK 24.50 per 
share. 

Kristian G. Lundkvist, who is a member of the board 
of directors of Blom ASA, is (indirectly) the 
majority shareholder of Urbex Invest AS, through the 
companies Middelborg AS and Merckx AS.

Following sale, Urbex Invest AS owns 5,506,517 
shares in Blom ASA, representing approximately 54.68 
% of the total shares and votes in the company. 
Inclusive of the shares comprised by the forward 
contract, Urbex Invest AS' number of shares and 
votes are unchanged.

This notification is made with reference to Sections 
4-2 and 4-4 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
