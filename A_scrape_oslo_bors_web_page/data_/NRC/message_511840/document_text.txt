CEO Henning Olsen has on 20 August 2020 purchased 20,000 shares in NRC Group ASA
at an average price of NOK 28.19. After the purchase, Henning Olsen holds 62,773
shares and 10,000 share options in NRC Group ASA.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements to section 5-12 of
the Norwegian Securities Trading act.
