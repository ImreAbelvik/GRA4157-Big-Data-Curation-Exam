Oslo, 7 April 2017. Reference is made to the announcement made by 
NRC Group ASA (the "Company"') on 31 March 2017 in connection with 
its decision to initiate a share buyback program for up to NOK 10 
million in the market. 

The Company has today bought 50.000 shares in the Company through 
broker at an average price of NOK 59,4223 per share.
The Company's total holding of treasury shares after this 
transaction is 168.165 shares.
