On 14 May 2018, Charlotte Holding AS entered into a forward contract to purchase
500,000 shares in NRC Group ASA at a price of NOK 64.02 per share. The term of
the forward contract expires on 12 November 2018. At the same time,
Charlotte Holding AS sold 500,000 shares in NRC Group ASA to the counterparty in
the forward contract, at a price of NOK 62.93 per share.

Charlotte Holding AS is wholly owned by Øivind Horpestad, who is the CEO of
NRC Group ASA.

Charlotte Holding AS owns 1,328,008 shares in NRC Group ASA,
representing approximately 3.14% of the outstanding shares and votes in the
company. Inclusive of the shares comprised by the forward contract, Charlotte
Holding AS' number of shares and votes are unchanged.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
