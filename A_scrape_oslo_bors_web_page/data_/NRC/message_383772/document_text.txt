Reference is made the stock exchange announcement 
made 19. August 2015 regarding the final result and 
allocation of NRC Group ASA ("Company") offering of 
up to 370,370 new shares (the "Subsequent 
Offering").  

Primary insiders in the Company have today been 
allocated shares in connection with the  subsequent 
Offering as follows:

Kristian Lundkvist, Chairman of the Board, has been 
allocated 49 331 shares through the company Urbex 
Invest AS. Kristian Lundkvist has following this a 
shareholding of 5 655 848 shares in the Company 
through Urbex Invest AS.

Lars Andrè Gjerdrum member of the Board, has been 
allocated 422 shares. Lars Andrè Gjerdrum has 
following this a shareholding of 5 422 shares in the 
Company.
