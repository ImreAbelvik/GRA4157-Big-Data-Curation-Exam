On 1 March 2016, Sora AS has purchased 40,000 shares 
in NRC Group ASA at a price of NOK 33.80 per share in 
accordance with the terms of the forward contract 
entered into on 9 September 2015. Furthermore, Sora AS 
has entered into a new forward contract to purchase 
40,000 shares in NRC Group ASA at a price of NOK 34.30 
per share. The term of the forward contract expires on 
19. August 2016. At the same time, Sora AS sold 40,000 
shares in NRC Group ASA to the counterparty in the 
forward contract, at a price of NOK 33.80 per share. 
Sora AS is wholly owned by Alfred Beck, who is the 
managing director of NRC Rail Group AS. 

Following these transactions, Sora AS owns 385,000 
shares in NRC Group ASA, representing approximately 
1.10% of the outstanding shares and votes in the 
company. Inclusive of the shares comprised by the 
forward contract, Sora AS' number of shares and votes 
are unchanged.

This notification is made with reference to Sections 
4-2 and 4-4 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
