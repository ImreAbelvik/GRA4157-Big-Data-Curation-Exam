NRC Group ASA (the "Company") has completed the 2016 share 
program for its employees in the Company's subsidiaries in 
Norway and Sweden where the employees have been offered the 
opportunity to purchase shares in the Company at a 20% 
discount for a total amount of either NOK 6,000, NOK 12,000 
or NOK 32,000. The offer period was completed on 23 November 
2016. The purchase price per share before the discount was 
NOK 68.75 corresponding to the trading price of the NRC 
Group ASA share on the Oslo Stock Exchange on 24 November 
2016.

The Company is very pleased with the reception of the share 
program among the employees, and by the end of the offer 
period a total of 82 employees had accepted the opportunity 
to become active owners in the Company. The majority of the 
participating employees opted for the largest package, and 
the Company has today completed the transfer of a total of 
43,025 of its shares held in treasury to the employees 
participating in the program. The Company holds 18,165 
shares in NRC Group ASA in treasury following the 
transaction. 

The following primary insiders purchased shares:
Chief Financial Officer Dag Fladby purchased 581 shares. 
Following this purchase, he owns 10,581 shares.
Marketing & Communications manager Cecilie Blaauw Cock 
purchased 581 shares. Following this purchase, she owns 602 
shares.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading 
Act
