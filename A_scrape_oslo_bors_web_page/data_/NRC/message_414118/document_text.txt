On 18 November 2016, Sora AS has entered into a 
forward contract to purchase 35,000 shares in 
NRC Group ASA at a price of NOK 45.77 per share. 
The term of the forward contract expires on 19 May 
2017. At the same time, Sora AS sold 35,000 shares in 
NRC Group ASA to the counterparty in the forward 
contract, at a price of NOK 44.90 per share. Sora AS 
is wholly owned by Alfred Beck, who is General Counsel 
of NRC Group ASA. 
 
Following these transactions, Sora AS owns 390,000 
shares in NRC Group ASA, representing approximately 
1.10% of the outstanding shares and votes in the 
company. Inclusive of the shares comprised by the 
forward contract, Sora AS' number of shares and votes 
are unchanged.
 
This notification is made with reference to Sections 
4-2 and 4-4 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
