On 19 August 2016, Sora AS has purchased 5,000 shares 
in NRC Group ASA at a price of NOK 34.30 per share in 
accordance with the terms of the forward contract 
entered into on 1 March 2016. Furthermore, Sora AS has 
entered into a new forward contract to purchase 35,000 
shares in NRC Group ASA at a price of NOK 34.87 per 
share. The term of the forward contract expires on 17 
February 2017. Sora AS is wholly owned by Alfred Beck, 
who is General Counsel of NRC Group ASA. 

Following these transactions, Sora AS owns 390,000 
shares in NRC Group ASA, representing approximately 
1.10% of the outstanding shares and votes in the 
company. Inclusive of the shares comprised by the 
forward contract, Sora AS' number of shares and votes 
are unchanged.

This notification is made with reference to Sections 
4-2 and 4-4 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
