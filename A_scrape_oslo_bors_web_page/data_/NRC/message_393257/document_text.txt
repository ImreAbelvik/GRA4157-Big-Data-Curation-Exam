Oslo, 19 January 2016. NRC Group ASA (the «Company») 
has implemented a share program for its employees in 
NRC Rail Group's subsidiaries in Norway and Sweden 
where the employees have been offered the opportunity 
to purchase shares in NRC Group ASA at a 20% discount 
for a total amount of either NOK 6,000, NOK 12,000 or 
NOK 32,000. The offer period was completed in December 
2015. The purchase price per share before the discount 
was NOK 46.60, corresponding to the trading price of 
the NRC Group ASA share on the Oslo Stock Exchange on 
30 November 2015, being the day before the offer 
period started. 
 
The Company is very pleased with the reception of the 
share program among the employees, and by the end of 
the offer period a total of 108 employees had accepted 
the opportunity to become active owners in the 
Company. The majority of the participating employees 
opted for the largest package, and the Company has 
today completed the transfer of a total of 85,262 of 
its shares held in treasury to the employees 
participating in the program. The Company holds 61,190 
shares in NRC Group ASA in treasury following the 
transaction. 
                
This information is subject of the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act.
