Nordiske Renholdsprodukter AS (Company partly owned by Hans Petter Grette, Sales and Marketing Director of SoftOx Solutions AS) on Wednesday 4/4 sold 40,000 shares in SoftOx Solutions AS at an average price of NOK 101.70. After the transaction, the company owns 81,400 shares in SoftOx Solutions AS. This was done for tax reasons and to enable the company to exercise options according to written agreement.

Furthermore, on Wednesday 15/4, Nordiske Renholdsprodukter AS called the following options in SoftOx Solutions AS: 27,600 options at price NOK 66,00 and 10,000 options at price NOK 90,00 - a total of 37,600 options.

After the transaction, Nordiske Renholdsprodukter AS and Hans Petter Grette owns a total of 119,000 shares in SoftOx Solutions AS.
