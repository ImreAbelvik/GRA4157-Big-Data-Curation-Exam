The board of SoftOx Solutions has approved the completion of a right issue approved in the General Assembly on the 26th of June 2018. The right issue is directed to primary insider; Beate Rygg Johnsen, Strategic Business Development Director in SoftOx Solutions AS with 21,800 shares at a price of NOK 22 per share.

The company's share capital after the issue of totally NOK 92,520 is divided between 4,626,200 shares with a nominal price of NOK 0.02/share.

Beate Rygg Johnsen, owns after this transaction 21,800 shares in SoftOx Solutions AS
