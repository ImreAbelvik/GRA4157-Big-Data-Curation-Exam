Oslo, Norway, 17 April 2023

Zalaris ASA has on 17 April granted 56,756 restricted stock units (RSUs) to corporate and regional management, as part of the Company’s annual bonus program. The RSUs granted are in lieu of cash bonuses. The RSUs vest after 36 months and each RSU corresponds to one share.  

The following primary insiders have been allocated RSUs as part of the grant:

-	Hans-Petter Mellerud, CEO: 14,954
-	Richard Schiørn: EVP Solution & Delivery Global Managed Services: 5,333
-	Øyvind Reiten, EVP BD - Global Managed Services: 11,432
-	Gunnar Manum, CFO: 5,431
-	Hilde Karlsmyr, CHRO: 5,006
