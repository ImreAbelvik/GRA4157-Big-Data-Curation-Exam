Hans-Petter Mellerud, CEO and primary insider of Zalaris 
ASA, has through his wholly owned investment company 
Norwegian Retail AS today sold 100'000 shares in Zalaris ASA 
(ZAL) at NOK 48,00 per share.  At the same time Norwegian 
Retail AS has bought on forward contracts for 100'000 shares 
in Zalaris ASA at a strike price of NOK 48,38 for delivery 
on 15th of March 2018. Norwegian Retail AS' total holding 
after this transaction is 2'941'482 shares in Zalaris ASA.
