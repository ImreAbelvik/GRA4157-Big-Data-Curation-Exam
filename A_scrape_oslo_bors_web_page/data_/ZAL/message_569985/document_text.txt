Oslo, Norway, 29 August 2022

On 26th August 2022, due to an internal rebalancing, entities connected to primary insider Kenth Eriksson, a member of the board, re-allocated Zalaris ASA shares. Athanase Industrial Partners Fund II sold 9 192 shares at NOK 25.1259 and Athanase Industrial Partners II KB bought 9 192 shares at NOK 25.1259. After the transaction entities connected to Kenth Eriksson hold in aggregate 803 269 shares representing 3.63% of the shares outstanding. The entities, Athanase Industrial Partners II KB and Athanase Industrial Partners Fund II own 732 576 and 70 693 shares respectively.
