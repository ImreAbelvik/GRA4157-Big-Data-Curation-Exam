Oslo, Norway, 9 December 2021 

 
Zalaris ASA has on 8 December completed the annual share purchase program for employees. As part of the program, Zalaris has sold 20,777 own shares to employees at a subscription price of NOK 43.24 per share. The shares will be transferred to the employees on or about 13 December 2021. The subscription price was based on the volume-weighted average share price in the period between 18 November to 30 November 2021, less 20% discount. To receive the discount the shares have a 12 months lock-up period. 

The following primary insiders have been allocated 277 shares each:

Hans-Petter Mellerud, CEO
Gunnar Manum, CFO
Hilde Karlsmyr, CHRO
Richard Schiørn, EVP Solution & Delivery MS
William Jackson, EVP and MD UK & Ireland 
Øyvind Reiten, EVP BD Global Managed Services
