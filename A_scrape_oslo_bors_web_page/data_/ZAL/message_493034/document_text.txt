Oslo, Norway, 8 January 2020

Zalaris ASA (“Zalaris” or the “Company”) has granted share options to executive management and key employees. 130,000 options were granted at a strike price of NOK 21.04 per share, 185,500 options were granted at a strike price of NOK 25.18 per share, 17,500 options were granted at a strike price of NOK 25.25 per share, and 10,000 options were granted at a strike price of NOK 25.78 per share. The strike price will be increased by 1% per month until fully vested. 60% of the options granted will vest after 36 months, while the remaining 40% will vest after 60 months. Each share option corresponds to one share. The Company has also granted 5,000 restricted stock units (RSUs), with settlement on 30 May 2022. Please refer to the annual report 2018 for further details regarding the Company’s share incentive plans.
