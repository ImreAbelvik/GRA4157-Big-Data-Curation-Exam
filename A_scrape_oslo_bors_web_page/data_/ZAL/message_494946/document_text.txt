Zalaris ASA ("Zalaris" or the "Company") has completed the annual share purchase
program for employees.

As part of the program, Zalaris has sold 52,460 own shares to employees at a
subscription price of NOK 20.62 per share. Following the sale Zalaris will hold
502,167 own shares. The shares will be transferred to the employees on or about
31 March 2020. The subscription price was based on the volume-weighted average
share price in the period between 18 December to 30 December 2019, less a 20%
discount. To receive the discount the shares have a 12 months lock-up period.
