
Oslo, 4 September 2025

Employees of Zalaris ASA has on 3 September exercised 680,000 share options, which expired after the release of the financial report for the second quarter 2025. The share options are settled in cash. For more information about Zalaris’ share incentive plans, please refer to the annual report for 2024. 

The following primary insiders of Zalaris have exercised share options:

• Hans-Petter Mellerud, CEO: 100,000 options
• Hilde Karlsmyr, CHRO: 56,000 options
• Halvor Leirvåg, CTO: 56,000 options
• Øyvind Reiten, EVP Group Commercial and Sales: 56,000 options
• Richard Schiørn, EVP Solution & Delivery Global Managed Services: 56,000 options
• Gunnar Manum, CFO: 56,000 options

Please refer to the attached schedules for further details.
