Hans-Petter Mellerud, CEO and primary insider of Zalaris ASA, has through his wholly owned investment company Norwegian Retail AS today sold 100'000 shares in Zalaris ASA (ZAL) at NOK 49,00 per share. At the same time Norwegian Retail AS increase forward contracts from 100'000 shares to 200'000 shares in Zalaris ASA at a strike price of NOK 49,24 for delivery on 21st of September 2018.  Norwegian Retail AS' total holding after this transaction is unchanged at 2'841'482 shares and 200'000 shares on forward contract in Zalaris ASA.
