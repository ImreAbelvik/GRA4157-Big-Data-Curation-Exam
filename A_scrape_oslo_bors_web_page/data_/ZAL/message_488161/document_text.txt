Primary insider Hans-Petter Mellerud, CEO of Zalaris ASA 

has today on the 30th of October 2019 acquired 1000 shares in Zalaris ASA at NOK 21.00 per share on behalf of his son Hans-Herman Eloranta Mellerud.  
After the transaction Hans-Petter Mellerud controls a total of 3’092’482 shares in Zalaris ASA distributed as 1000 shares held privately and 3’091’482 shares through his wholly owned investment company Norwegian Retail AS.
