29 October, 2021

Through an internal reorganisation, Jan M. Koivurinta, Zalaris ASA board member and primary insider, has on 29 October sold 295,139 shares in Zalaris ASA to Captium Oy, a company controlled by Mr. Koivurinta, at an average price of NOK 53.20 per share. The number of shares in Zalaris ASA owned, directly or indirectly, by Jan M. Koivurinta after the transaction is 295,139. Please refer to the attached filing for further information.
