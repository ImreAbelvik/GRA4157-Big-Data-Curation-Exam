
Oslo, 1 September 2025

Hans-Petter Mellerud, founder and CEO and primary insider of Zalaris ASA (the "Company"), has on 1 September bought 2,000 shares in the Company. 

Following the transaction, Hans-Petter Mellerud holds 25,365 shares in the Company in addition to the 2,891,482 shares that he owns through his 100% owned investment company Norwegian Retail AS representing approximately 13,2% of the share capital and votes in the Company.
