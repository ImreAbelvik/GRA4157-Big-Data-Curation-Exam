
Oslo, Norway, 23 April 2025

Zalaris ASA has on 22 April settled 37,974 fully vested restricted stock units (RSUs) through the transfer of the same number of shares to employees.

The following primary insiders will receive shares:

- Hans-Petter Mellerud, CEO: 11,272
- Richard Schiørn, EVP Solution & Delivery Global Managed Services: 4,020
- Øyvind Reiten, EVP Group Commercial and Sales: 6,155
- Gunnar Manum, CFO: 4,094
- Hilde Karlsmyr, CHRO: 3,773
- Halvor Leirvåg, CTO: 3,666
