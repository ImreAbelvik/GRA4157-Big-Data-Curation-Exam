Halvor Leirvåg, Chief Technology Officer at Zalaris ASA, has on Thursday the 12th of September 2019 transferred all holdings of his  Zalaris ASA shares between accounts in a Nordnet internal trade:

6410 Shares sold 12.09.2019 at 20,50NOK
6410 Shares bought back 12.09.2019 at 20,50NOK

And on Friday the 13th of September:

1415 additional shares bought at an average price of 20,50NOK.

Following these transactions Halvor Leirvåg holds 7.825 Shares in Zalaris.
