has sold in total 20.000 shares of Zalaris ASA. The 
purpose of the sale is to provide equity connected 
to real estate purchase. The shares were sold at a 
price of NOK 33.60.

129.608 shares of Zalaris ASA whereof 37.500 shares 
is owned by his company Westerland AS.
