Oslo, Norway, 17 April 2023

Zalaris ASA (“Zalaris” or the “Company”) has on 14 April granted 860,000 share options to employees at a strike price of NOK 36.93 per share. The options vest after 36 months. Each share option corresponds to one share. Please refer to the latest annual report for further details regarding the Company’s share incentive plans.  

The following primary insiders of Zalaris have been allocated share options as part of the grant: 
 
•	Hans-Petter Mellerud, CEO: 100,000 options   
•	Hilde Karlsmyr, CHRO: 55,000 options  
•	Halvor Leirvåg, CTO: 55,000 options  
•	Øyvind Reiten, EVP Business Development – GMS: 55,000 options  
•	Richard Schiørn, EVP Solution & Delivery – GMS: 55,000 options 
•	Gunnar Manum, CFO: 55,000 options
