Oslo, Norway, 25 June, 2021

Reference is made to the announcement by Zalaris ASA ("Zalaris" or the "Company"), on 10 June 2021, regarding the completion of a private placement of 2,322,300 shares in the Company (the "Offer Shares") at a price of NOK 60 per Offer Share at the date hereof (the "Private Placement"). 

Norwegian Retail AS ("NRAS"), a close associate of the Company's CEO, Hans Petter Mellerud, had entered into a share lending agreement with Arctic Securities AS, on behalf of the managers in the Private Placement, and the Company whereby NRAS lent 2,012,300 shares in the Company to Arctic Securities AS in order to facilitate delivery of listed shares to investors, who were allocated Offer Shares in the Private Placement. Following the registration of the share capital increase pertaining to the Offer Shares in the Norwegian Register of Business Enterprises, and issuance of the Offer Shares in VPS, the borrowed shares have been redelivered to NRAS.
