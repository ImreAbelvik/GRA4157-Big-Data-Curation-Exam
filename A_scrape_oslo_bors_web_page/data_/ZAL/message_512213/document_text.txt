Oslo, Norway, 26 August 2020

Zalaris ASA (“Zalaris” or the “Company”) has granted 7,227 restricted stock units ("RSUs") to executive management, as part of the Company’s annual bonus program. The RSUs granted are in lieu of cash bonuses for 2019. The RSUs vest after 36 months and each RSU corresponds to one share. Please refer to the annual report 2019 for further details regarding the Company’s share incentive plans.  

The following primary insiders have been allocated RSUs as part of the grant:

Executive VP UK & Ireland Will Jackson has been granted 1,402 RSUs. Following the grant, he holds 5,581 shares, 10,000 options and 20,402 RSUs in the Company.

CHRO Hilde Karlsmyr has been granted 2,522 RSUs. Following the grant, she holds 5,581 shares, 10,000 options and 21,522 RSUs in the Company.

CTO Halvor Leirvåg has been granted 1,715 RSUs. Following the grant, he holds 7,825 shares, 10,000 options and 35,556 RSUs in the Company.

Executive VP Northern Europe Sami Seikkula has been granted 1,588 RSUs. Following the grant, he holds 4,000 shares, 10,000 options and 20,588 RSUs in the Company.
