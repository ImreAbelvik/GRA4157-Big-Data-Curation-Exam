Oslo, Norway, 18 March 2024

Zalaris ASA has on 18 March granted 63,044 restricted stock units (RSUs) to
corporate and regional management, as part of the Company’s annual bonus
program. The RSUs granted are in lieu of cash bonuses. The RSUs vest after 36
months and each RSU corresponds to one share.

The following primary insiders have been allocated RSUs as part of the grant:

- Hans-Petter Mellerud, CEO: 16,667
- Richard Schiørn: EVP Solution & Delivery Global Managed Services:6,554
- Øyvind Reiten, EVP BD - Global Managed Services: 11,380
- Gunnar Manum, CFO: 6,550
- Hilde Karlsmyr, CHRO: 6,037
- Halvor Leirvåg, CTO: 6,061
