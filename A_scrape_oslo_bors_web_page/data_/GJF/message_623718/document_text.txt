On 16 July 2024, DNB acquired a total of 116 328 shares in Gjensidige Forsikring
ASA ("Gjensidige" or the "Company", OSE:GJF, LEI 5967007LIEEXZX6FAO47, ISIN
NO0010582521) on behalf of the Company. 93 548 shares have been sold to
employees at a price of NOK 180.6185 per share as part of Gjensidige's share
savings scheme for 2024. For a description of the share savings scheme, see the
stock exchange release dated 18 December 2023.

Acquisition of shares under the share savings scheme for the second quarter 2024
is now complete.

Primary insiders in Gjensidige have on 16 July 2024 received shares in the
Company as part of the share savings scheme for 2024. See attachment for details
on the acquisition of shares by primary insiders.

Following the purchase and sale of shares for the second quarter of 2024 in
connection with the share savings scheme, Gjensidige will own 48 275 own shares.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article
19 of Regulation (EU) No 596/2014 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
