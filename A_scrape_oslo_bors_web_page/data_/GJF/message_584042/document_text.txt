Executive personnel in Gjensidige Forsikring ASA, have on 1 March 2023 received
shares in Gjensidige Forsikring ASA (OSE:GJF), LEI 5967007LIEEXZX6FAO47, ISIN
NO0010582521, as part of the share-based payment arrangements.

19 818 shares have been allocated to executive personnel as variable pay. For a
description of share-based payment to executive personnel, reference is made to
note 22 in the annual report 2021.

Following the allocation of shares mentioned above, and the sale of shares to
employees in connection with the share savings scheme described in the stock
exchange release on 26 January 2023, Gjensidige owns 16 078 own shares.
