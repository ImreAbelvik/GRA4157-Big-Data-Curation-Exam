Gjensidige has on 18 February 2016 sold 686 shares to employees as part of the
share savings scheme 2015. The shares have been sold at a price of NOK 143.5911
per share. Gjensidige now owns 19,285 own shares.

For a description of the share savings scheme, see stock exchange release dated
December 18 2014.

This information is subject to disclosure under the Norwegian Securities Act
section §5-12.
