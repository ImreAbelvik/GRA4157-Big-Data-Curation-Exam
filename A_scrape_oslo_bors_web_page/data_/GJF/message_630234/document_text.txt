On 23 October 2024, DNB acquired a total of 99 291 shares in Gjensidige
Forsikring ASA ("Gjensidige" or the "Company", OSE:GJF, LEI
5967007LIEEXZX6FAO47, ISIN NO0010582521) on behalf of the Company. 79 908 shares
have been sold to employees at a price of NOK 200.00 per share as part of
Gjensidige's share savings scheme for 2024. For a description of the share
savings scheme, see the stock exchange release dated 18 December 2023.

Acquisition of shares under the share savings scheme for the third quarter 2024
is now complete.

Primary insiders in Gjensidige have on 23 October 2024 received shares in the
Company as part of the share savings scheme for 2024. See attachment for details
on the acquisition of shares by primary insiders.

Following the purchase and sale of shares for the third quarter of 2024 in
connection with the share savings scheme, Gjensidige will own 50 916 own shares.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article
19 of Regulation (EU) No 596/2014 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
