On 30 April 2025, DNB acquired a total of 101 876 shares in Gjensidige
Forsikring ASA ("Gjensidige" or the "Company", OSE:GJF, LEI
5967007LIEEXZX6FAO47, ISIN NO0010582521) on behalf of the Company. 82 031 shares
have been sold to employees at a price of NOK 241.74 per share as part of
Gjensidige's share savings scheme for 2025. For a description of the share
savings scheme, see the stock exchange release dated 19 December 2024.
Acquisition of shares under the share savings scheme for the first quarter of
2025 is now complete.

Primary insiders in Gjensidige have on 30 April 2025 received shares in the
Company as part of the share savings scheme for 2025. See attachment for details
on the acquisition of shares by primary insiders.

Following the purchase and sale of shares for the first quarter of 2025 in
connection with the share savings scheme, Gjensidige will own 28 862 own shares.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article
19 of Regulation (EU) No 596/2014 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
