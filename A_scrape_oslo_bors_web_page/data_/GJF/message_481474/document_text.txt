On behalf of Gjensidige (OSE:GJF), DNB has on 15 July 2019 acquired 52 247
shares. The shares have been acquired at an average price of NOK 179.3075 per
share.

42 030 shares have been sold to employees, at a price of NOK 179.3075 per share,
as part of the Gjensidige share savings scheme 2019.

Acquisition of shares for the share savings scheme for the second quarter 2019
is now completed.

A total of 9 589 bonus shares are allocated to employees pursuant to the terms
of previous years' share savings scheme. Bonus shares are allocated without
further consideration.

Gjensidige now owns 16 270 own shares.

Shares sold or allocated to primary insider employees are disclosed in the
attachment to this disclosure.

For a description of the share savings scheme, see stock exchange release dated
19 December 2018.

This information is subject to disclosure under the Norwegian Securities Act
section §5-12.
