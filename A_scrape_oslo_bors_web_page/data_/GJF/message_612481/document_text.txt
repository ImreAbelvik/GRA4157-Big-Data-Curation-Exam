Executive personnel in Gjensidige Forsikring ASA, have on 1 March 2024 received
shares in Gjensidige Forsikring ASA ("Gjensidige" or the "Company", OSE:GJF, LEI
5967007LIEEXZX6FAO47, ISIN NO0010582521), as part of the share-based payment
arrangements.

20 022 shares have been allocated to executive personnel as variable pay. For a
description of share-based payment to executive personnel, reference is made to
note 24 in the annual report 2023.

Following the allocation of shares mentioned above, and the sale of shares to
employees in connection with the share savings scheme described in the stock
exchange release on 25 January 2024, Gjensidige will own 34 764 own shares.
