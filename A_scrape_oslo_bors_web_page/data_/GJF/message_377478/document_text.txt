On behalf of Gjensidige (OSE:GJF), DNB has on 7 May 2015 acquired 49,940 shares.
The shares have been acquired at an average price of NOK 126.027 per share.

41,604 shares have been sold to employees, at a price of NOK 126.027 per share,
as part of the Gjensidige share savings scheme 2015.

Acquisition of shares related to the share savings scheme for the first quarter
2015 is now completed.

A total of 7,664 bonus shares are allocated to employees pursuant to the terms
of previous years' share savings scheme. Bonus shares are allocated without
further consideration.

Gjensidige now owns 65,601 own shares.

Shares sold to primary insider employees are, together with allocated bonus
shares, disclosed in the attachment.

For a description of the share savings scheme, see stock exchange release dated
18 December 2014.

This information is subject to disclosure under the Norwegian Securities Act
section §5-12.
