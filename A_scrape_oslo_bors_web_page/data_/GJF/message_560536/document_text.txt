On behalf of Gjensidige (OSE:GJF), DNB has on 28 April 2022 acquired 102 269
shares in Gjensidige Forsikring ASA, LEI 5967007LIEEXZX6FAO47, ISIN NO0010582521
on Oslo Børs. 82 289 shares have been sold to employees, at a price of NOK
199,2735 per share, as part of the Gjensidige share savings scheme 2022.
Acquisition of shares for the share savings scheme for the first quarter 2022 is
now completed.

Primary insiders in Gjensidige Forsikring ASA have on 28 April 2022 received
shares in Gjensidige Forsikring ASA as part of the Gjensidige share savings
scheme 2022.

Gjensidige now owns 36 413 own shares.

For a description of the share savings scheme, see stock exchange release dated
17 December 2021.
