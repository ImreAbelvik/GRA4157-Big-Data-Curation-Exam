On behalf of Gjensidige (OSE:GJF), DNB has on 27 January 2022 acquired 88 303
shares in Gjensidige Forsikring ASA, LEI 5967007LIEEXZX6FAO47, ISIN NO0010582521
on Oslo Børs. 71 052 shares have been sold to employees, at a price of NOK
216,4283 per share, as part of the Gjensidige share savings scheme 2021.
Acquisition of shares for the share savings scheme for the fourth quarter 2021
is now completed.

Primary insiders in Gjensidige Forsikring ASA have on 27 January 2022 received
shares in Gjensidige Forsikring ASA as part of the Gjensidige share savings
scheme 2021.

19 902 shares have been allocated to executive personnel as variable pay. For a
description of share-based payment to executive personnel, reference is made to
note 22 in the annual report 2020.

Gjensidige now owns 27 946 own shares.

For a description of the share savings scheme, see stock exchange release dated
17 December 2020.
