Carucel Finance, M25 Industrier and Stella have sold 128,484, 64,242 & 64,242 shares respectively in Agilyx AS at an average price of 51.20 per share. The share sale was executed over the period 21, 22 and 25 January. After this transaction Carucel Finance holds 589,416 shares, M25 Industrier holds 374,858 shares and Stella holds 215,158 shares in Agilyx AS.

Preben Rasch-Olsen sits on the Board of Directors in Agilyx AS and is a financial advisor for the abovementioned companies. Mr. Rasch-Olsen owns 166.800 shares in Agilyx and has not sold any shares since the listing.
