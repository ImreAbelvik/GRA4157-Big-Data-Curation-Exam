On 19 October 2015, chairman of the board, Tarmo Tuominen subscribed 75,037
shares in the ongoing Rights Issue in Nordic Mining at a subscription price of
NOK 0.45 per share. Tuominen has been allocated 75,037 subscription rights, and
he will receive 75,037 shares in the Rights Issue.
Tarmo Tuominen owns 300,641 shares in Nordic Mining prior to the transaction.
