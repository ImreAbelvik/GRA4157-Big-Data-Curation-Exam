CFO Lars K. Grøndahl has today through his company Magil AS subscribed 300,000
shares in the ongoing Rights Issue in Nordic Mining at a price of NOK 0.50 per
share. Magil AS has previously subscribed 1,000,000 shares in the Rights Issue.
Magil AS has been allocated 2,866,371 subscription rights, and Magil AS will in
total receive 1,300,000 shares in the Rights Issue.
Magil AS owns 6,500,000 shares in Nordic Mining prior to the subscriptions.
For questions please contact CFO Lars K. Grøndahl, telephone +47 901 60 941.
Oslo, 23 June 2016
