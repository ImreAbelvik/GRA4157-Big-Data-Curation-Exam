CFO Lars K. Grøndahl has today through his company Magil AS subscribed 1,300,000
shares in the ongoing Rights Issue in Nordic Mining at a price of NOK 0.45 per
share. Magil AS has been allocated 1,297,872 subscription rights, and Magil AS
will receive 1,297,872 shares in the Rights Issue. In addition, Magil AS may be
allocated up to 2,128 shares based on the subscription for shares without
subscription rights.
Magil AS owns 5,200,000 shares in Nordic Mining prior to the transaction.
Further, Lars K. Grøndahl through Magil AS has been awarded an option to buy
2,000,000 shares in Nordic Mining at a price of NOK 0.90 per share and an option
to buy 500,000 shares at a price of NOK 1.80 per share.
For questions please contact CFO Lars K. Grøndahl, telephone +47 901 60 941.
