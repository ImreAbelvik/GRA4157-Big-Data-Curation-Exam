On 21 June 2016, board member Hilde Myrberg subscribed 31,321 shares in the
ongoing Rights Issue in Nordic Mining at a subscription price of NOK 0.50 per
share. Myrberg has been allocated 31,321 subscription rights, and she will
receive 31,321 shares in the Rights Issue.
Hilde Myrberg owns 71,027 shares in Nordic Mining prior to the transaction.
