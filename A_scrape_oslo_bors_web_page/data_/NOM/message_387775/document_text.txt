Board member Kjell Roland has today subscribed 62,397 shares in the ongoing
Rights Issue in Nordic Mining at a subscription price of NOK 0.45 per share.
Roland has been allocated 62,397 subscription rights, and he will receive 62,397
shares in the Rights Issue.
Kjell Roland owns 250,000 shares in Nordic Mining prior to the transaction.
