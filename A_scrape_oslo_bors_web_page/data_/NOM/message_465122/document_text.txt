Reference is made to stock exchange release 21 November 2018 related to the
completed Rights Issue in Nordic Mining ASA on the same day. In the release, the
allocation of shares in the Subsequent Offering to Tarmo Tuominen, Chairman of
the Board, was informed on a preliminary basis. The exact number of allocated
shares to Mr. Tuominen has now been informed from the Finnish account controller
who allocates the shares under the share account. Mr. Tuominen has been
allocated in total 20,000 shares in the Rights Issue. All shares have been
purchased at a price of NOK 2.30 per share. Mr. Tuominen's new shareholding is
120,224 shares.

Oslo, 5 December 2018 Nordic Mining ASA
