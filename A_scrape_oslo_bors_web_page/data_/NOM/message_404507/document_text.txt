CEO Ivar S. Fossum has today subscribed 500,000 shares in the ongoing Rights
Issue in Nordic Mining at a subscription price of NOK 0.50 per share. Fossum has
been allocated 936,241 subscription rights, and he will receive 500,000 shares
in the Rights Issue.
Ivar S. Fossum owns 2,123,092 shares in Nordic Mining prior to the subscription.
