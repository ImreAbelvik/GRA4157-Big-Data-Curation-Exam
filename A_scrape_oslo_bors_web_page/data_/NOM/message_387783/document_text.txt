Board member Tore Viana-Rønningen has today through his related company ETVR
Invest AS subscribed 65,000 shares in the ongoing Rights Issue in Nordic Mining
at a subscription price of NOK 0.45 per share. ETVR Invest AS has been allocated
48,670 subscription rights and will receive 48,670 shares in the Rights Issue.
In addition, ETVR Invest AS may be allocated up to 16,330 shares based on the
subscription for shares without subscription rights.
ETVR Invest AS owns 195,000 shares in Nordic Mining prior to the transaction.
