Board member Mari Thjømøe has today through the family company Thjømøekranen AS
subscribed 100,000 shares in the ongoing Rights Issue in Nordic Mining at a
subscription price of NOK 0.45 per share. Thjømøekranen AS has been allocated
45,262 subscription rights, and Thjømøekranen AS will receive 45,262 shares in
the Rights Issue. In addition, Thjømøekranen AS may be allocated up to 54,738
shares based on the subscription for shares without subscription rights.
Thjømøekranen AS owns 181,346 shares in Nordic Mining prior to the transaction.
