Christian Sand Kirk, board member in Hugo Games, has today, 
on behalf of himself and related parties, subscribed a total 
of 129,942 shares in the ongoing Rights Issue in Hugo Games 
at a subscription price of NOK 1.50 per share. Christian Sand 
Kirk and related parties have been allocated 129,942 
subscription rights and will receive 129,942 shares in the 
Rights Issue. 

Christian Sand Kirk and related parties own 164,214 shares in 
Hugo Games prior to the transaction.

About Hugo Games
