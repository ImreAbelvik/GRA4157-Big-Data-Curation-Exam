Announcement no. 20/2017
Copenhagen, 18 May 2017

CEO subscribes for shares in the Rights Issue

COPENHAGEN (May 18, 2017) Henrik Kølle, CEO in Hugo Games 
A/S, has today through his wholly owned company Aula Holding 
ApS subscribed 11,571,000 shares in the ongoing Rights Issue 
in Hugo Games at a subscription price of NOK 0.63 per share. 
The amount corresponds to Aula Holding's current ownership 
(24.3%) of Hugo Games.

Aula Holding ApS has been allocated approximately 15,915,672 
subscription rights and will receive minimum 11,571,000 
shares in the Rights Issue.

Aula Holding ApS owns 12,089,658 shares in Hugo Games prior 
to the Rights Issue and will own 23,660,658 shares after the 
transaction.

Further, with reference to the prospectus dated 8 May 2017, 
Aula Holding ApS is participating in the underwriting 
consortium related to the Rights Issue for a total of NOK 
12.5 million.

About Hugo Games

Hugo Games is a Nordic mobile games publisher and developer 
located in Copenhagen and Nottingham, UK. At Hugo Games we 
are passionate about games and we are committed to making 
titles that will engage and bring genuine joy to people for 
years. We have a consistent portfolio of games globally 
available across a wide range of platforms including iOS, 
Android, Windows, Amazon and Facebook.
