Copenhagen, July 18, 2016

Chairman Bertel David Maigaard has through his related 
company Paw Capital ApS subscribed 2,500,000 shares in the 
ongoing Rights Issue in Hugo Games at a subscription price of 
NOK 1.50 per share. Bertel David Maigaard, Paw Capital ApS or 
other related parties do not currently own shares in the 
company. 
Depending on the total subscription of shares in the rights 
issue, Paw Capital ApS may be allocated up to 2,500,000 
shares based on the subscription for shares without 
subscription rights.
