Copenhagen, July 1, 2016

CFO subscribes for shares in the Rights Issue
 
Peter Ekman, CFO in Hugo Games, has today through his wholly 
owned company Ekman Holding ApS subscribed 166,666 shares in 
the ongoing Rights Issue in Hugo Games at a subscription 
price of NOK 1.50 per share.
Ekman Holding ApS has been allocated 18,945 subscription 
rights and will receive 18,945 shares in the Rights Issue. 
In addition, Ekman Holding ApS may be allocated up to 
147,721 additional shares based on the subscription for 
shares without subscription rights.
 
Ekman Holding ApS owns 23,941 shares in Hugo Games prior to 
the transaction.
 
Further, with reference to the stock exchange announcement 
as of 29 June 2016 regarding COO Søren Kokbøl Jensen's 
subscription of 83,333 shares through his wholly owned 
company Catalyzer ApS, it is hereby informed that Søren 
Kokbøl Jensen will not, as previously stated, transfer his 
personally owned subscription rights to his company due to 
tax reasons. As such, Catalyzer ApS may be allocated up to 
83,333 shares based on the subscription for shares without 
subscription rights.
