Announcement no. 21/2017
Copenhagen, 19 May 2017

CFO subscribes for shares in the Rights Issue

COPENHAGEN (May 19, 2017) Peter Ekman, CFO in Hugo Games A/S, has 
today through his wholly owned company Ekman Holding ApS subscribed 
205,278 shares in the ongoing Rights Issue in Hugo Games at a 
subscription price of NOK 0.63 per share. 

Ekman Holding ApS has been allocated 205,278 subscription rights and 
will receive 205,278 shares in the Rights Issue.

Ekman Holding ApS owns 155,931 shares in Hugo Games prior to the 
Rights Issue and will own 361,209 shares after the transaction.
About Hugo Games

Hugo Games is a Nordic mobile games publisher and developer located 
in Copenhagen and Nottingham, UK. At Hugo Games we are passionate 
about games and we are committed to making titles that will engage 
and bring genuine joy to people for years. We have a consistent 
portfolio of games globally available across a wide range of 
platforms including iOS, Android, Windows, Amazon and Facebook.

More information available at www.hugogames.com.
