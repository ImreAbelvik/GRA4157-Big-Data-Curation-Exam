Copenhagen, July 14, 2016

Rasmus Lund, board member in Hugo Games, has subscribed a 
total of 16,500 shares in the ongoing Rights Issue in Hugo 
Games at a subscription price of NOK 1.50 per share. The 
subscription is not done on the basis of subcsription rights. 

Rasmus Lund owns 68,028 shares in Hugo Games prior to the 
transaction.

About Hugo Games

Hugo Games is a Nordic mobile games publisher and developer 
located in Copenhagen. At Hugo Games we are passionate about 
games and we are committed to making titles that will engage 
and bring genuine joy to people for years. We have a
