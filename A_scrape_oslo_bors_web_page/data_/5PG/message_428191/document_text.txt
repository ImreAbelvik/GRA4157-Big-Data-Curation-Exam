Announcement no. 25/2017
Copenhagen, 24 May 2017

Board member subscribes for shares in the Rights Issue

COPENHAGEN (May 24, 2017) Henrik Nielsen, board member in Hugo Games 
A/S, has today subscribed 2,976,191 shares in the ongoing Rights 
Issue in Hugo Games at a subscription price of NOK 0.63 per share. 
This corresponds to the amount Mr. Nielsen has underwritten in the 
Rights Issue with reference to the prospectus dated 8 May 2017.

Henrik Nielsen will not subscribe on the basis of subscription 
rights in the Rights Issue and does not previously own shares in 
Hugo Games. As such, Henrik Nielsen's ownership in Hugo Games after 
the Rights Issue will be up to 2,976,191 shares.

About Henrik Nielsen:
Henrik Nielsen is previous COO and CFO of Unity Technologies through 
8 years and has great experience in the gaming industry.

About Hugo Games
Hugo Games is a Nordic mobile games publisher and developer located 
in Copenhagen and Nottingham, UK. At Hugo Games we are passionate 
about games and we are committed to making titles that will engage 
and bring genuine joy to people for years. We have a consistent 
portfolio of games globally available across a wide range of 
platforms including iOS, Android, Windows, Amazon and Facebook.

More information available at www.hugogames.com.
