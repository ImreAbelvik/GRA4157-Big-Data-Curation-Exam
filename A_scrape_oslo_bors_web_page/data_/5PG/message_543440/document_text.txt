COPENHAGEN, September 30th, 2021: 5th Planet Games A/S (OMX: 5PG) announces that Chief Accounting Officer in 5th Planet Games, Peter Ekman buys shares.

Mr. Peter Ekman has on September 29th, 2021, acquired 906,095 new shares in 5th Planet Games at an average price of NOK 0,88. The total purchasing price for the new shares amounts to NOK 796,829. 

Following this transaction Mr. Peter Ekman owns/controls 1,508,608 shares in 5th Planet Games.



About 5th Planet Games:
5th Planet Games is a games developer and publisher located in Copenhagen. 5th Planet Games works with IP-driven content such as the Adventures of Tintin and with premium sports brands such as Cristiano Ronaldo, Nyjah Huston and Peter "Snakebite" Wright; it also has its own IP in Hugo. Following a recent investment agreement 5th Planet Games is now working in partnership with LA-based Skybound Entertainment which will give access to IP such as The Walking Dead, Before your Eyes and Invincible.
