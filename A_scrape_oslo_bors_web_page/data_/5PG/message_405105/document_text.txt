Copenhagen 29 June 2016. 

Søren Kokbøl Jensen, COO in Hugo Games, has today through 
his wholly owned company Catalyzer ApS subscribed 83,333 
shares in the ongoing Rights Issue in Hugo Games at a 
subscription price of NOK 1.50 per share.
Søren Kokbøl Jensen has been allocated 53,832 subscription 
rights and will transfer these to Catalyzer ApS such that 
Catalyzer ApS will receive 53,832 shares in the Rights 
Issue. In addition, Catalyzer ApS may be allocated up to 
29,501 additional shares based on the subscription for 
shares without subscription rights. 
 
Søren Kokbøl Jensen owns 68,028 shares in Hugo Games prior 
to the transaction.
