Mr. Henrik Jørgen Skouboe Kølle, CEO of Hugo Games A/S, has on 
8th August 2015 acquired 21.000 shares in Hugo Games A/S at an 
average price of NOK 5,6344. The shares are acquired through his 
company Aula Holding ApS.

Following this transaction Aula Holding ApS owns 12.089.658 
shares in Hugo Games A/S which gives an ownership of 48,36%.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading 
Act.
