Oslo, 16 February 2022. Aeternum Capital AS ("Aeternum"), a company closely associated with board member and primary insider in Elektroimportøren AS ("Elektroimportøren") Gaute Gillebo (who has been appointed as representative for Aeternum at the board of directors of Elektroimportøren), has today acquired 1,600,000 shares in Elektroimportøren at a price of NOK 75.00 per share. 

Please see the attached notification for persons discharging managerial responsibilities and their close associates. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in articles 19 of the Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
