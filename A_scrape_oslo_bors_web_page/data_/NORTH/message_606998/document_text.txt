Oslo, 18 December 2023

Anders Onarheim, Chairman of the Board of Directors of North Energy ASA, has
today, 18 December 2023, purchased 35,175 shares at NOK 2.12 per share via his
fully owned company AB Investment AS. The total number of shares beneficially
owned by Onarheim after the transaction is 24,368,424, which equals 20.47%
ownership in North Energy ASA.

Rachid Bendriss, Co-CEO of North Energy ASA, has today, 18 December 2023,
purchased 35,175 shares at NOK 2.12 per share via his fully owned company Celisa
Capital AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Bendriss after the
transaction is 12,028,103, which equals 10.10% ownership in North Energy ASA.

Didrik Leikvang, Co-CEO of North Energy ASA, has today, 18 December 2023,
purchased 35,175 shares at NOK 2.12 per share via his fully owned company
Isfjorden AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Leikvang after
the transaction is 10,074,667, which equals 8.46% ownership in North Energy ASA.
