Limea Ltd, through its daughter company The company 
Rex Technologies Management Ltd, was allocated 
9,000,000 shares in North Energy ASA in the private 
placement which approved in the extraordinary general 
meeting 13. February 2014. These shares were then, 
November 2014, transferred direct to the mother 
company.

Limea Ltd. has January 18, 2017 sold all its 9,000,000 
shares in North Energy ASA.

This information is subject of the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 4-3 of the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act.
