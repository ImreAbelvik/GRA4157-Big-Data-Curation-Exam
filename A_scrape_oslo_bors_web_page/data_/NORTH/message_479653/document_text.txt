Oslo, 18 June 2019

Reference is made to the previously completed North Energy ASA share buy-back
programme and the approval from 28(th) of May by the Annual General Meeting of a
dividend of NOK 0.3 per share with an option for shareholders to subscribe for
new shares using the dividend amount. The subscription period for the election
of dividend as new shares ended yesterday, 17(th) of June, and a total of 117
shareholders, with a total ownership of 41,005,473 shares, equivalent to 34.44%
of the total share capital, has elected to receive the dividend as new shares.
At a price of NOK 1.60 per share the total number of shares to be distributed as
dividend is 7,688,566. The total number of shares own by the company after the
buy-back programme was 9,484,038. After the distribution of dividend as new
shares the company will have a remaining number of 1,795,472 own shares.

For those shareholders who will receive the dividend as cash, the payment date
is planned at 21(st) of June.

The following primary insiders have elected to receive the dividend as new
shares:

Anders Onarheim, Chairman of the Board of Directors of North Energy ASA, has
subscribed for 3,492,026 shares at NOK 1.60 per share via his fully owned
companies AB Investment AS, Spitsbergen AS, and Liju Invest AS. The total number
of shares beneficially owned by Onarheim after the distribution is 22,318,465,
which equals 18.75% ownership in North Energy ASA.

Rachid Bendriss, financial advisor to North Energy ASA and primary insider, has
subscribed for 1,367,258 shares at NOK 1.60 per share via his fully owned
company Celisa Capital AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by
Bendriss after the distribution is 8,659,300, which equals 7.27% ownership in
North Energy ASA.

Didrik Leikvang, financial advisor to North Energy ASA and primary insider, has
subscribed for 1,029,759 shares at NOK 1.60 per share via his fully owned
company Isfjorden AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Leikvang
after the distribution is 6,521,803, which equals 5.48% ownership in North
Energy ASA.

Knut Sæberg, CEO of North Energy ASA and primary insider, has subscribed for
153,530 shares at NOK 1.60 per share. The total number of shares owned by Sæberg
after the distribution is 972,352, which equals 0.82% ownership in North Energy
ASA.
