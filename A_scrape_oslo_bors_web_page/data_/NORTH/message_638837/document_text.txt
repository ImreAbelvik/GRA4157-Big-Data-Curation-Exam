Oslo, 14 February 2025

Didrik Leikvang, Co-CEO of North Energy ASA, has today, 14 February 2025, purchased 500,000 shares at NOK 2.8067 per share via his fully owned company Isfjorden AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Leikvang after the transaction is 11,669,242, which equals 9.8% ownership in North Energy ASA.


DISCLOSURE REGULATION
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
