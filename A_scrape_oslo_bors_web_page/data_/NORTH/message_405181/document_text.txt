


Oslo, 30 June 2016

Anders Onarheim, Chairman of the Board of Directors of North Energy ASA, has
purchased 475,000 shares at NOK 1.3 per share via his fully owned company AB
Investment AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Onarheim through
AB Investment AS after the transaction is 13,073,070, which equals 10.98%
ownership in North Energy ASA.

Didrik Leikvang, financial advisor to North Energy ASA and primary insider, has
purchased 475,000 shares at NOK 1.3 per share via his fully owned company
Kharybdis AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Leikvang through
Kharybdis AS after the transaction is 2,168,108 which equals 1.82% ownership in
North Energy ASA.

Rachid Bendriss, financial advisor to North Energy ASA and primary insider, has
purchased 475,000 shares at NOK 1.3 per share via his fully owned company Celisa
Capital AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Bendriss after the
transaction is 3,625,000 which equals 3.05% ownership in North Energy ASA.

Knut Sæberg, CEO of North Energy ASA, has purchased 60,953 shares at NOK 1.3.
Total number of shares owned after the transaction is 628,822 which equals
0.53% ownership in the company.

Kristen Berli, SVP Business Development, has purchased 60,952 shares at NOK
1.3. Total number of shares owned after the transaction is 399,315 which equals
0.34% ownership in the company.
