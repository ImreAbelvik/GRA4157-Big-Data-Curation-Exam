


Oslo, 18 January 2017

Anders Onarheim, Chairman of the Board of Directors of North Energy ASA, has
purchased 1,400,000 shares at NOK 2.10 per share via his fully owned company AB
Investment AS, and 400,000 shares at NOK 2.10 per share via his fully owned
company Liju Invest AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by
Onarheim after the transaction is 17,421,169 which equals 14.63% ownership in
North Energy ASA.

Rachid Bendriss, financial advisor to North Energy ASA and primary insider, has
purchased 526,900 shares at NOK 2.10 per share via his fully owned company
Celisa Capital AS, and 100,000 shares at NOK 2.10 privately. The total number of
shares beneficially owned by Bendriss after the transaction is 6,800,000 which
equals 5.71% ownership in North Energy ASA.

Didrik Leikvang, financial advisor to North Energy ASA and primary insider, has
purchased 448,163 shares at NOK 2.10 per share via his fully owned company
Isfjorden AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Leikvang after
the transaction is 5,164,371 which equals 4.34% ownership in North Energy ASA.

Knut Sæberg, CEO of North Energy ASA, has purchased 50,000 shares at NOK 2.10.
Total number of shares owned after the transaction is 768,822.
