




Oslo, 22 February 2017

Anders Onarheim, Chairman of the Board of Directors of North Energy ASA, has
increased his ownership in Spitsbergen AS. After this, the total number of
shares in North Energy ASA beneficially owned by Mr. Onarheim is 17.667.721
which equals 14.84% ownership in the company.
