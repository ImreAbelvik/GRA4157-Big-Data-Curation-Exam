


Oslo, 13 June 2017

Anders Onarheim, Chairman of the Board of Directors of North Energy ASA, has
purchased 14,757 shares at NOK 1.70 per share via his fully owned company AB
Investment AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Onarheim after
the transaction is 18,370,045, which equals 15.43% ownership in North Energy
ASA.

Rachid Bendriss, financial advisor to North Energy ASA and primary insider, has
purchased 14,757 shares at NOK 1.70 per share via his fully owned company Celisa
Capital AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Bendriss after the
transaction is 7,202,325, which equals 6.05% ownership in North Energy ASA.

Didrik Leikvang, financial advisor to North Energy ASA and primary insider, has
purchased 14,757 shares at NOK 1.70 per share via his fully owned company
Isfjorden AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Leikvang after
the transaction is 5,566,696, which equals 4.68% ownership in North Energy ASA.
