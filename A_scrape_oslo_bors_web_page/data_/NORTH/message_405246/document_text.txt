


Oslo, 30 June 2016

With reference to the stock exchange notice published on June 24(th) regarding a
resolution by the general meeting of Isfjorden AS to distribute 100% of its
shares held in North Energy ASA to shareholders.

Subsequently to the distribution of shares by Isfjorden AS, Mr Didrik Leikvang,
financial advisor to North Energy and primary insider, has entered into an
agreement with existing shareholders to acquire 100% ownership in Isfjorden AS.
Subsequently to Mr Leikvang acquiring 100% ownership in the company, Isfjorden
AS has entered into an agreement with Kharybdis AS, a company also 100% owned by
Mr Leikvang, to acquire 2,003,737 shares in North Energy ASA from Kharybdis AS
at a price of NOK 1.30 per share. After acquiring the shares, Isfjorden AS will
hold 2,003,737 shares in North Energy ASA, equal to 1.68% ownership in North
Energy ASA and Kharybdis AS will hold no shares in North Energy ASA.

After completing the transactions, the number of shares beneficially owned by Mr
Leikvang is unchanged at 2,168,108, equal to 1.82% ownership in North Energy
ASA.
