


Anders Onarheim, Chairman of the Board of Directors of North Energy ASA, has
purchased 800.000 shares at NOK 1.15 per share via his fully owned company AB
Investment AS. Total number of shares after the transaction is 800.000.

Didrik Leikvang, financial advisor to North Energy ASA and primary insider, has
purchased 200.000 shares via his fully owned company Kharybdis AS at an average
price of NOK 1.15. Total number of shares after the transaction is 200.000.

Anders Onarheim and Didrik Leikvang are both associated with Isfjorden AS, which
owns a total of 8,570,651 shares (7.2%) in North Energy ASA.
