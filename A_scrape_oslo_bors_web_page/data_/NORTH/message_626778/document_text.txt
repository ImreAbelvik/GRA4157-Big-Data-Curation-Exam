Oslo, 30 August 2024

Anders Onarheim, Chairman of the Board of Directors of North Energy ASA, has today, 30 August 2024, purchased 96,902 shares at NOK 2.5083 per share via his fully owned company AB Investment AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Onarheim after the transaction is 25,163,332, which equals 21.14% ownership in North Energy ASA.

Rachid Bendriss, Co-CEO of North Energy ASA, has today, 30 August 2024, purchased 96,903 shares at NOK 2.5083 per share via his fully owned company Celisa Capital AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Bendriss after the transaction is 12,542,546, which equals 10.54% ownership in North Energy ASA. 

Didrik Leikvang, Co-CEO of North Energy ASA, has today, 30 August 2024, purchased 96,903 shares at NOK 2.5083 per share via his fully owned company Isfjorden AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Leikvang after the transaction is 10,869,578, which equals 9.13% ownership in North Energy ASA.
