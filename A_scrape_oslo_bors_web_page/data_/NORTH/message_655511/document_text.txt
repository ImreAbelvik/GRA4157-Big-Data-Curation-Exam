Oslo, 16 September 2025

Anders Onarheim, Chairman of the Board of Directors of North Energy ASA, has today, 16 September 2025, purchased  163,646 shares at NOK 2.7 per share via his fully owned company AB Investment AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Onarheim after the transaction is 25,626,642, which equals 21.86% ownership in North Energy ASA. 

Rachid Bendriss, Co-CEO of North Energy ASA, has today, 16 September 2025, purchased 163,645 shares at NOK 2.7 per share via his fully owned company Celisa Capital AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Bendriss after the transaction is 12,706,191, which equals 10.84% ownership in North Energy ASA. 

Didrik Leikvang, Co-CEO of North Energy ASA, has today, 16 September 2025, purchased 163,645 shares at NOK 2.7 per share on his personal account. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Leikvang after the transaction is 12,082,887, which equals 10.31% ownership in North Energy ASA.

Jogeir Romestrand, board member of North Energy ASA and primary insider, has today, 16 September 2025, purchased 163,645 shares at NOK 2.7 per share via his fully owned company Rome AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Romestrand after the transaction is 2,050,849, which equals 1,75% ownership in North Energy ASA.
