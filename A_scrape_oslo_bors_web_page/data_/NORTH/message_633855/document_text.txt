Oslo, 4 December 2024

Jogeir Romestrand, board member of North Energy ASA and primary insider, has today, 4 December 2024, purchased 447,204 shares at NOK 2.6651 per share via his fully owned company Rome AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Romestrand after the transaction is 1,887,204, which equals 1,59% ownership in North Energy ASA.


DISCLOSURE REGULATION
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
