


Oslo, 6 May 2016

Anders Onarheim, Chairman of the Board of Directors of North Energy ASA, has
purchased 200,000 shares at NOK 1.22 per share via his fully owned company AB
Investment AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Onarheim through
AB Investment AS and Isfjorden AS after the transaction is 12,598,070, which
equals 10.58% ownership in North Energy ASA.

Knut Sæberg, CEO of North Energy ASA, has purchased 25,000 shares at NOK 1.22.
Total number of shares owned after the transaction is 567,869.

Kristen Berli SVP Business Development, has purchased 25,000 shares at NOK
1.22. Total number of shares owned after the transaction is 338,363.

Didrik Leikvang, financial advisor to North Energy ASA and primary insider, has
purchased 100,000 shares at NOK 1.22 per share via his fully owned company
Kharybdis AS. The total number of shares beneficially owned by Leikvang through
Kharybdis AS and Isfjorden AS after the transaction is 1,593,106, which equals
1.34% ownership in North Energy ASA.

Anders Onarheim and Didrik Leikvang are both associated with Isfjorden AS, which
owns a total of 8,570,651 shares (7.2%) in North Energy ASA.
