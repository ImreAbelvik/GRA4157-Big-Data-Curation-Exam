Hemen Holding Limited ("Hemen"), which is indirectly 
controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen 
for the benefit of his immediate family, has been 
allocated 1,342,281 new shares in the recently 
announced offering of new shares in Frontline Ltd. 
(the "Company"). 

Hemen's ownership in the Company will following 
settlement of the new shares be a total of 82,145,703 
shares, equalling 48.4% of the shares and votes in the 
Company (based on a total of 169,809,325 shares 
outstanding). 

***

This information is subject to the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act.
