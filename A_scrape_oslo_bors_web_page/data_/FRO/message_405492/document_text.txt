Frontline Ltd. ("Frontline" or the "Company") hereby 
announces that 1,170,000 share options have been 
awarded to employees and board members in accordance 
with the terms of the Company's Share Option Scheme. 
The share options will have a five-year term and will 
vest equally one third over a three-year vesting 
period. The exercise price is USD 8.00. The exercise 
price will be adjusted for any distribution of 
dividends made before the relevant options are 
exercised.

Furhter information enclosed.


July 5, 2016
The Board of Directors
Frontline Ltd.
Hamilton, Bermuda


This information is subject to the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 5-12 and 4-2 of the 
Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
