



Robert Hvide Macleod, CEO of Frontline Management AS, has on 31 August 2017
entered into TRS agreements with exposure to 150,000 shares in Frontline Ltd.
("Frontline") with expiry date 30 November 2017 and TRS price NOK 39.56 per
underlying share. In addition, Mr Macleod owns 270,000 shares in Frontline, and
holds options to acquire 798,000 shares in Frontline, as previously
disclosed.



This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
