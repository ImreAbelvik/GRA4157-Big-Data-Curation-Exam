


(2015-11-11) On 11 November 2015, Gro Brækken, board member of Kitron ASA
("Kitron"), purchased 24 000 shares in Kitron at an average price of NOK 3.70
per share.

Following the transaction Gro Brækken holds 24 000 shares in Kitron.

This announcement is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
