


(2015-02-17) On 17 February 2015, Päivi Marttila, member of the board in Kitron
ASA ("Kitron"), purchased 45 000 shares in Kitron at NOK 1.88 per share.

After the transaction Päivi Marttila holds 45 000 shares in Kitron.

This announcement is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
