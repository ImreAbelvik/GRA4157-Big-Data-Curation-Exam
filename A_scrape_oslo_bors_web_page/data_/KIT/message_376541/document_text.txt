


(2015-04-28) On 28 April 2015, Peter Nilsson, CEO of Kitron ASA ("Kitron"),
purchased 26 631 shares in Kitron at an average price of NOK 2.30 per share.

Following the transaction Peter Nilsson holds 126 631 shares and 1 500 000 share
options in Kitron.

This announcement is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
