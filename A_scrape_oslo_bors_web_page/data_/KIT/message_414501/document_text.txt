Amber Trust has today sold 2,720,191 shares in Kitron 
ASA ("KIT"). The shares were sold at a price of NOK 
6.00 per share. Following the completion of the sale, 
Amber Trust will no longer hold any shares in Kitron 
ASA. 
                                             
Martynas Cesnavicius, a member of the Board of 
Directors of Kitron ASA, serves as a board member of 
the management companies and acts as Investment 
Advisor for Luxembourg domiciled investment funds; KJK 
Fund SICAV-SIF, Amber Trust SCA and Amber Trust II SCA.
