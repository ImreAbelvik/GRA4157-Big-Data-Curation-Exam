


(2015-04-29) On 29 April 2015, Päivi Marttila, member of the board in Kitron ASA
("Kitron"), purchased 15 000 shares in Kitron at an average price of NOK 2.64
per share.

After the transaction Päivi Marttila holds 60 000 shares in Kitron.

This announcement is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
