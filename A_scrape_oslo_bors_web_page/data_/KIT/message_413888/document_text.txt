KJK Fund has today sold 5,327,191 shares in Kitron ASA 
("KIT"). The shares were sold at a price of NOK 5.45 
per share. Following the completion of the sale, KJK 
Fund will hold 686,717 shares in Kitron ASA, 
representing 0.39 per cent of the share capital and 
voting rights in the company. 

Martynas Cesnavicius, a member of the Board of 
Directors of Kitron ASA, serves as a board member of 
the management companies and acts as Investment 
Advisor for Luxembourg domiciled investment funds; KJK 
Fund SICAV-SIF, Amber Trust SCA and Amber Trust II 
SCA.
