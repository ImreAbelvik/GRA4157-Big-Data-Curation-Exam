27 February, 2019

Karl Erik Kjelstad, CEO of Akastor ASA, has today purchased 176 926 shares
through his company Byesvollen AS. Following the transaction, Kjelstad holds
300 000 shares in Akastor.
Leif Borge, CFO of Akastor ASA, has today purchased 50 000 shares through his
company Bohasi Invest AS. Following the transaction, Borge holds 300 000 shares
in Akastor.

The shares are purchased under Akastor Corporate Share Purchase Program with a
25 percent price reduction of the trading value and a three year lock-up period
from the date of delivery. The share price is NOK 13.46.



This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
