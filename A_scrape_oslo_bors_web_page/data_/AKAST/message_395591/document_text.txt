


Kathryn M. Baker, member of the Board of Directors in Akastor ASA, has today
purchased 45 683 shares in the company at an average price of NOK 6.03 per share
through her 100 % owned company, Lakeside AS.

Following today's transaction, Baker holds 45 683 shares in Akastor ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
