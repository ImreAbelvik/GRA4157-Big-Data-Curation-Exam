Luis Araujo, director on the board of Akastor ASA (OSE: AKAST), has today sold
25,757 shares at an average price of NOK 8.80 per share. The sale is associated
with Mr. Araujo moving from Norway to Portugal. Following the transaction, Mr.
Araujo no longer owns any shares in Akastor.

The shares were sold on the Oslo Stock Exchange.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article
19 in MAR and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
