


Maria Therese Victoria Badin, who is a related party to the CEO of Scanship
Holding, Mr. Henrik Badin, has today at 16:19 CET purchased 5349 shares at a
price of NOK 4.10 per share. After this transaction Mrs. Badin controls 5349
shares in the Company.

Mr. Henrik Badin controls, in addition, 10,500,000 shares in the Company through
his 100 % owned company Badin Invest Ltd.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
