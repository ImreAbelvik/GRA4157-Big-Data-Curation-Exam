


Maria Therese Victoria Badin, who is a related party to the CEO of Scanship
Holding ASA, Mr. Henrik Badin, has today purchased 18 000 shares at a price of
NOK 4.10 per share. After this transaction Mrs. Badin controls 23 349 shares in
the Company. Mr. Henrik Badin controls, in addition, 10 500 000 shares in the
Company through his 100 % owned company Badin Invest Ltd.

ABOUT SCANSHIP HOLDING ASA
Scanship is a cruise industry leader in advanced technologies for clean oceans.
Owners of vessels operating our systems have the solutions to convert all waste
and wastewater to inert materials, recyclables, clean flue gas and effluent,
which meets the highest international discharge standards. Our wastewater
purification system is the only system in operation for large cruise ships which
complies with the new effluent discharge standards for nutrient removal being
enforced from 2019. Our new technologies will recover energy from waste and
produce energy, providing tangible payback from the operations. We strive for
the highest quality, innovation and sustainable solutions.
