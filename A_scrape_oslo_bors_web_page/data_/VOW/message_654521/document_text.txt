Ulf Tore Hekneby, close associate of Cecilie Brænd Hekneby, CFO in Vow ASA, has
purchased 927,941 shares at an average price of NOK 2.0433 per share in Vow ASA.
After this transaction, Mrs. Hekneby and close associates own 4,171,299 shares
in the Company.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to article
19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act.
