On December 8, 2022, Cruella AS, a company fully owned by board member Cathrine Haavind, acquired 6,250 IWS-shares in connection with a demerger of a family-owned company. Cruella AS did not own any shares before the transaction. After the transaction, Cathrine Haavind`s total number of shares, including the shares owned by persons closely associated to her, is 6,250.
