AF Gruppen ASA (AFG) has completed the sale of 
90,920 own shares to key employees and management, 
of which 48,100 were sold to primary insiders. The 
sale is part of the company's bonus program where 
parts of the bonus may be used to buy AF Gruppen ASA 
shares at 20 % discount compared to the market price 
as of 31.12.14, NOK 79,00.

When the share registration is completed, the number 
of own shares held by AF Gruppen ASA will be 0.



Other notifiable trades:

Primary insiders and related parties have in 
addition bought 102,800 shares and sold 139,600 
shares.

Of these, OBOS BBL AS, a related party to board 
member of AF Gruppen has bought 54 800 shares and KB 
Gruppen Kongsvinger AS, related party to chairman of 
the board in AF Gruppen ASA has bought 18,000 shares.

Abovementioned transactions have been carried out at 
NOK 99.50 per share.
