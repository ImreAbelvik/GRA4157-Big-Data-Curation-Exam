TONITO AS bought 7 500 shares in AF Gruppen ASA December 6th. Board member in AF Gruppen ASA, Borghild Lunde is Chairman of the board in TONITO AS. The shares were bought at an average price of NOK 137,36 per share. Following this transaction, TONITO AS owns 25 000 shares in AF Gruppen ASA.
