Constructio AS (related party to Kristian Holth, board member in AF Gruppen ASA and board member in Constructio AS) has today, March 1, 2019, purchased 70,000 shares at 152.50 per share. Constructio AS owns 13,811,782 shares in AF Gruppen ASA after this trade. Flygind AS (also related party to Kristian Holth) continues to own 1,021,509 shares in AF Gruppen ASA. The two companies now own a total of 14.87 % of the total number of shares and voting rights in AF Gruppen ASA.
