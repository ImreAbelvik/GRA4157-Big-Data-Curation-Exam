In connection with the share purchase program in AF Gruppen ASA (AFG), primary insiders of the Company have subscribed 16,840 shares at NOK 128.80 per share, which corresponds to a 20 per cent discount compared to the average market price during the subscription period. Please see attachment for details. In connection with this, the Board has decided to sell 14,640 of the Company’s own shares at NOK 128.80 per share. The Company’s holding of own shares after this transaction will be 360 shares. The remaining 2 200 shares will be provided through the increase of the share capital by NOK 27,230.40 as notified in the stock exchange notification dated 20 June 2019, since two of the original subscribers in this capital increase have withdrawn.

The transactions are expected to be completed by July 15th 2019.
