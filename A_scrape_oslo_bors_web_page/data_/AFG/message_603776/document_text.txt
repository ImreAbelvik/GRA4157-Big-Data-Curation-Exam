Constructio AS (related party to Kristian Holth, board member in AF Gruppen ASA (“AF Gruppen ASA” or the “Company”) and board member in Constructio AS) has today, November 13, 2023, purchased 50,000 shares at an average price per share of NOK 110.30. Constructio AS owns 15,338,012 shares in AF Gruppen ASA after this trade. Flygind AS (also related party to Kristian Holth) continues to own 1,021,509 shares in AF Gruppen ASA. The two companies now own a total of 15.07 % of the total number of shares and voting rights in AF Gruppen ASA.
