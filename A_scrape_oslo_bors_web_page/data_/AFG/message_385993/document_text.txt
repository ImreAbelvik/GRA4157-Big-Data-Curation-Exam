ØMF Holding AS, related party to Tore Thorstensen, 
Chairman of the Board in AF Gruppen ASA and ØMF 
Holding AS, purchased 185,000 shares in AF Gruppen 
ASA on 23 September 2015 at NOK 108.50 per share.

ØMF Holding AS owns 13,884,030 shares in AF Gruppen 
ASA after this trade, corresponding to 15.12% of the 
total number of shares and voting rights in AF 
Gruppen ASA.
