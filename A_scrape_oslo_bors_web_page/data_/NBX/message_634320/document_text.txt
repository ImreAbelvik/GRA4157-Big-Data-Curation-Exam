Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement published by Norwegian Block Exchange AS (the "Company") 10 December 2024, regarding the final results of the rights issue (the "Rights Issue"). 

The Board of Directors has allocated shares to the following primary insiders and close associates of primary insiders in the Rights Issue. 

•Stig Alexander Kjos-Mathisen, who is the CEO of the Company, has been allocated 455,881 shares in the Company; 
•Observatoriet Invest AS, a company closely associated with Bjørn Kjos, who is a board member in the Company, has been allocated 1,223,287 shares in the Company; 
•Nye Km Aviatrix Invest AS, a company closely associated with Anna Helene Kjos-Mathisen, who is a board member in the Company, has been allocated 1,851,852 shares in the Company; and
•Dasha Invest AS, a company closely associated with Nils Sundling, who is the chairman of the board in the Company, has been allocated 1,851,852 shares in the Company.

Please see the attached notifications of trade for information regarding the primary insiders' and their close associates' allocation of shares in the Rights Issue.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article 19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
