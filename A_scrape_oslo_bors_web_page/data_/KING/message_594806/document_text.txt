Kats, Netherlands – July 5, 2023: Reference is made to the stock exchange notice by The Kingfish Company N.V. (the "Company") on July 5, 2023 (the "Notice") regarding the Company successfully raising EUR 32 million in an unsecured convertible loan (the "Convertible Loan"). All terms not defined herein shall have the meaning ascribed to such terms in the Notice. 

The following close associates of primary insiders of the Company have on July 5, 2023 been granted rights to subscribe for shares in the capital of the Company in connection with the issuance of Tranche 1 of the Convertible Loan as set out below: 

– Creadev International S.A.S., a close associate of Supervisory Board Member Alexandre Van Der Wees, has been granted 3,275,863 rights to subscribe for shares in the capital of the Company; and

– Stolt-Nielsen M.S. Ltd, a close associate of Supervisory Board Member Jordi Trias Fita, has been granted 1,247,277 rights to subscribe for shares in the capital of the Company.

Please see the enclosed forms for further details about the transactions. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 number 3 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
