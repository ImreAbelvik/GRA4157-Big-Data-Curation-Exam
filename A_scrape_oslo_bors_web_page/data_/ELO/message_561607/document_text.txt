Oslo, 9 May 2022: Ferd AS, close associate to board member Trond Solberg and board observer Manuel Arbiol, has today, acquired 3.896.267 shares in Elopak ASA at an average price of NOK 16,3762. Reference is made to the attached Trade Notification Form for further details of the transaction.
 
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in articles 19 of the Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
