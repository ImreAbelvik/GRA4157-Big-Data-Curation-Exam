Reference is made to the stock exchange notice published on 17 September 2024 regarding the completion of a secondary placement of existing shares in Elopak ASA by Ferd AS.

Ferd AS, a close associate of board member and primary insider Manuel Arbiol Pascual, has sold 20,000,000 shares in Elopak ASA at a price of NOK 41.0 per share on 17 September 2024. Following the transaction, Ferd AS owns 119,465,870 shares in Elopak ASA, equivalent to 44.4% of the company's share capital. 

See the enclosed form for further details about the transaction.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 number 3 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
