


Release of employee restricted share units in SalMar ASA ("the Company").

1. Participants in the Company's RSU program have on November 16th 2017 settled
a total number of 229 446 RSUs. Following the release, 229 446 shares will be
transferred from the Company's holding of own shares. Subsequent to the
transaction the Company holds 754 922 own shares.

2. Restricted share units released by primary insiders November 16th 2017:
- Geir Berg has received 1 908 shares in the Company
- Olav Andreas Ervik has received 5 090 shares in the Company
- Runar Sivertsen has received 1 874 shares in the Company
- Tom Aleksandersen has received 2 780 shares in the Company
- Tonette Bjørgård has received 3 360 shares in the Company
- Trond Tuvstein has received 5 273 shares in the Company
- Trond Williksen has received 1 348 shares in the Company
- Ulrik Steinvik has received 4 817 shares in the Company

3. Sale of shares
On November 16th 2017, the primary insiders mentioned below ordered for a
portion of the received shares to be immediately sold by a third party in order
to cover the tax liability.

The sales price subsequently obtained by the third party when selling the shares
in the market is NOK 257.3209.
- Geir Berg has sold 897 shares in the Company

- Tonette Bjørgård has sold 1 580 shares in the Company
