


Release of employee restricted share units in SalMar ASA ("the Company").

1. Participants in the Company's RSU program have on November 13th 2015 settled
a total number of 119 919 RSUs. Following the release, 119 919 shares will be
transferred from the Company's holding of own shares. Subsequent to the
transaction the Company holds 1 180 081 own shares.

Company

3. Sale of shares

portion of the received shares to be immediately sold by a third party in order
to cover the tax liability.

in the market is NOK 141.4669.
