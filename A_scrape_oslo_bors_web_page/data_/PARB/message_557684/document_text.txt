As announced in the stock exchange announcement on 03.03.2022, Pareto Bank ASA has granted [Pareto Securities AS] a mandate to purchase shares for NOK 10.000.000 related to the bonus and employee share purchase program. The stock exchange announcement on 03.03.2022 stated that the shares would be purchased until 25.03.2022. The buy-back program commenced 07.03.2022. The buy-back program has now been completed.
