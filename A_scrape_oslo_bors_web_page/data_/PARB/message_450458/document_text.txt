Pareto Pensions AS, a subsidiary of primary insider Pareto AS, has today purchased 200.000 shares at NOK 38,25 per share in Pareto Bank. 
Following the transaction, Pareto Pensions AS will own 200.000 shares in Pareto Bank ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securitites Trading Act.
