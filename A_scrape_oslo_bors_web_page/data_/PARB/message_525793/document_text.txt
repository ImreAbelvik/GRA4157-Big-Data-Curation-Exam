Primary insider Pareto AS has today purchased 200 000 shares at NOK 43.20 per share in Pareto Bank. The shares were acquired from the sister company Pareto Pensions AS. Following the transaction, Pareto AS, will own 13 823 610 shares in Pareto Bank ASA. 
 
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securitites Trading Act.
