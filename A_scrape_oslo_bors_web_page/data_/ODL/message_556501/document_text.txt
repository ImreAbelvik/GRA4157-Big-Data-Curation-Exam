Odfjell Partners Ltd has on 14 March 2022 in a private transaction acquired 104,590 common shares in Odfjell Drilling Ltd ("ODL") from the CEO of Odfjell Drilling AS and a primary insider in ODL, Simen Lieungh, at a price per share of NOK 23.80. Odfjell Partners Ltd is a closely associated person of Helene Odfjell, who is a primary insider and the chair of the board of ODL. 
 
Following the transaction, Simen Lieungh holds zero common shares in ODL and has options to acquire up to 960,000 additional common shares in ODL. 
 
Following the transaction Odfjell Partners Ltd owns 142,952,381 common shares in ODL representing approx. 60.4% of the common shares issued by ODL.
