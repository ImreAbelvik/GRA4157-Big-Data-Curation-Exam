Odfjell Partners Ltd has on 8 June 2021 in a private transaction acquired 371,600 common shares in Odfjell Drilling Ltd ("ODL") from the CEO of Odfjell Drilling AS and a primary insider in ODL, Simen Lieungh, at a price per share of NOK 22.84. Odfjell Partners Ltd is a closely associated person of Helene Odfjell, who is a primary insider and the chair of the board of ODL. 
 
Following the transaction, Simen Lieungh owns 104,590 common shares in ODL and has options to acquire up to 960,000 additional common shares in ODL. 
 
Following the transaction Odfjell Partners Ltd owns 142,847,791 common shares in ODL representing approx. 60.3% of the common shares issued by ODL.
