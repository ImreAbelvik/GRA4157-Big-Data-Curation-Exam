Ingeborg Øfsthus, Executive Vice President of Telenor Group 
and Chief Executive Officer of Telenor Montenegro and 
Serbia, has on 27 July 2016 acquired 460 Telenor shares at 
NOK 141.30 per share. After the transaction, Øfsthus (incl. 
related parties) holds 3,549 shares in Telenor.

Members of Telenor's Group Executive Management should at a 
minimum have, and keep, shareholdings in Telenor ASA 
corresponding to the value of one annual base salary. The 
share purchase was made to meet this requirement.
