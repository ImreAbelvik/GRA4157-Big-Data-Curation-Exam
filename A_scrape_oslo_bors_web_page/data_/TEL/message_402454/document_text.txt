Sharad Mehrotra, Executive Vice President of Telenor Group and 
Chief Executive Officer of Telenor India, has on 11 February 
2016 sold 229 Telenor shares at NOK 120.46 per share. Including 
the trades reported on 16 February 2016 and 1 March 2016, 
Mehrotra's total shareholding (including related parties) is 
7,122 shares.
