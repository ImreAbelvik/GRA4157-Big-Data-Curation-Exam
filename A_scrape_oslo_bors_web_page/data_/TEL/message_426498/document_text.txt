Jørgen C. Arentz Rostrup, Chief Financial Officer of Telenor 
Group, has on 4 May 2017 acquired 3,500 Telenor shares at 
NOK 137.15 per share. The transaction has been made through 
Roccasio a.s., which is 100% owned by Mr. Rostrup. After the 
transaction, Jørgen C. Arentz Rostrup (incl. related 
parties) holds 7,057 shares in Telenor.
