Christopher Adam Laska, Executive Vice President and Chief 
Operating Officer of Telenor Hungary, has on 29 October 2015 
sold 212 Telenor shares at NOK 173.15 per share. After this 
transaction, Laska (incl. related parties) held 7,952 shares in 
Telenor.

Including the trades reported on 1 March 2016, 16 February 2016 
and 17 November 2015, Laska's total shareholding as of 8 April 
2016 is 10,906 shares.
