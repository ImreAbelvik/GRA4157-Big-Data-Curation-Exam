Jesper Hansen, Executive Vice President of Telenor Group and 
Chief Executive Officer of Telenor Denmark, has on 21 July 2016 
acquired 576 Telenor shares at NOK 148.80 per share. After the 
transaction, Hansen (incl. related parties) holds 19,196 shares 
in Telenor.

Members of Telenor's Group Executive Management should at a 
minimum have, and keep, shareholdings in Telenor ASA 
corresponding to the value of one annual base salary. The share 
purchase was made to meet this requirement.
