Henrik Clausen, Executive Vice President and Head of Digital 
and Strategy in Telenor ASA, has on 3 June 2015 acquired 229 
shares in Telenor, at a price of NOK 180.74 per share. The 
acquisition is made in connection with reinvestment of 
dividends received on shares related to a long-term 
incentive programme and an employee share programme in 
Telenor. After the transaction, Henrik Clausen (incl related 
parties) holds 26,393 shares in Telenor.
