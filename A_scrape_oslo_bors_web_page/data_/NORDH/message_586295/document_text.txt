Helsinki, Finland 27 March 2023: Charles William MacBain, CEO and a primary
insider of Nordhealth AS (the "Company"), has through his majority owned company
Three Plus Group Luxembourg SA ("TPG") entered into a share exchange agreement
with Hanna Alanen. TPG exchanged 51,640 class A shares for the same number of
class B shares held by Hanna Alanen. Following the share exchange, Charles
MacBain owns 4,904,387 class A shares and 29,794,638 class B shares in
Nordhealth, through TPG and 4,185,746 class A shares directly.

Please refer to the attached notification of trading for further details.

About Nordhealth

Nordhealth is a publicly listed, fast-growing cloud-based healthcare SaaS
company on a mission to redefine digital healthcare. We build software that
empower veterinary and therapy professionals to provide the best possible care
experiences to their patients. Our products serve more than 50,000 veterinary
and therapy professionals across 10,000 clinics and hospitals located in over 30
countries. Headquartered in Helsinki, Finland, Nordhealth has a solid footprint
in the Nordic region and a growing presence internationally, with more than 350
employees working either remotely or from offices (collaboration hubs). Visit
the company's website at nordhealth.com (http://www.nordhealth.com)
