ASKER, NORWAY (15 August 2019) - Kristian Johansen, CEO of TGS, has today
purchased 7,000 shares in TGS at a price of NOK 205.93 per share. Following the
transaction Kristian Johansen owns 36,950 shares in TGS in addition to 136,000
free-standing warrants related to Performance Share Units (PSU) as part of the
Company's long-term incentive scheme.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
