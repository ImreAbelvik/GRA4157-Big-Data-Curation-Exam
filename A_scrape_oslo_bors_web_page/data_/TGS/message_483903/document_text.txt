ASKER, NORWAY (27 August 2019) - Reference is made to previous stock exchange
announcement on 5 August 2019 published by TGS-NOPEC Geophysical Company ASA
("TGS", OSE: TGS) regarding vesting of Performance Stock Units (PSUs) and
Restricted Stock Units (RSUs).

As a consequence of the vesting, TGS has now issued 182,941 shares.  The share
capital of TGS is thereafter NOK 29,726,694.50 consisting of 118,906,778 shares,
each with a nominal value of NOK 0.25.  TGS holds 1,045,000 of its share capital
as treasury shares.

The vesting of PSUs and RSUs granted by TGS in 2016 in accordance with the terms
of the 2016 Long Term Incentive Plan resulted in the right for a total of 59 TGS
employees to request the issuance of an aggregate of up to 239,094 shares of TGS
common stock pursuant to free-standing warrants subscribed by the employees at
the time of grant.  Employees had the right to request TGS to settle a portion
of the vested units in cash to allow satisfaction of employees' tax withholding
obligations arising as a result of the vest.

Following the vest of the PSUs and RSUs, an aggregate of 182,941 new shares of
TGS common stock have been issued to the 59 employees on 27 August 2019, which
amount is net of any units that were settled in cash.  The following reflects
the shares issued to the primary insiders:

Company summary
TGS-NOPEC Geophysical Company (TGS) provides multi-client geoscience data to oil
and gas Exploration and Production companies worldwide.  In addition to
extensive global geophysical and geological data libraries that include multi-
client seismic data, magnetic and gravity data, digital well logs, production
data and directional surveys, TGS also offers advanced processing and imaging
services, interpretation products, and data integration solutions.
For more information visit TGS online at www.tgs.com.
