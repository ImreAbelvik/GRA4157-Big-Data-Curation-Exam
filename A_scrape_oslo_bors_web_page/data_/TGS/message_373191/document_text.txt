Reference is made to the stock exchange release on 6 
February 2014 announcing a buyback program of USD 30 
million.
 
As part of this program, TGS-NOPEC Geophysical 
Company ASA (TGS) purchased 20,000 own shares on 11 
March 2015. The shares were purchased at an average 
price of NOK 186.6419 per share.
 
After the transaction TGS holds 1,900,112 own shares, 
representing 1.84% of the outstanding number of 
shares.
