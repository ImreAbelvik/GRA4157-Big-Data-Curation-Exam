Reference is made to the stock exchange release on 6 
February 2014 announcing a buyback program of USD 30 
million.
 
As part of this program, TGS-NOPEC Geophysical 
Company ASA (TGS) purchased 30,000 own shares on 30 
December 2014. The shares were purchased at an 
average price of NOK 161.76 per share.
 
After the transaction TGS holds 1,843,512 own shares, 
representing 1.79% of the outstanding number of 
shares.

As part of the buyback program announced on 6 
February 2014, TGS has in 2014 purchased 938,548 
shares for a total amount of USD 25 million. The 
company will continue purchasing own shares in 2015 
under the current authorizations to reach the 
targeted amount of USD 30 million.

requirements acc. to ยง5-12 vphl (Norwegian Securities 
Trading Act).
