(ASKER, NORWAY - 29 July 2019) - Kristian Johansen, CEO of TGS, has today purchased 1,500 shares in TGS at a price of NOK 219.22 per share. Following the transaction Kristian Johansen owns 29,950 shares in TGS in addition to 204,000 free-standing warrants related to Performance Share Units (PSU) as part of the Company's long-term incentive scheme. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
