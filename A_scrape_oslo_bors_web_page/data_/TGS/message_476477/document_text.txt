(ASKER, NORWAY - 9 May 2019) - Kristian Johansen, CEO of TGS, has today
purchased 2,250 shares in TGS at a price of NOK 220.99 per share.

Following the transaction Kristian Johansen owns 28,450 shares in TGS in
addition to 204,000 free-standing warrants related to Performance Share Units
(PSU) as part of the Company's long-term incentive scheme.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
