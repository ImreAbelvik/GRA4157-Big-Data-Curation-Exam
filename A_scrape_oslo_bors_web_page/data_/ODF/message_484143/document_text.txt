Norchem AS in Denmark (in which Laurence Odfjell, Chairman of the Board in Odfjell SE, has a controlling interest) has today acquired 67 126 Odfjell SE B-shares at NOK 24,9258 per share.
 
Following this transaction, Norchem AS owns 25 966 492 A-shares and 5 603 507 B-shares in Odfjell SE, equivalent to 36.38% of the total outstanding stock of
Odfjell SE. In addition Laurence Odfjell owns 463 012 B-shares.
