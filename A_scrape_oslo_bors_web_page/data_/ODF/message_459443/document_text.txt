Norchem AS in Denmark (in which Laurence Odfjell, Chairman of the Board in Odfjell SE, has a controlling interest) has today acquired 767 000 Odfjell B-shares in Odfjell at NOK 30.10 per share.

Following this transaction, Norchem AS owns 25 966 492 A-shares and 4 536 381 B-shares in Odfjell SE, equivalent to 35.15% of the total outstanding stock of
Odfjell SE. In addition Laurence Odfjell owns 463 012 B-shares.
