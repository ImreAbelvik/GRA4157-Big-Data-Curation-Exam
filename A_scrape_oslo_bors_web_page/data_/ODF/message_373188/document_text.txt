Norchem AS in Denmark (in which Laurence Odfjell, 
Chairman of the Board in Odfjell SE, has a 
controlling interest) has today acquired 1 121 287 
Odfjell B-shares in Odfjell at NOK 18.85 per share.
 
Following this transaction, Norchem AS owns 25 966 
492 A-shares and 3 536 381 B-shares in Odfjell SE, 
equivalent to 34% of the total outstanding stock of 
Odfjell SE.
