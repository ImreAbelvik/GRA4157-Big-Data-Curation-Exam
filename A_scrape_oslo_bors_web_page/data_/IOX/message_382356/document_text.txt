Andes Energia Plc ("Andes") has on 21 July 2015 
transferred all of its shares in Interoil Exploration 
and Production ASA ("Interoil") to Andes Interoil
Ltd, a newly established wholly owned subsidiary of 
Andes with the sole purpose of owning the Interoil 
shares. The transfer price is equivalent to the 
previous purchase price paid by Andes (i.e NOK 
1.10 per share) plus expenses. Following completion 
of the transfer, Andes does not hold any shares of 
Interoil whilst Andes Interoil Ltd holds 33,004,188 
shares of Interoil, equal to 51.02 % of the share 
capital. Pursuant to an exemption granted by Oslo 
BÃ¸rs, the intra-group transfer will not trigger any 
offer obligations.

Andes is represented on the Board of Directors of 
Interoil.
