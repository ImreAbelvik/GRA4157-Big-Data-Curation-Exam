Canacol Energy LTD has on September 12, 2017 sold 1,260,000 
shares in Interoil Exploration and Production ASA 
(the "Company") at a price of 3.91 NOK per share. Canacol

constituting approximately 14.67% of the Company's shares.

Interoil Exploration and Production ASA.
