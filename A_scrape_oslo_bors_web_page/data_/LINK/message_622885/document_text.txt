Karbon Invest AS ("Karbon Invest"), has pledged 15,945,105 shares in LINK Mobility Group Holding ASA (the "Company"), corresponding to the total number of shares in the Company held by Karbon Invest. 

Karbon Invest is a close associate of Jens Rugseth, a board member and primary insider in the Company. 

Primary insider notification pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation article 19 is attached.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act, EU Market Abuse Regulation Article 19 and Oslo Rule Book II.
