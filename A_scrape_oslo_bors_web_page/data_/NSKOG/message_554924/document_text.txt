Norske Skog ASA ("Norske Skog", Oslo Børs Ticker: NSKOG) has been notified that the following transaction has been completed today, 24 February 2022, by a primary insider in Norske Skog:

Arvid Grundekjøn, board member and primary insider in Norske Skog, has today indirectly bought 10,000 shares in Norske Skog through Cardid AS for an average price of NOK 47.4495 per share.
