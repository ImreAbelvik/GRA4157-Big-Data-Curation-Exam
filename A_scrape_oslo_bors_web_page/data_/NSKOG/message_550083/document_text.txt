Jen-Yue (John) Chiang, primary insider in Norske Skog ASA, has on 21 December 2021 purchased 50,000 shares in Norske Skog ASA at an average price of NOK 36.81 per share. After the transaction, John Chiang holds 76,315 shares in Norske Skog ASA.

John Chiang is the chairman of the board of directors of Norske Skog ASA.

NS Norway Holding AS, which is ultimately controlled by Oceanwood Capital Management LLP where John Chiang is a senior partner, holds 26,896,681 shares (equal to approximately 28.5%) in Norske Skog ASA.
