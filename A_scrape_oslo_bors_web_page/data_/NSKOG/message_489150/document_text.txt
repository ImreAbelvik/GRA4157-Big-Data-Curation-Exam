Oslo, 12 November 2019: Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement on 16 October 2019, in which Norske Skog ASA ("Norske Skog" or the "Company", OSE ticker code "NSKOG") announced the successful completion of its initial public offering (the "Offering") and the stock exchange announcement earlier today, 12 November 2019, regarding performed stabilisation activities in respect of the shares of Norske Skog and ABG Sundal Collier ASA, as stabilisation manager, on behalf of the Managers of the Offering, exercising its option to purchase, from NS Norway Holding AS, 338,614 shares in Norske Skog at a price of NOK 38.00 per share.

Following the sale of the 338,614 shares, NS Norway Holding AS owns 52,161,386 shares, representing 63.23% of the shares and votes in the Company.

NS Norway Holding AS is through its chairman Jen-Yue (John) Chiang represented on the board of directors of the Company.

About Norske Skog
Norske Skog is a leading producer of publication paper with strong market positions in Europe, where it occupies a top-tier publication paper position, and Australasia, where Norske Skog is a leading producer of publication paper in a concentrated market.

Norske Skog operates a total of seven paper mills strategically located close to attractive markets and with total paper production capacity of 2.6 million tons, comprising 1.7 million tons of newsprint and 0.9 million tons of magazine paper (super-calendered and coated mechanical).
