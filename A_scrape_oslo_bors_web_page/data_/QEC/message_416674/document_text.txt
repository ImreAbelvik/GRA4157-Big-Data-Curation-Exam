On December 21, 2016, certain directors and management of 
Questerre exercised a total of 980,000 stock options at an 
exercise price of C$0.61/share. The options were issued on 
December 31, 2011 and expire on December 31, 2016. In 
addition to the announcement made on December 20, 2016, this 
bring the total options exercised by insiders in December 
2016 to 1,120,000.

Following the exercise, the insiders reported below 
completed a subsequent sale consisting of 1,070,000 shares 
at an average price of 6.09 NOK. As a result of these 
transactions, the only shares sold were those issued from 
these expiring options. None of the insiders have sold any 
shares from their pre-existing holdings.

Michael Binnion, President & Chief Executive Officer - 
exercise of 450,000 stock options and participation of 
450,000 shares in placement. Total number of shares held 
unchanged at 8,976,182. Mr. Binnion also exercises control
