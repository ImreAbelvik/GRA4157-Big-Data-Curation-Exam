Michael Binnion, President & Chief Executive Officer, 
transferred 1,000,000 Common Shares of Questerre at a price of 
$0.20 per Common Share to the Leslie Binnion Foundation for 
Disabled Sports, a charitable organization.

Upon completion of this transfer, Mr Binnion holds, directly or 
indirectly, 15.71 million Common Shares and 2.1 million 
warrants.
