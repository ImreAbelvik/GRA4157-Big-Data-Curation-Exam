
On November 19, 2015, Questerre Energy Corporation reported 
that its Chairman, Bjorn Inge Tonnessen, acquired 30,000 Common 
Shares of Questerre at a price of NOK 1,55. After this 
transaction, Mr. Tonnessen holds 45,000 Common Shares of 
Questerre.

This information is subject of the Disclosure Requirements
pursuant two section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading
Act.
