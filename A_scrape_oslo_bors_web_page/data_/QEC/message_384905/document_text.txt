On September 3, 2015, Michael Binnion, President and Chief 
Executive Officer of Questerre, acquired a total of 
50,000 Common Shares at an average price of 1.38 NOK.
After this acquisition he directly holds 7,426,182 Common 
Shares and exercises direction over an additional 
4,735,792 Common Shares.

This information is subject of the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act.
