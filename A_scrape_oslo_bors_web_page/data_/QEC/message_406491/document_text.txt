The Company reported on the Common Shares and Warrants 
acquired by directors and officers in the recently completed 
equity placement for gross proceeds of 4.75 million. 

Michael Binnion, President and Chief Executive Officer, 
acquired, directly and indirectly, 13.45 million units at a 
price of $0.18 per unit. As previously reported, 9.25 
million units were subsequently vended to third parties at a 
price of $0.16 per unit. Upon completion of these 
transactions, Mr. Binnion holds, directly and indirectly, 
16.71 million Common Shares and 2.1 million warrants.

Dennis Sykora, Director of the Company, acquired 278,000 
units at a price of $0.18 per unit. Mr. Sykora currently 
holds 304,750 Common Shares and 139,000 warrants.

Mr. D'Silva, Chief Financial Officer, acquired 137,500 units 
at a price of $0.18 per unit. Mr. D'Silva currently holds 
3,170,640 Common Shares and 68,750 warrants.

The Company seeks safe harbour.
