Ålesund, 21 November 2022: Trond Håkon Schaug-Pettersen, CFO and interim CEO of
Salmon Evolution ASA (the "Company"), has today transferred 400,000 shares in
the Company at a price of NOK 8.35 per share to Troha Invest AS, a company
wholly owned by, and closely associated with, Trond Håkon Schaug-Pettersen.
Following the transaction, Troha Invest AS holds a total of 400,000 shares in
the Company and Trond Håkon Schaug-Pettersen holds no shares directly in the
Company. However, Trond Håkon Schaug-Pettersen indirectly holds 400,000 shares
in the Company through Troha Invest AS.

Please see attached notification forms in accordance with the Market Abuse
Regulation article 19.
