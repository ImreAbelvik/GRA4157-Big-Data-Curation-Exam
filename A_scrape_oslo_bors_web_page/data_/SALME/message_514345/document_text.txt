Board member Frode Kjølås, a primary insider in Salmon Evolution Holding AS, has on September 24th, through Kjølsås Stansekniver AS, a company he controls, purchased 200.000 shares in the Salmon Evolution Holding AS at a price of NOK 6,6033 per share.

After the transaction Frode Kjølsås holds 9.506.006 shares through his company Kjølsås Stansekniver AS. Mr. Kjøslås also owns 50% of the Shares in Møring AS, which owns 1 800 000 shares in Salmon Evolution Holding AS.
