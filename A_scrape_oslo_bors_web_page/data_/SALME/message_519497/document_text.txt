Board member Frode Kjølås, a primary insider in Salmon Evolution Holding AS, has
on December 1[st] purchased 20.000 shares in the Salmon Evolution Holding AS at
a price of NOK 5,5587 per share.

After the transaction Frode Kjølsås holds 9.706.006 shares through his company
Kjølsås Stansekniver AS and 20.000 shares personally. Mr. Kjøslås also owns 50%
of the Shares in Møring AS, which owns 2.000.000 shares in Salmon Evolution
Holding AS.
