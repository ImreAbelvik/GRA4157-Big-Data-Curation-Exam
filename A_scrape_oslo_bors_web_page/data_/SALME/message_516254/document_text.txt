Board member Frode Kjølås, a primary insider in Salmon Evolution Holding AS, has on October 23rd, through Møring AS a company where he owns 50%, purchased 200.000 shares in the Salmon Evolution Holding AS at a price of NOK 5,5502 per share.

After the transaction Frode Kjølås holds 9.506.006 shares through his company Kjølsås Stansekniver AS. Mr. Kjølås also owns 50% of the Shares in Møring AS, which owns 2.000.000 shares in Salmon Evolution Holding AS.
