Oslo, May 30. Reference is made to the announcement by Sikri Holding AS (the "Company") 27 May 2022 regarding allocation to primary insiders in the employee share purchase program for 2022.
Below is a correction regarding details on the allocation to board member Torbjørn Krøvel. The reason for the correction is that Torbjørn Krøvel was allocated shares personally and not through Full Storm Invest AS.

Torbjørn Krøvel, board member, was allocated 1,405 shares under the 2022 ESPP. After completion of the share capital increase Torbjørn Krøvel controls 1,405 shares in the Company and holds the right to 468 bonus shares under the 2022 ESPP: In addition, Full Storm Invest AS, a company where Torbjørn Krøvel holds 50% of the shares, controls 1,282 shares in the Company and  holds the right to 427 Bonus Shares under the 2020 ESPP.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation Article 19 and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
