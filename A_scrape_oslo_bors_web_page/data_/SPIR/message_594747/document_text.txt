Oslo, 4 July 2023: Reference is made to the announcement by Sikri Group ASA (the "Company") on 4 July 2023 regarding delivery of Bonus Shares under the retail offering completed in connection with admission of the Company to trading on Oslo Børs (the "Retail Offering") and the issuance of compensation shares to Karbon Invest AS ("Karbon").

Karbon, a close associate of Jens Rugseth, has today transferred for no consideration a total of 114,074 shares to the Company to be further distributed by the Company to the investors qualifying for Bonus Shares under the Retail Offering. Subsequently, Karbon has subscribed for 114,366 compensation shares at a subscription price equal to the nominal value of the Company's shares of NOK 0.02, in the subsequent share capital increase.
 
Furthermore, the following primary insiders, or close associates of primary insiders, will be allocated Bonus Shares in connection with the Retail Offering:

* Emma Havaas Lorentzen, a close associate of Anne Mette Havaas, Director Marketing and Communication, has been allocated 125 bonus shares in connection with the Retail Offering.
* Jonas Havaas Lorentzen, a close associate of Anne Mette Havaas, Director Marketing and Communication, has been allocated 125 bonus shares in connection with the Retail Offering.
* Vilde Sørensen Vabo, a close associate of Anne Mette Havaas, Director Marketing and Communication, has been allocated 125 bonus shares in connection with the Retail Offering.

Primary insider notifications and their close associates pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation Article 19 are attached.
