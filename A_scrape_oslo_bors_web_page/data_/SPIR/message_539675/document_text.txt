Oslo, 13 August 2021. Carucel N59 AS and its parent company Carucel Finance AS, each a closely associated person of the board members and primary insiders in Sikri Holding AS ("Sikri"), Preben Rasch-Olsen and Fredrik Cappelen, merged without consideration on 13 August 2021, with Carucel Finance AS as the surviving entity. 

Following the merger, Carucel N59 AS' shareholding in Sikri is now held by Carucel Finance AS. Carucel Finance AS, after the merger, holds 2,099,474 shares in Sikri. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation.
