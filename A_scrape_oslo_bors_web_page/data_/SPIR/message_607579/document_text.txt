Oslo, 23 December 2023. Ulf Tore Hekneby, a person closely associated with Cecilie M. Brænd Hekneby, appointed new Chief Financial Officer (CFO) at Spir Group ASA (effective from 1 April 2024 or as further to be agreed between the parties), has acquired 118,663 shares in Spir Group ASA at NOK 6.07 per share. Ulf Tore Hekneby’s total shareholding after this trade is 118,663 shares. In addition, Ulrik Hekneby, a person closely associated with Cecilie M. Brænd Hekneby, holds a total of 12,400 shares, and Cecilie M. Brænd Hekneby holds 836,820 shares personally.

Primary insider notification pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation Article 19 is attached.
