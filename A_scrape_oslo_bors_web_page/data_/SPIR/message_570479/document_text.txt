Oslo, 3 September 2022. Reference is made to the announcement by Sikri Holding ASA (the “Company”) on 2 September 2022 regarding the completed private placement and the proposed subsequent offering. 

The following closely related parties of primary insiders in the Company subscribed for shares in the private placement: 

Karbon Invest AS, closely related party to Jens Rugseth (board member), subscribed for 10,482,638 shares in the private placement.
 
Carucel Finance AS, closely related party to Preben Rasch-Olsen (board member), subscribed for 3,440,758 shares in the private placement. 

Stella Industrier AS, closely related party to Fredrik Cappelen (advisor to the Company on M&A) , subscribed for 3,315,114 shares in the private placement. 

Varner Kapital AS, closely related party to Espen Karlsen (observer on the board), subscribed for 2,761,490 shares in the private placement. 

Primary insider notifications pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation Article 19 are attached.
