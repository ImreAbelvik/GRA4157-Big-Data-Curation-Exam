22 September 2020. Karbon Invest AS ("Karbon") has today resolved to merge with its 100% owned subsidiary KCM Holding AS ("KCM"). 
Karbon holds today, indirectly through KCM, 7,211,273 shares in Sikri Holding AS (the "Company"). Following completion of the merger, expected to take place beginning of November 2020, the shares in the Company will be held directly by Karbon. Karbon's ownership in the Company will remain unchanged as a result of the merger.
Karbon is indirectly controlled by chairman of the board in the Company, Jens Rugseth. Karbon is directly controlled by Rugz AS and Rugz II AS (together referred to as "Rugz") and Rugz is directly controlled by Mr. Rugseth.
