Chairman of the Board in Protector Forsikring ASA (Ticker PCT) 
Jostein Sørvoll owns the majority of shares in Gabler Rådgivning 
AS which in turn owns 1 702 751 shares in Protector Forsikring 
ASA.

In connection with reorganization of Gabler Rådgiving AS, which 
is sent the Norwegian Brønnøysund Register Centre today, the 
shares owned by Gabler Rådgiving AS in Protector Forsikring ASA 
- valued at NOK 102 164 060, or NOK 60 per share - will be 
transferred to Tomma Invest AS as of 30. September 2015.

The ownership of Tomma Invest AS is identical to the ownership 
of Gabler Rådgiving AS, and Jostein Sørvoll is the majority 
owner of Tomma Invest AS. After the transaction Gabler Rådgiving 
AS will own 0 shares, and Tomma Invest will own 1 702 751 shares 
in Protector Forsikring ASA.
