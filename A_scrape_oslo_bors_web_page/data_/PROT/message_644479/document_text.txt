
AWC AS, a related party to vice chairman in Protector Forsikring ASA Arve Ree, has today 25 April 2025 acquired 353,305 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 362.9973 per share. Following this transaction, AWC AS holds 16,181,312 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
