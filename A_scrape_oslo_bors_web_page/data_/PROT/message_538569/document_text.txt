Kjetil Garstad, board member in Protector Forsikring ASA, has today 23 July 2021 acquired 25,000 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA through his related party Steel City AS at an average price of NOK 92.50 per share. Following this transaction, Steel City AS holds 144,706 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
