Primary insider and deputy CEO, Hans Didring, has today 30 July transferred 20,000 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA as a gift to his wife Ebba Adelsohn Didring. Following this transaction, Hans Didring holds 260,166 shares and Ebba Adelsohn Didring holds 28,000 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
