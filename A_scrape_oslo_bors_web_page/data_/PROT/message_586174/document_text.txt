AWC AS, a related party to vice chairman in Protector Forsikring ASA Arve Ree, has today 24 March 2023 acquired 148,399 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 134.9787 per share. Following this transaction, AWC AS holds 11,345,875 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
