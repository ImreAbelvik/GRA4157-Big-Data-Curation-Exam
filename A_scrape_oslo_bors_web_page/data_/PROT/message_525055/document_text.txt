Awilhelmsen Capital Holdings AS has today, 16 February 2021, purchased 300’000 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at NOK 72.40 per share. 

Arve Ree, CEO of Awilhemsen Capital Holdings AS, is vice chairman of Protector Forsikring ASA.

After the purchase Awilhelmsen Capital Holdings AS owns 8’335’777 shares or 9.68% of the registered shares in Protector Forsikring ASA.
