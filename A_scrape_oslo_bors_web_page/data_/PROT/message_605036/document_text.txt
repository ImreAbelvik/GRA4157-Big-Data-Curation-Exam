Christoffer Skyrud, primary insider and deputy board member elected by and amongst employees in Protector Forsikring ASA, has today 27 November 2023 sold 10,000 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 192.5276 per share. Following this transaction, Mr. Skyrud holds 14,503 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
