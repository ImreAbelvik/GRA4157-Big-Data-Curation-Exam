Steel City AS, a related party to board member in Protector Forsikring ASA, Kjetil Garstad, has today 10 February 2025 acquired 2,700 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 314.8421 per share. Following this transaction, Steel City AS holds 213,406 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
