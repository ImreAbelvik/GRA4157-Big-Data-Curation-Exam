Arve Ree, vice chairman of the board in Protector Forsikring ASA, has today 7 March 2022 acquired 19,917 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA through his related party Reeco AS at an average price of NOK 102.05960 per share. Following this transaction, Reeco AS holds 619,917 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
