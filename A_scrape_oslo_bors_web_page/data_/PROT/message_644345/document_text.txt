Steel City AS, a related party to board member in Protector Forsikring ASA, Kjetil Garstad, has today 24 April 2025 acquired 1,800 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 353.1338 per share. Following this transaction, Steel City AS holds 215,206 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
