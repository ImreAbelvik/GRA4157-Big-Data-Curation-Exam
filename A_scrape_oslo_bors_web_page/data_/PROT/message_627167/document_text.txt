AWC AS, a related party to vice chairman in Protector Forsikring ASA Arve Ree, has today 5 September 2024 acquired 182,854 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 218.25 per share. Following this transaction, AWC AS holds 15,828,007 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
