Awilhelmsen Capital Holdings AS has today, 7 December 2020, purchased 250’000 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at NOK 52.22 per share. 

Arve Ree, CEO of Awilhemsen Capital Holdings AS, is vice chairman of Protector Forsikring ASA.

After the purchase Awilhelmsen Capital Holdings AS owns 7’885’777 shares or 9.15% of the registered shares in Protector Forsikring ASA.
