AWC AS, a related party to vice chairman in Protector Forsikring ASA Arve Ree, has today 27 July 2023 acquired 312,626 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 163.9998 per share. Following this transaction, AWC AS holds 12,714,092 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
