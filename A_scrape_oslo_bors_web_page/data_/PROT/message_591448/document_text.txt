Mr. Jostein Sørvoll, primary insider and Chairman of the Board in Protector Forsikring ASA has today 24 May 2023 acquired 1,250 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 163.00 per share. 

Following this transaction, Mr. Sørvoll holds 504,001 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
