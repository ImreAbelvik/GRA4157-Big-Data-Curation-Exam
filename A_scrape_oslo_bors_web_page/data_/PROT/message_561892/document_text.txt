Protector Forsikring ASA has transferred 51,286 shares today to own employees at a price of NOK 97.80 in connection with the company's share purchase program for 2022.

In addition, 13,461 shares have been transferred free of charge to own employees in connection with the company's share purchase program for 2020, where one bonus share is granted for every fifth share retained during the two-year period.

Protector Forsikring ASA has also transferred 90,129 shares to own employees at a price of NOK 99.70 in connection with the company's long-term bonus program for senior executives and key personnel, and transferred 1,500 shares free of charge to own employees in connection with bonuses to employees of the year.

A total of 156,376 own shares have been transferred to own employees.
