AWC AS, a related party to vice chairman in Protector Forsikring ASA Arve Ree, has today 9 November 2023 acquired 744,408 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 180,00 per share. Following this transaction, AWC AS holds 14,988,856 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
