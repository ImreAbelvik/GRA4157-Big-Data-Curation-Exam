AWC AS, a related party to vice chairman in Protector Forsikring ASA Arve Ree, has today 16 Mach 2023 acquired 144,028 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 137.3844 per share. Following this transaction, AWC AS holds 11,197,476 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
