Awilhelmsen Capital Holdings AS has today, 18 February 2021, purchased 35’051 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at NOK 72.6236 per share. 

Arve Ree, CEO of Awilhemsen Capital Holdings AS, is vice chairman of Protector Forsikring ASA.

After the purchase Awilhelmsen Capital Holdings AS owns 8’370’828 shares or 9.72% of the registered shares in Protector Forsikring ASA.
