Hans Didring, primary insider and Deputy CEO in Protector Forsikring ASA, has today 31 August 2023 sold 15,600 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 172.2762 per share. Following this transaction, Hans Didring and closely related parties holds 278,044 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
