CEO in Protector Forsikring ASA; Sverre Bjerkeli, has today April 
3, 2017, purchased 375,000 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA 
through Hvaler Invest AS at a price of NOK 64.375 per share.
 
"The family Bjerkeli "investment committee" (Mr and Mrs Bjerkeli) 
have unanimously decided to buy for the first time since 2008. No 
shares has been sold the last ten years. The family investment 
committee would also like to inform the market that exactly the 
same number of shares will be sold when the share price reaches NOK 
100. You could call it an "Investment committee compromise" since 
Mr and Mrs Bjerkeli do have a different view on this matter."  says 
Mr. Bjerkeli.
 
After the transaction Sverre Bjerkeli holds 3,186,809 shares in 
Protector Forsikring ASA.
