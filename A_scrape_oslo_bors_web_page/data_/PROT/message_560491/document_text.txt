Steel City AS, close associate to primary insider Kjetil Garstad, board member in Protector Forsikring ASA, has today 28 April 2022 acquired 10 000 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at an average price of NOK 98.2129 per share. Following this transaction, Steel City AS holds 206,706 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
