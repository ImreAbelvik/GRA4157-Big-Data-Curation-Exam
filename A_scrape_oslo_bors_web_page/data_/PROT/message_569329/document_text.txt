AWC AS, related party to primary insider and vice chairman in Protector Forsikring ASA Arve Ree, has today 22 August 2022 acquired 80,000 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 120.5574 per share. Following this transaction, AWC AS holds 8,615,828 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
