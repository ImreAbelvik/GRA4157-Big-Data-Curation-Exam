Ebba Adelsohn Didring, closely related party to primary insider and deputy CEO, Hans Didring, has today 25 October sold 8,000 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 284.3615 per share.

Following this transaction, Ebba Adelsohn Didring holds 20,000 shares and Hans Didring holds 260,166 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
