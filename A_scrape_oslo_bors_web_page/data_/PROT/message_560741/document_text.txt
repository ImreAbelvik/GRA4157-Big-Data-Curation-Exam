AWC AS, close associate to primary insider Arve Ree, vice chairman in Protector Forsikring ASA , has today 29 April 2022 acquired 100,000 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA at a price of NOK 103 per share. Following this transaction, AWC AS holds 8,470,828 shares in Protector Forsikring ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
