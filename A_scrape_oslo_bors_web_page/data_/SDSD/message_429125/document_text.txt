Apollo Asset Ltd, a company controlled by Arne Fredly , 
board member of S.D Standard Drilling Plc, has on 7th of 
June, purchased 100,000 shares at NOK 1.60 per share.

After the transaction, Arne Fredly will hold, through Apollo 
Asset Ltd., 12 333 525 shares in SD Standard Drilling Plc, 
corresponding to 3.45% of the total outstanding share 
capital.
