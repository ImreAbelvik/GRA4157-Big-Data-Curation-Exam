Standard Drilling PLC ("SDSD") for gross proceeds of 
approx. up to NOK 6,175,000 and the today's stock 
exchange notice regarding completion of the Subsequent 
Offering.

related to General Manager Evangelia Panagides, has 
been allocated 344,920 New Shares at the subscription

Following completion of the Subsequent Offer and 
issuance of the New Shares, Yiannis Panagides will 
hold,  558,520 shares in SDSD, corresponding to 0.07%
