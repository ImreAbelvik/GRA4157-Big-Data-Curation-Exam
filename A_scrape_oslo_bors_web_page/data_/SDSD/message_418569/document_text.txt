Reference is made to the stock exchange notice dated 
25 January 2017 regarding a completed private 
placement of up to 220,297,158 new shares (the "New 
Shares") in S.D. Standard Drilling PLC ("SDSD") for 
gross proceeds of approx. up to NOK 198.27 million 
(the "Private Placement").

In the Private Placement, Arne Fredly, Board member, 
has been allocated 2,343,184 New Shares through Apollo 
Asset Ltd. at the subscription price of NOK 0.90 per 
New Share.

Following completion of the Private Placement and 
issuance of the New Shares, Arne Fredly will hold, 
through Apollo Asset Ltd., 35,200,573 shares in SDSD, 
corresponding to 3.44% of the total outstanding share 
capital.
