S.D. Standard ETC PLC (the "Company") hereby announces that Ioannis Panagidis has today, 5 March 2025, sold 186,173 shares in the Company at NOK 1,81 per share.

Ioannis Panagidis is the spouse of Mrs. Evangelia Panagidi, general manager of S.D. Standard ETC PLC, and is considered a closely related party pursuant to applicable regulations.

Following the transaction, Ioannis Panagidis holds zero shares in S.D. Standard ETC PLC.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
