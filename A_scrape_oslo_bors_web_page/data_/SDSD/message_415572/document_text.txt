Reference is made to the stock exchange notice dated 8 
December 2016 regarding a contemplated private 
placement of up to 423,076,924 new shares (the "New 
Shares") in S.D. Standard Drilling PLC ("SDSD") for 
gross proceeds of approx. up to NOK 275 million 
(the "Private Placement") and today's stock exchange 
notice regarding completion of the Private Placement.

In the Private Placement, Martin Nes, Board member, 
has been allocated 1,378,900 New Shares through his 
wholly owned company Hanekamb Invest AS at the 
subscription price of NOK 0.65 per New Share.

Following completion of the Private Placement and 
issuance of the New Shares, Martin Nes will hold, 
through Hanekamb Invest AS, 1 789 662 shares in SDSD, 
corresponding to 0.26 % of the total outstanding share 
capital.
