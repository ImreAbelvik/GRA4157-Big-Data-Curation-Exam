S.D. Standard ETC (the "Company") has been informed that Saga Pure ASA ("Saga Pure") has on 5 March acquired 18,878,157 shares in the Company at an average share price of NOK 1.807, with the highest share price being NOK 1.83. Saga Pure is a close associate to Martin Nes, Chairman of the Company's Board of Directors and to Espen Lundaas, a consultant to the Company. Further information about the transaction pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation article 19 is attached to this notice.

As a result of the above, the price per share under the mandatory offer obligation of Saga Pure, as announced yesterday, will be increased to NOK 1.83.

Following the acquisition, Saga Pure and Ferncliff Listed DAI hold a total of 189,796,266 shares in SDSD, where Saga Pure holds 33,878,157 shares and Ferncliff Listed DAI holds 155,918,109 shares, corresponding to a consolidated ownership of approximately 36.2% of the total outstanding shares in SDSD.
