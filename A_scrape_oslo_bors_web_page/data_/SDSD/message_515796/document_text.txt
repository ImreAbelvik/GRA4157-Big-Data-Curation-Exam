Reference is made to the announcement issued earlier today, 19 October 2020, regarding a contemplated sale by Saga Tankers ASA ("Saga Tankers") of shares in S.D. Standard Drilling Plc (the "Company").

Saga, who is represented on the Board of Directors of S.D Standard Drilling Plc (“Company”), has on 19 October 2020 sold all of its holding in the Company, corresponding to 105,846,245 shares and representing 18.4% of the Company, in a secondary placing directed towards the 30 largest shareholders of Saga Tankers. The shares were sold at a price per share of NOK 0.65,-.

After the transaction Saga Tankers ASA will have no holding of shares in the Company.
