Martin Nes, Board member, has today sold 500 000 
shares through his wholly owned company Hanekamb 
Invest AS at the price of NOK 0.90 per share to EL 
Investment AS, a wholly owned company by Espen 
Lundaas, acting CFO.

Following the transaction Martin Nes will hold, 
through Hanekamb Invest AS, 2,292,062 shares in SDSD, 
corresponding to 0.33% of the total outstanding share 
capital.

Following the transaction Espen Lundaas will hold, 
through EL Investment AS, 2,339,662 shares in SDSD, 
corresponding to 0.34% of the total outstanding share 
capital.
