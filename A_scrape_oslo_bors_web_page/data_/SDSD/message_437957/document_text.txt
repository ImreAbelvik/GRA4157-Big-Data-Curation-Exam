Limassol, 6 November, 2017

Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement earlier today, whereby S.D. Standard Drilling PLC ("SDSD" or the "Company") announced the results of Tranche 2 of the equity offering (the "Equity Offering"). 

Saga Tankers ASA (incl. Strata Marine & Offshore AS), represented on the Board of Directors by Martin Nes, has been allocated a total of 1,427,069 new shares at the subscription price of NOK 1.31 per share in Tranche 2 of the Equity Offering. Saga Tankers ASA (incl. Strata Marine & Offshore AS) currently owns 101,558,234 shares in the Company (including shares to be issued in Tranche 1 of the Equity Offering) and will, following delivery of the new shares to be issued in Tranche 2 of the Equity Offering, own a total of 102,985,303 shares, which will represent approximately 20.22% of the outstanding shares after completion of the full Equity Offering.  

Apollo Asset Management Limited (controlled by Board member Arne Fredly), has been allocated a total of 348,553 new shares at the subscription price of NOK 1.31 per share in Tranche 2 of the Equity Offering. Apollo Asset Management Limited currently owns 24,804,989 shares in the Company (including shares to be issued in Tranche 1 of the Equity Offering) and will, following delivery of the new shares to be issued in Tranche 2 of the Equity Offering, own a total of 25,153,542 shares, which will represent approximately 4.94% of the outstanding shares after completion of the full Equity Offering.
