Reference is made to the stock exchange notice dated 
20 May 2015 and the offer document dated 17 June 2015 
prepared in connection with the mandatory offer made 
by Saga Tankers ASA ("Saga") to acquire all issued 
and outstanding shares in S.D. Standard Drilling Plc 
("SDSD") that are not already owned by Saga or 
persons acting in concert with Saga, at an offer 
price of NOK 1.35 per SDSD share (the "Mandatory 
Offer").

On 9 July 2015, Saga has received acceptances under 
the Mandatory Offer representing in aggregate 10,000 
shares in SDSD. Consequently, Saga and persons acting 
in concert with Saga owns 157,013,630 SDSD shares and 
has a right and an obligation to acquire a total of 
21,975,516 SDSD shares upon consummation of the 
Mandatory Offer. Based on this, Saga and persons 
acting in concert with Saga will own 178,989,146 SDSD 
shares following completion of the Mandatory Offer, 
corresponding to 68.32% of the shares in SDSD.

Offer Period: From and including 17 June 2015 to and 
including 15 July 2015 at 17.30 CET.

Saga is considered a primary insider of SDSD due to 
Saga being represented on SDSD's Board of Directors, 
cf. the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 4-2 
(1).
