Apollo Asset Ltd, a company controlled by Arne Fredly , 
board member of S.D Standard Drilling Plc, has on 31 May 
purchased 500 000 shares at NOK 1.60 per share.

After the transaction, Arne Fredly will hold, through Apollo 
Asset Ltd., 12 233 525 shares in SD Standard Drilling Plc, 
corresponding to 3.4% of the total outstanding share capital.
