


Anders Olstad, CFO of Entra ASA, has on 30 October 2015 through his 100 % owned
company Hasselodden AS bought 15,000 shares in Entra ASA at a price of NOK 73.8
per share. Following this transaction Anders Olstad owns and controls 15,000
shares in Entra ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
