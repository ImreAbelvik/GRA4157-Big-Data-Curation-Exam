



Reference is made to the stock exchange notice on 28th of May 2015 regarding
Entra's share saving scheme for employees.

On behalf of Entra ASA, Swedbank has on 4 of June 2015 purchased 19,100 treasury
shares for use in the group's share saving scheme. The treasury shares have been
acquired at a price of NOK 72.43 per share.

Before distribution to the employees, Entra has purchased 70,200 treasury shares
in connection with the share saving scheme which equals Entra's total holding of
treasury shares. The total number of shares allocated to the employees in the
program have been adjusted to 81,446 shares.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
