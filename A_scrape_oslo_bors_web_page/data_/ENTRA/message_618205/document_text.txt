8.5.2024 14:25:45 CEST | Entra ASA | Mandatory notification of trade primary

Ottar Ertzeid, Chairman of the Board has today acquired 12,500 shares in Entra
ASA for an average price of NOK 106.74 per share. Following this transaction Mr
Ertzeid holds 20,000 shares in the company.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to article
19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act.
