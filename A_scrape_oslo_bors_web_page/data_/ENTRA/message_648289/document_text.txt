3.6.2025 18:56:08 CEST | Entra ASA | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

All employees in Entra have been offered the opportunity to purchase shares for
up to NOK 150,000 at a 25 % discount, in accordance with the authorisation
granted at the annual general meeting on 29 April 2025.

The offer price was NOK 86.00 per share, calculated as the volume-weighted
average share price during the period from 30 March to 28 April 2025, minus a 25
% discount.

Notification of allocation of shares to PDMRs under the share saving plan for
employees is attached hereto.

DISCLOSURE REGULATION

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
