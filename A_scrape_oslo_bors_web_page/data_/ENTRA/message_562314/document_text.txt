13.5.2022 16:00:01 CEST | Entra ASA | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

All employees in Entra have received an offer to purchase shares for up to NOK
150,000 at a 25 % discount in accordance with the authorization given on the
annual general meeting on 24 April 2022. The offer price was NOK133.1 per share
and was calculated as the dividend adjusted volume weighted average share price
in the period from 22 March to 21 April 2022 minus a 25 % discount. 

 The following PDMR's have ordered and been allocated shares under Entra's share
saving plan for employees in Entra.
