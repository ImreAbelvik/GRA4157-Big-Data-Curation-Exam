12.5.2021 16:39:20 CEST | Entra ASA | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

All employees in Entra have received an offer to purchase shares for up to NOK
150,000 at a 20 % discount in accordance with the authorization given on the
annual general meeting on 23 April 2021. The offer price was NOK147.9 per share
and was calculated as the dividend adjusted volume weighted average share price
in the period from 24 March to 23 April 2021 minus a 20 % discount.
