


Sonja Horn, EVP Oslo in Entra ASA, has on 15 June 2016 bought 2,000 shares in

following the allocation in the share savings program for employees Sonja Horn
owns 12,099 shares in Entra ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
