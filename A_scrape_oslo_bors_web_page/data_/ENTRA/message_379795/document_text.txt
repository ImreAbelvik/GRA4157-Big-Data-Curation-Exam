



Reference is made to the stock exchange notice on 28th of May 2015 regarding
Entra's share saving scheme for employees.

On behalf of Entra ASA, Swedbank has on 5 of June 2015 purchased 11,646 treasury
shares for use in the group's share saving scheme. The treasury shares have been
acquired at a price of NOK 73.01 per share.

Entra now holds 81,446 treasury shares which equals the total number of shares
allocated in the share saving scheme for employees. The share buy back program
in connection with the share saving scheme is then completed.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to sectionn
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
