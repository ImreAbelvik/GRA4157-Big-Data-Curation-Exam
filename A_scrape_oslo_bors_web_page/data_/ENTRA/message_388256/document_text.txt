


Arve Regland, CEO of Entra ASA, has on 30 October 2015 through his 100 % owned
company K9 Invest AS bought 5,000 shares in Entra ASA at a price of NOK 73.8 per
share. Following this transaction Arve Regland owns and controls 21,066 shares
in Entra ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
