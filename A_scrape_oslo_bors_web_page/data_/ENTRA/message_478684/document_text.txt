Anders Olstad, CFO of Entra ASA, has on 5 June 2019 bought 5,292 shares in Entra
ASA at a price of NOK 123.5 per share. Following this transaction Anders Olstad
owns and controls 49,559 shares in Entra ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
