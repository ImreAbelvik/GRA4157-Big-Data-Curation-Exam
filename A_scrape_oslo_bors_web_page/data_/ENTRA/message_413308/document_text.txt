


Anders Olstad, CFO of Entra ASA, has on 10 November 2016 through his 100 % owned
company Hasselodden AS bought 2,000 shares in Entra ASA at a price of NOK 85,25
per share. Following this transaction Anders Olstad owns and controls 31,536
shares in Entra ASA.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
