



Reference is made to the stock exchange notices on 28(th) May 2015 and 15(th)
June 2015 regarding Entra's share saving scheme for employees.

Entra ASA has today bought an additional 1,683 shares in connection with the
share savings scheme for employees at a price of NOK 74.5 per share. The shares
will be transferred to participants in the share savings scheme for employees at
a price of NOK 59.4 per share. After the transfer Entra ASA owns zero treasury
shares. The share buy back program in connection with the share saving scheme is
now completed.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
