21.11.2024 11:05:47 CET | Entra ASA | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

Ottar Ertzeid, Chairman of the Board, has today acquired 5,000 shares in Entra
ASA at an average price of NOK 110.40 per share. Following this transaction, Mr.
Ertzeid now holds 25,000 shares in the company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article
19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act.
