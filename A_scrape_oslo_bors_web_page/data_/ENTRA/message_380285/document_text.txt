



Reference is made to the stock exchange notice on 5th of June 2015 regarding
Entra's share saving scheme for employees and on 2nd of June 2015 regarding
allocation of shares to primary insider employees.

Entra ASA has on the 12th of June 2015 sold 81,446 treasury shares to
participants in the Employee Share Saving Scheme at a price of NOK 59.4 per
share.

Entra ASA owns zero treasury shares after the transfer.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
