Klevenstern AS, an investment company controlled by Kristian 
Adolfsen, has today sold 55,017 preference shares in Pioneer 
Property Group ASA in the market at a share price of NOK 
100.75 per share. As a result, Klevenstern AS will decrease 
its holding of preference shares from 160,832 to 105,815. 

Simultaneously, Mecca Invest AS, an investment company 
controlled by Roger Adolfsen, has today sold 55,017 
preference shares in Pioneer Property Group ASA in the 
market at a share price of NOK 100.75 per share. As a 
result, Mecca Invest AS will decrease its holding of 
preference shares from 170,832 to 115,815. 

Simultaneously, HI Capital Invest AS, a company controlled 
by Kristian and Roger Adolfsen, has today sold 101.466 
preference shares in Pioneer Property Group ASA at an 
average price of NOK 100.75. After the sale, HI Capital AS 
owns no preference shares in Pioneer Property Group ASA.
Simultaneously, Hospitality Invest AS, a company controlled 
by Roger Adolfsen, has today sold 10.000 preference shares 
in Pioneer Property Group ASA at an average price of NOK 
100.75. After the sale, Hospitality Invest AS owns no 
preference shares in Pioneer Property Group ASA.

Roger Adolfsen, who is a major shareholder in Hospitality 
Invest AS, holds the position as Chairman of the Board of 
Pioneer Property Group ASA and is defined as primary insider 
subject to disclosure duties under the Norwegian Securities 
Trading Act Section 4-2.
