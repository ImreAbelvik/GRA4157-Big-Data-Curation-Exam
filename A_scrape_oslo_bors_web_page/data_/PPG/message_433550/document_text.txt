Mecca Invest AS, an investment company controlled by primary insider 
Roger Adolfsen, has today agreed to sell 50,000 preference shares in 
Pioneer Property Group ASA to Eidissen Consult AS at a share price of 
NOK 100.75 per share. As a result, Mecca Invest AS will decrease its 
holding of preference shares from 240,832 to 190,832. Mecca Invest AS 
also owns 1,417,852 ordinary shares. After the transaction, Mecca 
Invest AS will reduce its ownership from 10.2% to 9.9% of the total 
shares in PPG (ordinary and preference shares combined), and hold 2.9% 
of the preference shares, and 13.7% of the total voting capital 
(accounting for the 1/10th voting right of the preference shares).
