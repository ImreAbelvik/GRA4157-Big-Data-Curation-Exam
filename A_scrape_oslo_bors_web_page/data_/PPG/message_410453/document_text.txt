Date: 30 September 2016

Hospitality Invest AS has on 30 September 2016 
purchased 110,000 preference shares in Pioneer 
Property Group ASA (PPG PREF) at a price of NOK 97.00 
per share. The purchase was completed in a private 
transaction with the seller being Norlandia Care Group 
AS, a company controlled by Hospitality Invest AS. 

Roger Adolfsen is a major shareholder in Hospitality 
Invest AS, holds the position as Chairman of the Board 
of Pioneer Property Group ASA and is defined as 
primary insider subject to disclosure duties under the 
Norwegian Securities Trading Act Section 4-2.

Following the transaction, Hospitality Invest AS will 
own 110.000 preference shares (1.69%), whereas 
Norlandia Care Group AS will own 499,369 preference 
shares (7.68%).

Hospitality Invest AS' consolidated ownership in 
Pioneer Property Group ASA remains unchanged.
