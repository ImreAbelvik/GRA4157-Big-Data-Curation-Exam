Norlandia Care Group AS, a company controlled by Kristian 
and Roger Adolfsen, has today sold 4.849 preference shares 
in Pioneer Property Group ASA at an average price of NOK 
101.00. As a result, Norlandia Care Group AS will decrease 
its holding of preference shares from 99,369 to 94,520. 
Roger Adolfsen holds the position as Chairman of the Board 
of Pioneer Property Group ASA and is defined as primary 
insider subject to disclosure duties under the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act Section 4-2.
