Pilgrim Global ICAV acquired 69,286 shares in MPC Container Ships ASA on 30th of December 2020 in a transaction on the Oslo Stock Exchange at a price of NOK 6.0248 per share. 
Following this trade, Pilgrim Global ICAV holds 24,752,667 shares (6.28%) in MPC Container Ships ASA. 
Pilgrim is represented at the board of directors of MPC Container Ships ASA by Darren Maupin.
