Pilgrim Global ICAV acquired 83,582 shares in MPC Container Ships ASA on 28th of December 2020 in a transaction on the Oslo Stock Exchange at a price of NOK 6.0936 per share. 
Following this trade, Pilgrim Global ICAV holds 23,989,001 shares in MPC Container Ships ASA. 
Pilgrim is represented at the board of directors of MPC Container Ships ASA by Darren Maupin.
