Pilgrim Global ICAV acquired 694,380 shares in MPC Container Ships ASA on 29th of December 2020 in a transaction on the Oslo Stock Exchange at a price of NOK 6.0133 per share. 
Following this trade, Pilgrim Global ICAV holds 24,683,381.00 shares (6.26%) in MPC Container Ships ASA. 
Pilgrim is represented at the board of directors of MPC Container Ships ASA by Darren Maupin.
