Pilgrim Global ICAV acquired 3,919,627 shares in MPC Container Ships ASA (ISIN INO0010791353) today, the 31st of August 2020 in a transaction on the Oslo Stock Exchange at a price of NOK 2.1961 per share. 
Following this trade, Pilgrim Global ICAV holds 23,825,046 shares or 6.04% shareholding in MPC Container Ships ASA. Pilgrim is represented at the board of directors of MPC Container Ships ASA by Darren Maupin.
