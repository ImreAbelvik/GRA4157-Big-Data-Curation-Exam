Pilgrim Global ICAV acquired 80,373 shares in MPC Container Ships ASA (ISIN INO0010791353) today, the 1st Sept 2020 in a transaction on the Oslo Stock Exchange at a price of NOK 2.20 per share. 
Following this trade, Pilgrim Global ICAV holds 23,905,419 shares or 6.06% shareholding in MPC Container Ships ASA. 
Pilgrim is represented at the board of directors of MPC Container Ships ASA by Darren Maupin.
