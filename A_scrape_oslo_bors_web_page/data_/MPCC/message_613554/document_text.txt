MPC Container Ships ASA has been notified of the following transactions by
primary insiders and Member of the Board of Directors, whereby MPC CSI GmbH
consolidates and increases its shareholding in MPC Container Ships:

Dr. Axel Schroeder, primary insider and Member of the Board of Directors, has
sold 2,299,706 shares at a price of NOK 13.64 per share.

Elbrock GmbH, a company closely associated with Dr. Axel Schroeder sold 171,404
shares at a price of NOK 13.64 per share.

Ulf Holländer, primary insider and Chairman of the Board of Directors, has sold
165,637 shares at a price of NOK 13.64 per share.

MPC CSI GmbH, associated party and closely associated persons of, among others,
members of the Board of Directors of MPCC, has bought 3,692,181 shares in MPC
Container Ships ASA at a price of NOK 13.64 per share. Following the
transaction, MPC CSI GmbH will hold a total of 73,994,977 shares representing
16.68% of the share capital and voting rights in MPCC.

Please see enclosed primary insider notification forms pursuant to the
requirements of the Market Abuse Regulation.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to MAR
Article 19 and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
