James Berger, Chief Commercial Officer in Hofseth BioCare ASA, has today
01.10.2021 purchased 30,000 shares through his wholly-owned company Nevera AG,
at an average price of NOK 6,65 per share.

Following this transaction, James Berger owns 3,297,955 shares corresponding to
0.92 % of the outstanding shares in Hofseth BioCare ASA and share options for a
further 5,351,280 shares according to the stock exchange notice of 18.10.2019.
