


Torsen Holding AS, a wholly owned company by CEO Tor Erik S. Andersen in Hofseth
BioCare ASA, has today 29/12/2016 purchased 100,000 shares at an average price
of 1.255 per share in Hofseth BioCare ASA. After this, Torsen Holding holds
1,064,285 shares in direct ownership and Tor Erik S. Andersen has additionally
942,857 shares privately in Hofseth BioCare ASA, a total of 2,007,142 shares
corresponding to 0.86% of the shares in Hofseth BioCare ASA.



This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
