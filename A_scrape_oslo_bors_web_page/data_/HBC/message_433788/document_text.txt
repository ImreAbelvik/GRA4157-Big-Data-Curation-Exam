


Karl Inge Slotsvik, Chief Operating Officer in Hofseth BioCare ASA, has today
30.08.2017 purchased 15,000 shares at an average price of 1.46 per share in
Hofseth BioCare ASA. After this, Mr. Slotsvik holds 15,000 shares in Hofseth
BioCare ASA, corresponding to 0.006% of the total number of outstanding shares
in Hofseth BioCare ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
