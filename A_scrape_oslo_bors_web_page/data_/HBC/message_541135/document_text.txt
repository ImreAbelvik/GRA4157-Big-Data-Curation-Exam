Roger Hofseth has today, 30 August 2021, bought 50,000 shares at an average
price of NOK 7.2867 per share.

Roger Hofseth is CEO and primary insider of Hofseth BioCare ASA. After the
transaction, Mr. Hofseth owns 50,000 shares privately and 51,500,000 shares
through his a wholly owned company RH Industri AS, totalling 51,550,000 shares
and corresponding to 14.41 % of the outstanding shares in Hofseth BioCare ASA.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to sections
4-2 and 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
