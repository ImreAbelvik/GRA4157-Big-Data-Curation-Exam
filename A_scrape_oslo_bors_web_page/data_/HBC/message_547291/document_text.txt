Roger Hofseth has today, 18 November 2021, bought 55,000 shares at an average
price of approx. NOK 7.25 per share.

Roger Hofseth is CEO and primary insider of Hofseth BioCare ASA. After the
transaction, Mr. Hofseth owns 105,000 shares privately and 51,500,000 shares
through his a wholly owned company RH Industri AS, totalling 51,605,000 shares
and corresponding to 14.42 % of the outstanding shares in Hofseth BioCare ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
