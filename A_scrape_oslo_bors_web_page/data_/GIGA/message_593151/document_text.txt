Kjell Lorentsen is board member in Gigante Salmon AS. Kapnord AS, a company in control of Kjell Lorentsen through Gigante Havbruk AS, has acquired 17 272 728 shares in Gigante Salmon AS at an average price of NOK 11.5 per share. Following the transaction, Kapnord AS holds 17 272 728 shares in Gigante Salmon AS, corresponding to an ownership of 16.36%.

Gigante Havbruk AS, a company in control of Kjell Lorentsen, board member in Gigante SalmonAS, has sold 10 000 000 shares in Gigante Salmon AS at an average price of NOK 11.5 per share. Following the transaction, Gigante havbruk AS holds 54 238 000 shares in Gigante Salmon AS, corresponding to an ownership of 51.38 %.

About Gigante Salmon:
Gigante Salmon is a land-based salmon farming company with its first production facility under construction on Lille Indre Rosøya in Rødøy, Norway. The company’s aquaculture concept is based on a flow-through system, combining the benefits of conventional and land-based aquaculture while simultaneously eliminating issues associated with conventional, sea based, farming, i.e., sea lice and escapes as well as reduced mortality, emissions, feed waste and impact on nearby surroundings.

For further information about the company please visit www.gigantesalmon.no.
