primary insiders have, on 7 December 2022, bought the following number of shares
at a price of NOK 101.7508 per share in Multiconsult ASA (Company):

following which she holds 44 392 shares in the Company

  · Leif Olav Bogen, EVP Region Norway, purchased 1 095 shares,
following which he holds 12 623 shares in the Company

  · Kristin Olsson Augestad, EVP Region Oslo, purchased 1 095 shares,
following which she holds 14 888 shares in the Company

  · Geir Juterud, EVP Project Director, purchased 1 095 shares,
following which he holds 11 866 shares in the Company

  · Torben Halland Wedervang, employee representative on the board, purchased 1
095 shares,
following which he holds 5 398 shares in the Company

  · Karine Gjersø, employee representative on the board, purchased 1 000 shares,
following which she holds 2 948 shares in the Company

  · Arve Lønes, deputy employee representative on the board, purchased 1 095
shares,
following which he holds 6 184 shares in the Company

share traded on Oslo Børs in the period between 2 - 8 November 2022.
