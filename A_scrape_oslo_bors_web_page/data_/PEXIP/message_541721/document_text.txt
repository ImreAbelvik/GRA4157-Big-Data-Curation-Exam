Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been
notified that these transactions have been completed today, September 6, 2021 by
primary insiders in Pexip:

Øystein Hem (CFO and Interim CEO) has today September 6, 2021 bought 1,400
shares in Pexip for an average price of NOK 73.12 per share. After the
transaction, Mr. Hem will hold 92,468 shares and rights to 127,500 shares.

Tom-Erik Lia, Chief Commercial Officer and Co-Founder has today, September 6,
2021, through Lia Investments Ltd, controlled by Lia, bought 7,000 shares at NOK
73.83 per share. After the transaction, Mr. Lia and Lia Investments Ltd will
hold 1,411,252 shares and rights to 60,000 shares.

Giles Russell Chamberlin, Chief Technology Officer and Co-Founder has today
September 6, 2021, bought 1,400 shares at NOK 72.95 per share. After the
transaction, Mr. Chamberlin will hold 1,516,101 shares and rights to 60,000
shares.

Nicolas Cormier, Chief Operating Officer has today September 6, 2021, bought
1,400 shares at NOK 72.89 per share. After the transaction, Mr. Cormier will
hold 230,573 shares and rights to 60,000 shares.

The information included in this announcement is such that the Company is
required to disclose in accordance with the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and
is publicly disclosed in accordance with MAR and section 5-12 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act.

The announcement is made by the contact person.

About Pexip | pexip.com

Pexip simplifies complex videoconferencing to empower organizations with face-to
-face collaboration, no matter the location or technology. Our scalable platform
enables high-quality video meetings, with enterprise-grade security, and is
easily adapted to fit customers' IT requirements and existing infrastructure.
This makes Pexip the leading provider for  large enterprises and public sector
organizations. The solution is sold through 300 channel partners in 75 countries
and used in more than 190 countries. Pexip was listed on the Oslo Børs in May
2020.
