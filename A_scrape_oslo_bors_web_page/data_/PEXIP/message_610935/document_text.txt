Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been notified that the following Primary Insiders has purchased shares in the Company today, 15 February 2024.

Kjell Skappel, Chair of the Board and primary insider in Pexip has purchased 40,937 shares in the Company at an average price of NOK 24.40 per share through Stavanger Venture ASAS, which is controlled by Kjell Skappel. After the transaction Stavanger Venture holds 2,102,000 shares in Pexip.

In addition, TD Veen AS, where Kjell Skappel is CEO, holds 6,146,946 shares in Pexip and Veen Eiendom AS, where Kjell Skappel is CEO, holds 2,133,496 shares in Pexip. After today’s transactions, the companies controlled by Kjell Skappel own 10,382,442 shares in Pexip, corresponding to an ownership of 9.94%.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

This stock exchange announcement was published by Øystein Dahl Hem,CFO, Pexip Holding ASA, on 15 February 2024.
