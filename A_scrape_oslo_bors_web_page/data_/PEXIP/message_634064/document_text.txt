Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been notified that the following Primary Insiders has purchased shares in the Company today, 06 December 2024.

Patricia Auseth, Chief Marketing Officer and primary insider in Pexip has purchased 2,400 shares in Pexip for an average price of NOK 45.95 per share. After the transaction, she will hold 32,134 shares and rights to 255,000 shares.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act. This stock exchange announcement was published by Julie Tangen, Investor Relations Manager, Pexip Holding ASA, on 06 December 2024.
