Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been notified that Øystein Dahl Hem, CFO and primary insider in Pexip today, 14 August 2025, has purchased 2,000 shares in Pexip for an average price of NOK 62.00 per share. 

After the transaction, Hem will hold 238,249 shares and rights to 320,000 shares.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

This stock exchange announcement was published by Julie Tangen, Investor Relations Manager, Pexip Holding ASA, on 14 August 2025.
