Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been notified that the following Primary Insiders has purchased shares in the Company today, 6 May 2024.

Trond K. Johannessen, Chief Executive Officer and primary insider in Pexip has purchased 15,000 shares in Pexip for an average price of NOK 28.35 per share. After the transaction, he will hold 140,000 shares and rights to 670,000 shares.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

This stock exchange announcement was published by Øystein Dahl Hem,CFO, Pexip Holding ASA, on 6 May 2024.
