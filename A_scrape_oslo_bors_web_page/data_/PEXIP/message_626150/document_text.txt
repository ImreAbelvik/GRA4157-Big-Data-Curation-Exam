Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been notified that Patricia Auseth, CMO and primary insider in Pexip, 22 August 2024, has purchased 2,930 shares in Pexip for an average price of NOK 35.8 per share. 

After the transaction, Auseth will hold 29,734 shares and rights to 255,000 shares.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

This stock exchange announcement was published by Øystein Hem, CFO Pexip Holding ASA, on 25 August 2024.
