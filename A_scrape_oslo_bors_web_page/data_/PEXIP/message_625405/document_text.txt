Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been notified that Geir Langfeldt Olsen, board member and primary insider in Pexip, has transferred shares in the Company between two wholly-owned companies on the 15th of August 2024. 

After the transaction, Geir Langfeldt Olsen will continue to indirectly hold 1,178,312 shares in the Company. 

Please see attached notification of trades for further details.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to article 19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
