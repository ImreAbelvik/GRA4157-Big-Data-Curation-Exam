Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been
notified that board member Kjell Skappel today, August 24, 2021, has purchased
22,000 shares in the company at an average price of NOK 68.18 through Stavanger
Venture AS. Stavanger Venture is wholly owned company by Radka Invest AS. Radka
Invest AS is wholly owned by Kjell Skappel. After this transaction, Stavanger
Venture holds now 1,822,018 Pexip Shares. In addition, Radka Invest holds 22,500
Pexip shares. In addition, Kjell Skappel is CEO in TD Veen that holds 4,606,764
shares in Pexip.

The information included in this announcement is such that the Company is
required to disclose in accordance with the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and
is publicly disclosed in accordance with MAR and section 5-12 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act. The announcement is made by the contact person.
