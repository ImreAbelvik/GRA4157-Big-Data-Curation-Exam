Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been notified that the following Primary Insiders has purchased shares in the Company today, 26 August 2025.

Helge Hoff Hansen, Chief Operating Officer and primary insider in Pexip has purchased 1,600 shares in Pexip for an average price of NOK 62.90 per share. After the transaction, he will hold 14,318 shares and rights to 180,000 shares.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act. This stock exchange announcement was published by Julie Hansson Tangen, Investor Relations Manager, Pexip Holding ASA, on 26 August 2025.
