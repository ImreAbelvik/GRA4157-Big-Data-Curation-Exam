Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been
notified that participants in the Company's option program have on November 22,
2021, exercised 231,412 options with an average strike price of NOK 24.68 per
share. Following the exercise, 231,412 shares will be transferred from the
Company's holding of own shares. After the transaction, Pexip Holding ASA holds
900,205 own shares.

Options exercised by primary insiders:

Øystein Hem (Interim CEO and CFO) has exercised 7,500 options in the Company,
corresponding to 7,500 shares at the strike price of NOK 25.00. Prior to the
exercise he held 92,468 shares and rights to 127,500 shares. After the exercise,
Mr. Hem will hold 99,968 shares and rights to 120,000 shares.

Ingrid Woodhouse (Chief People Officer) has exercised 7,500 options in the
Company, corresponding to 7,500 shares at the strike price of NOK 25.00. Prior
to the exercise she held 17,430 shares and rights to 35,625 shares. After the
exercise, Mrs. Woodhouse will hold 24,930 shares and rights to 28,125 shares.

The information included in this announcement is such that the Company is
required to disclose in accordance with the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and
is publicly disclosed in accordance with MAR article 17 and section 5-12 of the
Norwegian Securities Trading Act. The announcement is made by the contact
person.

Pexip simplifies complex videoconferencing to empower organizations with face-to
-face collaboration, no matter the location or technology. Our scalable platform
enables high-quality video meetings, with enterprise-grade security, and is
easily adapted to fit customers' IT requirements and existing infrastructure.

organizations. The solution is sold through 300 channel partners in 75 countries
and used in more than 190 countries. Pexip was listed on the Oslo Børs in May
