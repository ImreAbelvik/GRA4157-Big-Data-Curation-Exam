Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been
notified that the following primary insiders in Pexip have purchased shares
today, 2 November 2023:

Trond K. Johannessen, CEO and primary insider in Pexip has purchased 15,000
shares in Pexip for an average price of NOK 20.05 per share. After the
transaction, Johannessen will hold 115,000 shares and rights to 670,000 shares.

Åsmund Olav Fodstad, Chief Revenue Officer and primary insider in Pexip, has
purchased 10,000 shares in Pexip for an average price of NOK 19.54 per share
through Metrix Invest AS. After the transaction, Mr. Fodstad and Metrix Invest
AS, controlled by Mr. Fodstad, will hold 797,275 shares and rights to 320,000
shares.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU
Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.

This stock exchange announcement was published by Øystein Hem, CFO, Pexip
Holding ASA, on 2 November 2023.
