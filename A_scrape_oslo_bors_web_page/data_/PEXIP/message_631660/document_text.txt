Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been notified that Åsmund Fodstad, Chief Revenue Officer and primary insider in Pexip, 7 November 2024, has sold 250,000 shares in Pexip for an average price of NOK 40.67 per share through Metrix Invest, which is 100% controlled by Mr Fodstad. 

After the transaction, Fodstad will hold 557,275 shares through Metrix Invest and personally as well as rights to 320,000 shares.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

This stock exchange announcement was published by Øystein Hem, CFO Pexip Holding ASA, on 7 November 2024.
