Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been
notified that Trond K. Johannessen, CEO and primary insider in Pexip, has on
November 15, 2022, bought 19,000 shares in Pexip for an average price of NOK
11.00 per share. After the transaction, Johannessen will hold 75,000 shares and
rights to 270,000 shares.
The information included in this announcement is such that the Company is
required to disclose in accordance with the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and
is publicly disclosed in accordance with MAR and section 5-12 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act. The announcement is made by the contact person.

About Pexip | pexip.com

Pexip is a global technology company, solving customer communication scenarios
with a secure and flexible communications platform helping users standardize
their experience with seamless interoperability to any other meetings
technology. Pexip helps embedding video into critical business functions
connecting people, technology and devices to support workflows across the
enterprise, government and public sector. With a global network of 300 partners
in 75 countries, Pexip has a customer base of over 4,400 companies in 190
countries. Pexip was listed on the Oslo Børs in May 2020.
