Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been notified that Øystein Dahl Hem, CFO and primary insider in Pexip today, 21 August 2024, has purchased 6,000 shares in Pexip for an average price of NOK 34.91 per share. 

After the transaction, Hem will hold 161,468 shares and rights to 412,500 shares.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

This stock exchange announcement was published by Øystein Hem, CFO Pexip Holding ASA, on 21 August 2024.
