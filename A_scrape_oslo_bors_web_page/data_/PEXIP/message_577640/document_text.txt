On 8 December 2022, Pexip Holding ASA (Pexip) has been notified that the two companies Stavanger Venture AS, a major shareholder in Pexip, and Radka Invest AS have merged, thereby affecting its shareholding in Pexip. 

Radka Invest AS, where Pexip board member Kjell Skappel holds 100% of the shares and which has previously held 100% of the shares in Stavanger Venture AS, will after the merge be a part of Stavanger Venture. 

Following the merge, Stavanger Venture will increase its holdings in Pexip by 22,500 shares to 2,061,063 shares. This corresponds to 1.97% of shares in Pexip. The total number of shares in Pexip held between the two companies remains unchanged.
 
In addition, T.D. Veen AS, where Kjell Skappel is the CEO, holds 6,146,946 shares in Pexip and Veen Eiendom AS, where Kjell Skappel is CEO, holds 2,133,496 shares in Pexip.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Sections 4-3 and 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
