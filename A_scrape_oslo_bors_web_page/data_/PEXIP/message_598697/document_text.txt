Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been
notified that Ingrid Woodhouse, Chief People Officer and primary insider in
Pexip, today, 4 September 2023, has purchased 6,200 shares in Pexip for an
average price of NOK 19.54 per share.

After the transaction, Woodhouse will hold 45,280 shares and rights to 268,125
shares.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market
Abuse Regulation (MAR) and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

This stock exchange announcement was published by Christine Arnesen Karsrud,
Investor Relations, Pexip Holding ASA, on 4 September 2023.
