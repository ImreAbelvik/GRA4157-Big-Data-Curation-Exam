Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been notified that the following Primary Insiders has sold shares in the Company yesterday, 04 March 2025.

Helge Hoff Hansen, Chief Operating Officer and primary insider in Pexip has sold 12,786 shares in Pexip for an average price of NOK 42.90 per share. After the transaction, he will hold 52,718 shares and rights to 180,000 shares.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act. This stock exchange announcement was published by Øysteion Hem, CFO, Pexip Holding ASA, on 5 March 2025.
