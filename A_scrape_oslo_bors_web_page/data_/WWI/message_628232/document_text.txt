Wilh. Wilhelmsen Holding ASA completed yesterday, 19 September 2024, a share program for employees. A close associate of primary insider Geir Flæsen, EVP New Energy, participated in the program, acquiring 29 WWI class a-shares. Following completion of the transactions, Geir Flæsen and close associates will own 58 WWI class a-shares.
Please refer to the attached notification of transactions for further details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
