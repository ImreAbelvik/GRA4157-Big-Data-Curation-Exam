The foundation for shares and cabins in the WW-group has offered employees in Wilh. Wilhelmsen Holding ASA, including employees in Norwegian subsidiaries owned with more than 90 per cent, the possibility to buy shares in Wilh. Wilhelmsen Holding ASA (ser. A/WWI) at a 20 per cent discount. Shares may be subscribed for up to NOK 10 000 or NOK 25 000.

The deadline for subscribing for shares was today, 10 September 2020 at 16:30. The share purchase price under the program has been set at NOK 116.00 per share, equal to today closing price. This means that each employee taking part in the offer will receive 86 or 215 shares, depending on amount subscribed for.
