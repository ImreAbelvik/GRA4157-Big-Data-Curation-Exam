Following demerger of a family company, primary insider and board member Cathrine Løvenskiold Wilhelmsen will through her fully owned company Hetija Invest AS receive 730 A-shares in Wilh. Wilhelmsen Holding ASA. The demerger was formalized on 12 June, but transfer of shares will not be carried through before late August/early September.

Upon completion of the transaction, Cathrine Løvenskiold Wilhelmsen will own 730 A-shares and nil B-shares in Wilh. Wilhelmsen Holding ASA.
