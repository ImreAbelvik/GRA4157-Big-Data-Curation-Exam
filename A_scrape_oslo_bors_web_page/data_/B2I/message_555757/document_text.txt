Erik J. Johnsen, Chief Executive Officer of B2Holding ASA, has on 7 March 2022, acquired 40,000 shares in B2Holding ASA at an average price of NOK 8.62 per share.

Following this transaction, Erik J. Johnsen and related parties (Erling Johnsen AS) hold 2,240,000 shares in the Company.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
