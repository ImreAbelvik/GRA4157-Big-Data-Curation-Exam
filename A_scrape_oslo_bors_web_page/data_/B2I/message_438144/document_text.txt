Primary insider Henrik Wennerholm, Regional Director Scandinavia of B2Holding ASA (the "Company", ticker: B2H) has exercised share options to purchase 400,000 shares in the Company at a subscription price per share of NOK 9.85 for 200,000 of the shares and NOK 10.85 for the remaining 200,000 new shares, total subscription amount NOK 4,140,000. 

As a result of the exercise of share options, the Company's Board of Directors has, pursuant to an authorisation granted by the General Meeting on 24 May 2017, resolved to increase the Company's share capital with NOK 40,000 by issuing 400,000 new shares, each with a par value of NOK 0.10. Following completion of the transaction, the Company's share capital will be NOK 36,952,059.08 divided into 369,520,598 shares. 

Upon receipt of the shares, Henrik Wennerholm will in total hold 2,381,342 shares of which 1,981,342 are held through Femven AS. Henrik Wennerholm has 200,000 remaining share options in the Company which can be exercised from September to December 2018.
