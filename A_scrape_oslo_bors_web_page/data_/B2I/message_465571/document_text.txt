On 12 December 2018 Olav Dalen Zahl, Chief Executive Officer, acquired 30,000 shares in B2Holding ASA through his related party Fjordsyn AS at an average price of NOK 12.60.

After this transaction, Olav Dalen Zahl and related parties hold 2,459,786 shares in B2Holding ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
