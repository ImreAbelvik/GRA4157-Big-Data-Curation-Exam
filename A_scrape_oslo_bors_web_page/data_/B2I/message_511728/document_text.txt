Trond Kristian Andreassen, member of the Board of Directors of B2Holding ASA, has on 20 August 2020 acquired 125,000 shares in B2Holding ASA through his related party Vimar AS at an average price of NOK 6.08. Following the transaction, Trond Kristian Andreassen and related party hold 125,000 shares in the Company. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
