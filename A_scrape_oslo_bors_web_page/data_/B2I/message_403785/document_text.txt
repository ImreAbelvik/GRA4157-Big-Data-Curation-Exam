Henrik Wennerholm, Group Business Development Director, has 
on 8 June 2016 June transferred 1,981,342 shares in B2Holding 
ASA from Femwen AB to Femwen AS. Femwen AB and Femwen AS are 
wholly owned by Henrik Wennerholm. The transaction was done 
at the introductory price of NOK 12 per share.
 
After this transaction, Femwen AS owns 1,981,342 shares and 
Femwen AB owns 0 shares in B2Holding ASA. 
 
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to sections 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading 
Act.
