On 30 November 2018 Jon H. Nordbrekken, Chairman of the Board of Directors in B2Holding ASA, acquired 111,504 shares in B2Holding ASA at an average price of NOK 13.54 per share. Following this transaction, Jon H. Nordbrekken and related party hold 27,200,000 shares in B2Holding ASA which represents 6,65% of the share capital.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
