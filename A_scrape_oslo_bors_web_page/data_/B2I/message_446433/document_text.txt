The following primary insiders in B2Holding ASA "the Company") have been allocated shares in the Private Placement in the Company conducted 13 March 2018.

- Prioritet Group AB, represented on the Board of Directors by deputy member Niklas Wiberg was allocated 1,081,481 shares and holds 52,200,000 shares after the transaction, representing 12.8% of the outstanding shares and votes in the Company.

- Jon Harald Nordbrekken through Valset Invest AS, being the Chairman of the Board, was allocated 500,000 shares and holds 24,500,000 shares after the transaction, representing 6.0% of the outstanding shares and votes in the Company.

- Indigo Invest AS, represented in the Board of Directors in B2Holding by Per Kristian Spone, was allocated 740,741 shares and holds 16,383,360 shares after the transaction, representing 4.0% of the outstanding shares and votes in the Company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements set out in section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
