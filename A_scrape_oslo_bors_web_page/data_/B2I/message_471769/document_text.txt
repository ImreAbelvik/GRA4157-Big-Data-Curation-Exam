On 12 March 2019, Prioritet Group AB represented by Niklas Wiberg as Board member in B2Holding ASA, has acquired 420,415 shares in B2Holding ASA at an average price of NOK 12.74 per share. Following this transaction, Prioritet Group AB holds 52,913,000 shares in B2Holding ASA which represents 12,91% of the share capital.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
