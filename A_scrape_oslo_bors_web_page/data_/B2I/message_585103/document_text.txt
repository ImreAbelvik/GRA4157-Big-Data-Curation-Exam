Erik Just Johnsen, Chief Executive Officer of B2Holding ASA, has on 13 March 2023 acquired 50,000 shares in B2Holding ASA at an average price of NOK 7.45.

Following the transaction, Erik Just Johnsen and related parties hold 2,275,680 shares in the Company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
