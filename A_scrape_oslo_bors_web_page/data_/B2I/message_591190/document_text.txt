Stenshagen Invest AS, a related party to Kjetil Garstad, Member of the Board of
Directors of B2Holding ASA, has on 22 May 2023 purchased 4,000,000 shares in
B2Holding ASA at an average price of NOK 7.5017.

Following this transaction, Stenshagen Invest AS holds 30,500,143 shares in the
Company representing 7.60% of the share capital.
