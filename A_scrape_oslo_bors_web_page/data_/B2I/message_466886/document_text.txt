J. Harald Henriksen, Chief Compliance Officer in B2Holding ASA, has exercised his share options to purchase 900,000 shares in the Company at a subscription price per share of NOK 8.356, adjusted for dividends paid in June 2017 and 2018, total subscription amount NOK 7,520,400.

As a result of the exercise of share options, the Company's Board of Directors will, pursuant to an authorisation granted by the general meeting on 25 May 2018, resolved to increase the Company's share capital with NOK 90,000 by issuing 900,000 new shares, each with a par value of NOK 0.10.

Following completion of the transaction, the Company's share capital will be NOK 40,993,259.80, divided into 409,932,598 shares. Upon receipt of the shares,  
J. Harald Henriksen will hold in total 920,057 shares.
