Harald Thorstein, Chair of the Board of Directors of B2Holding ASA, has on 20 August 2020 acquired 125,000 shares in B2Holding ASA at an average price of NOK 6.068. Following the transaction, Harald Thorstein holds 125,000 shares in the Company. 

Tore Krogstad, Head of Personnel, Improvement & Restructuring, has on 20 August 2020, acquired 32,000 shares in B2Holding ASA through his related party Engelia Invest AS at an average price of NOK 6.35. Following this transaction, Tore Krogstad and related parties holds 120,000 shares in the Company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
