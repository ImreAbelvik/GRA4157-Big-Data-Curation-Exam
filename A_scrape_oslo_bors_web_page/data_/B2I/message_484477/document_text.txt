On 4 September 2019 Stenshagen Invest AS, represented by Kjetil Garstad as member of the Board of Directors of B2Holding ASA, has acquired 1.000.000 shares in B2Holding ASA at an average price of NOK 6.65 per share. After this transaction, Stenshagen Invest AS holds 18,893,376 shares, which represents 4,49% of the share capital. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
