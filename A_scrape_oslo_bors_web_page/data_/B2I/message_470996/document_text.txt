On 28 February 2019, Stenshagen Invest AS represented by Kjetil Garstad as Deputy board member in B2Holding ASA, has acquired 500,000 shares in B2Holding ASA at an average price of NOK 13.21 per share. Following this transaction, Stenshagen Invest AS holds 17,893,376 shares in B2Holding ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
