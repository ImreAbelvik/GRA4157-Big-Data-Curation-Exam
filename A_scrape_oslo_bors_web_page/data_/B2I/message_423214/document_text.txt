On 22 March 2017 Erik J. Johnsen, Chief Financial Officer, 
acquired 20.000 shares in B2Holding ASA through his related 
party Pine AS at the average price of NOK 14.05 per share. 
After this transaction, Pine AS holds 310.000 shares in 
B2Holding ASA. 
 
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading 
Act.
