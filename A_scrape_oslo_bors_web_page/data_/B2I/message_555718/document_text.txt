Trond Kristian Andreassen, member of the Board of Directors of B2Holding ASA, has on 7 March 2022 acquired 40,000 shares in B2Holding ASA through his related party Vimar AS at an average price of NOK 8.295.

Following the transaction, Trond Kristian Andreassen and related party hold 255,000 shares in the Company. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
