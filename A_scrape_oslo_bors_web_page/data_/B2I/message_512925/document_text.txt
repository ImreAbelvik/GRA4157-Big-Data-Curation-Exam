Erik J. Johnsen, Chief Executive Officer of B2Holding ASA, has on 4 September 2020, acquired 100,000 shares in B2Holding ASA through his related party Erling Johnsen AS at an average price of NOK 6.142. Following this transaction, Erik J. Johnsen and related parties hold 2,010,000 shares in the Company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
