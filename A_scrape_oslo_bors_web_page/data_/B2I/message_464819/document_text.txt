On 30 November 2018 Kjetil Garstad, Deputy board member in  B2Holding ASA, acquired 60,000 shares in B2Holding ASA through his related party Steel City AS at an average price of NOK 13.38 per share. Following this transaction, Kjetil Garstad and related party hold 451 425 shares in B2Holding ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
