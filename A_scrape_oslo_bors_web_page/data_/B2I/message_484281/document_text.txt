On 30 August March 2019 at CET 07:00, Board member Per Kristian Spone transferred 66,708 shares from his private holding to his wholly owned company Spone Invest AS. The shares were transferred at a price of NOK 6,17 per share.    

Following this transaction, the number of shares owned by Per Kristian Spone and related parties remains unchanged at 66,708 shares.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
