Tore Krogstad, Head of Personnel, Improvement & Restructuring, has on 7 September 2020, transferred 56,000 shares in B2Holding ASA to his related party Engelia Invest AS at an average price of NOK 6.66 per share. Following this transaction, Tore Krogstad and related party hold 120,000 shares in B2Holding ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
