Erik J. Johnsen, CEO of B2Holding ASA, has transferred 1,910,000 shares in B2Holding ASA from Pine AS to Erling Johnsen AS as of 20 August 2020, following an internal upstream simplified merger between Pine AS and Erling Johnsen AS (both companies 100% owned by Erik J. Johnsen). 

Following this merger, Erik J. Johnsen and related party hold 1,910,000 shares in B2Holding ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
