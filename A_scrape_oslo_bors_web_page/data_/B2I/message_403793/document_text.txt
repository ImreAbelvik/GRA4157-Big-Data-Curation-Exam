Oslo, 9 June 2016. 

Jens Harald Henriksen (Chief Executive Officer) has on 9 June 
2016 acquired 17,000 shares in B2Holding ASA at a price of 
NOK 12.50 per share. Following completion of the acquisition, 
Jens Harald Henriksen holds 20,057 shares in B2Holding. 

On 9 June 2016, Rasmus Hansson (Business 
development/Strategy/M&A), through his related party RMH 
Invest AS, acquired 37,000 shares in B2Holding ASA at a price 
of NOK 12.50 per share. Following completion of the 
acquisition, Rasmus Hansson holds 40,057 shares in B2Holding, 
through his related party RMH Invest AS.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to sections 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading 
Act.
