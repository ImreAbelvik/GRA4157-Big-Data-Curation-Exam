


Jon Amund Eriksen, Chief Technology Innovation Officer of Targovax ASA, has on
17 November 2016 acquired 3,951 shares in Targovax ASA at a price of NOK 9.60
per share. Following the transaction, Jon Amund Eriksen and his close associates
hold 728,601 shares and 160,000 options in Targovax ASA.
