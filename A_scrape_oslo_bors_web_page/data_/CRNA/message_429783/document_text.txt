


Lars Lund-Roland, member of the Board of Targovax ASA, has on 15 June 2017
subscribed for 16,394 shares in Targovax ASA at a price of NOK 0.10 per share in
connection with settlement of vested restricted stock units ("RSUs") which was
granted at the AGM in 2016 and 2017. Following the transaction, Lars Lund-Roland
and his close associates will hold 20,811 shares and 10,051 RSUs in Targovax
ASA.
