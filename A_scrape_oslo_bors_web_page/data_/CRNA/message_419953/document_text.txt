




Lars Lund-Roland, board member of Targovax ASA, has on 13 February 2017
subscribed 4,417 shares in Targovax ASA at a price of NOK 0.10 per share in
connection with settlement of vested restricted stock units ("RSUs") which was
granted at the 2016 AGM. Following the transaction, Lars Lund-Roland and his
close associates will hold 4,417 shares and 16,394 RSUs in Targovax ASA.
