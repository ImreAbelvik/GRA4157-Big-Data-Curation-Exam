


Reference is made to stock exchange notice of 4 November 2016 regarding increase
of the share capital in Targovax ASA (the "Company") relating to exercise of
employee options and settlement of restricted stock units. As per 18 November
2016, 56,799 shares each with a par value of NOK 0.10 were subscribed by
employees holding vested options. No restricted stock unites were settled and no
shares were accordingly subscribed for by any holders of restricted stock units.

The new share capital in the Company will accordingly be NOK 4,219,080. The
share capital increase will be registered in the Register of Business
Enterprises (NW: Foretaksregisteret) as soon as practically possible.
