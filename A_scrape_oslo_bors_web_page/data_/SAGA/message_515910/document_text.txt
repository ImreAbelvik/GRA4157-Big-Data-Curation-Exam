Reference is made to the stock exchange notice from Saga Tankers ASA (the
"Company") dated 20 October 2020 regarding completion of a private placement of
new shares in the Company (the "Private Placement").

Hanekamb Invest AS, a company controlled by Martin Nes, Chairman and primary
insider, has on 20 October 2020 acquired 850,000 shares in the Company in the
Private Placement at a price of NOK 1.30 per share.

After the transaction, Martin Nes and associated companies have a holding of
1,850,000 shares in the Company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
