Reference is made to the stock exchange notice from Saga Tankers ASA (the
"Company") dated 20 October 2020 regarding completion of a private placement of
new shares in the Company (the "Private Placement").

EL Investment AS, a company controlled by CEO and primary insider of the
Company, Espen Lundaas, has on 20 October 2020 acquired 850,000 shares in the
Company in the Private Placement at a price of NOK 1.30 per share.
