Reference is made to the stock exchange notice from Saga Pure ASA (the
"Company") dated 14 December 2020 regarding completion of a private placement of
new shares in the Company (the "Private Placement").

Martin Nes, chairman and primary insider, has on 14 December 2020 been allocated
150,000 shares in the Company in the Private Placement at a price of NOK 2.10
per share through his controlled company Hanekamb Invest AS. After the
transaction, Martin Nes and associated companies have a holding of 2,000,000
shares in the Company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
