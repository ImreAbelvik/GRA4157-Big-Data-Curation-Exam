Hanekamb Invest AS, a company controlled by Martin Nes, chairman of Saga Tankers ASA, has on 30. January purchased 500 000 shares in Saga Tankers ASA at NOK 1,00 per share.
 
After the transaction, Martin Nes and associated companies have a total holding of 500 000 shares in Saga Tankers ASA, corresponding to 0,19 % of the total outstanding shares.
