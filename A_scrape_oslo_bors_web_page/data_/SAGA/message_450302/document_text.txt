Oslo, 2 May 2018

Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement on 2 May 2018 regarding the
potential sale of shares in Saga Tankers ASA ("Saga Tankers" or the "Company",
ticker "SAGA" on Oslo Axess). Allum Holding AS and Ferncliff AS have today sold
all of their 47,726,655 shares in Saga Tankers, representing approximately
17.9% of the share capital (the "Secondary Sale"). Ferncliff AS has sold
6,235,316 shares in the Company and Allum Holding AS has sold 41,491,339 shares
in the Company. The price per share in the Secondary Sale was NOK 1.65. DNB
Markets, a part of DNB Bank ASA, acted as sole bookrunner for the Secondary Sale
(the "Bookrunner").

Allum Holding AS and Ferncliff AS are both owned by Øystein Stray Spetalen,
member of the Board of Directors of Saga Tankers. Following the Secondary Sale,
Øystein Stray Spetalen and associated companies will own 172,841,799 shares in
the Company, representing approximately 64.9% of the outstanding shares in the
Company.

The reason for the Secondary Sale is to increase free-float and maintain a stock
with public interest.

In relation the Secondary Sale, Øystein Stray Spetalen has entered into a lock-
up agreement with the Bookrunner, for his and his associated companies'
shareholdings in the Company for a period of 12 months following completion of
the Secondary Sale.
