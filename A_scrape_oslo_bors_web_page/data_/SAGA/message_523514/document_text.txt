Oslo 1 February

Reference is made to the stock exchange notice from Saga Pure ASA (the "Company") dated 28 January 2021 regarding completion of subsequent offerings of new shares in the Company (the "Subsequent Offerings"). Tore Jakob Berg, Chief Accounting Officer and primary insider, has on 28 January 2021 been allocated 32,583 shares in the Company in the Subsequent Offer I at a price of NOK 2.10 per share, and 34,396 shares in the Company in the Subsequent Offer II at a price of NOK 2.90 per share. After the transaction, Tore Jakob Berg have a holding of 566,979 shares in the Company. This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Sections 4-2 and 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
