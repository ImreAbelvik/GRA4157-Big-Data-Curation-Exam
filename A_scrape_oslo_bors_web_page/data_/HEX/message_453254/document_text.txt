


Frank Häberli, Group Vice President Strategic Development of Hexagon Composites
and primary insider, has today bought 3,000 shares in Hexagon Composites ASA at
the price of NOK 24.92 per share. After the transaction Frank Häberli holds
21,713 shares in the Company.



This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
