


Skjalg S. Stavheim, Managing Director of Hexagon Ragasco and primary insider,

incentive scheme for the executive management.

After the transaction Skjalg S. Stavheim holds 144,715 shares in the company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
