


Daniel Aasheim, Chief Strategy Officer of Hexagon Composites and primary
insider, has today bought 2,293 shares in Hexagon Composites ASA at the average
price of NOK 21.80 per share. This transaction is a mandated part of the Group's
incentive scheme for the executive management.

After the transaction Daniel Aasheim holds 5,293 shares in the Company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
