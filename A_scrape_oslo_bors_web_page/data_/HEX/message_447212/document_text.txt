


On 21 March 2018, Flakk Invest AS purchased 100,000 shares in Hexagon Composites
ASA at a price of NOK 23.36 per share. Flakk Invest AS is a company controlled
by Knut Flakk, Chairman of Hexagon Composites ASA.

After the transaction Flakk Invest AS holds 1,100,000 shares. The total holding
of Knut Flakk and his close associates, is 30,815,717 shares in Hexagon
Composites ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
