


Reference is made to the stock exchange release dated 24 May 2018 regarding
Hexagon Composites' share buy-back program.

Hexagon Composites ASA has on 24 May 2018 bought 33,500 shares in Hexagon
Composites ASA at an average price of NOK 20.82 per share. After the transaction
Hexagon Composites ASA holds 1,199,575 shares in the company.
