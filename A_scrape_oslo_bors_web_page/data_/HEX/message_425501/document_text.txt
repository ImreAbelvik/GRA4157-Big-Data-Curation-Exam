


Skjalg Sylte Stavheim, Managing Director of Hexagon Ragasco and primary insider,
has today bought 4,500 shares in Hexagon Composites ASA at the price of NOK
26.80 per share. This transaction is a mandated part of the Group's incentive
scheme for the executive management.

After the transaction Skjalg Sylte Stavheim holds 29,215 shares in the company.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
