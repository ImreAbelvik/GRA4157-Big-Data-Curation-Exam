


In accordance with resolutions no 6 and no 13.2 of the Ordinary Shareholders'
Meeting held in Aalesund on April 22(nd) 2014, the Board of Directors of Hexagon
Composites ASA has issued 975,000 share options under the Company's management
incentive program. Of the 975,000 options, 600,000 options have been granted to
senior executives and managers listed as primary insiders.



Of the options granted to primary insiders:

  Jack Schimenti       100,000             100,000 options and 38,041 shares

  David Bandele        100,000             100,000 options and 29,545 shares

  Ragnar Holthe          50,000            50,000 options and 2,870 shares

  Daniel Aasheim         50,000            50,000 options and 3,000 shares

  Solveig Sæther         25,000            25,000 options and 1,146 shares

  Geir Torset            25,000            25,000 options and 31,146 shares

  Gunnar Engkrog         25,000            25,000 options and 1,146 shares

  Trond Seth             25,000            25,000 options and 1,146 shares


The share options give rights to buy shares in Hexagon Composites ASA at NOK 25
per share.


The options may be exercised in part or in full within three weeks following the
official announcement of the financial results for the fourth quarter of 2017,
first quarter of 2018 or second quarter of 2018.
