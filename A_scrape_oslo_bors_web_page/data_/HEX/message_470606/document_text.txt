On February 26th 2019, Flakk Gruppen AS purchased 81,802 shares in Hexagon
Composites ASA from Mrs. Line K. Flakk at a price of NOK 33.40 per share. Mrs.
Line K. Flakk is married to Mr. Knut T. Flakk who is Chairman of Hexagon
Composites ASA. Mr. Knut T. Flakk is also the controlling shareholder in Flakk
Gruppen AS.

After the transaction Flakk Gruppen AS holds 113,217 shares and Mrs. Line K.
Flakk holds 500,000 shares in Hexagon Composites ASA. Mr. Knut T. Flakk and his
close associates controls the same number of shares in Hexagon Composites ASA as
before the transaction; namely 30,947,132 shares.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
