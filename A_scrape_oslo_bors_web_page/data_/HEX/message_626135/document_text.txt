23 August 2024: As part of a family succession plan, Jon Erik Engeset, CEO of
Hexagon Composites ASA, has today transferred 204,150 shares in the Company to
close family members. After the transfer, Engeset controls 259,448 shares, and
has earned all rights to 121,913 additional, not yet exercisable, shares under
the Company's incentive program.

Please refer to the attached notification of trading for further details.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements set out in the Market
Abuse Regulation EU 596/2014 Article 19 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
Section 5-12.
