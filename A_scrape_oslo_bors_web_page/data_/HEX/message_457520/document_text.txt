


On 17 August 2018, Flakk Gruppen AS purchased 31,415 shares in Hexagon
Composites ASA at an average price of NOK 23.50 per share. Flakk Gruppen AS is a
company controlled by Knut Flakk, Chairman of Hexagon Composites ASA.

After the transaction Flakk Gruppen AS holds 31,415 shares. The total holding of
Knut Flakk and his close associates, is 30,947,132 shares in Hexagon Composites
ASA.




This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
