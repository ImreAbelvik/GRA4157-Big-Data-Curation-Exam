Ms. Birthe Iren Grotle, member of the board of directors of Fjordkraft Holding ASA ("Fjordkraft"), has today acquired 2,900 shares in the Fjordkraft at a price per share of NOK 31. Following this acquisition, Ms. Grotle holds 2,900 shares in  Fjordkraft Holding ASA.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Act.
