Robert Olsen, member of the board of directors of Fjordkraft Holding ASA ("Fjordkraft"), has today acquired 16,150 shares in the Fjordkraft at a price per share of NOK 30.90. Following this acquisition, Robert Olsen holds 16,150 shares in the Fjordkraft.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Act.
