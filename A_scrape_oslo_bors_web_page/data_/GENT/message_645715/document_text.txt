Moss, 8 May 2025

Gentian Diagnostics ASA ("Gentian") has today been notified that the following transaction has been completed:
 
Norda ASA has purchased 101,848 shares in Gentian at NOK 51.50 per share. Following the transaction Norda ASA owns 716,099 shares in Gentian.  Norda ASA owns about 4.64% of the total outstanding shares following the transaction.  Norda ASA will together with the Group of companies Kvantia AS, Obligasjon 2 AS, Caaby AS, RWD AS, Insr ASA and Victoria India Fund AS following the share transaction hold in total 3,524,218 shares in Gentian, corresponding to approx. 22.85% of the total outstanding shares.
