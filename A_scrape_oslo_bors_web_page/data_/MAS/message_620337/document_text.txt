31.5.2024 09:37:29 CEST | Måsøval AS | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

Frøy Kapital AS has today acquired 3 277 233 shares in Måsøval AS. CEO of Frøy
Kapital AS, Roger Granheim, is a board member and a primary insider of Måsøval
AS.

After the transaction Frøy Kapital AS and its parent company, Gåsø
Næringsutvikling AS, own 9 394 269 shares in Måsøval AS, totalling 7,67 percent
of outstanding shares.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to MAR
article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

ABOUT MÅSØVAL AS

Måsøval is an integrated producer of Atlantic salmon with more than 50 years of
operational experience. Located in Central Norway in the region west of
Trondheim, Måsøval has both significant farming operations at sea and inhouse
smolt production. Through its majority ownership in the processing and
distribution company Pure Norwegian Seafood AS, Måsøval is processing and
exporting high quality Atlantic salmon to a global marketplace.
