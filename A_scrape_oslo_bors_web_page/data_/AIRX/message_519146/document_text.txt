Pursuant to authorization granted by the Company’s Annual General Meeting, the Company’s share capital will be increased by NOK 2 994 by issuing 299 400 new shares, of par value NOK 0.01. Following the increase, the Company’s share capital is 
NOK 1 706 056.37 divided into 170 605 637 shares.
