An associated company to a primary insider in Eidesvik Offshore ASA (“Eidesvik”) has bought shares in Eidesvik:

Vest-Norsk Handelskompani AS, an associated company to Kristine Skeie, Board Member in Eidesvik, has on November 18, 2021, bought 25,000 shares in Eidesvik at a price of NOK 4.36 per share. Following the transaction, Kristine Skeie and associates holds 25,000 shares in Eidesvik.

Details of the trade are set forth in the attached notification.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
