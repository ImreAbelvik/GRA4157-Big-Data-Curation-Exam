Oslo, Norway 2 July 2015: Luigi Costa, CEO of Nordic Nanovector ASA, has today
purchased 9,186 shares in Nordic Nanovector ASA at an average share price of NOK
24,64 per share.
Following the transaction, Luigi Costa will own 73,186 shares in Nordic
Nanovector ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
