PRESS RELEASE

Nordic Nanovector - Mandatory Notification of trade - primary insider

Oslo, Norway, 7 June 2023

Jon Magne Asmyr, the Chairman of the Board has today purchased 400.000 shares in
Nordic Nanovector ASA (the "Company") at an average share price of NOK 1,09 per
share. Following this transaction, Jon Magne Asmyr owns 4.150.000 shares in the
Company.

This information is subject to duty of disclosure pursuant to Section 4-2 and
Section 5-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
