Oslo, Norway, 8 December 2016

On 7 December 2016, primary insider Jostein Dahle, Chief Scientific Officer of
Nordic Nanovector ASA, sold 70,000 shares in Nordic Nanovector ASA at an average
price of NOK 116.09 per share.
Following this transaction, Jostein Dahle, and his related parties, own 204,958
shares in Nordic Nanovector ASA. Jostein Dahle also holds 135,000 options in
Nordic Nanovector ASA.
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 4
-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
