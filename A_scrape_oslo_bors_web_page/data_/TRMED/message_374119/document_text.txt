Marco Renoldi, who is the Chief Business Officer of Nordic Nanovector ASA, has
today purchased 70,000 shares in Nordic Nanovector ASA at an average share price
of NOK 34,92 per share.
Following the transaction, Marco Renoldi will own 70,000 shares and 278,137
options in Nordic Nanovector ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
