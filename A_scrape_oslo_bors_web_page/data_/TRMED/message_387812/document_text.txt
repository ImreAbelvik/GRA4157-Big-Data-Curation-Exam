Oslo, Norway 26 October 2015:

Roy H. Larsen, member of the Board of Directors of Nordic Nanovector ASA, has
today purchased 38,000 shares in Nordic Nanovector ASA through his wholly owned
company Sciencons AS at an average share price of NOK 13.02 per share. Following
the transaction, Roy H. Larsen directly and indirectly will own 1,800,000 shares
in Nordic Nanovector ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
