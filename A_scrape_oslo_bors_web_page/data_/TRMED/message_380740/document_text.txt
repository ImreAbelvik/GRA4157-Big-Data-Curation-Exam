Oslo, Norway 19 June 2015: Ludvik Sandnes, chairman of the Board of Directors of
Nordic Nanovector ASA, has today purchased 2,626 shares in Nordic Nanovector ASA
at an average share price of NOK 30,00 per share, through his 100% owned company
Ekornhuset AS.

Following the transaction, Ludvik Sandnes directly and indirectly will own
125,000 shares in Nordic Nanovector ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
