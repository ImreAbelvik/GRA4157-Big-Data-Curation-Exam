


The Chairman Erling Øverland has today, 26(th) January 2015, sold 29.445
subscription rights to Trifolium AS at a price per subscription right of NOK
1,00. Trifolium AS is wholly owned by Erling Øverland and his wife. Trifolium AS
exercised all subscription rights immediately after the transaction was
completed.

Erling Øverland now owns 32.500 shares privately and 29.445 shares through
Trifolium AS. This gives Erling Øverland a total shareholding in the company of
61.945 shares and 0 subscription rights.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
