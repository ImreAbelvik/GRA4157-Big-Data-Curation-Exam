Oslo, 7 November 2022 

Martin Prytz, CFO of Lumi Gruppen and primary insider, has bought 2,280 shares in Lumi Gruppen AS. The number of shares in Lumi Gruppen owned by Martin Prytz directly after the transaction is 5,370. Please refer to the attached filing for further information. 


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
