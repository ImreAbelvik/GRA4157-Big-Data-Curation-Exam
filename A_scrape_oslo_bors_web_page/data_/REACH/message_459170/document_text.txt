Reach Subsea ASA - Mandatory notifications of trade and disclosure of shareholding

North Energy ASA has through its subsidiary North Energy Capital AS purchased 82,800 shares in Reach Subsea ASA at a price of NOK 2.5962 per share. The total number of shares owned by North Energy ASA after the transaction, through the subsidiaries North Energy Capital AS and Accello Partners I AS is 42,043,343, which constitutes a 29.4% ownership in Reach Subsea ASA.
