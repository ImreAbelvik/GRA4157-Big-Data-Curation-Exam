Reach Subsea ASA - Mandatory notifications of trade and disclosure

Oslo, 29 June 2017
North Energy has through its subsidiary Accello Partners I AS 
"Accello" purchased 91,665 shares in Reach Subsea ASA at a price of 
NOK 1.8981 per share. The total number of shares owned by Accello 
after the transaction is 40,331,563 , which equals 28.16 % ownership 
in Reach Subsea ASA.
