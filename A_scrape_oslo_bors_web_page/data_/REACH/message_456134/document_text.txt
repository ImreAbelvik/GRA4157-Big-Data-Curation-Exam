Oslo, 25 July 2018 

North Energy ASA has through its subsidiary North Energy Capital AS purchased 152 160 shares in Reach Subsea ASA at a price of NOK 2.68 per share. The total number of shares owned by North Energy ASA after the transaction, through the subsidiaries North Energy Capital AS and Accello Partners I AS, is 41,555,057 which constitutes a 29% ownership in Reach Subsea ASA.
