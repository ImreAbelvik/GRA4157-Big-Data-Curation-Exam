AB Investment AS (a company owned by deputy chairman of the Board in Reach Subsea ASA, Mr Anders Onarheim) has purchased 50,000 shares in Reach Subsea ASA at a price of NOK 2.35 per share. The total number of shares owned by AB Investment AS after the transaction 1,755,743, which constitutes a 1.2 % ownership in Reach Subsea ASA.
