TRADE NOTIFICATION

Anders Onarheim, Vice Chairman of the Board of Directors of Reach 
Subsea ASA ("Reach"), has today purchased 467,082 REACH shares at 
NOK 1.967 per share via his fully owned company AB Investment AS. 
Total number of shares owned after the transaction is 1,489,229. 
Anders Onarheim is also part owner in, and represents in the Board, 
the company Accello Partners I AS, currently owning 40,109,711 
shares in Reach.

Rachid Bendriss, Observer in the Board of Reach, has today purchased 
467,082 REACH shares at NOK 1.967 per share via his fully owned 
company Celisa Capital AS. Total number of shares owned after the 
transaction is 717,082. Rachid Bendriss is also part owner in and 
Chairman of the Board of the company Accello Partners I AS, 
currently owning 40,109,711 shares in Reach.
