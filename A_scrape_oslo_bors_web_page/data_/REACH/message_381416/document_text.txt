Anders Onarheim, Vice Chairman of the Board of 
Directors of Reach Subsea ASA, has today purchased 
54.448 REACH shares at NOK 2,3 per share via his 
fully owned company AB Investment AS. Total number 
of shares after the transaction is 354.448.  
Anders Onarheim is also part owner in, and 
represents in the Board, the company Accello 
Partners I AS, owning 23.444.254 shares in Reach 
Subsea ASA.
 
Rachid Bendriss, Observer in the Board of Reach 
Subsea ASA, has today purchased 50.000 REACH shares 
at NOK 2,3 per share via his fully owned company 
Celisa Capital AS. Total number of shares after the 
transaction is 150.000.  Rachid Bendriss is 
also part owner in, and represents in the Board, the 
company Accello Partners I AS, owning 23.444.254 
shares in Reach Subsea ASA.
