Today, 22. november 2019, Arnt Roger Aasen, Webstep director internal communications and marketing, through his fully owned company Aravi AS,  has sold 110,000 shares at a price of NOK 23,5 per share in Webstep ASA ("Webstep"). 

Following the transaction, Arnt Roger Aasen through his related parties hold 23,461 options and 14.530 shares in Webstep ASA.
