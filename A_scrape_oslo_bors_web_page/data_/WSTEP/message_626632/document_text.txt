Oslo, 29 August 2024: Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement
published by Webstep ASA (the "Company") earlier today, on 29 August 2024,
regarding the resolution by the Company's board of directors to increase the
Company's share capital with NOK 218,690 by the issuance of 218,690 new shares,
each with a par value of NOK 1.00, in order to facilitate the delivery of new
shares to the option holders having exercised 218,690 options under the
Company's long-term incentive program.
