Oslo, 14 December 2021: Reference is made to Webstep ASA's (the "Company") stock
exchange announcement earlier today, on 14 December 2021, regarding the result
of the offer of new shares to employees in the Company and its subsidiary
Webstep AS under a share purchase programme (the "Share Purchase Program") and
the board of directors' resolution to increase the share capital of the Company.
