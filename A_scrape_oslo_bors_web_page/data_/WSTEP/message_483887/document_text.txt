Liv Annike Kverneland, CFO of Webstep ASA, has, through her fully owned company, LAK Invest AS, bought 5,285 shares at a price of NOK 23,96 per share. Following this transaction, Ms Kverneland and related parties own 31,751 shares in Webstep ASA.

The transaction was completed 27 August 2019.
