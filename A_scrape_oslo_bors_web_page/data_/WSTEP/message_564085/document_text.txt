Oslo, 2 June 2022: Geir Jåthun Hindenes, Regional Manager Stavanger, has today
sold  36,442 shares in Webstep ASA at an average sale price of NOK 30.95 per
share, for a total sale amount of NOK 1,127,840.

Reference is made to the attached notification of trading for further details.
