Today, 12 November 2019, Embro Eiendom AS has purchased 111 526 shares at a price of NOK 23,2 per share in Webstep ASA ("Webstep").  

Embro Eiendom AS is controlled by Jørgen Cato Broch, and is represented on the board of Webstep.

Following the transaction, Jørgen Cato Broch through his related parties hold 6,556,369 shares and votes in Webstep, corresponding to 24.3 % of the currently outstanding shares and votes of Webstep.

This information is published in accordance with section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
