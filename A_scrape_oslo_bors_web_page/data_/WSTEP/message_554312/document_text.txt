Oslo, 18 February 2022: Trond K. Johannessen, Chair of the Board of Webstep ASA,
has today purchased 6,075 shares in Webstep ASA at an average price of NOK 33.50
per share.

Following this transaction, Trond K. Johannessen owns 63,000 shares in Webstep
ASA.
