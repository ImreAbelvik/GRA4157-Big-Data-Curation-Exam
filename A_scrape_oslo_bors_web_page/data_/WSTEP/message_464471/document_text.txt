Oslo, 27 November 2018. Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement by
Webstep ASA ("Webstep") on 26 November 2018 regarding the offer of shares to
employees (the "Offer").
The application period in the Offer commenced on 26 November 2018. As of today,
27 November 2018, the following primary insiders have applied for shares in the
Offer:

- Kjetil Bakke Eriksen (CEO), for an amount of NOK 15,000 (excluding the
discount given in the Offer);

- Liv Annike Kverneland (CFO), for an amount of NOK 15,000 (excluding the
discount given in the Offer);

- Rolf Helle (Director Business Development), for an amount of NOK 15,000
(excluding the discount given in the Offer);

- Arnt Roger Aasen (CCO), for an amount of NOK 15,000 (excluding the discount
given in the Offer);

- Marianne Styrman (Director IoT), for an amount of NOK 15,000 (excluding the
discount given in the Offer);

- Ole Jørgen Kirkeluten (Senior consultant) for an amount of NOK 15,000
(excluding the discount given in the Offer);

- Anders Bjørnestad (Senior consultant) for an amount of NOK 15,000 (excluding
the discount given in the Offer);

and

- Morten Evenrud (Finance Manager), for an amount of NOK 15,000 (excluding the
discount given in the Offer).

The total amount of shares acquired by the primary insiders and their aggregate
holding of shares following the acquisition will be included in a stock exchange
announcement following the expiry of the application period, when the purchase
price for the shares in the Offer has been determined.
