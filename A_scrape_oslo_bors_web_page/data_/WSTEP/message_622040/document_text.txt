Oslo, 20 June 2024: Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement
published by Webstep ASA (the "Company") earlier today, on 20 June 2024,
regarding the resolution by the Company's board of directors to increase the
Company's share capital with NOK 150,135 by the issuance of 150,135 new shares,
each with a par value of NOK 1.00, in order to facilitate the delivery of new
shares to the option holders having exercised 150,135 options under the
Company's long-term incentive program.

Embro Eiendom AS, a closely related party to the Chair of the Board at Webstep,
Kjell Magne Leirgulen, has lent out 150,135 shares in the Company to DNB
Markets, a part of DNB Bank ASA pursuant to a share lending agreement entered
into on 20 June 2024.

Reference is made to the attached notifications of trading for further details
of the transactions carried out by the primary insiders and their closely
associated persons.
