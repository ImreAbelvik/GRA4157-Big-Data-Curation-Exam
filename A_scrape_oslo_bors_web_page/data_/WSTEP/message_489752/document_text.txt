Oslo, 20 November 2019. Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement by
Webstep ASA ("Webstep") on 19 November 2019 regarding the offer of shares to
employees (the "Offer").

- Fredrik Skuland Olsen (Group Business Controller), for an amount of NOK 15,000

 and

The total amount of shares acquired by the primary insiders and their aggregate
