Reference is made to Havyard Group ASA's ("Havyard") stock exchange announcement on 13 June 2022 regarding an extraordinary general meeting at which Havyard decided to distribute extraordinary dividends in kind in the form of total 10,000,000 shares in HAV Group ASA (the “HAV-shares”).

Havila Holding AS and Innidimman AS have on 28 June 2022 received 4,044,324 and 521,717 HAV-shares respectively as dividend at a price of NOK 14,06 per share, which is closely associated companies of Chair of the board and primary insider of HAV Group ASA, Vegard Sævik.

After receipt of the dividends, Havila Holding AS holds 8 737 824 shares and Innidimman AS holds 1 127 179 shares in HAV Group ASA.
