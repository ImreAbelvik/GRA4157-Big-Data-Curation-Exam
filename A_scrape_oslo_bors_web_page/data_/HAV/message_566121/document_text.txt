Reference is made to Havyard Group ASA's ("Havyard") stock exchange announcement on 13 June 2022 regarding an extraordinary general meeting at which Havyard decided to distribute extraordinary dividends in kind in the form of total 10,000,000 shares in HAV Group ASA (the “HAV-shares”).

Kamato AS has on 28 June 2022 received 85 703 HAV-shares as dividend at a price of NOK 14,06 per share, which is a closely associated company of board member and primary insider of HAV Group ASA, Helge Simonnes.

After receipt of the dividend, Kamato AS holds 225 499 shares in HAV Group ASA. In addition, Helge Simonnes holds 16 556 shares in own person. 

Please see the attached document for further details of the transaction.
