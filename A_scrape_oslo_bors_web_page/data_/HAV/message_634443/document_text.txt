Havila Holding AS, a closely associated company of primary insiders Vegard Sævik (Chair of the board) and Hege Sævik Rabben (board member), has on 11 December 2024 purchased 3,466,829 shares at a price of NOK 8,50 per share. 

After the transaction, Havila Holding AS holds 12,204,656 shares in HAV Group ASA. Vegard Sævik holds additionally 1,127,179 shares through his private company Innidimman AS, while Hege Sævik Rabben holds additionally 1,127,179 shares through her private company HSR Invest AS.
