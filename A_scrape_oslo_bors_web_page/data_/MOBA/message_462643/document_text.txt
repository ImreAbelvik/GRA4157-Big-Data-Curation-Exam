The following primary insiders of Komplett Bank has on 1 November 2018 exercised options to subscribe new shares:

Enok Hanssen, Product Director for POS Finance and primary insider in Komplett Bank ASA, has exercised options to subscribe 15,491 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1. Following the transaction Enok Hanssen controls 99,084 shares, and options to subscribe for 62,361 shares in Komplett Bank ASA.

Annika Dolve, Product Director for Loans Sweden and Finland and primary insider in Komplett Bank ASA, has exercised options to subscribe 30,008 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1. Following the transaction Annika Dolve controls 50,008 shares, and options to subscribe for 87,230 shares in Komplett Bank ASA.

Jan Haglund, CFO/CSO and primary insider in Komplett Bank ASA, has exercised options to subscribe 65,785 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1. Following the transaction Jan Haglund controls 560,000 shares, and options to subscribe for 541,229 shares in Komplett Bank ASA.

Kent Erik Tommy Österlund, CCRO and primary insider in Komplett Bank ASA, has exercised options to subscribe 610,375 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1. Following the transaction Kent Erik Tommy Österlund controls 2,171,778 shares, and options to subscribe for 24,580 shares in Komplett Bank ASA.
