Enok Hanssen, Product Director for POS Finance and primary insider in Komplett Bank ASA, has exercised options to subscribe 12,758 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1. 
Following the transaction Enok Hanssen owns 83,593 shares, and options to subscribe for 63,081 shares in Komplett Bank ASA.
 
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
