Steffen Ryengen, Chief Marketing and Information Officer in Komplett Bank ASA, has on 20 May 2020 sold 350,029 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a price of NOK 6.29 per share to Contribute AS, a company which is wholly owned by Steffen Ryengen. Following the transaction, the holdings of Steffen Ryengen and his close associates is unchanged with a total of 1,621,279 shares and 522,259 options in Komplett Bank ASA, of which Contribute AS holds 1,621,279 shares.
