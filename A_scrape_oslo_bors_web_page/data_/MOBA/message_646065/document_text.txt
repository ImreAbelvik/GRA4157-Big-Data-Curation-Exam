Øyvind Oanes (CEO) has today, on 12 May 2025, bought 30,000 shares in Morrow Bank ASA at an average price of NOK 12.119 per share. Following the transaction, Øyvind Oanes and close associates hold 482,727 shares, 462,980 share options and 1,250,000 warrants in Morrow Bank ASA.

This information is considered to be inside information pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation article 7 and is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act. This stock exchange announcement was published by Henning Fagerbakke, Head of Finance, Morrow Bank ASA, on 12 May 2025 at 14:20 CEST.
