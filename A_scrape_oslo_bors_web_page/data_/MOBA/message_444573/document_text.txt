Incentive AS, in its capacity as discretionary investment manager of funds and managed accounts, has on the 16th of February purchased 680.000 shares in Komplett Bank ASA for NOK 11.934.000 at a price of NOK 17.55 per share. Before this transaction, funds and managed accounts under investment management by Incentive AS owned 5.500.000 shares in Komplett Bank ASA and after these transactions funds and managed accounts under investment management by Incentive AS own 6.180.000 shares in Komplett Bank ASA. Hermann Alexander Kopp, a board member and primary insider in Komplett Bank ASA, is partner, board member and part of the investment team of Incentive AS
