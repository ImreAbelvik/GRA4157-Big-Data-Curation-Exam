Reference is made to the stock exchange issued by Komplett Bank ASA (the "Company") earlier today where it was announced that the Extraordinary General Meeting of Komplett Bank ASA had approved the (i) share capital increase pertaining to the issue of 18,181,818 shares in the private placement that was successfully placed on 16 February 2023, and (ii) the issuance of 5,250,000 warrants to certain members of the Company's management.

The following persons discharging managerial responsibilities ("PDMRs") have subscribed for the following number of shares in the Private Placement (partially through close associates) and warrants: 
- Stig Eide Sivertsen, Chairman of the Board, has subscribed for a total of 100,000 shares 
- Bodil Palma Hollingsæter, board member, has subscribed for a total of 140,000 shares 
- Anna Karin Østlie, board member, has subscribed for a total of 90,909 shares 
- Thomas Bjørnstad, board member, has subscribed for a total of 72,727 shares 
- Nishant Fafalia, board member, has subscribed for a total of 41,818 shares 
- Øyvind Oanes, Chief Executive Officer, has subscribed for a total of 72,727 shares and 1,250,000 warrants
- Eirik Holtedahl, Chief Financial Officer, has subscribed for a total of 636,363 shares and 800,000 warrants
- Wilhelm B. Thomassen, Chief Operating Officer, has subscribed for a total of 45,454 shares and 800,000 warrants
- Martin Valland, Chief Technology Officer, has subscribed for a total of 36,363 shares and 800,000 warrants
- Enok Hanssen, Chief Commercial Officer, has subscribed for a total of 9,090 shares and 800,000 warrants
- Annika Ramstedt, Chief Credit Risk Officer, has subscribed for a total of 800,000 warrants
