The following primary insiders of Komplett Bank has exercised options to subscribe new shares:

-	Jan Haglund, Chief Strategy Officer, has exercised options to subscribe 25,000 shares in Komplett Bank at a subscription price per share of NOK 1. Following the transaction Jan Haglund owns 494,215 shares and holds options to subscribe 577,332 shares in Komplett Bank.

-	Annika Dolve, Product Director for Loans Sweden and Finland and Director for Collection, has exercised options to subscribe 20,000 shares in Komplett Bank at a subscription price per share of NOK 1. Following the transaction Annika Dolve owns 20,000 shares and holds options to subscribe 94,010 shares in Komplett Bank.

-	Enok Hanssen, Product Director for POS Finance, has exercised options to subscribe 14,704 shares in Komplett Bank at a subscription price per share of NOK 1. Following the transaction Enok Hanssen owns 70,835 shares and holds options to subscribe 63,076 shares in Komplett Bank.

All exercised options were originally granted as part of Komplett Bank's annual share option programme where employees can choose to receive parts of their remuneration as options based on full market value of the options at the time of allotment. 

The board of directors of Komplett Bank has, pursuant to the authorisation granted by the general meeting on 22 March 2017, resolved to increase the company's share capital with NOK 65,704 to NOK 171,446,325 to facilitate the issuance and subscription of 65,704 shares in accordance with the exercising of options as set out above. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
