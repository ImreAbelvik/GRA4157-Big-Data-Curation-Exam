The following primary insider in Komplett Bank ASA has in accordance with the Bank's share option plan exercised stock options as set out below:
 
Bodil Palma Hollingsæter, (Vice Chair of the Board of Directors and Chair of the Audit and Risk Committee), has exercised options to subscribe 41,277 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1.00 plus a variable element equivalent to the social security tax at the date of exercise of NOK 0.94 per share. 
 
Following the transactions Bodil Palma Hollingsæter and close associates hold 41,277 shares and 0 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA. 
 
All allocations of options have been done in accordance with the share option plan, which allows for an employee to opt for receiving options at market price instead of parts of his/her remuneration. The option value is established based on full market value calculated using observed trading prices and Black and Scholes's option pricing model, including an element of social security tax. Each option is issued with Komplett Bank ASA as issuer and provides the holder with a right to subscribe one share. The options are subject to a five years exercise period.
