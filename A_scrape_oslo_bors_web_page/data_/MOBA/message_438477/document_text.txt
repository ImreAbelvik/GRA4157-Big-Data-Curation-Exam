Incentive AS, in its capacity as discretionary investment manager of funds and managed accounts, has on the 10th of November purchased 2.500.000 shares in Komplett Bank ASA for NOK 47.839.055 at a price of NOK 19.14 per share.  Before this transaction, funds and managed accounts under investment management by Incentive AS owned 3.000.000 shares in Komplett Bank ASA and after this transaction funds and managed accounts under investment management by Incentive AS own 5.500.000 shares in Komplett Bank ASA.  Hermann Alexander Kopp, a board member and primary insider in Komplett Bank ASA, is partner, board member and part of the investment team of Incentive AS.
