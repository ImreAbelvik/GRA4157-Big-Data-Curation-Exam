The following primary insiders in Komplett Bank ASA (the “Bank”) have in accordance with the Bank's share option plan exercised stock options as set out below: 

Henning Fagerbakke, Head of Finance, has exercised options to subscribe for 57,946 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1.00 plus a variable element equivalent to the social security tax at the date of exercise of NOK 0.73 per share. Following the transaction Henning Fagerbakke and close associates hold 309,173 shares and 118,014 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA. 

Eivind Bagås, CCO, has exercised options to subscribe for 11,298 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1.00 plus a variable element equivalent to the social security tax at the date of exercise of NOK 0.73 per share. Following the transaction Eivind Bagås and close associates hold 50,917 shares and 215,745 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA. 

Ove Holstangen, CRO, has exercised options to subscribe for 3,542 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1.00 plus a variable element equivalent to the social security tax at the date of exercise of NOK 0.73 per share. Following the transaction Ove Holstangen and close associates hold 10,067 shares and 41,188 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA.

Eirik Holtedahl, CFO, has exercised options to subscribe 55,780 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1.00 plus a variable element equivalent to the social security tax at the date of exercise of NOK 0.73 per share. Also, Eirik Holtedahl has entered into an agreement to sell 55,780 shares to Belair AS, a company fully owned by Eirik Holtedahl¸ for a price of NOK 6.89 per share. The shares will be transferred to Belair AS when they have been issued. Following the transactions Eirik Holtedahl and close associates hold 960,577 shares and 110,130 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA.

This information is considered to be inside information pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation article 7 and is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act. This stock exchange announcement was published by Henning Fagerbakke, Head of Finance, Komplett Bank ASA, on 27 May 2022 at 00:25 CEST.
