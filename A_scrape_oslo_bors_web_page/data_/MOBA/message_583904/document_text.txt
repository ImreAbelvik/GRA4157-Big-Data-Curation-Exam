The following primary insiders in Komplett Bank ASA (the “Bank”) have in accordance with the Bank's share option plan exercised stock options as set out below: 

Annika Ramstedt, Chief Credit Risk Officer, has exercised options to subscribe for 20,298 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1.00 plus a variable element equivalent to the social security tax at the date of exercise of NOK 1.14 per share. Following the transaction Annika Ramstedt and close associates hold 148,422 shares and 306,521 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA. 

Wilhelm B. Thomassen, Chief Operating Officer, has exercised options to subscribe for 21,542 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1.00 plus a variable element equivalent to the social security tax at the date of exercise of NOK 0.59 per share. Following the transaction Wilhelm B. Thomassen and close associates hold 1,903,911 shares and 336,322 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA. 

Jonna Kyllönen, Board member, has exercised options to subscribe for 3,000 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1.00 plus a variable element equivalent to the social security tax at the date of exercise of NOK 0.59 per share. Following the transaction Ove Holstangen and close associates hold 9,343 shares and 17,200 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA.

This information is considered to be inside information pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation article 7 and is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act. This stock exchange announcement was published by Henning Fagerbakke, Head of Finance, Komplett Bank ASA, on 28 February 2023 at 17:52 CET.
