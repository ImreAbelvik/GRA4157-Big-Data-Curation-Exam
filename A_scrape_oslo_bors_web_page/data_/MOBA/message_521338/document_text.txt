The following primary insider in Komplett Bank ASA have in accordance with the Bank's share option plan been allocated options as set out below: 

Ove Holstangen (CRO), has been allocated 6,213 options based on an option value of NOK 6.84. Following the transaction Ove Holstangen and close associates hold 0 shares and 6,213 options in Komplett Bank ASA.

All allocations of options have been done in accordance with the share option plan, which also allows for an employee to opt for receiving options at market price instead of parts of his/her remuneration. The option value is established based on full market value calculated using observed trading prices and Black and Scholes's option pricing model, including an element of social security tax. Each option is issued with Komplett Bank ASA as issuer and provides the holder with a right to subscribe one share. The options are subject to a five years exercise period. Under certain circumstances the holder may be eligible for an extension up to additional 3 years.
