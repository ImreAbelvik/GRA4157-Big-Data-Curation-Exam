Wilhelm B. Thomassen(Director Legal & HR) and through Dingja Invest AS has on the 12 August 2021 sold 158,092 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at an average price of NOK 8.76 per share. Following the transaction, Wilhelm B. Thomassen and close associates hold 1,859,961 shares and 330,470 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA.

Steffen Ryengen(Director Chief Marketing and Information Officer) has on the 12 August 2021 sold 146,445 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at an average price of NOK 8.76 per share. Following the transaction, Steffen Ryengen and close associates hold 1,621,279 shares and 443,734 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA.

This information is considered to be inside information pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation article 7 and is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act. This stock exchange announcement was published by Henning Fagerbakke, CFO, Komplett Bank ASA, on 12 August 2021 at 17:24 CEST.
