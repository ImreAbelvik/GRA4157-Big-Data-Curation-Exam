As part of an internal reorganisation within the Canica group, Komplett AS has on 29 December 2017 sold 8,000,000 shares of Komplett Bank ASA (the "Company" or "Komplett Bank") at a price of NOK 18.90 per share to its parent company, Canica Invest AS. Canica Invest AS is a wholly owned subsidiary of Canica AS.
 
After the transaction, Komplett AS owns 26,157,285 shares, equivalent to approximately 15.26 % of the issued share capital and voting rights in Komplett Bank. 
 
This notification is made pursuant to Section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act on behalf of Komplett AS. Komplett AS is represented on the board of directors of Komplett Bank.

In connection with the IPO of Komplett Bank, Komplett AS agreed to a lock-up of its shares in Komplett Bank for a period of 12 months after the first day of listing of the Komplett Bank's shares on the Oslo Stock Exchange. Komplett AS lock up undertaking with respect to the shares to be sold to Canica Invest AS has been waived by ABG Sundal Collier ASA, Pareto Securities AS and Skandinaviska Enskilda Banken AB (publ), Oslo branch (the "Managers") against similar lock up undertakings from Canica Invest AS in favour of the Managers for the remainder of said 12 months period.
