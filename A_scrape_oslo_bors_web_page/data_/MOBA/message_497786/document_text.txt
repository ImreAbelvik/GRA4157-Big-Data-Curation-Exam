The following primary insiders in Komplett Bank ASA have in accordance with the Bank's share option plan exercised stock options as set out below:

Henning Fagerbakke (Chief Financial Officer), has exercised options to subscribe 44,977 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1.00 plus a variable element equivalent to the social security tax at the date of exercise of NOK 0.64 per share. Following the transactions Henning Fagerbakke and close associates hold 162,638 shares and 6,952 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA. 

Christina Pedersen (Chief Operating Officer), has exercised options to subscribe 173,462 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1.00 plus a variable element equivalent to the social security tax at the date of exercise of NOK 0.64 per share. Following the transactions Christina Pedersen and close associates hold 205,462 shares and 100,686 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA.

Eivind Bagås (Chief Compliance and Risk Control Officer), has exercised options to subscribe 38,396 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1.00 plus a variable element equivalent to the social security tax at the date of exercise of NOK 0.64 per share. Following the transactions Eivind Bagås and close associates hold 39,619 shares and 10,922 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA. 

Silje Christoffersen (Board Member, employee representative), has exercised options to subscribe 5,930 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at a subscription price per share of NOK 1.00 plus a variable element equivalent to the social security tax at the date of exercise of NOK 0.64 per share. Following the transactions Silje Christoffersen and close associates hold 6,530 shares and 6,068 stock options in Komplett Bank ASA. 

All allocations of options have been done in accordance with the share option plan, which allows for an employee to opt for receiving options at market price instead of parts of his/her remuneration. The option value is established based on full market value calculated using observed trading prices and Black and Scholes's option pricing model, including an element of social security tax. Each option is issued with Komplett Bank ASA as issuer and provides the holder with a right to subscribe one share. The options are subject to a five years exercise period.
