Eirik Holtedahl, Product Director Cards and primary insider in Komplett Bank ASA, has on 25 October purchased 150,000 shares in Komplett Bank ASA at an average price per share of NOK 14.75. 

Following the transaction, Eirik Holtedahl owns 640,502 shares, and options to subscribe for 1,029 shares in Komplett Bank ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
