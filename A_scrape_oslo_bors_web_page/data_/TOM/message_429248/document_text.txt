Investment AB Latour has 8 June 2017 bought 349 000 shares 
in TOMRA Systems ASA at an average price of  98,22 
NOK/share. Investment AB Latours holdings in TOMRA Systems 
ASA now amounts to 39 000 000 shares representing 26,3 
percent of the shares.
Investment AB Latour's CEO, Jan Svensson, is represented at 
the TOMRA Systems ASA's Board of directors.
