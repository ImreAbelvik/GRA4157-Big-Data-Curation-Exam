As part of the share purchase program for board of directors in TOMRA Systems
ASA, the following board of directors have purchased shares in TOMRA:

Bodil Sonesson, represented at TOMRA System ASA's board of directors, has today
acquired 550 shares in TOMRA Systems ASA at a price of NOK 276 per share. After
the acquisition Bodil Sonesson holds 550 shares in TOMRA Systems ASA.

Bjørn Matre, represented at TOMRA System ASA's board of directors, has today
acquired 3,686 shares in TOMRA Systems ASA at a price of NOK 277 per share
through his wholly owned company Lille Oslo Eiendom AS. After the acquisition
Bjørn Matre holds 3,686 shares in TOMRA Systems ASA.

Hege Skryseth, represented at TOMRA System ASA's board of directors, has today
acquired 483 shares in TOMRA Systems ASA at a price of NOK 277 per share. After
the acquisition Hege Skryseth holds 483 shares in TOMRA Systems ASA.

Pierre Couderc, represented at TOMRA System ASA's board of directors, has today
acquired 410 shares in TOMRA Systems ASA at a price of NOK 276 per share. After
the acquisition Pierre Couderc holds 910 shares in TOMRA Systems ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
