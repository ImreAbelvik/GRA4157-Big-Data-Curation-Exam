TEF Invest AS, a related party to Bergen Carbon Solutions AS board member Terje Fatnes, has today 1 September 2023 acquired 11,000 shares in Bergen Carbon Solutions AS at a price of NOK 9.5155 per share. 
Following this transaction, TEF Invest AS owns 96,000 shares in Bergen Carbon Solutions AS. Terje Fatnes is also closely related to Awilco AS which owns 4,754,119 shares in Bergen Carbon Solutions AS.
