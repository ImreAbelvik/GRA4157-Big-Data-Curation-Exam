(Bergen, 22 November 2022): Bergen Carbon Solutions AS (BCS, OSE:BCS)

Jon André Løkke, Chairman of the Board of Bergen Carbon Solutions AS has today purchased 50,000 shares in BCS at an average price of NOK 10.4779 per share. 

Reference is made to the resolution made by the Extraordinary General Meeting of Bergen Carbon Solutions AS on 22. November 2022, authorising the Board of Directors, without participation of the Chairman, to issue shares under a share option program.
Jon André Løkke has been granted 300,000 share options. 

After the transaction, Mr. Løkke holds 50,000 shares and 300,000 options in BCS.
