(Bergen, 06. September 2023): Bergen Carbon Solutions AS (BCS, OSE:BCS)  
 
Bodil Holst, member of the Board of Directors of Bergen Carbon Solutions AS, has today acquired 6,800 shares in BCS at an average price of NOK 10.88 per share.  
 
Following this transaction, Holst holds 6,800 shares in BCS.
