(Bergen, 23 November 2022): Bergen Carbon Solutions AS (BCS, OSE:BCS) 

Dag Vikar Skansen, member of the Board of Directors of Bergen Carbon Solutions AS has today purchased 30,000 shares in BCS at an average price of NOK 10.8846 per share. 

After the transaction, Mr. Skansen holds 316,111 shares in BCS.
