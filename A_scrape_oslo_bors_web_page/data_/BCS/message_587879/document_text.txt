(Bergen, 18 April 2023: Bergen Carbon Solutions AS (BCS, OSE: BCS) 

Bergen Carbon Solutions AS has on April 18th granted 1,000,000 stock options to employees at a strike price of NOK 9.62 per share. The strike price for the options granted is based on the volume-weighted average price for the 10 trading days after the publication of the annual report of March 29th, 2023. 
The options vests after 48 months. Each stock option corresponds to one share. 

The following primary insiders of Bergen Carbon Solutions have been awarded stock options as part of the grant: 

• Jeanette Solheim, CHRO: 70,000 options 
• Finn Blydt-Svendsen, COO: 70,000 options
• Karina Brudeseth, CFO: 70,000 options 
• Håvard Husby, CTO: 70,000 options 
• Fredrik Øksnes, CCO: 70,000 options
• Hammad Majeed, CINO: 70,000 options


Primary insiders have previously been awarded a total of 240,000 stock options at a strike price of NOK 47.15 per share.
