Chairman of the board Dag Vikar Skansen has 13th of December 2021 through Skansen Holding Bergen AS purchased 13,000 shares in Bergen Carbon Solutions (BCS)at a price of NOK 50.00. Mr Dag Vikar Skansen controls Skansen Holding bergen AS, which after this transaction holds a total 275 000 of shares in Bergen Carbon Solutions AS.

The shares are sold by primary insider, Chief commercial officer Fredrik Øksnes, which after the transaction holds 0 shares.

“I have been looking for an opportunity to further increase my position in the Company, and since one of our employees are entering into a share option agreement, CCO Fredrik Øksnes felt that is was ok to let his private position go. We are both satisfied, and Mr.Øksnes will continue to take part of the growth in the company true the share option agreement” says Chairman Dag Skansen.


The information included in this announcement is made in accordance with the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and is publicly disclosed in accordance with section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act. The announcement is made by Jan B. Sagmo, CEO, at 14:30:00 CET 13th of December 2021.
