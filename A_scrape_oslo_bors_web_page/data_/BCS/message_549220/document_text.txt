(Oslo, 13 December 2021) Saga Pure ASA (Saga Pure, OSE: SAGA) today announces the sale of one million shares in Bergen Carbon Solutions AS (Bergen Carbon Solutions) to a tier-1 European institutional investor for NOK 50 per share.

“Saga Pure was approached by an investor seeking an equity stake in Bergen Carbon Solutions, and has sold one million shares to an institutional investor on the European mainland, “ says Bjørn Simonsen, Chief Executive Officer of Saga Pure. 

Following the divestment, Saga Pure holds 9,454,000 shares in Bergen Carbon Solutions, corresponding to 25.3% of the company.

Saga Pure ASA is closely associated with Bjørn Simonsen, board member and primary insider in Bergen Carbon Solutions.
