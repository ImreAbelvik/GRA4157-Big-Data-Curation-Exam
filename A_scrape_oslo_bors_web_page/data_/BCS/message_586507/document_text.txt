TEF Invest AS, a related party to Bergen Carbon Solutions AS board member Terje Fatnes, has today 29 March 2023 acquired 16,889 shares in Bergen Carbon Solutions AS at a price of NOK 10,4044 per share. 
Following this transaction, TEF Invest AS owns 85,000 shares in Bergen Carbon Solutions AS. 
Terje Fatnes is also closely related to Awilco AS which owns 4,754,119 shares in Bergen Carbon Solutions AS. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
