Reference is made to Aker BP's ("Aker BP") stock exchange 
announcement today regarding a private placement 
(the "Private Placement") of 22,376,438 new shares
in Aker BP.

BP Global Investments Limited ("BP") has subscribed for,
and been allocated, 6,712,148  new shares in the Private 
Placement.

Following the completion of the share capital increase under 
the Private Placement, BP will own 108,021,449 shares in 
Aker BP, corresponding to 30 per cent of the outstanding 
shares in the company.
