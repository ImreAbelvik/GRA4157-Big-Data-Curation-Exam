Kjell Pedersen, board member in Det norske 
oljeselskapASA, has on 3. August 2016 bought 4000 
shares in Det norske oljeselskap ASA at a price of NOK 
102 per share. The shares were bought through his and 
his wife's wholly owned company, Casi Invest AS. 
Following this transaction, Kjell Pedersen holds 4000 
shares in Det norske oljeselskap ASA.
