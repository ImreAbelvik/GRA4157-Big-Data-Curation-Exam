On November 20 2019, Tycoon Industrier AS, a company controlled by board member Øystein Stray Spetalen, acquired 100 000 shares at average purchase price of NOK 9.97,- per share.

After the transaction Øystein Stray Spetalen and associated companies have a holding of 8 596 285 shares in Vistin Pharma ASA corresponding to 19.4 % of the outstanding shares.
