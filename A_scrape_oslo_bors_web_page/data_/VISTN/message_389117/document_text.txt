On November 10 2015, Strata Marine & Offshore AS, a 
company controlled by board member Øystein Stray 
Spetalen, acquired 114 158 shares at average purchase 
price of NOK 18,- per share


After the transaction Øystein Stray Spetalen and 
associated companies hold 2 995 806 shares in Vistin 
Pharma ASA corresponding to 17.6 % of the outstanding 
shares.
