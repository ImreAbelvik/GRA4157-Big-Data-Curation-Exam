On January 4 2019, Saga Tankers ASA, a company controlled by board member Øystein Stray Spetalen, acquired 768 337 shares at average purchase price of NOK 6.88,- per share.

After the transaction Øystein Stray Spetalen and associated companies have a holding of 8 496 285 shares in Vistin Pharma ASA corresponding to 19.16 % of the outstanding shares.
