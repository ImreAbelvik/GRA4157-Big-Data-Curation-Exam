On October 29 2015, Strata Marine & Offshore AS, a 
company controlled by board member Øystein Stray 
Spetalen, acquired 159 641 shares at average purchase 
price of NOK 17.95,- per share


After the transaction Øystein Stray Spetalen and 
associated companies hold 2 881 648 shares in Vistin 
Pharma ASA corresponding to 16.9 % of the outstanding 
shares.
