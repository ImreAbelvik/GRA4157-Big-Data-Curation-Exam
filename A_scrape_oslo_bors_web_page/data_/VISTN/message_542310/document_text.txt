Tycoon Industrier AS has on 13 September 2021 sold 784,280 shares in Vistin
Pharma ASA at NOK 21.1 per share.

Ferncliff Listed DAI AS has on 13 September 2021 purchased 784,280 shares in
Vistin Pharma ASA at NOK 21.1 per share.

The total number of shares owned by the Ferncliff group (controlled by Øystein
Stray Spetalen) is unchanged after the transactions.

Tycoon Industrier AS and Ferncliff Listed DAI AS are closely associated with
board member Øystein Stray Spetalen.
