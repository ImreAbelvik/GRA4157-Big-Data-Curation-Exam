On March 28, 2023, Fiducia AS, a company controlled by Jon Aksel Torgersen, sold in the open market 95,517 shares in Awilco LNG ASA at a price of NOK 8.16 per share. Following the aforementioned transactions Fiducia AS own a total of 443,158 shares in Awilco LNG ASA, or 0.33% of the registered share capital. Jon Aksel Torgersen is a member of the board of directors of Awilco LNG ASA.
