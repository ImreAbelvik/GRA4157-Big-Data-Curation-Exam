Fiducia AS, a company controlled by Jon Aksel Torgersen, member of the board of Awilco LNG ASA, has on June 30, 2021 purchased in the open market 150,000 shares in Awilco LNG ASA  at a price of NOK 2.1405 per share. After this transaction Fiducia AS own a total of 577,564 shares in Awilco LNG ASA, or 0.44% of the registered share capital.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the market abuse regulation article 19 and 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
