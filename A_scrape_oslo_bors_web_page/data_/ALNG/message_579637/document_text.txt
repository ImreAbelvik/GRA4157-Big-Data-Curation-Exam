On January 4, 2023, Fiducia AS, a company controlled by Jon Aksel Torgersen, bought in the open market 150,000 shares in Awilco LNG ASA at a price of NOK 7.741 per share. Following the aforementioned transactions Fiducia AS own a total of 594,675 shares in Awilco LNG ASA, or 0.45% of the registered share capital. Jon Aksel Torgersen is a member of the board of directors of Awilco LNG ASA.
