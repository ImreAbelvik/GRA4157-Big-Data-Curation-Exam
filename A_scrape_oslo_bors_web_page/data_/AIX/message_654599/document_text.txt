Godthåb Holding AS has today bought 105.000  shares in Ayfie Group AS at 6,0000 NOK.

With this purchase Godthåb Holding AS currently owns 1.386.943 shares representing 5,55  %
of the outstanding shares.

Andreas Akselsen is a shareholder in Godthåb Holding AS and represented as board member of Ayfie AS.
