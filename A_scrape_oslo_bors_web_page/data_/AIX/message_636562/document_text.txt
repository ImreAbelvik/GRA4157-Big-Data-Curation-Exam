On 30 December 2024/14th January 2025, a demerger was completed in Mertoun Capital AS, a close associate of Jostein Devold, member of the board of directors of Ayfie International AS (the "Company"), through a resolution in the Company's general meeting. In this connection, Mertoun Capital AS has disposed of 992.250 shares in the Company as consideration to the assignee company in the demerger. Following the demerger, Jostein Devold holds a total of 82.264 shares in the Company, equal to approximately 0,39% of the outstanding shares in the Company, through Mertoun Capital AS.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
