Jan Erik Kjerpeseth, managing director at Sparebanken Vest, has 
on 30 November 2015 purchased 6,294 subscription rights (SVEG 
T) in Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a price of NOK 6,18 per 
subscription right.  
 
The new holding, including 18,277 allocated subscription 
rights, comprises 26,777 SVEG T.
