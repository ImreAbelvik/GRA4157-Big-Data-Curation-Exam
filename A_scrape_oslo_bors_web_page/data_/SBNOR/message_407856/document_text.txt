Sparebanken Vest has today purchased 6,000 equity 
certificates (SVEG) at an average price of NOK 36,525.

The bank's new total holding after the purchase is 8,721 
equity cerificates (SVEG).

Upon purchase, it was revealed that the bank through an 
oversight has not reported sales of 4,857 SVEG at NOK 34,0 
on 17.06.2016, so that the bank's holding before today's 
purchase was 2,721 SVEG.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements  
pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading  
Act
