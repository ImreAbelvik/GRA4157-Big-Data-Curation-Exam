Dobbel EMM AS, controlled by board member Magne Morken
of the Board of Directors of  Sparebanken Vest, has on 
23 of November 2017 purchased 1000 Equity Certificates 
(EC) issued by Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a  price of  
NOK 53,50 per certificate.      
   
The new holding for Magne Morken, including Dobbel EMM AS controlled by Magne Morken, comprises 1,000 SVEG.
