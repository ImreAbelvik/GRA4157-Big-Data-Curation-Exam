Today, employees of Sparebanken Vest have purchased 
and been allocated 57,510 equity certificates in 
Sparebanken Vest(SVEG) at a price of NOK 46,30. 
 
The bank's holding after the sell-down is 11,248 
equity certificates. 
 
In total, 355 employees subscribed for 162 equity 
certificates each, and the purchase price for each 
employee amounted to NOK 7,500. 
 
The allocation takes place as part of a discount 
arrangement for all the bank's employees. The discount 
is 20% of the purchase price.
