Sparebanken Vest has today sold Equity certificates to 
employees in Sparebanken Vest and Eiendomsmegler Vest.

In total, 16 employees subscribed for 158 Equity 
certificates each, and the purchase price amounted to 
NOK 7,500. 

The allocation takes part of a discounted scheme to 
all employees of the bank and its subsidiaries. The 
discount amounts to 20% of the purchase price.

Total equity certificates in this transaction, 2528 
(SVEG) at NOK 47.46.

No insiders subscribed.

Bank's new total holdings after the sale is 8.720 SVEG
