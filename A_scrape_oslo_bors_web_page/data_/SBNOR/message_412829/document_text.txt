Ahb Invest AS, controlled by board member Arild Bødal 
of the Board of Directors of  Sparebanken Vest, has on 
3 of November 2016 purchased 361 Equity Certificates 
(EC) issued by Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a  price of 
NOK 45,7 per certificate.     
  
The new holding, including Ahb Invest AS controlled by 
Arild Bødal, comprises 8,361 SVEG.
