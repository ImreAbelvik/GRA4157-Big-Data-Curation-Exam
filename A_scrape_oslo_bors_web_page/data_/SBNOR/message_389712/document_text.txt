Ahb Invest AS, controlled by board member Arild Bødal of the 
Board of Directors of  Sparebanken Vest, has on  18 November 
2015 purchased 8,000  Equity Certificates (EC) issued by 
Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a  price of NOK 44,90 per 
certificate.    
 
The new holding, including Ahb Invest AS controlled by Arild 
Bødal, comprises 8,000 SVEG.
