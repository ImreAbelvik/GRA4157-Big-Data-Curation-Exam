Reference is made to stock exchange notice published by Sparebanken Vest 29 november 2018 regarding a program for employees for acquisition of equity certificates. 

Sparebanken Vest received acceptances for acquisition of a total of 257,091 equity certificates, and changes from previous notification do not affect primary insiders. The equity certificates have been allocated from the bank’s own holding of certificates. Sparebanken Vest’s own holding of equity certificates is after this 87,084.
