Trygve Bruvik, chairman of the Board of Directors of Sparebanken Vest, has on 04.09.2018, purchased 2,000 equity certificates issued by Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a price of NOK 52.80 per equity certificate. 
 
The new holding, including Havna AS controlled by Trygve Bruvik, comprises 122,567 SVEG.
