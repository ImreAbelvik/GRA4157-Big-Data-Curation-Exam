AHB Invest AS, controlled by Arild Bødal chairman of the Board of Directors of Sparebanken Vest, has on 26 August 2019 purchased 4940 Equity Certificates (EC) issued by Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a price of NOK 51.80 per certificate. The new holding for Arild Bødal, including AHB Invest AS controlled by Arild Bødal, comprises 56299 SVEG.
