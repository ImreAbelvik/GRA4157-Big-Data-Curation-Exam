Trygve Bruvik, chairman of the Board of Directors of 
Sparebanken Vest, has on the 1st of September 2016 
purchased 1,500 Equity Certificates (EC) issued by 
Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a price of NOK 38,50,- per 
certificate. The new holding, including Havna AS 
controlled by Trygve Bruvik, comprises 113,393 SVEG.
