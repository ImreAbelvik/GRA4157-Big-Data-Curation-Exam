Magne Morken, deputy chairman of the board of directors and primary insider in Sparebanken Vest has today, 28 November 2022, acquired 721 equity capital certificates in Sparebanken Vest at a price of NOK 87.61 per equity capital certificate. The acquisition is conducted through Morken's wholly-owned company Dobbel Emm AS.

Updated shareholding after the transaction is 10.000 equity capital certificates.
