Dobbel EMM AS, controlled by Magne Morken deputy chair of the Board of Directors of Sparebanken Vest, has on 16 August 2019 purchased 5000 Equity Certificates (EC) issued by Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a price of NOK 53.62 per certificate. 

The new holding for Magne Morken, including Dobbel EMM AS controlled by Magne Morken, comprises 6077 SVEG.
