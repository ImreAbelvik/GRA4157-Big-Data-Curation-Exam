Reference is made to stock exchange notice published by Sparebanken Vest 22 November 2017 regarding adoption of a program for employees for acquisition of equity certificates (the «SVEG Program»). 

Upon expiry of the offer period in the SVEG Program on 15 December 2017, Sparebanken Vest has received acceptances for acquisition of a total of 196,180 equity certificates. The price to be paid per equity certificate is NOK 37.3. The price is based on the average closing price per day in the offer period, taking into consideration the cost for a lock-up period of 36 months and a discount.   

The offer period in the bank's ordinary program (the «Ordinary Program») has run parallel to the offer period in the SVEG Program. Upon expiry of the offer period in the Ordinary Program, Sparebanken Vest has received acceptances for acquisition of a total of 117,177 equity certificates. The price to be paid per equity certificate in this program is NOK 42,6. The price is based on the average closing price per day in the offer period, and a discount of 20 per cent in accordance with applicable provisions in the Norwegian Taxation Act. 

Following the above, Sparebanken Vest has received acceptances for acquisition of a total of 313,357 equity certificates.
