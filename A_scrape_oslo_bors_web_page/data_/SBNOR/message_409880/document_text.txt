Trygve Bruvik, chairman of the Board of Directors of  
Sparebanken Vest, has on the 21st of September 2016  
purchased 1,500 Equity Certificates (EC) issued by  
Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a price of NOK 40,80,- per  
certificate. 

The new holding, including Havna AS controlled by Trygve 
Bruvik, comprises 114,893 SVEG.
