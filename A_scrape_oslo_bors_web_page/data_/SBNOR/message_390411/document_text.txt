Trygve Bruvik, chairman of the Board of Directors of 
Sparebanken Vest, has subscribed for 20.000 new equity 
certificates (EC) in Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) in Sparebanken 
Vest's ongoing rights issue. In addition, Havna AS, a 
company controlled by Bruvik, has subscribed for 35,000 new 
equity certificates in the ongoing rights issue.
 
The subscriptions were made 27 November 2015. The equity 
certificates are subscribed for by exercise of subscription 
rights and on the terms applicable to the rights issue.  
 
The subscription period for the rights issue is from 24 
November 2015 and expires 8 December 2015. The subscription 
price in the rights issue is NOK 27.50 per new equity 
certificate. 
 
Allocation in the rights issue is expected made 11 December 
2015.
