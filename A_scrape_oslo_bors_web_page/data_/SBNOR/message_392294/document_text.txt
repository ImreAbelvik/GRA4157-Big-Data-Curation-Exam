Today, employees of Sparebanken Vest have purchased and been 
allocated 74,044 equity certificates in Sparebanken Vest 
(SVEG) at a price of NOK 35,00.

The bank's holding after the sell-down is 1,254 
certificates.

In total, 346 employees subscribed for 214 certificates 
each, and the purchase price for each employee amounted to 
NOK 7,500.

The allocation takes place as part of a discount arrangement 
for all the bank's employees. The discount is 20% of the 
purchase price. The settlement date is 28 December 2015.
