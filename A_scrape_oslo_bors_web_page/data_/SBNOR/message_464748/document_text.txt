Sparebanken Vest: Mandatory notification of trade

Reference is made to stock exchange notice published by Sparebanken Vest 24 october 2018 regarding a program for employees for acquisition of equity certificates (the «SVEG Program»). 

Upon expiry of the offer period in the SVEG Program on 29 November 2018, Sparebanken Vest has received acceptances for acquisition of a total of 165,059 equity certificates. The price to be paid per equity certificate is NOK 36.6. The price is based on the average closing price per day in the offer period, taking into consideration the cost for a lock-up period of 36 months and a discount.   

The offer period in the bank’s ordinary savings program (the «Savings Program») has run in parallel to the offer period in the SVEG Program. Upon expiry of the offer period in the Savings Program, Sparebanken Vest has received acceptances for acquisition of a total of 94,423 equity certificates. The price to be paid per equity certificate in this program is NOK 41.8. The price is based on the average closing price per day in the offer period, and a discount of 20 per cent in accordance with applicable provisions in the Norwegian Taxation Act. 

Following the above, Sparebanken Vest has received acceptances for acquisition of a total of 259,482 equity certificates. The equity certificates have been allocated from the bank’s own holding of certificates. Sparebanken Vest’s own holding of equity certificates is after this 84,693.
