Sparebanken Vest: Mandatory notification of trade

Reference is made to stock exchange notice of 14 November 2019 regarding commencement of bookbuilding period and application period in Sparebankstiftelsen Sparebanken Vest’s offer of equity capital certificates in Sparebanken Vest (the "Offering") as described in the Prospectus (the "Prospectus") published on 13 November 2019 and available at the Bank’s and the managers webpages (www.spv.no, www.paretosec.com, www.swedbank.no and www.norne.no). Definitions used in this stock exchange announcement shall have the same meaning as corresponding definitions used in the Prospectus.

As described in the Prospectus, Sparebanken Vest has on 21 November 2019 applied for Offer ECCs in the Institutional Offering at a value of NOK 18 405 000. Pursuant to the sales agreement between the Foundation, Sparebanken Vest and the Managers as of 13 November 2019 ("the Sales Agreement"), the bank is guaranteed allocation in order to deliver ECCs to employees in the SVEG Programme and the Savings Programme as defined in stock exchange notice of 11 November 2019.

The bank will in addition to the application mentioned above, purchase ECCs from the Foundation for delivery in the Employee Offering pursuant to the Sales Agreement.

A complete notification of Sparebanken Vest's holding of own ECCs will be given once the Offer Price has been set and allocation has been made, expected on or about 21 November 2019.

Bergen, 21 November 2019
