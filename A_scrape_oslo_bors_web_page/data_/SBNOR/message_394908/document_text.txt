Trygve Bruvik, chairman of the Board of Directors of 
Sparebanken Vest, has on the 12 February 2016 
purchased 3000 Equity Certificates (EC) issued by 
Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a price of NOK 32.10,- per 
certificate. The new holding, including Havna AS 
controlled by Trygve Bruvik, comprises 111.893 SVEG.
