Trygve Bruvik, chairman of the Board of Directors of Sparebanken Vest, has on 23.11.2017, purchased 2,000 equity certificates issued by Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a price of NOK 53.50 per equity certificate.

The new holding, including Havna AS controlled by Trygve Bruvik, comprises 116,893 SVEG.
