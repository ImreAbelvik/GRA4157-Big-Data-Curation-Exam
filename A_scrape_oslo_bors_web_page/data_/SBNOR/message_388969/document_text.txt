Trygve Bruvik, chairman of the Board of Directors of 
Sparebanken Vest, has on the 9 November 2015 purchased 1,000 
Equity Certificates (EC) issued by Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a 
price of NOK 45,60 per certificate.   

The new holding, including Havna AS controlled by Trygve 
Bruvik, comprises 56,750 SVEG.
