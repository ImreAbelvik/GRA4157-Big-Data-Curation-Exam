AHB Invest AS, controlled by Arild Bødal chairman of the Board of Directors of Sparebanken Vest, has on 19 August 2019 purchased 18400 Equity Certificates (EC) issued by Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a price of NOK 55.05 per certificate. The new holding for Arild Bødal, including AHB Invest AS controlled by Arild Bødal, comprises 51359 SVEG.
