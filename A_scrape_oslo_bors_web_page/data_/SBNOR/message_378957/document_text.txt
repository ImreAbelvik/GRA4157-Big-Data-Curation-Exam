Trygve Bruvik, chairman of the Board of Directors of 
Sparebanken Vest, has on the 26 May 2015 purchased 
1,000 Equity Certificates (EC) issued by Sparebanken 
Vest (SVEG) at a price of NOK 56,50 per certificate. 
The new holding, including Havna AS controlled by 
Trygve Bruvik, comprises 53,750 SVEG.
