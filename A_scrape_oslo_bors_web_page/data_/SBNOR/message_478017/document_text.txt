Ahb Invest AS, controlled by Arild Bødal chairman of the Board of Directors of 
Sparebanken Vest, has on 27 May 2019 purchased 9000 Equity Certificates (EC) issued by Sparebanken Vest (SVEG) at a price of NOK 52.60 per certificate. The new holding for Arild Bødal, including Ahb Invest AS controlled by Arild Bødal, comprises 32,959 SVEG.
