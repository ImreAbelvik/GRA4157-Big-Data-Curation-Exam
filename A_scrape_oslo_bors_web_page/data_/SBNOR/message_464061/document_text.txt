Reference is made to stock exchange notice published by Sparebanken Vest 24 October 2018 regarding a program for employees for acquisition of equity certificates (the «SVEG Program»). 

Sparebanken Vest has on 20 November 2018 received acceptances for acquisition of equity certificates by primary insiders as indicated in the attached overview. 

The price to be paid per equity certificate will be based on the average closing price per day in the offer period, taking into consideration the cost for a lock-up period of 36 months and a discount. The offer period expires on 29 November 2018.  A complete notification of trade by primary insiders will be provided upon expiry of the offer period.
