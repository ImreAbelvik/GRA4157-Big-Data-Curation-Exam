Freng Invest AS, a company controlled by Bjørn Isaksen, Director of Himalaya
Shipping Ltd., has on March 14, 2022, bought 20 000 shares in Himalaya Shipping
Ltd. at a price of NOK 51,05 per share. Following the transaction, Bjørn Isaksen
and Freng Invest AS owns 20 000 shares, 150 000 share options and a forward
contract to purchase 300,000 shares in Himalaya Shipping Ltd.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the
market abuse regulation article 19 and 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading
Act.
