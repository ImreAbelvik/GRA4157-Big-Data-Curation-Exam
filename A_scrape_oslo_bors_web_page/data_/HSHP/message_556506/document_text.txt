Herman Billung, Contracted CEO of Himalaya Shipping Ltd., has on March 14, 2022,
bought 20 000 shares in Himalaya Shipping Ltd. at a price of NOK 50,61 per
share. Following the transaction, Herman Billung owns 20 000 shares and 100 000
share options in Himalaya Shipping Ltd.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the
market abuse regulation article 19 and 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading
Act.
