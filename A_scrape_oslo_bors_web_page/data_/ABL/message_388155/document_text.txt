


Kristin Brown Ranvik, a related party to Christian Opsahl, CFO of Aqualis ASA
(Aqualis), has on 29 October 2015 acquired 9,000 shares at an average price of
NOK 5,694 per share. Following this transaction Christian Opsahl and related
parties own a total of 1,157,050 shares in Aqualis.



This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
