


Aqualis  Holdco Ltd ("Holdco"), an  employee shareholding vehicle established in
2013 for  Aqualis group employees,  will on 7 November  2016 transfer all of its
2,187,500 shares  (representing 5.03% of the total  number of outstanding number
of  shares)  in  Aqualis  ASA  ("Aqualis")  to  its  employee  shareholders. The
transferred  Aqualis shares are valued at  NOK 2.95 per share, the closing price
of  the  Aqualis  shares  on  4 November  2016. After completion of the transfer
Holdco  will  not  hold  any  shares  in  Aqualis.  Holdco  will subsequently be
voluntarily liquidated.


The following primary insiders receives shares as part of the transfer:

Rodger  Dickson, Group  Marine Director,  receives 122,207 Aqualis shares. After
completion of the transfer Rodger Dickson and related parties will hold 512 068
shares in Aqualis.

Santosh  George,  Group  QHSE  Manager,  receives  122,207 Aqualis shares. After
completion of the transfer Santosh George and related parties will hold 196 963
shares in Aqualis.

Ben  Lazenby, Regional Director  - Middle East,  receives 61,103 Aqualis shares.
After  completion  of  the  transfer  Ben  Lazenby and related parties will hold
134 892 shares in Aqualis.

Cherry  Lenox,  related  party  to  Phil  Lenox, Director Asia Pacific, receives
12,221 Aqualis shares. After completion of the transfer Cherry Lenox and related
parties will hold 897,819 shares in Aqualis.

Stuart  Mill, Group  Commercial Director,  receives 61,103 Aqualis shares. After
completion  of the transfer  Stuart Mill and  related parties will hold 100 768
shares in Aqualis.

Amish   Sanghavi,   Group  Controller,  receives  36,662 Aqualis  shares.  After
completion of the transfer Amish Sanghavi and related parties will hold 176 994
shares in Aqualis.



This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
