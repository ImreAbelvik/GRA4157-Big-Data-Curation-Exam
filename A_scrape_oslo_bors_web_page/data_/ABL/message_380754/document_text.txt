




Oslo, 21 June 2015



Philip Alan Lenox, Director Asia Pacific in Aqualis, acquired 176'654 shares at
an average purchase price of NOK 9.77 per share on 2 June 2015. After this
transaction Philip Alan Lenox, and related parties, hold 878'944 shares in
Aqualis ASA. Immediate notification was not made to Aqualis' management
following the purchase of the shares. Aqualis' management notified Oslo Stock
Exchange immediately after they were informed of the purchase.
