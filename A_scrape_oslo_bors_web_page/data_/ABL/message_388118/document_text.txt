ALSTO Consultancy Ltd, a company controlled by David 
Wells, CEO of Aqualis ASA ("Aqualis"), has on 29 
October 2015 acquired 140,000 shares at a price of NOK 
6 per share. David Wells, and related parties, owns 
after this purchase a total of 786,766 shares in 
Aqualis.

Aman Marine Ltd, a company controlled by Reuben Segal, 
Director Middle East at Aqualis ASA, has on 29 October 
2015 through his company acquired 300,000 shares at a 
price of NOK 6 per share. Reuben Segal, and related 
parties, owns after this purchase a total of 1,263,869 
shares in Aqualis.

Bader Diab, Director of Engineering and North America 
in Aqualis ASA ("Aqualis"), has on 29 October 2015 
acquired 180,000 shares at a price of NOK 6 per share. 
Bader Diab owns after this purchase a total of 
1,001,302 shares in Aqualis.

This information is subject of the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act.
