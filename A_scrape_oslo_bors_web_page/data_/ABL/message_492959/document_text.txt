The following primary insiders have today bought shares in AqualisBraemar ASA (“AqualisBraemar”):

Gross Management AS, a company controlled by Glen Rødland, Chairman, has bought 116,781 shares in Aqualis, at an average price of NOK 3.58 per share. Following the transaction, Rødland and his close associates holds 9,484,777 shares in AqualisBraemar, representing 13.5% of the outstanding share capital.

Haakon Brandrud, Director of Strategy and Corporate Development, has bought 15,200 shares at an average price of NOK 3.58 per share. Following the transaction, he holds 125,200 shares and 135,000 share options in AqualisBraemar.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
