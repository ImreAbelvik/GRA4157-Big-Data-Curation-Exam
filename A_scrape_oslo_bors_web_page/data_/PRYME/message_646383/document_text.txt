Rotterdam, 14 May 2025

Further to Pryme N.V.’s publication made earlier today, 14 May 2025, in respect of the issue of 5,655,652 new shares in its capital, attached please find the notification form regarding the transaction conducted by Taranis Investment Limited, a close associate of Mr. Emmanuel Colombel, a supervisory board member of Pryme N.V., and the notification form regarding the transaction conducted by Circular Plastics Coöperatief U.A., a close associate of Mr. Jan Willem Muller, a supervisory board member of Pryme N.V.

This information is made public with reference to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article 19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
