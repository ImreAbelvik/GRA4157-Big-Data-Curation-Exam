This notification follows on to the notification made on 9 September 2022 relating to the purchase of shares by Circular Plastics Coöperatief U.A. and Stichting Multistrat from OMNIS C.V. The mentioned transaction for the sale and purchase of shares was completed 28 September 2022. OMNIS C.V., a close associate of Mr. J.D. van der Endt, a member of the supervisory board of Pryme N.V., has sold 1,300,000 shares, of which 650,000 shares have been sold to Circular Plastics Coöperatief U.A., a close associate of Mr. J.W. Muller, a member of the supervisory board of Pryme N.V., and 650,000 shares have been sold to Stichting Multistrat, a close associate of Mr. B.R. van Vliet, a managing director of Pryme N.V. The transaction price is Euro 0.575 per share. The required filings are attached to this notice.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article 19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
