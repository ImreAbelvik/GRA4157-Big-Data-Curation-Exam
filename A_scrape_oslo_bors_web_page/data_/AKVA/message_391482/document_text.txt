



Employees of AKVA group ASA ("AKVA group" or the "Company") and its subsidiaries
were invited to order shares in the Company in connection with a share incentive
program (the "Share Incentive Program") announced on 10 September 2015.
155 employees decided to invest in the Company, and will be allocated a total of
122.698 shares.  The price per  share was NOK  27,09, which included a rebate of
20 per  cent from the average price the  Company has paid for shares acquired up
to 27 November 2015 in the share buyback program initiated on 10 September 2015
(being  NOK 33,86 per share). Of  the total consideration for  the shares of NOK
3.323.889, NOK  793.445 is to  be paid  through a  one-time salary  deduction in
January  2016, whereas  NOK  2.530.444 is  to  be  paid  through  monthly salary
deductions  over 12 months starting in January  2016, in line with the statutory
and  regulatory requirements  for such  credit arrangements.  The shares will be
delivered to the participating employees in January 2016.

About AKVA group
AKVA  group  is  a  technology  and  service partner to the aquaculture industry
worldwide.  The company  has 701 employees,  offices in  8 countries and a total
turnover of NOK 1.2 billion in 2014. We are a public listed company operating in
one  of the world's fastest growing industries and supply everything from single
components  to  complete  installations,  both  for  cage farming and land based
aquaculture. AKVA group is recognized as a pioneer and technology leader through
more than 30 years. The Corporate Headquarter is in Bryne, Norway.
