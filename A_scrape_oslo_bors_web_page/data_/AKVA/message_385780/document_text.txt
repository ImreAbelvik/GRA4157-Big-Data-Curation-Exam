On September 18, 2015 AKVA group ASA ("AKVA group" or 
the "Company") purchased 3 000 of its own shares 
under the buyback program announced by the Company on 
September 10, 2015. The shares were purchased at an 
average price of NOK 28,10.
Following this transaction, the Company holds 20 370 
of its own shares.
About AKVA group
AKVA group is a technology and service partner to the 
aquaculture industry worldwide. The company has 701 
employees, offices in 8 countries and a total 
turnover of NOK 1.2 billion in 2014. We are a public 
listed company operating in one of the world's 
fastest growing industries and supply everything from 
single components to complete installations, both for 
cage farming and land based aquaculture. AKVA group 
is recognized as a pioneer and technology leader 
through more than 30 years. The Corporate Headquarter 
is in Bryne, Norway.
