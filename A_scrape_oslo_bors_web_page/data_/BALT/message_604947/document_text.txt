Oslo, 24th November 2023. UAB Baltic Equity (Buyer) - a company controlled by Chairman and primary insider in Baltic Sea Properties AS (BALT), James Andrew Clarke - has acquired 3,000 shares in Baltic Sea Properties AS (BALT) at a price per share of NOK 48.00.
 
UAB Baltic Equity owns upon completion of the transaction, 1,832,721 shares, equal to 27.40 %, in Baltic Sea Properties AS.
