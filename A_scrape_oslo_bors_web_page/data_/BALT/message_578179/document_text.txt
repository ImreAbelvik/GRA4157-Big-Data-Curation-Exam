Oslo, 14th December 2022. UAB Baltic Equity (Buyer) - a company controlled by Chairman and primary insider in Baltic Sea Properties AS (BALT), James Andrew Clarke - has on the 14th of December 2022 acquired 40,000 shares in Baltic Sea Properties AS (BALT) at a price per share of NOK 49.00.
 
UAB Baltic Equity owns upon completion of the transaction, 1,824,721 shares, equal to 27.28 %, in Baltic Sea Properties AS.
