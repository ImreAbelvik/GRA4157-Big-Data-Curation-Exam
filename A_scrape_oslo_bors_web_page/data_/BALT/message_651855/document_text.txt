Oslo, 29th July 2025. Eiendomskapital Norge V AS (Seller) - a company associated with Bjørn Bjøro, board member and primary insider in Baltic Sea Properties AS (BALT) - has sold 5,560 shares in Baltic Sea Properties AS (BALT) at a price per share of NOK 53.00. 

Eiendomskapital Norge V AS owns upon completion of the transaction, 160,000 shares, equal to 1.84 %, in Baltic Sea Properties AS.
