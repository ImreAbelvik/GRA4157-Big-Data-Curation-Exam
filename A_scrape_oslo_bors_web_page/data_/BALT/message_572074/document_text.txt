Oslo, 27th September 2022.
 
UAB Baltic Equity (Buyer) - a company controlled by Chairman and primary insider in Baltic Sea Properties AS (BALT), James Andrew Clarke - has on the 26th of September 2022 acquired 5,000 shares in Baltic Sea Properties AS (BALT) at a price per share of NOK 50.00.
 
UAB Baltic Equity owns upon completion of the transaction, 1,784,721 shares, equal to 26.68 %, in Baltic Sea Properties AS.
