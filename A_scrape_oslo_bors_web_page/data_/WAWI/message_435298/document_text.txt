The Foundation for WW group employees' share acquisitions 
has offered employees the possibility to buy shares in 
Wallenius Wilhelmsen Logistics ASA at a discount, for the 
amount of NOK 7 500 or NOK 15 000.

The deadline for subscribing for shares was today, 22 
September 2017 at 16:30. Number of shares to be acquired 
will depend on the closing share price on 26 September 2017.

Please see the attachment for an overview of the primary 
insiders who have ordered shares in Wallenius Wilhelmsen 
Logistics ASA as part of the share offer.

The purchase price and the number of shares acquired by the 
primary insiders, as well as their updated shareholdings, 
will be notified the Stock Exchange following determination 
of the purchase price.
