Torbjørn Wist, EVP and CFO of Wallenius Wilhelmsen ASA, has on 10 February 2021 acquired 5,000 shares in Wallenius Wilhelmsen ASA, listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange, at an average price of NOK 22.80 per share. Following this transaction, Mr. Wist holds 5,000 shares in Wallenius Wilhelmsen ASA.
