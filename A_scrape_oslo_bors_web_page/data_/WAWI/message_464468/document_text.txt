Share incentive program for employees

Wallenius Wilhelmsen ASA (“WALWIL”) has offered Norwegian based employees the possibility to buy shares in WALWIL at a 20 per cent discount. Shares may be subscribed for NOK 7 500 or NOK 15 000.
 
The deadline for subscribing for shares was today, 26 November 2018 at 16:30. The share purchase price under the program (before discount) has been set at NOK 30.20 per share, equal to todays’ closing price. This means that each employee taking part in the offer will receive 248 or 496 shares, depending on amount subscribed for.
