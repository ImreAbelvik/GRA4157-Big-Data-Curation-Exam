Otovo ASA (the "Company") has awarded 4,810,000 options under the Company’s share option programme that was authorised by the extraordinary general meeting 9 February 2023. The strike price of the options was set at NOK 15.60 corresponding to the close price on 22 February 2023, and the options will vest over three years with 1/3 each year. 

The following primary insiders have been allocated options:

Andreas Thorsheim (CEO): 210,000
Petter Ulset (CFO): 210,000
Simen Fure Jørgensen (CPO): 210,000
Paulina Ackermann (Managing Director): 210,000
Inigo Amoribieta (Managing Director): 210,000
Jean Rosado (Managing Director): 210,000
Fabio Stefanini (Managing Director): 210,000
Lars Ekeland (General Counsel): 120,000
Alejandro Diaz Burgers (board employee representative): 30,000
Julie Orzechowski (board employee representative): 30,000

The primary insider notification form pursuant to the requirements of the Market Abuse Regulation will be published in due course.

The remaining options will be allocated to employees of the Group. Adjustments to the total number of outstanding options will be reported quarterly in the Group’s financial reports.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
