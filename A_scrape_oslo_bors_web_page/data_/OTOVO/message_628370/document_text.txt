The Otovo board of directors has on 23 September 2024 resolved to deliver 117,520 shares to participants in the company’s share purchase program. The program was established on 30 April 2021 and the participants that are still employed after three years receive one retention share for every two shares purchased at the start of the program. The shares delivered are part of the company’s treasury shares and are delivered against no payment. The average amount the participants have paid per share under the program will be NOK 7.80 after the delivery of the retention shares.

The following primary insiders, both members of Otovo’s executive management, will receive retention shares:

- Fabio Stefanini (CRO): 42,735
- Jean Rosado (COO): 42,735

Details of the transactions are attached.
