On 15 February 2023, AxSol AB (“AxSol”), a close associate of primary insider and Otovo board member Johan Bergström, bought 2,474,958 shares in Otovo ASA at an average price of NOK 17.9672. After the transaction, AxSol holds 35,173,663 shares corresponding to 23.64 percent of the outstanding shares in Otovo ASA.

The primary insider notification form is attached to this message.

This information is subject to disclosure under the Norwegian Securities Act section §5-12.
