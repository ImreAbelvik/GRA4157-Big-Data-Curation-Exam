The Otovo board of directors has on 20 December 2021 resolved to issue 733,810 shares under the authorisation given at the company’s general meeting on 16 February 2021, in relation to the company’s share option programme.

The following primary insiders and close associated have subscribed for shares in the issue:

Agder Energi Invest AS (Tor Øystein Repstad, board member): 60,000 shares at average price NOK 3.5875 per share

Obos BBL (Ingunn Andersen Randa, board member): 20,000 shares at average price NOK 4.69 per share

Peter Mellbye, chair of the board: 13,330 shares at average price NOK 4.69 per share.

Details of the primary insider transactions are attached, together with one notification form that was missing in the message published 13 December 2021.
