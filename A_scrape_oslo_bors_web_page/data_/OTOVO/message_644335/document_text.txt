Reference is made to the stock exchange notice on 1 December 2023 regarding the resolution by the extraordinary general meeting (the “EGM”) of Otovo ASA, to grant Otovo’s Board of Directors an authorisation to increase the company’s share capital in connection with the company’s share option programmes, that, as set out in the notice of the EGM, were to be restructured.

On  31 March 2025 the Board of Directors resolved to issue 7,123,998 options under the existing share option programme. Today, the Company has awarded these options to key employees. 

The strike price of the new options was set in line with previous policy at NOK 1.41, corresponding to the volume-weighted average price on the five days following the close of the portfolio transaction on 28 March 2025. The options vest over three years, with one third each year, and first vesting on 1 January 2026.

The following primary insiders have been allocated the following number of new options:

Andreas Thorsheim (CEO): 450,000
Simen Fure Jørgensen (COO): 450,000 
Petter Ulset (CFO): 600,000
Alexander Berson (CMO): 450,000
Fabio Stefanini (CRO): 450,000
Ida Groth (CPO): 450,000

The primary insider notification form pursuant to the requirements of the Market Abuse Regulation are attached.

The remaining options will be allocated to key employees of the Otovo group. 

In addition, the Board of Directors has decided to change the strike price of the existing 7,222,332 options outstanding to NOK 1.41 and prolong the vesting period, to match the structure for the new options issued. All other terms remain unchanged.

In total the Company has 14,346,330 options outstanding including the issuance of new options. Adjustments to the total number of outstanding options will be reported quarterly in the company’s financial reports.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation (EU) 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
