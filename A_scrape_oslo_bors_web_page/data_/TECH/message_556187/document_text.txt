Today, 911,112 shares in Techstep ASA were distributed from Palos Norge AS to
its shareholders, in which Idekapital AS, a company controlled by board member
Anders Brandt, received 152,158 shares.

After this transaction, Anders Brandt controls 1,949,690 shares in Techstep ASA,
corresponding to 0.93% of the total shares outstanding.

Please see the attached form for further information.

The information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the Market
Abuse Regulation article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading
Act.
