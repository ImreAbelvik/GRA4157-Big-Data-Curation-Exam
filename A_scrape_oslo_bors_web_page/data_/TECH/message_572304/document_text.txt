Reference is made to Techstep ASA's ("the Company" or "Techstep") stock exchange
announcement on 29 September 2022 regarding completion of the private placement
of NOK 103 million ("the Private Placement").

In the Private Placement, Datum AS was allocated 21,739,130 new shares in the
Company. Datum AS is owned by the deputy board member Jan Haudemann-Andersen.
Following completion of the Private Placement, Jan-Haudemann Andersen indirectly
holds in aggregate 59,954,776 shares in Techstep, representing 19.67% of the
shares and votes of the Company. The shares are held through Datum AS
(58,354,776 shares) and Datum Vekst AS (1,600,000 shares). Further, Datum AS has
on 29 September 2022 entered into a share lending agreement to lend 19,492,760
shares to Arctic Securities AS and Sparebank 1 Markets AS (the "Managers") to
facilitate DVP settlement in the Private Placement.

Karbon Invest AS has been allocated 21,739,130 shares and Rugz AS has been
allocated 1,739,130 shares in the Private Placement. Following completion of the
Private Placement, chairman Jens Rugseth indirectly holds in aggregate
45,458,104 shares in Techstep, representing 14.91% of the shares and votes of
the Company. The shares are held through Karbon Invest AS (43,718,974 shares)
and Rugz AS (1,739,130 shares). Further, Karbon Invest AS has on 29 September
2022 entered into a share lending agreement to lend 19,492,760 shares to the
Managers to facilitate DVP settlement in the Private Placement.

CEO Børge Astrup was allocated 86,957 shares in the Private Placement through
his company Giraff AS. Following completion of the Private Placement, Børge
Astrup controls 1,455,363 shares in Techstep, representing 0.48% of the shares
and votes of the Company.

COO Fredrik Logenius was allocated 14,347,826 shares through the conversion of
sellers' credit. The shares are held through Stobor Invest AB, in which Fredrik
Logenius owns 50%. Following completion of the Private Placement, Fredrik
Logenius controls 23,817,225, corresponding to 7.82% of the shares and votes of
the Company.
