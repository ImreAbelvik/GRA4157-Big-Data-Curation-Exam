Today, 3,055,555 shares in the Company were distributed from Palos Norge AS to
its shareholders, in which Idekapital AS, a company controlled by Anders Brandt,
member of the board of directors of the Company, received 510,287 shares in the
Company.

Following the transfer from Palos Norge AS to Idekapital AS, Anders Brandt holds
(through Idekapital AS) 1,797,532 shares in the Company, corresponding to 1.1%
of the total number of issued shares in the Company.

This notification is given pursuant to Section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
