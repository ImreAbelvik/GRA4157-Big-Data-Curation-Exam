Cipriano AS, a company wholly-owned by Einar J. Greve, chairman of 
the board of Techstep ASA ("TECH"), has today acquired 1 000 000 
shares in TECH at a share price of NOK 4.065 per share, and will 
thereafter hold 4,651,375 shares in TECH, representing 3.2% of the 
shares and the votes in TECH. In addition, Cipriano AS has, as 
previously announced, the right to acquire 3,000,000 Techstep shares 
from Zono Holding AS. Given exercise of the options Cipriano AS will 
hold 7 651 375 shares, representing 5.2% of the shares and votes in 
in TECH.

This notification is made with reference to section 4-2 and 4-3 of 
the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
