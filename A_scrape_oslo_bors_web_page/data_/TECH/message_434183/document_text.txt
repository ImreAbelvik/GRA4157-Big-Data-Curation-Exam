05 September 2017 -  each of Gaute Engbakk (CEO) and Inge Paulsen 
(COO) in Techstep ASA have today acquired 25,000 and 50,000 shares 
respectively in Techstep ASA at a price of NOK 4,0887 per share. 
Following the transaction, Gaute Engbakk (CEO) owns 579,838 shares and 
3,000,000 share options, and Inge Paulsen (COO) owns 50,000 shares and 
1,000,000 share options in Techstep ASA.

This notification is made with reference to section 4-2 and 4-3 of 
the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
