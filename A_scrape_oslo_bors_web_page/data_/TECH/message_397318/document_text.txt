Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement 
regarding the use of own shares in connection with the share 
consolidation in Birdstep Technology ASA ("BIRD" or the 
"Company") on 11 March 2016. 

In connection with the implementation of the share 
consolidation, the Board of Directors of BIRD has, as 
previously communicated, resolved to use a portion of BIRD's 
own holding of shares to round up existing shareholder's 
number of shares, free of charge. BIRD has used a total of 
192 own shares to round up existing shareholders. Following 
implementation of the share consolidation, BIRD holds a 
total of 1,914 own shares. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to Chapter 4 of the Norwegian Securities Trading 
Act.
