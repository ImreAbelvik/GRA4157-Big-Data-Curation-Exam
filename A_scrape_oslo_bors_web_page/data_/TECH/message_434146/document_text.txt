Middelborg Invest AS, a company wholly-owned by Kristian G. 
Lundkvist (through Middelborg AS), member of the board of 
directors of Techstep ASA ("TECH"), has today acquired 1 000 
000 shares in TECH at a share price of NOK 4.065 per share, 
and will thereafter hold 27,402,421 shares in TECH, 
representing 18.7% of the shares and the votes in TECH. 

This notification is made with reference to section 4-2 of 
the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
