Giraff AS, a company owned 100% by primary insider CEO Børge Astrup, has on 15
February 2022 purchased 286,000 shares in Techstep ASA at a price of NOK 3.4967
per share. After this transaction Børge Astrup controls 1,283,082 shares in
Techstep ASA.

In connection with the financing of the abovementioned purchase, including
purchases made on 6 January 2022, 7 January 2022 and 14 February 2022, Giraff AS
has entered into an agreement to pledge the respective shares, 1,104,686 shares
in total.
