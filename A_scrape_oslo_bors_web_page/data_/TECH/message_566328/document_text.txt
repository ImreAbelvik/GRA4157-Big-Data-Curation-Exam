In accordance with the authorisation granted by the annual general meeting on 21
April 2022, the Board of Directors of Techstep ASA has resolved to grant share
options pursuant to the 2022 incentive program. Under the program, a total of
3,170,648 share options have been granted and distributed among the following
primary insiders:

grant has a holding of a total of 838,519 share options
  · Bartosz Leoszewski (Chief Technology Officer) has been granted 340,648 share
options and following this grant has a holding of a total of 340,648 share
options
  · Sheena Lim (Chief Marketing Officer) has been granted 340,648 share options
and following this grant has a holding of a total of 340,648 share options
  · Erik Haugen (Chief Transformation Officer) has been granted 288,241 share
options and following this grant has a holding of a total of 1,444,967 share
options

and following this grant has a holding of a total of 340,648 share options
  · Gunnar Aasen (Chief Revenue Officer) has been granted 340,648 share options
and following this grant has a holding of a total of 340,648 share options
  · Fredrik Logenius (Chief Operating Officer) has been granted 340,648 share
options and following this grant has a holding of a total of 570,308 share
options
  · Mads Vårdal (Chief Product Officer) has been granted 340,648 share options
and following this grant has a holding of a total of 1,497,374 share options

The share options will become exercisable (vest) with 1/3 yearly after the grant
date and must be exercised within five years. The exercise price is NOK 3.245
per share.
