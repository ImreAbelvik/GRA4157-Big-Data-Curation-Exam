On October 26, 2016, System Integration ApS acquired 370,000 
shares of Atea ASA at an average price of NOK 73.75.

System Integration ApS is indirectly owned and controlled by 
Ib Kunøe, primary insider and the Chairman of the Board of 
Atea ASA.

Following the transaction, System Integration ApS holds 
26,658,510 shares in Atea ASA, representing 
approx. 25.2% of the share capital. Ib Kunøe and close 
associates hold 27,006,210 shares in Atea ASA, 
representing approx. 25.5% of the share capital.

About Atea
Atea is the leading supplier of IT infrastructure and system 
integration in the Nordic and Baltic regions with 6,800 
employees. Atea is present in 89 cities in Norway, Sweden, 
Denmark, Finland, Lithuania, Latvia and Estonia. Atea 
delivers IT products from leading vendors and assists its 
customers with specialist competencies within IT 
infrastructure services. Atea had revenue of approximately 
NOK 28 billion in 2015 and is listed on Oslo Stock Exchange.
