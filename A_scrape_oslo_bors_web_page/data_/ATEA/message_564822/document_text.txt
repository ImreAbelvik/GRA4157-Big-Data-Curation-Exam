On June 13, 2022, Systemintegration ApS, controlled by Ib Kunøe acquired 109,043
shares in Atea ASA at a price of NOK 102.35. Ib Kunøe is primary insider and the
Chairman of the Board of Atea ASA. Following the transaction, Ib Kunøe and close
associates controls 31,858,649 shares in Atea ASA, representing 28.3% of the
share capital.
