shares in Atea ASA at an average price of NOK 161. Ib Kunøe is primary insider
and the Chairman of the Board of Atea ASA. Following the transaction, Ib Kunøe
and close associates controls 29,862,333 shares in Atea ASA, representing 26.6%
of the share capital.
