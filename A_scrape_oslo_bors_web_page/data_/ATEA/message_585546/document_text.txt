On 16 March 2023, key employees in the Atea Group were granted share options in
Atea ASA.

The following primary insiders were granted options (in alphabetic order, by
last name):

Arunas Bartusevicius (Managing Director, Atea Baltics) has been granted 70,000
options vesting on December 15, 2026. Following the grant, Arunas Bartusevicius
holds 280,000 options and 100,194 shares in Atea ASA.

Kathrine Forsberg (Managing Director, Atea Denmark) has been granted 100,000
options vesting on December 15, 2026. Following the grant, Kathrine Forsberg
holds 310,000 options and 0 shares in Atea ASA.

Robert Giori (CFO, Atea Group) has been granted 125,000 options vesting on
December 15, 2026. Following the grant, Robert Giori and close associates hold
493,570 options and 126,787 shares in Atea ASA.

Carl-Johan Hultenheim (Managing Director, Atea Sweden) has been granted 150,000
options vesting on December 15, 2026. Following the grant, Carl-Johan Hultenheim
holds 700,000 options and 14,000 shares in Atea ASA.

Ole Petter Saxrud (Managing Director, Atea Norway) has been granted 100,000
options vesting on December 15, 2026. Following the grant, Ole Petter Saxrud
holds 230,000 options and 1,500 shares in Atea ASA.

Juha Sihvonen (Managing Director, Atea Finland) has been granted 100,000 options
vesting on December 15, 2026. Following the grant, Juha Sihvonen holds 430,000
options and 0 shares in Atea ASA.

Steinar Sønsteby (Chief Executive Officer, Atea Group) has been granted 225,000
options vesting on December 15, 2026. Following the grant, Steinar Sønsteby
holds 1,466,307 options and 125,000 shares in Atea ASA.

Linus Wallin (Managing Director, Atea Sweden) has been granted 100,000 options
vesting on December 15, 2026. Following the grant, Linus Wallin holds 285,000
options and 0 shares in Atea ASA.

The options have a strike price of NOK 115.00 and expire on December 15, 2027.
