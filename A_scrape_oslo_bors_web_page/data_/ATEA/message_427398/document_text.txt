Reference is made to the stock exchange announcements on 28 April 2017 and 11
May 2017 regarding the exercise of employee share options in Atea ASA.  The
shares issued by Atea ASA to employees who exercised share options were
transferred on 12 May 2017.

On 12 May 2017, Chief Financial Officer Robert Giori sold 33,634 shares of Atea
ASA at an average price of NOK 104.34.  Following this transaction, Robert Giori
holds 26,366 shares and 176,667 share options in Atea ASA.  Robert Giori and
close associates hold 28,366 shares and 176,667 share options in Atea ASA.

About Atea
Atea is the leading supplier of IT infrastructure and system integration in the
Nordic and Baltic regions with 6,900 employees. Atea is present in 86 cities in
Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, Lithuania, Latvia and Estonia. Atea delivers
IT products from leading vendors and assists its customers with specialist
competencies within IT infrastructure services. Atea had revenue of
approximately NOK 31 billion in 2016 and is listed on Oslo Stock
Exchange. www.atea.com
