On September 21, 2021, Systemintegration ApS, controlled by Ib Kunøe acquired
111,836 shares in Atea ASA at an average price of NOK 152.56. Ib Kunøe is
primary insider and the Chairman of the Board of Atea ASA. Following the
transaction, Ib Kunøe and close associates controls 29,999,140 shares in Atea
ASA, representing 26.8% of the share capital.
