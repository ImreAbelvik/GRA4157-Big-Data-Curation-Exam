On 8 March 2021, key employees in the Atea Group were granted share options in
Atea ASA.

The following primary insiders were granted options (in alphabetic order, by
last name):

Arunas Bartusevicius (Managing Director, Atea Baltics) has been granted 70,000
options vesting on December 15, 2024. Following the grant, Arunas Bartusevicius
holds 400,000 options and 100,194 shares in Atea ASA.

Kathrine Forsberg (Managing Director, Atea Denmark) has been granted 70,000
options vesting on December 15, 2024. Following the grant, Kathrine Forsberg
holds 152,000 options and 0 shares in Atea ASA.

Robert Giori (CFO, Atea Group) has been granted 125,000 options vesting on
December 15, 2024. Following the grant, Robert Giori and close associates hold
735,000 options and 16,327 shares in Atea ASA.

Carl-Johan Hultenheim (Chief Operating Officer, Atea Group) has been granted
150,000 options vesting on December 15, 2024. Following the grant, Carl-Johan
Hultenheim holds 775,000 options and 14,000 shares in Atea ASA.

Michael Jacobs (Managing Director, Atea Norway) has been granted 100,000 options
vesting on December 15, 2024. Following the grant, Michael Jacobs holds 400,000
options and 0 shares in Atea ASA.

Juha Sihvonen (Managing Director, Atea Finland) has been granted 100,000 options
vesting on December 15, 2024. Following the grant, Juha Sihvonen holds 380,000
options and 0 shares in Atea ASA.

Steinar Sønsteby (Chief Executive Officer, Atea Group) has been granted 225,000
options vesting on December 15, 2024. Following the grant, Steinar Sønsteby and
close associates hold 2,025,000 options and 2,000 shares in Atea ASA.

Linus Wallin (Managing Director, Atea Sweden) has been granted 70,000 options
vesting on December 15, 2024. Following the grant, Linus Wallin holds 115,000
options and 0 shares in Atea ASA.

The options have a strike price of NOK 145.50 and expire on December 15, 2025.
