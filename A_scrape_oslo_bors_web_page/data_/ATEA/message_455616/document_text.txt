Following primary insiders have acquired shares in Atea ASA:

On July 13, 2018, Ib Kunøe acquired 20,000 shares of Atea ASA at an average
price of NOK 110.44. Ib Kunøe is the Chairman of the Board of Atea ASA.
Following the transaction, Ib Kunøe and close associates hold 27,131,443 shares
in Atea ASA, representing 25.2% of the share capital.

On July 13, 2018, Carl-Johan Hultenheim, Country Manager of Atea Sweden,
acquired 4,000 shares of Atea ASA at an average price of NOK 110.60. Following
the transaction, Carl-Johan Hultenheim holds 14,000 shares and 475,000 share
options in Atea ASA.

About Atea
Atea is the leading supplier of IT infrastructure and system integration in the
Nordic and Baltic regions with 7,200 employees. Atea is present in 87 cities in
Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, Lithuania, Latvia and Estonia. Atea delivers
IT products from leading vendors and assists its customers with specialist
competencies within IT infrastructure services. Atea had revenue of
approximately NOK 32 billion in 2017 and is listed on Oslo Stock
Exchange. www.atea.com
