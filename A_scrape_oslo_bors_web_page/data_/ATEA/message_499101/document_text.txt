Atea ASA at an average price of NOK 70.82. Ib Kunøe is primary insider and the
Chairman of the Board of Atea ASA. Following the transaction, Ib Kunøe and close
associates controls 27,934,718 shares in Atea ASA, representing 25.4% of the
share capital.
