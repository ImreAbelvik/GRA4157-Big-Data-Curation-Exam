As announced on January 31, 2017, Lorna Stangeland has taken the position of
Vice President of Supply Chain Management for Atea Group as of March 27.

Through her position as a key employee and member of the Senior Management Group
of Atea, the Board of Directors in Atea ASA has granted her 150,000 share
options in Atea ASA. The options have a strike price of NOK 96.50 and expire 4
years from the grant date. 50,000 options vest 1 year from the grant date,
50,000 options vest 2 years from the grant date and 50,000 options vest 3 years
from the grant date. Following the grant, Lorna Stangeland holds 150,000 options
and 0 shares in Atea ASA.

About Atea

Atea is the leading supplier of IT infrastructure and system integration in the
Nordic and Baltic regions with 6,900 employees. Atea is present in 86 cities in
Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, Lithuania, Latvia and Estonia. Atea delivers
IT products from leading vendors and assists its customers with specialist
competencies within IT infrastructure services. Atea had revenue of
approximately NOK 31 billion in 2016 and is listed on Oslo Stock
Exchange. www.atea.com
