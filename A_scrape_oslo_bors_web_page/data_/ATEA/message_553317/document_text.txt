On February 10, 2022, Systemintegration ApS, controlled by Ib Kunøe acquired
587,000 shares in Atea ASA at a price of NOK 133,90. Ib Kunøe is primary insider
and the Chairman of the Board of Atea ASA. Following the transaction, Ib Kunøe
and close associates controls 30,836,140 shares in Atea ASA, representing 27.5%
of the share capital.
