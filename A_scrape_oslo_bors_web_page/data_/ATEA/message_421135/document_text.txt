Reference is made to the stock exchange announcements on 10 February 2017 and 22
February 2017 regarding the exercise of employee share options in Atea ASA. The
shares issued by Atea ASA to employees who exercised share options were
transferred on 24 February 2017.

Atea ASA at an average price of NOK 90.50. Following this transaction, Robert
Giori holds 10000 shares and 226667 share options in Atea ASA. Robert Giori and
close associates hold 12000 shares and 226667 share options in Atea ASA.

About Atea

Atea is the leading supplier of IT infrastructure and system integration in the
Nordic and Baltic regions with 6,900 employees. Atea is present in 86 cities in
Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, Lithuania, Latvia and Estonia. Atea delivers
IT products from leading vendors and assists its customers with specialist
competencies within IT infrastructure services. Atea had revenue of
approximately NOK 31 billion in 2016 and is listed on Oslo Stock Exchange.
