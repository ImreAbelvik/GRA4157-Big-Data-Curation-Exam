On October 24, 2019, System Integration ApS acquired 25,000 shares in Atea ASA
at an average price of NOK 117.71. System Integration ApS is indirectly owned
and controlled by Ib Kunøe, primary insider and the Chairman of the Board of
Atea ASA. Following the transaction, Ib Kunøe and close associates hold
27,432,548 shares in Atea ASA, representing 25% of the share capital.
