On July 20, 2021, Central European Food Production Company ApS, owned by Sven
Madsen acquired 12,971 shares in Atea ASA at an average price of NOK 160.97.
Sven Madsen is a member of the Board and primary insider in Atea ASA

On July 20, 2021, L1 Holding ApS, owned by Lone Schøtt Kunøe acquired 12,000
shares in Atea ASA at an average price of NOK 160.68.  Lone Schøtt Kunøe is
closely associate with primary insider and the Chairman of the Board of Atea
ASA, Ib Kunøe.

The shares in Atea ASA owned by Central European Food Production Company ApS and
L1 Holding ApS are controlled by primary insider and the Chairman of the Board
of Atea ASA, Ib Kunøe with a voting agreement.

Following the transaction, Ib Kunøe and close associates controls 29,887,304
shares in Atea ASA, representing 26.7% of the share capital.
