About Atea
Atea is the leading supplier of IT infrastructure and system integration in 
the Nordic and Baltic regions with 6,800 employees. Atea is present in 89 
cities in Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, Lithuania, Latvia and Estonia. 
Atea delivers IT products from leading vendors and assists its customers 
with specialist competencies within IT infrastructure services. Atea had 
revenue of approximately NOK 28 billion in 2015 and is listed on Oslo Stock 
Exchange. [http://www.atea.com]
