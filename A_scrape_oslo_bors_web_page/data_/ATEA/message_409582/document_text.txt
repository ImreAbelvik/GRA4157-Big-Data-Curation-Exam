On September 15, 2016, System Integration ApS acquired 
295,000 shares of Atea ASA at an average price of NOK
79.085.

System Integration ApS is indirectly owned and 
controlled by Ib Kunøe, primary insider and the 
Chairman of the Board of Atea ASA.

Additionally, on September 15, 2016, L1 Holding ApS 
acquired 30,000 shares of Atea ASA at an average price of
of NOK 79.085.

L1 Holding ApS is owned and controlled by Lone Kunøe, 
who is CEO of System Integraton ApS.

Following the transaction, System Integration ApS 
holds 26,288,510 shares in Atea ASA, representing 
approx. 25.0% of the share capital. Ib Kunøe and close 
associates hold 26,636,210 shares in Atea ASA, 
representing approx. 25.3% of the share capital.
