On August 6, 2019, System Integration ApS acquired 36,955 shares in Atea ASA at
an average price of NOK 110.604. System Integration ApS is indirectly owned and
controlled by Ib Kunøe, primary insider and the Chairman of the Board of Atea
ASA. Following the transaction, Ib Kunøe and close associates hold 27,407,548
shares in Atea ASA, representing 25% of the share capital.
