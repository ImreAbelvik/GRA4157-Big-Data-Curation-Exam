On September 19, 2022, Systemintegration ApS, controlled by Ib Kunøe acquired
86,496 shares in Atea ASA at a price of NOK 100,90. Ib Kunøe is primary insider
and the Chairman of the Board of Atea ASA. Following the transaction, Ib Kunøe
and close associates controls 31,945,145 shares in Atea ASA, representing 28.4%
of the share capital.
