On March 5, close associates of Chief Executive 
Officer Steinar Sønsteby acquired shares in Atea ASA.

Steinar Sønsteby purchased 2,000 shares of Atea ASA 
on behalf of his two children, Siril and Sondre, 
1,000 shares for each of them. The shares were 
acquired at a price of NOK 86.00.

Following the transaction, Steinar Sønsteby holds 0 
shares and 800,000 share options in Atea ASA.
Following the transaction, Siril Sønsteby holds 
1,000 shares and 0 share options in Atea ASA.
Following the transaction, Sondre Sønsteby holds 
1,000 shares and 0 share options in Atea ASA.

About Atea
Atea is the leading supplier of IT infrastructure in 
the Nordic and Baltic regions with approximately 
6,500 employees. Atea is present in 90 cities in 
Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, Lithuania, Latvia 
and Estonia. Atea delivers IT products from leading 
vendors and assists its customers with specialist
competencies within IT infrastructure services. Atea 
had revenue of approximately NOK 25 billion in 2014 
and is listed on Oslo Stock Exchange.
