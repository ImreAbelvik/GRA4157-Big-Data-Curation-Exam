1. Employees in the Atea Group have exercised 357,800 options.
With reference to the share repurchase program announced on 6 May 2021, Atea
have used 357,800 of its own shares to fulfill the company's obligations under
its employee stock option agreements. Following this transaction, the company
holds 480,200 of its own shares.

2. Options exercised by primary insider 16 July 2021
Robert Giori has exercised 310,000 options in the Company, corresponding to
310,000 shares at the average strike price of NOK 71.32.

3. Transfer of rights and sale of shares
The primary insider above, Robert Giori has chosen to purchase 110,000 of the
underlying shares at an average price of 29.18 per share.  For the remaining
200,000 shares, Robert Giori has transferred rights to receive shares resulting
from the exercise of options to a third party. The sales amount will be finally
determined based on the price subsequently obtained by the third party in the
market. The results of the sales process will be announced in a separate stock
exchange notice when known.

4. New holding
After exercising options, Robert Giori and close associates holds 425,000
options and 126,327 shares in the Company.
