On October 21, 2020, Systemintegration ApS, controlled by Ib Kunøe acquired
161,646 shares in Atea ASA at an average price of NOK 113.18. Ib Kunøe is
primary insider and the Chairman of the Board of Atea ASA. Following the
transaction, Ib Kunøe and close associates controls 28,096,364 shares in Atea
ASA, representing 25.5% of the share capital.
