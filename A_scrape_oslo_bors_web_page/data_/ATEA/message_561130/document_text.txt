On May 4, 2022, Systemintegration ApS, controlled by Ib Kunøe acquired 81,509
shares in Atea ASA at a price of NOK 109,83. Ib Kunøe is primary insider and the
Chairman of the Board of Atea ASA. Following the transaction, Ib Kunøe and close
associates controls 31,043,806 shares in Atea ASA, representing 27.6% of the
share capital.
