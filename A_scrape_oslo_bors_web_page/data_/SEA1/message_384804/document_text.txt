On 2 September 2015, DG-Invest AS a company owned by 
the CFO of Siem Offshore Inc. Dagfinn B. Lie 
subscribed for an additional 608,000 shares in the 
rights issue in Siem Offshore Inc. at a subscription 
price of NOK 1.80 per share. 

Before allocation DG-Invest AS owns 428,161 shares 
and has in total subscribed for 1,110,000 shares in 
the rights issue in Siem Offshore Inc.


On 2 September 2015, SMY Invest AS a company owned 
by the Group Vice President of Siem Offshore Inc. 
Svein Erik Mykland subscribed for an additional 
830,000 shares in the rights issue in Siem Offshore 
Inc. at a subscription price of NOK 1.80 per share.

Before allocation SMY Invest AS owns 240,000 shares 
and has in total subscribed for 1,110,000 shares in 
the rights issue in Siem Offshore Inc.
