George Town, Cayman Islands, 13 June 2017
 
Siem Industries Inc. announces that upon completion of the 
recent subscription period for the Siem Offshore Inc. Rights 
Issue, the Company has subscribed for and will receive 
82,986,357 new Siem Offshore Shares. Upon receipt of the 
shares, the Company will own 782,096,365 shares of Siem 
Offshore, or 83.0%.  
 
Kristian Siem, Eystein Eriksrud and Michael Delouche are 
officers of the Company and Directors of Siem Offshore Inc.
