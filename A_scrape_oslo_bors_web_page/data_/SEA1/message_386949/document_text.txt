13 October 2015 - Reference is made to the stock exchange 
announcement of 6 October 2015 regarding the mandatory offer 
made by Siem Europe S.a r.l. ("Siem Europe" or the 
"Offeror") to purchase all the outstanding shares in Siem 
Offshore Inc. ("Siem Offshore" or the "Company") that are 
not already owned by Siem Europe, at an offer price of 
NOK1.80 per share (the "Offer").

On 12 October 2015, Siem Europe received acceptances for 
1,243,925 shares in Siem Offshore under the Offer. This 
brings the accumulated total of received acceptances since 
the commencement of the offer period to 1,357,780 shares. 
Based on the aggregate number of accumulated total 
acceptances and the 551,833,483 shares already owned by the 
Offeror, the Offeror will, at completion of the Offer, 
control 553,077,408 shares in the Company, representing 
65.7% of all the issued shares of the Company.
The acceptance period of the Offer expires 4 November 2015, 
at 16:30 CET.
