Swordfish AS has sold, and Chief Executive Officer 
Terje Sørensen has acquired, 1,950,000 shares in Siem 
Offshore Inc. on 2 July 2015 at a price of NOK 1.85 
per share.
 
Swordfish AS is a company owned by Terje Sørensen.
 
Following the transaction Terje Sørensen owns 
1,950,000 shares in Siem Offshore Inc. and holds 
3,000,000 options in Siem Offshore Inc.
