This is a duplicate of the release earlier tody on issuer Siem Industries Inc.

On 1 June 2018, Siem Industries Inc. announced its purchase of NOK100,000,000- nominal amount of FRN Siem Offshore Inc. Senior Unsecured Bond Issue 2014/2019, ISIN NO 001 0708670 ("SIOFF02"). The purchase price for this transaction is 87.5625% of the nominal value of the SIOFF02 convertible bonds.  The earlier indicated price of 70.05% was based on the face value of bonds prior to the exchange of regular SIOFF02 bonds for the new SIOFF02 convertible bonds at 80% of face value which exchange was effective on 4 May 2018.
