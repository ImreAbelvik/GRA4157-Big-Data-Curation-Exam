SIEM EUROPE S.À R.L.: PRELIMINARY RESULTS OF MANDATORY OFFER 
FOR SIEM OFFSHORE INC.

NOT FOR DISTRIBUTION IN OR INTO AUSTRALIA, CANADA, JAPAN OR 
ANY OTHER JURISDICTION IN WHICH SUCH DISTRIBUTION OR RELEASE 
WOULD BE UNLAWFUL

Reference is made to the mandatory offer document (the 
"Offer Document") dated 6 October 2015 regarding the 
mandatory offer (the "Mandatory Offer") to acquire all 
issued and outstanding shares (the "Shares") in Siem 
Offshore Inc. ("Siem Offshore") made by Siem Europe S.a r.l. 
("Siem Europe" or the "Offeror").

The acceptance period for the Mandatory Offer expired on 4 
November 2015 at 16:30 (CET). The Offeror has received 
acceptance of the Mandatory Offer for a total of 144,650,932 
Shares, representing approximately 17.2 per cent of the 
outstanding Shares and votes in Siem Offshore. Together with 
the 551,719,628 Shares owned by Siem Europe and its parent 
company Siem Industries Inc. prior to the launch of the 
Mandatory Offer, the Offeror and Siem Industries Inc. now 
owns and holds rights to a total of 696,370,560 Shares, 
representing approximately 82.7 per cent of the Shares and 
voting rights in Siem Offshore. Neither the Offeror nor Siem 
Industries Inc. hold other Shares or rights to Shares.
Please note that the calculation of the number of Shares 
tendered in the Mandatory Offer is preliminary and remains 
subject to potential adjustments through a verification 
process currently being undertaken by Fondsfinans as 
receiving agent for the Mandatory Offer. The final result of 
the Mandatory Offer will be announced once confirmed by the 
receiving agent. 

Settlement of the Mandatory Offer will be made as soon as 
possible, and no later than 11 November 2015. 
The Offeror is considered a primary insider of Siem Offshore 
due to the Offeror being represented on Siem Offshore's 
Board of Directors, cf. the Norwegian Securities Trading Act 
section 4-2 (1). This notice also serves as a mandatory 
notification of trade for acceptances received under the 
Mandatory Offer on 4 November 2015 representing in aggregate 
108,845,346 Shares in Siem Offshore.

Fondsfinans is acting as receiving agent and Advokatfirmaet 
Wiersholm AS is acting as legal advisor to Siem Europe in 
connection with the Mandatory Offer.
