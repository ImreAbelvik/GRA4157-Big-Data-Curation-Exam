28 October 2015 - Reference is made to the stock exchange 
announcement of 6 October 2015 regarding the mandatory offer 
made by Siem Europe S.a r.l. ("Siem Europe" or the 
"Offeror") to purchase all the outstanding shares in Siem 
Offshore Inc. ("Siem Offshore" or the "Company") that are 
not already owned by Siem Europe, at an offer price of 
NOK1.80 per share (the "Offer").

On 27 October 2015, Siem Europe received acceptances for 
1,294,246 shares in Siem Offshore under the Offer. This 
brings 
the accumulated total of received acceptances since the 
commencement of the offer period to 15,103,701 shares. Based 
on the aggregate number of accumulated total acceptances and 
the 566,529,083 already owned by the Offeror, the 
Offeror will, at completion of the Offer, control 
566,823,329 shares in the Company, representing 67.3% of all 
the issued shares of the Company.

The acceptance period of the Offer expires 4 November 2015, 
at 16:30 CET.
