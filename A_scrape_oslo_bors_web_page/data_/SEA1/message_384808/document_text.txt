On 2 September 2015, Siem Europe S.a r.l. subscribed 
to 156,256,793 shares in the Rights Issue conducted 
by Siem Offshore Inc. at a subscription price of 
NOK1.80 per share.
 
Prior to the allocation, Siem Europe S.a r.l. owns 
133,279,421 shares and 156,256,793 subscription 
rights in Siem Offshore Inc.
