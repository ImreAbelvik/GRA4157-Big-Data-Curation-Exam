Siem Industries Inc. ("Siem Industries") has this 30 
September 2015 purchased 1,091,772 shares in Siem 
Offshore Inc. (the "Company") at a price of NOK1.78 
per share. This will increase the beneficial 
shareholding in the Company by Siem Industries and its 
wholly-owned subsidiary, Siem Europe S.a r.l. ("Siem 
Europe"), from 550,627,856 shares, or 65.4% of the 
issued and outstanding shares and votes, to 
551,719,628 shares, or 65.5% of the issued and 
outstanding shares and votes.

As discussed in the stock exchange notice dated 4 
September 2015, Siem Europe will make a mandatory 
offer for the Company at an offer price of NOK1.80 per 
share.

Siem Europe is represented on the Board of Directors 
of Siem Offshore.
