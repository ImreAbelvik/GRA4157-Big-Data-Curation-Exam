Laburnum AS a company owned by the chairman of Siem 
Offshore Inc. Eystein Eriksrud has today subscribed 
to 100,000 shares in the rights issue in Siem 
Offshore Inc. at a subscription price of NOK 1.80 per 
share.

Before allocation Laburnum owns 45,000 shares and 
52758 subscription rights in Siem Offshore Inc.
