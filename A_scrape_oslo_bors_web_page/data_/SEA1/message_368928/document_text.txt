GEORGE TOWN, GRAND CAYMAN, 9 January 2015 - SIEM 
INDUSTRIES INC. announces the transfer of its 
shareholding of 133,279,421 shares of Siem Offshore 
Inc. (OSE Symbol: SIOFF) to its wholly-owned 
subsidiary, Siem Europe S.a r.l. As the parent 
company, Siem Industries remains the beneficial owner 
of these shares. The transfer was priced based on the 
closing market price on 10 December 2014.
