KTHU AS, a related party to Kenneth Thu, CFO in Western Bulk Chartering AS, has today 17 December 2024 acquired 5,379 shares in Western Bulk Chartering AS at a price of NOK 13.5 per share. 

Following this transaction, KTHU AS holds 92,000 shares in Western Bulk Chartering AS.
