KTHU AS, a related party to Kenneth Thu, CFO in Western Bulk Chartering AS, has today 29 December 2023 acquired 10,000 shares in Western Bulk Chartering AS at a price of NOK 25.5553 per share. 

At the same time Kenneth Thu, CFO in Western Bulk Chartering AS, has transferred 8,621 shares in Western Bulk Chartering AS at a price of NOK 25.5553 per share to his private investment company KTHU AS. 

Following these transactions, KTHU AS holds 86,621 shares in Western Bulk Chartering AS and Kenneth Thu holds 0 shares in Western Bulk Chartering AS.  

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act.
