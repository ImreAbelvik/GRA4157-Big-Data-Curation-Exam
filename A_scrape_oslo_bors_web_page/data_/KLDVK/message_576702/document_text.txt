Trondheim, 24 November 2022: AR-Invest AS, a company wholly owned by Asle Rønning, Chairman of the Board in Ice Fish Farm AS (the "Company"), has today bought 13,000 shares in the Company at a price of NOK 24.1782 per share.
Following the transaction, AR-Invest AS holds a total of 32,589 shares in the Company. Asle Rønning holds no other shares in the Company, directly or indirectly.
