Rørvik, 23. November 2020

Reference is made to today’s announcement by NTS ASA ("NTS") under the ticker “NTS” regarding the share purchase agreements with Måsøval Eiendom AS and Måsøval Fishfarm AS (hereof «Måsøval») with regards to shares in Norway Royal Salmon ASA ("NRS") and Havbruksinvest AS ("Havbruksinvest") settled with Ice Fish Farm AS (“ICE”) shares.

Midt-Norsk Havbruk AS ("MNH"), a 100% owned subsidiary of NTS, has today signed two share purchase agreements with Måsøval regarding sale of 30 020 121 shares in ICE, corresponding to 55,59% of the total outstanding shares and voting rights in ICE, receiving (i) 5.573.111 in NRS and (ii) 1.051 shares in Havbruksinvest.

In addition to the shares above, in the event that Ice by 31.12.2022 has farming permits of at least 24 000 tonnes (including existing permits, MNH will receive an additional payment of NOK 150 million.

The Transaction is done at NOK 43.45 per ICE share. Following the Transaction, MNH will no longer hold any shares in ICE.

NTS is represented in the Board of Directors of ICE, through its ownership in MNH, and is therefore obliged, under the Merkur Market reporting requirements paragraph 6.2, to notify the Transaction.
