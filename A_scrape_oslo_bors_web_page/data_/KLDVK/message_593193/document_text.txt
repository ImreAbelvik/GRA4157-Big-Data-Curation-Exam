Sistranda, Norway, 14 June 2020: Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement published by Ice Fish Farm AS("IFISH" or the "Company", OSE ticker code "IFISH") on 31 May 2023 regarding registration of prospectus and commencement of subscription period for a subsequent offering of up to 1,810,536 new shares at a subscription price of NOK 27.60 per share (the "Subsequent Offering"). 

Laxar eignarhaldsfelag ehf., a close associate of board member Adalsteinn Ingolfsson, has today subscribed for and been allocated 1,773,404 new shares in the Subsequent Offering. 

Through his ownership in Laxar eignarhaldsfelag ehf. and Krossey ehf., Adalsteinn Ingolfson will indirectly, together with other shareholders in his closely associated companies, hold in total 17,721,661 shares in the Company, representing a total shareholding in the Company of 14.5%, following completion of the Subsequent Offering.
