20 December 2020 – Áning Ásbru ehf, a company owned by the Manager of Developments of Ice Fish Farm AS, Jonatan Thordarson, sold 10,000 shares in Ice Fish Farm AS on 16 December 2020 at a price per share of NOK 43.5922 and sold 10,000 shares on 17 December 2020 at a price per share of NOK 43.1183. Following the transactions, Áning Ásbru ehf's total shareholding in Ice Fish Farm AS is 892.593 shares.
