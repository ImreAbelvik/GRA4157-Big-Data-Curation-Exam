Eggjahvita ehf., a close associate of Guðmundur Gíslason, CEO of Ice Fish Farm AS (the "Company"), has entered into an agreement regarding a sale of 3,437,119 shares from Eggjahvita ehf. to a company to be established (the "Newco"), corresponding to 2.85%, of the shares in the Company, at a price per share of NOK 28.75. Guðmundur Gíslason will own 50,1% of the shares in Newco.

 

After the transaction, Newco will hold 3,437,119 shares, corresponding to 2.85% of the shares in the Company and Eggjahvita ehf. will hold 5,835,543 shares, corresponding to 4.84% of the shares in the Company.

 

Please see the attached notification forms for further information.

 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to MAR article 19 and Section 5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
