Nordic Aqua Partners AS (Euronext Growth Ticker: NOAP) has been notified that
the following transaction has been completed by a close associate of a primary
insider in Nordic Aqua Partners:

Atle Eide, Chairman of the board of directors in Nordic Aqua Partners AS, has
indirectly bought 20,000 shares in Nordic Aqua Partners through Maringto AS for
a price of NOK 92 per share. After the transaction, Maringto AS holds 157,146
shares.

Please see attached the notification in accordance with Regulation (EU) 596/2014
(MAR) Article 19 and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
