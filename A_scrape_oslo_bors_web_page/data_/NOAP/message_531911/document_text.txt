Ove Nodland, primary insider in Nordic Aqua Partners A/S, has on the 30. of April 2021 purchased 7,600 shares in the Company (NOAP) at a price of NOK 65.4594 per share.
After the transaction, Ove Nodland holds 27,600 shares directly in the Company and 604,700 shares indirectly through Nordic Aqua Partners Holding ApS, in total 632,300 shares. Ove Nodlands direct and indirect shareholding in Nordic Aqua Partners A/S makes up 6,15 % of total shares in Nordic Aqua Partners A/S.
