Ove Nodland, a primary insider in Nordic Aqua Partners A/S, has on the 25 February 2021 purchased 20,000 shares in the Company (NOAP) at a price of NOK 75.5392 per share.
After the transaction, Ove Nodland holds 20,000 shares directly in the Company and 604,700 shares indirectly through Nordic Aqua Partners Holding ApS. Ove Nodlands direct and indirect shareholding in Nordic Aqua Partners A/S makes up 6,1 % of total shares in Nordic Aqua Partners A/S.
