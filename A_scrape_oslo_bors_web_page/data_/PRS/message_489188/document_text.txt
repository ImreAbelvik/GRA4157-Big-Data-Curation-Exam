Ryan Stewart, Chief Commercial Officer, has on 12 November 2019 purchased
45,000 shares in Prosafe SE at an average price of NOK 4.2565 per share.

Following the transaction, Ryan Stewart owns 45,260 shares in Prosafe SE.

Prosafe is a leading owner and operator of semi-submersible accommodation
vessels. The Company is listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange with ticker code PRS.
