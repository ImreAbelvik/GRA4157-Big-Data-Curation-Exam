Gross Management AS, a company owned by Glen O. Rødland, Chair of the Board of
Directors of Prosafe SE, has today acquired 17,500 shares in Prosafe SE at an
average price of NOK 57.9526 per share.

Following this transaction, Gross Management AS owns 228,667 shares in Prosafe
SE.

Prosafe is a leading owner and operator of semi-submersible accommodation
vessels. The company is listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange with ticker code PRS.
For more information, please refer to www.prosafe.com (http://www.prosafe.com)
