


Roger Cornish, Director of the Board of Prosafe SE, has today sold 70 shares in
Prosafe SE at a share price of NOK 11.93.

Following the transaction, Roger Cornish owns no shares in Prosafe SE.

Prosafe is a leading owner and operator of semi-submersible accommodation
vessels. The company is headquartered in Larnaca, Cyprus and listed on the Oslo
Stock Exchange with ticker code PRS. For more information, please refer to
www.prosafe.com
