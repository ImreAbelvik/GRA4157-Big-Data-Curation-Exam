Sjækerhatten AS, a company owned by a close associate of Nina Udnes Tronstad,
member of the Board of Directors in Prosafe SE, has today sold 22,406 shares in
Prosafe SE at an average price of NOK 158.141 per share.

Following this transaction, Sjækerhatten AS owns no shares in Prosafe SE.

Prosafe is a leading owner and operator of semi-submersible accommodation
vessels. The company is listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange with ticker code PRS.
For more information, please refer to www.prosafe.com (http://www.prosafe.com)
