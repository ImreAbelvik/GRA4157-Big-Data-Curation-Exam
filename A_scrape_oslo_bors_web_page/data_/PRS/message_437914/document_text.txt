


Nonito AS, a company wholly owned by Martin Kolnes, Exec. Director Corporate
Accounting, has today sold 600 shares in Prosafe SE at a share price of NOK
14.35.

Following the transaction, Martin Kolnes owns no shares in Prosafe SE.

Prosafe is a leading owner and operator of semi-submersible accommodation
vessels. The company is headquartered in Larnaca, Cyprus and listed on the Oslo
Stock Exchange with ticker code PRS. For more information, please refer to
www.prosafe.com
