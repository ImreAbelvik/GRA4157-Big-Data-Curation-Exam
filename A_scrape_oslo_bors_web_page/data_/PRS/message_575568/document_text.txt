Nina Udnes Tronstad, member of the Board of Directors of Prosafe SE, has today
purchased 6,000 shares in Prosafe SE at an average price of NOK 157.0 per share.

Following this transaction, Nina Udnes Tronstad owns 6,000 shares in Prosafe SE.

Prosafe is a leading owner and operator of semi-submersible accommodation
vessels. The company is listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange with ticker code PRS.
For more information, please refer to www.prosafe.com (http://www.prosafe.com)
