


On 23 September 2016, Frode Ravndal, Executive Director Planning and Control,
has sold 8,381 shares in Prosafe SE at a price of NOK 0.53 per share.

After the transaction, Frode Ravndal owns 0 shares in Prosafe SE

Prosafe is the world's leading owner and operator of semi-submersible
accommodation vessels. The company is headquartered in Larnaca, Cyprus and
listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange with ticker code PRS. For more information,
please refer to www.prosafe.com
