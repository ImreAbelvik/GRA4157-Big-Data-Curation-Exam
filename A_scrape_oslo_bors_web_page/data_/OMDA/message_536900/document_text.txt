Oslo, Norway, 25 June 2021:

In connection with the liquidation of eHealth AS, which until now has been a joint holding company for the shares in CSAM Health Group AS (“CSAM Health Group”) beneficially owned by primary insiders and one minority investor of CSAM Health Group AS, the following share transfers from eHealth AS have now been completed:

2.197.038 shares have been transferred from eHealth AS to Equilibrium AS.
216.813 shares have been transferred from eHealth AS to Ipec AS.

Equilibrium AS is jointly controlled by Sverre Flatby (CEO) and Einar Bonnevie (CFO).
Ipec AS is controlled by external investor Hans Erik Robbestad.

Following the transfer, eHealth AS will be liquidated.

The above transfers do not imply any change in the beneficial ownership to the shares and have consequently been implemented without any consideration.
