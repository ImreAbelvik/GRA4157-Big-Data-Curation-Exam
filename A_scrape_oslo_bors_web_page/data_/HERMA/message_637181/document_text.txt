About Hermana:
Hermana is a royalty business – we offer financing in return from revenue streams. We look for assets in the resource-based Nordic economies, and for interesting intellectual property. We share the risk of declining revenues in return for exposure to interesting assets. Hermana is an owner, manager, investor and developer of services that supplement regular banks and investment businesses. The company can also engage in other investments.
