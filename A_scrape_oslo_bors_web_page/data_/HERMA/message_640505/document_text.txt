Morten Strømgren, SVP Ownership and Investments at Hermana Holding ASA, has purchased 3,986 shares in Hermana Holding ASA today 5 March 2025 at an average price of NOK 10.60 per share.

Following this acquisition, Mr. Strømgren controls 3,986 shares in Hermana Holding ASA, which equals 0.03 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.
