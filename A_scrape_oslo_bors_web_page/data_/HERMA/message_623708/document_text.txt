Stein Bjørnstad, CEO of Hermana Holding ASA, has purchased 30,000 shares in Hermana Holding ASA today 16 July 2024 at an average price of NOK 2.6999 per share. 

Following this acquisition, Stein Bjørnstad controls 45,000 shares in Hermana Holding ASA, which equals 0.05 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.
