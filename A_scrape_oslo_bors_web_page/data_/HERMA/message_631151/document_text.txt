Stein Bjørnstad, CEO of Hermana Holding ASA, has purchased 25,000 shares in Hermana Holding ASA (“Hermana”) today 4 November 2024 at an average price of NOK 2.07 per share.

Following this acquisition, Mr. Bjørnstad controls 100,000 shares in Hermana Holding ASA, which equals 0.11 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.

Bård Olsen, CFO of Hermana Holding ASA, has purchased 20,000 shares in Hermana Holding ASA (“Hermana”) today 4 November 2024 at an average price of NOK 2.1 per share.

Following this acquisition, Mr. Olsen controls 145,000 shares in Hermana Holding ASA, which equals 0.16 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.

Disclosure regulation:
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

About Hermana:
Hermana is a royalty business – we offer financing in return from revenue streams. We look for assets in the resource-based Nordic economies, and for interesting intellectual property. We share the risk of declining revenues in return for exposure to interesting assets. Hermana is an owner, manager, investor and developer of services that supplement regular banks and investment businesses. The company can also engage in other investments.
