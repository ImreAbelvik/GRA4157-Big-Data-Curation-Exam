eBank 1 SR-Bank ASA has on 4 April granted a total of 115 882 own shares to
employees as a part of the bank's incentive scheme. The distribution of 27.952
shares are granted to primary insiders at price of NOK 40,60. 87 930 shares are
granted other employees at a price of NOK 40,60.
