SpareBank 1 SR-Bank ASA has on 3 April granted a total of 102 226 own shares to
employees as a part of the bank's incentive scheme. The distribution of 26 368
shares are granted to primary insiders at price of NOK 64,00. 75 858 shares are
granted other employees at a price of NOK 64,00.

SpareBank 1 SR-Bank has as a consequence of this reduced its holding of own
shares to 206 757 shares. Based on the achievements in 2016 and previous years
