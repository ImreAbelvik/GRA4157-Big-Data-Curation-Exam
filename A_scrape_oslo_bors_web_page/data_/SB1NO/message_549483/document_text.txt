SpareBank 1 SR-Bank ASA has on 15 December 2021 purchased 30 000 own shares at
an average price of NOK 123,8868 Total holdings of own shares after the purchase
is 71 273 shares.

The shares were acquired as part of SpareBank 1 SR-Bank's savings scheme for
employees. For more information about the saving scheme reference is made to the
stock exchange notice dated 10 December 2020, and the stock exchange notice
dated 29 April 2021.

As part of SpareBank 1 SR-Bank ASA savings scheme the company will acquire
additional shares, up to a number of 75 000, within 31 December 2021.
