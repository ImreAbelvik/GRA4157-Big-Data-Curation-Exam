Dag Mejdell, chair of the board of directors in SpareBank 1 SR-Bank ASA, has
today bought 5.101 shares in SpareBank 1 SR-Banks ASA at an average price of NOK
67,42 and 5.000 shares on behalf of his company Nobel Partners at a price of NOK
66,75. Following the transaction Mejdell and related parties own 25.000 shares
in SpareBank 1 SR-Bank ASA.
