Tor Dahle, member of the board of directors in SpareBank 1 SR-Bank and CEO in
SpareBankstiftelsen SR-Bank, has today bought 1,500 shares in SpareBank 1 SR
-Bank ASA at a price of NOK 73.60. New total holding is 38,553 shares. In
addition, Dahle represents 72,419,305 shares on behalf of the
Sparebankstiftelsen SR-Bank.
