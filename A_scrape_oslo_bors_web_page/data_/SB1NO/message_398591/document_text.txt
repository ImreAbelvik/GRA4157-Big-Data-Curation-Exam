SpareBank 1 SR-Bank ASA has on 4 April granted a total of 115 861 own shares to
employees as a part of the bank's incentive scheme. The distribution of 27.952
shares are granted to primary insiders at price of NOK 40,60. 87 909 shares are
granted other employees at a price of NOK 40,60.

SpareBank 1 SR-Bank has as a consequence of this reduced its holding of own
shares to 109 537 shares. Based on the achievements in 2015 and previous years
the following primary insiders have been allocated share, according to the
attached list.
