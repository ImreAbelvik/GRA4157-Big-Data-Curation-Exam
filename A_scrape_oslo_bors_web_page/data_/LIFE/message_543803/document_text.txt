Bergen, Norway 05.10.2021

Reference is made to the announcement published on 5 October 2021 by Lifecare AS (the “Company”) regarding the completion of a private placement (the “Private Placement”).

The following primary insider has been allocated shares in the Private Placement: Hereid Invest AS, a company wholly owned by the Company’s investor relations and controller Kine Hereid, has been allocated 117,647 Offer Shares and will following completion of the Private Placement own 117,647 shares in the Company.
