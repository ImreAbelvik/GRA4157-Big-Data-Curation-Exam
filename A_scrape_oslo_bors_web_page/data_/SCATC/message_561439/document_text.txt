Oslo,6 May 2022: MMH Nysteen Invest AS, a company controlled by board member
Maria Moræus Hanssen and related parties has on 6 May bought 2,750 shares in
Scatec ASA at an average price per share of NOK 90.58. After the transaction MMH
Nysteen Invest AS owns 5,510 shares in Scatec ASA.

Jørgen Kildahl, board member of Scatec ASA, has on 6 May bought 1,000 shares in
Scatec ASA at an average price of NOK 91.30. After the transaction, Jørgen
Kildahl owns 3,000 shares in Scatec ASA.

Attached is the notifications of the transaction in accordance with the Market
Abuse Regulation Article 19.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the
Market Abuse Regulation article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading act.
