


Oslo, October 24, 2018: Chief Executive Officer, Raymond Carlsen has today
bought 10,000 shares in Scatec Solar ASA at the price of NOK 56.73 per share.
Mr. Carlsen, as CEO, is a primary insider in Scatec Solar ASA.

After the transaction, Mr. Carlsen holds 2,765,760 shares in Scatec Solar ASA
privately and through Argentos AS, representing 2.44% of the issued shares in
Scatec Solar ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
