


Oslo, October 29, 2015

Buzz  Aldrin AS, a company controlled by Executive Vice President Roar Haugland,
has  today sold 92 289 shares in Scatec Solar ASA, at an average price per share
of NOK 43,0655.

After  the transaction and as of the  date hereof, Executive Vice President Roar
Haugland,  together  with  consolidated  parties,  owns 385 735 shares in Scatec
Solar ASA.

This information is subject to disclosure pursuant to the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act section 4-2.
