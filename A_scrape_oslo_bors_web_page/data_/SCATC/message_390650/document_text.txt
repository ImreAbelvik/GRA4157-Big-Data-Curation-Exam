


Oslo,  December 2, 2015: Scatec Solar Ansatte AS, a company owned 100% by Scatec
AS  ("Scatec"), has today  transferred a total  of 46,710 shares in Scatec Solar
ASA  ("Scatec Solar"  or the  "Company") to  Scatec at  the price  NOK 41.70 per
share.  This transfer does not change  Scatec's direct and indirect ownership in
Scatec  Solar.  Scatec  holds  19,482,339 shares  in  Scatec Solar, representing
approximately 20.8 percent of the outstanding shares in the Company.

Scatec  is represented on the Board of Directors in the Company by John Andersen
(Chairman) and Alf Bjørseth.

This  information is subject to the  disclosure requirements pursuant to section
4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
