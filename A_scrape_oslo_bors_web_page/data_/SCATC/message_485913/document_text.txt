Oslo,  25 September  2019: Reference  is  made  to the announcement published by
Scatec  Solar ASA  ("SSO" or  the "Company")  on 24 September 2019 regarding the
private placement (the "Private Placement").

Argentos,  a company  owned 100% by  Raymond Carlsen,  CEO of  Scatec Solar, was

shares and 141,952 share options.

Gisele Marchand, Board member in Scatec Solar, was allocated 2,586 shares. Total
holding after the transaction is 2,586 shares.
