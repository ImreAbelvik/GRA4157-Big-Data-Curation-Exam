


Oslo, October 23, 2017: Board Member Mari Thjømøe has today bought 2,700 shares
in Scatec Solar ASA at the price of NOK 45.281 kroner per share. Mari Thjømøe is
a primary insider in Scatec Solar ASA.

Ms Thjømøe holds 25,338 shares in Scatec Solar ASA after this transaction.

This information is subject to disclosure pursuant to the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act section 4-2.
