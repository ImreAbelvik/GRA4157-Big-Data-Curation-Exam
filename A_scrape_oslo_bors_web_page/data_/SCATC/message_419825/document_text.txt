


Oslo, February 10, 2017: Buzz  Aldrin AS, a company controlled by Executive Vice
President  Roar Haugland, has  today sold 150,000 shares in Scatec Solar ASA, at
an average price per share of NOK 37.50,-.

After the transaction and as of the date hereof, Executive Vice President Roar
Haugland, together with consolidated parties, owns 235,735 shares in Scatec
Solar ASA.

This information is subject to disclosure pursuant to the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act section 4-2.
