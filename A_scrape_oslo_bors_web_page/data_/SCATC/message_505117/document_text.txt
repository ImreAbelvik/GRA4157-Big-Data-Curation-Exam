Oslo, 11 May 2020: MMH Nysteen Invest AS, a company controlled by Board member
Maria Moræus Hanssen and related parties, has on 8 May 2020 bought 1,500 shares
in Scatec Solar ASA at an average price per share of NOK 166.80. After the
transaction and as of the date hereof, Maria Moræus Hanssen and related parties
own 1,500 shares in Scatec Solar ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
