


Oslo,  19 August 2015 - Board member Alf  Bjørseth has received 39 835 shares in
Scatec Solar ASA. The shares were received after Kanorådet Invest AS distributed
its  shareholding in  Scatec Solar  ASA to  its shareholders.  Alf Bjørseth owns
18.03 % in Kanorådet Invest AS.

Subsequent to the transfer, Alf Bjørseth owns 39 835 shares in Scatec Solar ASA,
corresponding to a shareholding of 0.04%. In addition, Alf Bjørseth owns 51.50 %
of Scatec AS,a company which together with consolidated parties, own 27 482 339
shares in Scatec Solar ASA, corresponding to a shareholding of 29.29 %.

This  information is subject to the  disclosure requirements pursuant to section
4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
