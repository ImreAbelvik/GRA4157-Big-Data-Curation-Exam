


Oslo, October 23, 2017: Board Member Jan Skogseth has today bought 4,360 shares
in Scatec Solar ASA at the price of NOK 46.185 kroner per share. Jan Skogseth is
a primary insider in Scatec Solar ASA.

Mr. Skogseth holds 10,479 shares in Scatec Solar ASA after this transaction.

This information is subject to disclosure pursuant to the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act section 4-2.
