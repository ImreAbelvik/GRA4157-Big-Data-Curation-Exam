


Oslo, 20  August 2015: Board  member Mari  Thjømøe, has,  through her 100% owned
investment  company  ThjømøeKranen  AS,  on  20(th) August 2015 purchased 2,400
shares  in Scatec Solar  ASA. Subsequent to  the share purchase ThjømøeKranen AS
owns  15,557 shares  in  Scatec  Solar  ASA,  corresponding to a shareholding of
0.0165%.

This  information is subject to  the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
