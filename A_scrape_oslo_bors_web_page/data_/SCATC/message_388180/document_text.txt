


Oslo, October 29, 2015

Executive  Vice President & General Counsel  Snorre Valdimarsson, has today sold
29 532 shares in Scatec Solar ASA, at an average price per share of NOK 43,0655.

After  the transaction  and as  of the  date hereof,  Executive Vice President &
General Counsel Snorre Valdimarsson owns 275 220 shares in Scatec Solar ASA.

This information is subject to disclosure pursuant to the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act section 4-2.
