


Oslo,  November  1, 2017: Executive  Vice  President  &  General  Counsel Snorre
Valdimarsson,  has today sold 275,000 shares in Scatec Solar ASA, at a price per
share of NOK 45.

After  the transaction  and as  of the  date hereof,  Executive Vice President &
General  Counsel Snorre Valdimarsson  owns 67,261 share options  in Scatec Solar
ASA.

The  reason for the sale of shares is to finance tax, repayment of debt, as well
as finance the acquisition of two properties.

This  information is subject to disclosure  pursuant to the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act section 4-2.
