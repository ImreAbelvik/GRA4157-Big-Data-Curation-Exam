Oslo, November 1, 2018: Board Member John Giverholt has today bought 4,000
shares in Scatec Solar ASA at the price of NOK 58.41 per share. John Giverholt
is a primary insider in Scatec Solar ASA.

Mr. Giverholt holds 4,000 shares in Scatec Solar ASA after this transaction.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
