Kjetil Grønskag, Chairman and CEO of RomReal Ltd, has today Friday
21 December 2018 purchased 83,912 shares at a price of NOK 2.20 per share in
RomReal Ltd.

Following the trade, Kjetil Grønskag controls privately and through holding
companies 4,414,629 shares in RomReal.



This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
