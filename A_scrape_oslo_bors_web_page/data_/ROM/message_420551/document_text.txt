


Kjetil Grønskag, Chairman and CEO of RomReal Ltd, has today  Friday 17 February
2017 purchased 150,000 shares at a price of NOK 2.90 per share in RomReal Ltd.

Following the trade, Kjetil Grønskag controls privately and through holding
companies 4,288,179 shares in RomReal.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
