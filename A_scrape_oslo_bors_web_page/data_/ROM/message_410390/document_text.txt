


Kjetil Grønskag, Chairman and CEO of RomReal Ltd, has today purchased 38,142
shares at a price of NOK 1.89 per share in RomReal Ltd.

Following the trade, Kjetil Grønskag controls privately and through holding
companies 4,138,179 shares in RomReal, representing 10% of the total
shareholding in RomReal Ltd.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
