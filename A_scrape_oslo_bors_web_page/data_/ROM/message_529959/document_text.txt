Kjetil Gronskag, Chairman and CEO of RomReal Ltd has today 09 April 2021,
purchased 9 shares of RomReal at NOK 1.75 per share in RomReal Ltd.

Following this trade, Kjetil Gronskag controls privately and through holding
companies 4,475,739 shares in RomReal Ltd.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
