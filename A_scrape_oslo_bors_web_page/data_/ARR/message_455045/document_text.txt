An investment consortium, consisting of Torstein Tvenge, Øystein Tvenge and Jan
Opsahl has acquired 2,149,840 shares in Hiddn Solutions ASA (the "Company").
Following the completion of the transaction, the following primary insiders have
increased their shareholdings in the Company:

Dallas Asset Management AS, a company controlled by Jan Opsahl, board member in
the Company has acquired 500,000 shares in the Company. Dallas Asset Management
AS holds 6,197,626 shares equaling 6.76% of the total outstanding shares in the
Company after completion of the transaction.

Intelco Concept AS, represented in the Company's board by chairman Øystein
Tvenge, has acquired 500,000 shares in the Company. Intelco Concept AS holds
11,105,969 shares equaling 12.11% of the total outstanding shares in the Company
after completion of the transaction. Øystein Tvenge also holds 3,732,271 shares
in the Company equaling 4.07% of the total outstanding shares in the Company.
