About Hiddn Solutions ASA
Hiddn Solutions ASA is a public limited company situated in Oslo, Norway, listed
on the Oslo Stock Exchange under the ticker HIDDN.  Hiddn is supplying
impenetrable proprietary hardware-based authentication and encryption products.
Hiddn's encryption product suite offers a distinctly superior level of safety
and ensures that sensitive information stays confidential and unavailable to
unauthorised access, even if the device is lost or stolen. Hiddns products are
currently being used amongst others by Norwegian Armed Forces, national and
Dutch Authorities and on NATO's Northrop Grumman's Global Hawks surveillance
drone. The group is also supplying secure cabinets and physical filing systems.
For more information, please visit www.hiddnsolutions.com
