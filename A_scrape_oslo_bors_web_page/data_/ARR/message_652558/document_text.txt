Company One AS, a company wholly owned by the Chairman of the Board Håkon Reistad Fure, has today acquired 1,084,377 shares in the company at a share price of NOK0.6413. 

Following this transaction, Company One AS holds a total of 34,693,513 shares representing approximately 7.2% of the current shares and voting rights outstanding. In addition Company One AS holds 25,000,000 warrants granting the right to acquire further shares, representing a further 5.2% of the currently outstanding shares.
