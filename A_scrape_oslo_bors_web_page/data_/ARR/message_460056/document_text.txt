Finn Clausen Gruppen AS, a company owned 100% by Wollebekkgruppen AS, has today acquired 500,000 shares in Hiddn Solutions ASA (the "Company") at a price per share of NOK 1.70.  Wollebekkgruppen AS is owned by the Company's CEO, Carl Espen Wollebekk, and his wife. 

Following the completion of the transaction, Wollebekkgruppen AS owns 4,067,086 shares equaling 4.43% of the total outstanding shares in the Company, of which 500.000 shares are indirectly held through Finn Clausen Gruppen AS.
