


Svein S Jacobsen, chairman of the board in PSI Group ASA, has on the 14.08,
through his fully owned company Sveinja Invest AS, sold 7 311 shares in PSI
Group ASA to an average price of NOK 10.00. New holding is 442 689 shares, 1.0 %
of the outstanding shares.
