Ann  Merete Stabell,  Group controller  in StrongPoint  ASA, has  today acquired
2.800 shares  to an  average price  of NOK  13,05. New holding is 22.800 shares,
0,1 % of the outstanding shares.

This  is a part of the share program for the employees, where the employees gets
20 %  discount for purchase  up to NOK  35.000. The shares was purchased through
Oslo Børs.
