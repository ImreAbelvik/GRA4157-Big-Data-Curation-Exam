About StrongPoint | www.strongpoint.com (http://www.strongpoint.com)

StrongPoint is driving productivity for European retailers by providing
innovative, integrated technology solutions for multi-channel retailing based on
long-standing relationships with customers and partners. StrongPoint offers
best-in-class service and consultancy expertise through its team of 530
employees in Norway, Sweden, the Baltics, France, Germany, Belgium, Spain and
Russia. StrongPoint is headquartered in Rælingen, Norway, and listed on the Oslo
Stock Exchange (ticker: STRONG).
