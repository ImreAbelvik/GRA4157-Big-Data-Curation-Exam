


Roine Gabrielsson, CEO in CashGuard AB, acquired  6 May 2015 30.000 shares
in PSI Group ASA to an average price of NOK 8,70. New holding is 74.108 shares
in PSI Group ASA, 0,2 % of the outstanding shares.

This is a part of the stock program for the executive management, where the
employee gets 20 % taxable discount when buying shares in the company. The
shares were purchased through Oslo Børs.
