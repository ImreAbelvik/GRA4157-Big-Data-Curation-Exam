


Per Herseth, Senior Vice President & Business development, M&A in StrongPoint
ASA, has today acquired 5.000 shares to an average price of NOK 15,40 per share
through the family company Gardd McGillan Co. AS. New holding is 129.043 shares,
0,3 % of the outstanding shares.

Leif Persson, Senior Vice President & Managing Director Label Solutions, has
today acquired 2 800 shares to an average price of NOK 15,30. New holding is
16.950 shares, 0,0 % of the outstanding shares.

This is a part of the stock program for the executive management, where the
employee gets 20 % taxable discount when buying shares in the company. The
shares were purchased through Oslo Børs.
