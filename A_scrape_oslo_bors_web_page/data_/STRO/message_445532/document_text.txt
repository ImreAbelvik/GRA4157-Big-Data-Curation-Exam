


Evaldas Budvilaitis, SVP StrongPoint Technology, has today through his fully
owned company UAB NV Invest, sold 25 098 shares to an average price of NOK 9,78.

After this sale Evaldas Budvilaitis owns 373 463 shares in StrongPoint ASA
through his fully owned company, UAB NV Invest. This represent 0,8 per cent of
the outstanding shares.

The shares were sold through Oslo Børs.
