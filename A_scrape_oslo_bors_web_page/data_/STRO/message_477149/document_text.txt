StrongPoint ASA has today bought 10 970 own shares. The price for the shares was
NOK 9,8139. New holding is 132 799 shares, 0,3 % of the outstanding shares.

The shares were purchased in accordance with the stock exchange notice issued on
26 April 2019 regarding buy-back of 200 000 own shares.
