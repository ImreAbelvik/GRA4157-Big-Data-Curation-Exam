Morthen Johannessen, chairman of the board of StrongPoint ASA, has today
acquired 30.000 shares to an average price of NOK 9,8501 through his fully owned
company Motri AS. New holding is 35.388 shares, 0,1 % of the outstanding shares.

The shares were purchased through Oslo Børs.
