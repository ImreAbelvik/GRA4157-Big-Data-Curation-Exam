


Lars-Åke Köpper, CEO in SQS Security, has today acquired 3.500 shares in PSI
Group ASA to an average price of NOK 9,89. New holding is 7.000 shares in PSI
Group ASA, 0,0 % of the outstanding shares.

This is a part of the stock program for the executive management, where the
employee gets 20 % taxable discount when buying shares in the company. The
shares were purchased through Oslo Børs.
