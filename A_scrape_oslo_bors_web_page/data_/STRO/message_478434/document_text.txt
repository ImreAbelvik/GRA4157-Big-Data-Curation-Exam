The share repurchasing program announced on 26 April 2019 is completed.

In total 126.876 shares were acquired during the share buy-back program,
announced on 26. April 2019 at an average price of 9.8117.

The company has all together 212 108 own shares, 0,5 % of the outstanding
shares.

Further repurchase of shares will be considered by the board during Q3.
