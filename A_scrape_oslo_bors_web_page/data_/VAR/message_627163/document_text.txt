Sandnes, Norway, 5 September 2024: A total of 549 956 shares were purchased on
behalf of employees in Vår Energi ASA (OSE: VAR) participating in the company's
employee share saving programme. The average purchase price was NOK 33.6899 per
share. The transactions are based on savings made in July, August, and September
2024. 86% of Vår Energi employees took part in the share saving for these
months.

As part of the programme, primary insiders in Vår Energi ASA (OSE: VAR)
purchased 17 644 shares at an average price of NOK 33.6899 per share.

Details on individual allocation of shares to primary insiders is included in
the attached notification of trading.

About Vår Energi

Vår Energi is a leading independent upstream oil and gas company on the
Norwegian continental shelf (NCS). We are committed to deliver a better future
through responsible value driven growth based on over 50 years of NCS
operations, a robust and diversified asset portfolio with ongoing development
projects, and a strong exploration track record. Our ambition is to be the
safest operator on the NCS, the partner of choice, an ESG leader with a tangible
plan to reduce emissions from our operations by 50% within 2030.

Vår Energi has around 1400 employees and equity stakes in 43 producing fields.
We have our headquarters outside Stavanger, Norway, with offices in Oslo,
Hammerfest and Florø. To learn more, please visit varenergi.no.
