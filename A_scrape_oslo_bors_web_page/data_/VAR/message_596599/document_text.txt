Sandnes, Norway, 11 August 2023: With reference to the release issued 10 August 2023 regarding shares allocated as part of Vår Energi ASA’s (OSE: VAR) long-term incentive (LTI) program for executive management; The attached form is updated to include shares allocated to board representative and primary insider Hege Susanne Blåsternes, which was omitted in the release issued 10 August 2023.


About Vår Energi
Vår Energi is a leading independent upstream oil and gas company on the Norwegian continental shelf (NCS). We are committed to deliver a better future through responsible value driven growth based on over 50 years of NCS operations, a robust and diversified asset portfolio with ongoing development projects, and a strong exploration track record. Our ambition is to be the safest operator on the NCS, the partner of choice, an ESG leader with a tangible plan to reduce emissions from our operations by 50% within 2030.
Vår Energi has around 1000 employees and equity stakes in 39 fields and the company produced net 208,000 boe per day in first half of 2023. We have our headquarters outside Stavanger, Norway, with offices in Oslo and Hammerfest. To learn more, please visit varenergi.no.
