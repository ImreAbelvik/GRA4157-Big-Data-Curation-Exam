MH Capital AS, a company controlled by Magnus Halvorsen, Chief Executive Officer
of 2020 Bulkers, has on July 8, 2020 bought 50 000 shares in 2020 Bulkers Ltd.
at a price of NOK 52 per share. Following the transaction, Magnus Halvorsen and
MH Capital AS combined owns 1 582 118 shares and 400 000 share options in 2020
Bulkers.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to §4-2
Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
