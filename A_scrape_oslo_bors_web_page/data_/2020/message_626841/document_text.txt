Oslo, Norway, September 2, 2024

2020 Bulkers is pleased to announce that Lars-Christian Svensen has commenced as
Chief Commercial Officer on September 1, 2024, as well as Chief Commercial
Officer of Himalaya Shipping Ltd, under the management agreement between 2020
Bulkers Management AS and Himalaya Shipping Ltd.

The Board of Directors of 2020 Bulkers has approved a long-term incentive plan
which includes a grant of 80,000 share options, each of which gives the holder
the right to purchase one share in the Company at an exercise price of US$16.7
per share. The exercise price will be reduced by any dividends and cash
distributions paid. The share options will vest equally over a three year
period, commencing one year from date of grant and will expire five years after
the grant date.

The options have been granted to employees of the Company, including the
following persons discharging managerial responsibilities (PDMRs) as defined by
the Market Abuse Regulation:

Lars-Christian Svensen (CCO of 2020 Bulkers Management AS), was granted 30,000
share options, following which he holds 30,000 share options in 2020 Bulkers
Ltd.

Vidar Hasund (CFO of 2020 Bulkers Management AS), was granted 20,000 share
options, following which he holds 20,000 share options and 90,000 shares in 2020
Bulkers Ltd.

Following the grants, the total number of outstanding share options in the
Company is 140,000.
