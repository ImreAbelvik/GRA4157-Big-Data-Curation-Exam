Lars-Christian Svensen, Chief Commercial Officer of 2020 Bulkers Ltd., has on
December 3, 2024, bought 4,100 shares in 2020 Bulkers Ltd. at an average price
of NOK 121.7 per share.

Following the transaction, Lars-Christian Svensen owns 4,700 shares and 30,000
share options in 2020 Bulkers Ltd.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the
market abuse regulation article 19 and 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
