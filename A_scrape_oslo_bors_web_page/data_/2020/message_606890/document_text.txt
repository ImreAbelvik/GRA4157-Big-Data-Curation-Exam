Vidar Hasund, Chief Financial Officer of 2020 Bulkers, has on December 18, 2023,
exercised 75,000 share options. The share options had a strike price of US$4.445
per share.

Following the transaction, Vidar Hasund owns 90,000 shares in 2020 Bulkers Ltd.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the
market abuse regulation article 19 and 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading
Act.
