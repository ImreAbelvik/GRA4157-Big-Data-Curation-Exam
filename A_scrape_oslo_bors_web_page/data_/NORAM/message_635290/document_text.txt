Oslo, Norway, December 19, 2024

NorAm Drilling AS today received a trade notification that Marius Furuly, Investor Relations and Strategy Director of the Company has on December 19, 2024 acquired a total of 2,000 shares in NorAm Drilling AS at a price of NOK 30.85 per share. Following the transaction, Marius Furuly holds a total of 18,108 shares in the Company. 

The notification concerning acquisition of shares is attached hereto. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act. 

About NorAm Drilling 
NorAm Drilling owns and operates a portfolio of eleven advanced drilling rigs upgraded to maximize drilling efficiency in the Permian Basin in Texas, which is the largest oil producing region in North America. NorAm Drilling is debt free and has industry leading operating margins. The Company has a full pay-out dividend strategy, targeting frequent cash distributions in a profitable market.
