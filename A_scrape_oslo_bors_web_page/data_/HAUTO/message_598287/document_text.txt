Oslo, 30 August 2023: Damgård Invest AS, a company fully controlled by Andreas Enger, CEO of Höegh Autoliners ASA and primary insider, has on 29 August 2023 bought 142,000 shares in Höegh Autoliners ASA at an average price of 73.70 per share. Damgård AS, a company fully controlled by Andreas Enger, CEO of Höegh Autoliners ASA and primary insider, has simultaneously sold 142,000 shares in Höegh Autoliners ASA at the same price. The total number of shares controlled by Andreas Enger, directly or indirectly, is unchanged at 1,862,135. 

Please refer to the attached filing for further information. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
