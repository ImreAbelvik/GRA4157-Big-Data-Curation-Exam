Oslo, 9 February 2023: Andreas Enger, CEO of Höegh Autoliners ASA and primary insider, has through his wholly owned company Damgård AS, on 9 February bought 10,000 shares in Höegh Autoliners ASA at an average price of NOK 61.00 per share. The number of shares in Höegh Autoliners ASA owned by Andreas Enger, directly or indirectly, after the transaction is 1,850,135. Please refer to the attached filing for further information.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
