Oslo, 8 March 2023: Kjersti Aass, member of the Board of Directors of Höegh Autoliners ASA and primary insider, on 8 March 2023, bought 4,500 shares in Höegh Autoliners ASA at an average price of NOK 64.000 per share. The number of shares in Höegh Autoliners ASA owned by Kjersti Aass, directly or indirectly, after the transaction are 4,500 shares. Please refer to the attached filing for further information.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
