Oslo, 20 September 2022: Leif Höegh & Co Holdings AS, a closely associated entity of Leif O. Høegh (chair of the board of directors), Morten W. Høegh (member of the board of directors) and Martine Vice Holter (member of the board of directors), has today entered into a certain 3 years USD 20m financing facility and in connection therewith pledged its shares in Höegh Autoliners ASA as security. Please see attached notification for further details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
