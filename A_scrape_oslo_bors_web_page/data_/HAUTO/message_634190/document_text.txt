Oslo, 9 December 2024: Damgård Invest AS, a company controlled by Andreas Enger, CEO of Höegh Autoliners ASA and primary insider, has today sold 450 000 shares in Höegh Autoliners ASA at an average price of NOK 114,6351 per share. At the same time , Andreas Enger has sold his entire direct shareholding of 116 584 shares in Höegh Autoliners ASA to Damgård Invest AS at a price of NOK 114,6351 per share

After the transactions, Damgård Invest AS holds 908 719 shares in Höegh Autoliners ASA representing approximately 0,48% of the share capital and votes in Höegh Autoliners ASA.  

Please refer to the attached filing for further information. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
