30.12.2024 08:51:57 CET | Cyviz | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

The Board of Directors of Cyviz AS ("Cyviz" or the "Company") has resolved to
grant share options to executive management and key employees, in accordance
with the authorization granted by the Annual General Meeting on 16 May 2024
regarding share incentive program.

Each option, when exercised, will give the right to acquire one share in the
Company at a strike price of NOK 26.70. The options vest over three years, of
which 1/3 in December 2025, 1/3 in December, 2026 and the last 1/3 in December,
2027.

Of the 595,000 share options, 460,000 have been granted to the following primary
insiders according to the terms described above: 

 * Espen Gylvik, CEO, has been allocated 150,000 share options
 * Ellen Kristine Lome, CPCO, has been allocated 55,000 share options
 * Karl Peter Gombrii, CFO, has been allocated 35,000 share options
 * Javier Colado, CSO, has been allocated 35,000 share options
 * Gøran Hansen, CTO, has been allocated 30,000 share options
 * John Harald Grønningen, EVP Europe, has been allocated 30,000 share options
 * Chris Lewis, EVP North-America, has been allocated 30,000 share options
 * Peter Stewart, EVP BD, Global Alliances & Strategy, has been allocated 30,000
   share options
 * Anas Al-Hawari, EVP ME and APAC, has been allocated 25,000 share options
 * Eirik Simonsen, EVP Business Development, has been allocated 25,000 share
   options
 * Jan Petter Lie, President Europe, has been allocated 15,000 share options

See attached primary insider notification forms in accordance with the Market
Abuse Regulation article 19.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article
19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act.

ABOUT CYVIZ

About Cyviz 

Cyviz is a global technology provider for comprehensive conference and control
rooms as well as command and experience centers. Since 1998, we have created
next level collaboration spaces, assuring inclusive meeting experiences for in
person and remote attendance.

Cyviz serves global enterprises and governments with the highest requirements
for usability, security, decision making and quality. The cross-platform
experience Cyviz delivers to manage and control systems and resources across the
enterprise, makes Cyviz the preferred choice for customers with complex needs.

Find out more on www.cyviz.com - http://www.cyviz.com or visit one of our Cyviz
Experience Centers in Atlanta, Benelux, Dubai, Houston, Jakarta, London, Oslo,
Paris, Riyadh, Singapore, Stavanger, or Washington DC.

Cyviz is listed on Euronext Growth at the Oslo Stock Exchange (ticker: CYVIZ).
