2.5.2025 17:28:02 CEST | Cyviz | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

Cyviz AS has completed an exercise window for vested share options under the
Cyviz share option program 2 for board members, executive management, and key
employees, where in total 49,500 share options have been exercised.

Each exercised share option gives the right to acquire one share in the Company
at a strike price of NOK 21.75. The Company's board of directors will, pursuant
to the authorization granted by the general meeting on 16 May 2024, resolve to
increase the Company's share capital to facilitate the delivery of new shares to
the option holders having exercised their options under the share option
program.

A total of 21,500 share options have been exercised by the following primary
insiders, all at a strike price of NOK 21.75: 

 * Ingeborg Hegstad, Board member, has exercised 15,000 share options
 * Javier Colado, CSO, has exercised 6,500 share options

See the attached primary insider notification forms for more information about
the transactions.

ABOUT CYVIZ

About Cyviz 

Cyviz is a global technology provider for comprehensive conference and control
rooms as well as command and experience centers. Since 1998, we have created
next level collaboration spaces, assuring inclusive meeting experiences for in
person and remote attendance.

Cyviz serves global enterprises and governments with the highest requirements
for usability, security, decision making and quality. The cross-platform
experience Cyviz delivers to manage and control systems and resources across the
enterprise, makes Cyviz the preferred choice for customers with complex needs.

Find out more on www.cyviz.com - http://www.cyviz.com or visit one of our Cyviz
Experience Centers in Atlanta, Benelux, Dubai, Houston, Jakarta, London, Oslo,
Paris, Riyadh, Singapore, Stavanger, or Washington DC.

Cyviz is listed on Euronext Growth at the Oslo Stock Exchange (ticker: CYVIZ).
