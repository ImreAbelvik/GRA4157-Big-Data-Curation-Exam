Helge Singelstad, the Chairman of the Board of Directors in Pelagia Holding AS has today 21 June 2022 purchased 15.000 bonds issued by Pelagia Holding AS. The bonds were purchased at NOK 96,375. The holding after the transaction is 15.000 bonds.

See www.pelagia.com for further information about the company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
