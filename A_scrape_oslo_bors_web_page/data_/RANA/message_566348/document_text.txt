TunComp AS, a company which is wholly owned by Frode Nilsen who is a member of the board of directors of Rana Gruber ASA (the "Company"), has on Friday 1st of July purchased 22,400 shares in the Company at a price of NOK 44.5 per share. 

TunComp AS is considered as a close associate to Frode Nilsen. Following the transaction, TunComp AS owns a total of 92,400 shares in the Company, equal to 0.25% of the Company's share capital.
