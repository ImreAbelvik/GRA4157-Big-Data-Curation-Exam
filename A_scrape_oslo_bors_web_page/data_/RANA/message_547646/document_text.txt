TunComp AS, a company which is wholly owned by Frode Nilsen who is a member of the board of directors of Rana Gruber AS (the "Company"), has today purchased 70,000 shares in the Company at a price of NOK 43.77 per share. TunComp AS is considered as a close associate to Frode Nilsen.
 
Following the transaction, TunComp AS owns a total of 70,000 shares in the Company, equal to 0.19% of the Company's share capital.
