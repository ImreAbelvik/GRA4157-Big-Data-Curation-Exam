Forsa AS, a company controlled by Åge Remøy, has as of 
3 January 2017 sold 263 031 SOFF A shares at an 
average price of NOK 11.77 per share. After the sale, 
Forsa AS holds 5 284 208 SOFF A shares and 6 000 000 
SOFF B  shares, equivalent to approximately 12.7% of 
the issued share capital and approximately 8.1% of the 
voting rights in Solstad Offshore ASA. After the sale, 
Åge Remøy and affiliated companies own in total 12 355 
146 shares, including 5 284 208 SOFF A shares and 7 
070 938 SOFF B shares, equivalent to approximately 
13.9% of the issued share capital and approximately 
8.3% of the voting rights in Solstad Offshore ASA. 
 
This notification is made pursuant to Section 4-2 (1) 
third sentence of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
