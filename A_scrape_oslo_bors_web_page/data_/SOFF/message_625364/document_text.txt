AMSC ASA has today purchased 980,000 shares in Solstad Maritime Holding AS
("SMH") at a price of NOK 21.00 per share.

Following the transaction, AMSC ASA owns a total of 91,422,601 shares in SMH.

The shares in SMH are linked to the shares in Solstad Offshore ASA ("SOFF"), cf.
the Market Abuse Regulation article 19.

AMSC ASA is a person closely associated with SOFF's board member Frank Ove
Reite. Frank Ove Reite also owns 356,509 shares in SOFF and 356,509 shares in
SMH, through Fausken Invest AS.
