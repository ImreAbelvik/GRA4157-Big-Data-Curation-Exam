Forsa AS, a company controlled by Åge Remøy, has on 22 
December 2016 completed a sale of 450,000 SOFF A 
shares at an average price of NOK 11.2285 per share. 
Forsa AS has now also completed the sale of 2 761 SOFF 
A shares at an average price of NOK 12.5 per share, in 
connection with the voluntary offer from Åge Remøy to 
the shareholders in Rem Offshore ASA. After the 
aforementioned sales, Forsa AS holds 5 547 239 SOFF A 
shares and 6 000 000 SOFF B  shares, equivalent to 
approximately 13% of the issued share capital and 
approximately 8.5% of the voting rights in Solstad 
Offshore ASA. Further, Åge Remøy has on 20 December 
2016 through other affiliated companies sold 41  063 
SOFF B shares at an average price of NOK 9.4 per 
share. After the aforementioned sales, Åge Remøy and 
affiliated companies own in total 12 618 177 shares, 
including 5 547 239 SOFF A shares and 7 070 938 SOFF B 
shares, equivalent to approximately 14% of the issued 
share capital and approximately 8.6% of the voting 
rights in Solstad Offshore ASA. 
 
This notification is made pursuant to Section 4-2 (1) 
third sentence of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
