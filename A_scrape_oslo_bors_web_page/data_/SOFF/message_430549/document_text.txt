Hemen Holding Limited ("Hemen"), which is indirectly 
controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the 
benefit of his immediate family, has today acquired 
1,500,000 shares at a price of NOK 9.50 per share in Solstad 
Farstad ASA ("SOFF"). After the acquisition Hemen holds 
48,689,231 shares in SOFF, representing 16.7 % of the shares 
and votes in SOFF. Hemen does not hold rights to shares in 
SOFF. 

This disclosure is made pursuant to section 4-2 of the 
Securities Trading Act.
