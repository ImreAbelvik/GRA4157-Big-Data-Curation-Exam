Oslo, 12 May 2022: EGD Shipholding AS has completed an internal sale of 8 805 128 shares in Klaveness Combination Carriers ASA (“KCC”) to its 100% owned subsidiary EGD Shipping Invest AS. Following the sale, EGD Shipping Invest AS owns 8 805 128 shares in Klaveness Combination Carriers ASA, corresponding to 16.81% of total shares outstanding.
 
Following the sale EGD Shipholding AS does not hold any shares in Klaveness Combination Carriers ASA directly but will continue to hold 55 691 warrants, each with a right to subscribe for one share in Klaveness Combination Carriers ASA, corresponding to 0.11% of the current total number of shares outstanding in Klaveness Combination Carriers ASA and not taking into account the dilutive effect of the warrants.

The consolidated ownership of shares and warrants of EGD Shipholding AS and EGD Shipping Invest AS after the sale is consequently 8 805 128 shares and 55 691 warrants, which is equal to the previous ownership of EGD Shipholding AS, and which corresponds to 16.92% of the current total number of shares outstanding in Klaveness Combination Carriers ASA and not taking into account the dilutive effect of the warrants.

In connection with the sale, all 8 805 128 shares have been pledged by EGD Shipping Invest AS in favour of DNB Bank ASA.

EGD Shipping Invest AS and EGD Shipholding AS are classified as closely associated parties to a primary insider of KCC and further information on the sale is provided in the attached forms.

This announcement is made in accordance with Sections 5-12 and 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act and Article 19 of the European Market Abuse Regulation.
