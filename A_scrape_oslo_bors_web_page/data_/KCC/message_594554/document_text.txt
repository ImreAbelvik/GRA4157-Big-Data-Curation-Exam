Oslo, 30 June 2023: Klaveness Combination Carriers ASA (“KCC” or “the Company”) advises that E Dahm Invest AS, a company closely associated with Engebret Dahm, CEO of KCC, has entered into an agreement to pledge 10,000 shares in KCC as security for a loan from the Company.
