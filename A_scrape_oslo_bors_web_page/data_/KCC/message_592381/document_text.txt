Oslo, 02 June 2023: Klaveness Combination Carriers ASA (“KCC” or “the Company”) advises that primary insider and Member of the Board of Directors Brita Eilertsen, 02 June 2023 purchased 2,000 shares in KCC of NOK 69.45 per share. Eilertsen does not own shares in KCC prior to the transaction and the shareholding corresponds to 0.003% of the Company's shares.
