Proximar Seafood AS ("Proximar" or "The Company") has been notified that Grieg Kapital AS, a company closely associated with Per Grieg, board member and primary insider in Proximar, today, August 30 2024, has completed a transfer of in total 790,000 shares issued by the Company.
 
The shares have been transferred to companies controlled by other members of the Grieg family as part of an internal split of shareholding pursuant to existing agreements. Following the transfer, Grieg Kapital AS holds 14,690,049 shares in Proximar.


Please see the attached primary insider notification form for further information, cf. the requirements of the EU Market Abuse Regulation art. 19.
 
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements under Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
