Member of the Board, Espen Aubert has 30th of December 2021 transferred 3,996,238 shares in Proximar Seafood AS from Daimyo AS to Daimyo Invest AS. Both companies are fully owned by Peak XV AS.

After this transaction, Daimyo Invest AS holds a total of 3,996,238 shares in Proximar Seafood AS.
