16 March 2023 

Jakobsen Energia AS, a company 100% owned by Viktor E Jakobsen, CEO of Energeia AS (the "Company"), has on the 15th of March 2023 acquired 117 749 shares in the Company at a price of NOK 1,8969 per share (the "Transaction"). Following the Transaction, Jakobsen Energia AS holds 18 716 349 shares, representing 15,7% of the outstanding shares in Energeia AS.
