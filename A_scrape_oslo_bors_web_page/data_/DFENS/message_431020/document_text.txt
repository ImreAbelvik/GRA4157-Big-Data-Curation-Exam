Arne Blystad, chairman of the board of Songa Bulk ASA ("Songa 
Bulk"),has today transferred 842 000 shares of Songa Bulk from Songa 
Shipholding AS to Songa Trading Inc. at NOK 39.60 per share. The buyer 
and the seller are both companies 100 % controlled by Arne Blystad and 
his related parties. The transfer was made directly between the seller 
and buyer.

The total shareholding controlled by Arne Blystad is 4 711 900 shares 
of Songa Bulk.
