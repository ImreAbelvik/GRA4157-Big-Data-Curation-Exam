Oslo, 16 June 2017
SBULK: Primary insider notification.

Billung Bulk AS, owned by Herman Billung (CEO of Songa Bulk ASA), 
acquired 2,500 shares of Songa Bulk AS on 16th of June 2017 at an 
average price of NOK 39 per share. The shares were acquired over 
Oslo Axess.

Following the transaction, the total holding controlled by Billung 
Bulk AS are 71,000 shares.
