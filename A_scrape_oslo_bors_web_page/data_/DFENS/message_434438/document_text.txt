Oslo, 11 September 2017
Christine Rødsæther, member of the Board of Songa Bulk ASA, acquired 
3,124 shares of Songa Bulk ASA on 7th September 2017 at 
a price of NOK 43.99 per share. The shares were acquired over 
Oslo Axess.

Following the transaction, the total holding controlled by Christine 
Rødsæther is 3,124 shares.
