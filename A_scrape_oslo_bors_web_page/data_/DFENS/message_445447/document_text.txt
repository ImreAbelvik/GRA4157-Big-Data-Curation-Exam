Magnus Leonard Roth, member of the board of Songa Bulk ASA ("Songa Bulk"), has today transferred 981 102 shares of Songa Bulk to Canomaro Bulk AS at NOK 44.50 per share. The buyer is a company controlled by Magnus Leonard Roth. The transfer was made directly between the seller and buyer. 

The total shareholding controlled by Magnus Leonard Roth is
5 652 502 shares of Songa Bulk.
