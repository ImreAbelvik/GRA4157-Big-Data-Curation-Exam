Magnus Leonard Roth, member of the board of Songa Bulk ASA ("Songa Bulk"), has on 7 September 2018 transferred 6 209 036 shares of Songa Bulk to Canomaro Shipping AS at NOK 4.19 per share. The buyer is a company controlled by Magnus Leonard Roth. The transfer was made directly between the seller and buyer. 
 
The total shareholding controlled by Magnus Leonard Roth is 6 209 036 shares of Songa Bulk.
