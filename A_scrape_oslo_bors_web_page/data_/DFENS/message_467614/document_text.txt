Songa Trading Inc, Songa Bulk Chartering AS and Agmably AS, all companies controlled by Mr. Arne Blystad, chairman of Songa Bulk ASA ("Songa Bulk"), and his related parties, sold 923 007 shares of Songa Bulk on 16 January 2019 at an average price of NOK 0,6547 per share on the Oslo Stock Exchange.

Following the transaction, the total holding controlled by Mr. Blystad and his related parties is 8 419 113 shares corresponding to 23,48 % of the shares of Songa Bulk.
