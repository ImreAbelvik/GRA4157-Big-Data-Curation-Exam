Oslo, 18 May 2017
SBULK-ME: Primary insider notification.

Arne Blystad, chairman of the board of Songa Bulk ASA ("Songa 
Bulk"), through companies controlled by him, transferred 25,000 
shares of Songa Bulk to each of AGMABLY AS, AKB AS and JJB AS, in 
total 75,000 shares. The buyers are companies controlled by members 
of Arne Blystad's immediate family. The transfer was made in 
connection with the last private placement in Songa Bulk on the 10th 
of February and the price was NOK 42 per share. The transfer was 
made directly between the seller and buyers.

The total shareholding controlled by Arne Blystad is 4.711.900 of 
shares.
