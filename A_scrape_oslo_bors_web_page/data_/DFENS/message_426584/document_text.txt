Oslo, 5 May 2017
SBULK-ME: Primary insider notification

PK Shipping AS, owned by Per Kristian Aamlid (COO of Songa Bulk AS), 
acquired 8,000 shares of Songa Bulk AS on 5th of May 2017 at an 
average price of NOK 40.81 per share. The shares were acquired over 
Merkur Market.

Following the transaction, the total holding controlled by PK 
Shipping AS are 34,000 shares.
