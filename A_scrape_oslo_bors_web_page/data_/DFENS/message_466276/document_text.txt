AKB AS and JJB AS, companies controlled by Arne Blystad (Chairman of Songa Bulk ASA) and his related parties, each sold 25.000 shares of Songa Bulk ASA on 20 December 2019 at a price of NOK 0.15 per share. 

Following the transactions, the total holding controlled by Mr. Blystad and his related parties is 9,342,120 shares corresponding to 26,05% of the Company
