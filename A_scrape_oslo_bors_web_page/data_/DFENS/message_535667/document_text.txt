Havila Holding AS, a closely associated company to board member and primary insider Njål Sævik, has on 10 June 2021 sold 4,800,000 shares in Axxis Geo Solutions ASA at an average price of NOK 0.4968 per share. After the transaction, Havila Holding AS will hold 15,549,434 shares in Axxis Geo Solutions ASA and Njål Sævik will hold 354,888 shares in Axxis Geo Solutions ASA.
