Oslo, 26 April 2017
SBULK-ME: Primary insider notification.

Billung Bulk AS, owned by Herman Billung (CEO of Songa Bulk AS), 
acquired 2,500 shares of Songa Bulk AS on 26th of April 2017 at an 
average price of NOK 44.50 per share. The shares were acquired over 
Merkur Market.

Following the transaction, the total holding controlled by Billung 
Bulk AS are 68,500 shares.
