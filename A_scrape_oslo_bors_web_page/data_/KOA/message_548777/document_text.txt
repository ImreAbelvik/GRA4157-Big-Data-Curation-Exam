Martina  Schüle, Vice President Group Finance and primary insider of Kongsberg
Automotive ASA has 3 December 2021 sold 36.684 shares in the company at an
average price of NOK 3.0157 per share. Following this transaction, Martina
Schüle and close associates now hold 7.031 shares in Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the
Market Abuse Regulation article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
