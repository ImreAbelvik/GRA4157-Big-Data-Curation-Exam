Zurich, March 14, 2024: Brian Kristoffersen, Board member and primary insider of
Kongsberg Automotive ASA has, through his closely related company B.K. Company
ApS, purchased 582,087 shares in the company at an average price of NOK 1.4108
on March 13, 2024. After the purchase, Brian Kristoffersen and his closely
related parties hold 12,091,505 shares in Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

Please refer to the attached notification of trading for further details. This
information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the
Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the
Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
