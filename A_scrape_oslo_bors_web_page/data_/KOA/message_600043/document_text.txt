Zurich, September 21, 2023: Dzeki Mackinovski, Executive Vice President
Purchasing and primary insider of Kongsberg Automotive ASA has on 20 September
2023 sold 508.684 shares in the company at an average price of NOK 2,26 per
share. Following this transaction, Dzeki Mackinovski now holds 345.166 shares in
Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the
Market Abuse Regulation article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
