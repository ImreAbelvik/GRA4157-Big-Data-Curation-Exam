Bob Riedford, President Powertrain & Chassis and primary insider of Kongsberg
Automotive ASA has on 27 September sold 411.000 shares in the company at an
average price of NOK 2,235 per share. Following this transaction, Bob Riedford
now holds 814 shares in Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

Bob Riedford has resigned as the President of Powertrain & Chassis to pursue
opportunities outside of KA effective October 1, 2022.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the
Market Abuse Regulation article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
