Teleios Capital Partners LLC ("Teleios"), represented on the board of directors of Kongsberg Automotive ASA ("KOA") has on 23 June 2020, traded in KOA stock as follows:

•	Bought on Merkur Market 8,000,000 shares (ISIN NO 001 0883630) at an average price of 0.1498 NOK per share

•	Sold on Oslo Børs 8,000,000 shares (ISIN NO 000 3033102) at an average price of 0.2673 NOK per share

Consequently, Teleios continues to control through Teleios Global Opportunities Master Fund, Ltd. 1,723,846,758 shares and votes in KOA, corresponding to 23.15% of the issued share capital.
