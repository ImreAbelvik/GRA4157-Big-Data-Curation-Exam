Teleios Capital Partners LLC ("Teleios"), represented on the board of directors of Kongsberg Automotive ASA ("KOA") has on 24 June 2020, traded in KOA stock as follows:

• Bought on Merkur Market 13,500,000 shares (ISIN NO 001 0883630) at an average price of 0.1329 NOK per share
• Sold on Oslo Børs 13,500,000 shares (ISIN NO 000 3033102) at an average price of 0.2307 NOK per share

Consequently, Teleios continues to control through Teleios Global Opportunities Master Fund, Ltd. 1,723,846,758 shares and votes in KOA, corresponding to 23.15% of the issued share capital.
