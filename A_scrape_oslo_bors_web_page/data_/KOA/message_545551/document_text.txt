Frank Heffter, Chief Financial Officer and primary insider of Kongsberg
Automotive ASA has 29 October 2021 bought 345,000 shares in the company at an
average purchase price of NOK 2,6388. Following this transaction, Frank Heffter
owns 345,000 shares in Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the
Market Abuse Regulation article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
