Kongsberg, August 12, 2025: Trond Fiskum, President & CEO of Kongsberg
Automotive ASA and a primary insider, has today purchased 400,000 shares at an
average price of NOK 1.570 per share. After this transaction, Trond Fiskum owns
900,000 shares in Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

This information was disclosed in accordance with Article 19 of the EU Market
Abuse Regulation and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
