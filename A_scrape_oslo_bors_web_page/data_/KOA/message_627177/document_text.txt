Kongsberg, September 6, 2024: Christian Johansson, Chief Financial Officer and
primary insider of Kongsberg Automotive ASA has purchased 100,000 shares in the
company at an average price of NOK 1.46 on September 5, 2024. After the
purchase, Christian Johansson holds 200,000 shares in Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

Please refer to the attached notification of trading for further details.
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the
Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the
Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
