Kongsberg, May 27, 2025: Volldal Invest AS owned by Olav Volldal, Chairman and
primary insider of Kongsberg Automotive ASA, has on May 27, 2025, purchased
357,232 shares in the company at an average price of NOK 1.604. After the
purchase, Olav Volldal holds 400,000 shares in Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

This information was disclosed in accordance with Article 19 of the EU Market
Abuse Regulation and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
