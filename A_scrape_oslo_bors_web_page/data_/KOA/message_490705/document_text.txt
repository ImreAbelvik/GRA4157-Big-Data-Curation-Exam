Teleios Capital Partners LLC ("Teleios"), represented on the board of directors of Kongsberg Automotive ASA ("KOA") has on 2 December 2019, bought 915,454 shares in KOA at a price of NOK 4.7857 per share.

Consequently, Teleios controls through Teleios Global Opportunities Master Fund, Ltd. 103,345,861 shares and votes in KOA, corresponding to 23.07% of the issued share capital.
