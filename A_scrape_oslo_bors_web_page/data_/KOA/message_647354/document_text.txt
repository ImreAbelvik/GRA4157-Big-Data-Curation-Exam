Kongsberg, May 23, 2025: Edan Invest AS owned by Synnøve Gjønnes, Board member
and primary insider of Kongsberg Automotive ASA, has on May 23, 2025, purchased
80,000 shares in the company at an average price of NOK 1.5919. After the
purchase, Synnøve Gjønnes holds 80,000 shares in Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

This information was disclosed in accordance with Article 19 of the EU Market
Abuse Regulation and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
