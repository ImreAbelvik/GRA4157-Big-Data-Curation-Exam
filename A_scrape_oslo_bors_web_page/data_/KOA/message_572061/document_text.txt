Emese Weissenbacher, Board member and primary insider of Kongsberg Automotive
ASA has on 26 September 2022 received 38.052 shares in the company. After the
transferal, Emese Weissenbacher holds 38.052 shares in Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

The shares are transferred as part of the remuneration to the board of directors
as resolved by the Annual General Meeting 2 June 2022 (See notice to Oslo Stock
Exchange of same day with attached
minutes (https://www.kongsbergautomotive.com/globalassets/investor
-relations/events-and-presentations/events--presentations-2022/koa---minutes
-from-agm-2022.pdf)). The shares are restricted in the way that they cannot be
sold before 3 June 2024.

Please refer to the attached notification of trading for further details. This
information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the
Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the
Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
