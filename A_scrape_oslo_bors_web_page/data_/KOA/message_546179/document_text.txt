Martina Schuele, Vice President Group Accounting & Reporting and primary insider
of Kongsberg Automotive ASA has 5 November 2021 sold 201,753 shares in the
company at an average purchase price of NOK 2,87. Following this transaction,
Martina and close associates now hold 43,715 shares in Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the
Market Abuse Regulation article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
