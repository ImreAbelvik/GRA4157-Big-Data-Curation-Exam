Kongsberg, August 18, 2025: Bård Klungseth, Deputy Chair and primary insider of
Kongsberg Automotive ASA, has today purchased 96,000 shares at an average price
of NOK 1,78 per share. After this transaction, Bård Klungseth owns 173,000
shares in Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

This information was disclosed in accordance with Article 19 of the EU Market
Abuse Regulation and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
