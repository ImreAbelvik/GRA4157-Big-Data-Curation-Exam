Jakob Bronebakk, Interim IR Manager and primary insider of Kongsberg Automotive
ASA has on 9 August 2022 purchased 25,000 shares in the company at an average
purchase price of NOK 2,60. Following this transaction, Jakob Bronebakk holds
25,000 shares in Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the
Market Abuse Regulation article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
