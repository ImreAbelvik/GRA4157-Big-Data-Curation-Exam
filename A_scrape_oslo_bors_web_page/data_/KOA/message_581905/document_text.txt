Bjørn Ivan Ødegård, employee elected Board Director and primary insider of
Kongsberg Automotive ASA has on 9 February 2023 sold 20.000 shares in the
company at an average price of NOK 3,0347 per share. Following this transaction,
Bjørn Ivan Ødegård now holds 1.972 shares in Kongsberg Automotive ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the
Market Abuse Regulation article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
