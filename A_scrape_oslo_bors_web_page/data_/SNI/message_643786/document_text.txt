Stolt-Nielsen Limited ("Stolt-Nielsen") has been informed that the following primary insider has acquired shares in Stolt-Nielsen as further described in the attached form of notification:
Udo Lange, CEO of Stolt-Nielsen, has acquired 5,000 shares at an average price of NOK 222,00 per share. After this transaction, Udo Lange owns 13,000 shares in Stolt-Nielsen Limited.
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Article 19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
