In December 2014 no stock options were exercised by 
employees of Stolt-Nielsen Limited.
 
As a consequence, no new Common Shares were issued.

The total number of issued Common Shares as of 
December 31, 2014 is 64,133,796.

Total number of Common Shares held by Stolt-Nielsen 
Limited as of December 31, 2014 is 7,908,431.

Total number of Common Shares therefore outstanding 
as of December 31, 2014 is 56,225,365.
