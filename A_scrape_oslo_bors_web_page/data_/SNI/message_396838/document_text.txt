On March 8, 2016, Stolt-Nielsen Limited (SNI) purchased a 
further 43,000 of its Common Shares on the Oslo Stock Exchange 
at an average price of NOK 88.7444. The shares were purchased 
in accordance with the buy-back programme announced on March 2, 
2016.

After these transactions Stolt-Nielsen Limited holds 9,136,941 
own shares, representing 14.25% of the issued Common Shares.
