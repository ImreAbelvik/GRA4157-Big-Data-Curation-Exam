On October 28, 2015, Stolt-Nielsen Limited purchased 
350,000 of its Common Shares (SNI) on the Oslo Stock 
Exchange at an average price of NOK 113.00 per share. 
The shares were purchased in accordance with the buy-
back programme announced on November 18, 2014.

After these transactions Stolt-Nielsen Limited holds 
8,758,431 own shares, representing 13.66% of the 
number of issued Common Shares.
