On October 28, 2015, Stolt-Nielsen Limited purchased a 
further 263,510 of its Common Shares (SNI) on the Oslo 
Stock Exchange at a price of NOK 113.50, in addition 
to the 350,000 shares announced purchased earlier in 
the day at a price of NOK 113.00. The shares were 
purchased in accordance with the buy-back programme 
announced on November 18, 2014.

After these transactions Stolt-Nielsen Limited holds 
9,021,941 own shares, representing 14.07% of the 
issued Common Shares.
