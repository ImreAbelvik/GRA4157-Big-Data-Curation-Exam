On March 2, 2016, Stolt-Nielsen Limited (SNI) purchased 18,000 
of its Common Shares on the Oslo Stock Exchange at an average 
price of NOK 85.7071. The shares were purchased in accordance 
with the buy-back programme announced on March 2, 2016.

After these transactions Stolt-Nielsen Limited holds 9,039,941 
own shares, representing 14.10% of the issued Common Shares.
