On March 10, 2016, Stolt-Nielsen Limited (SNI) purchased a 
further 52,000 of its Common Shares on the Oslo Stock Exchange 
at an average price of NOK 87.0291. The shares were purchased 
in accordance with the buy-back programme announced on March 2, 
2016.

After these transactions Stolt-Nielsen Limited holds 9,118,941 
own shares, representing 14.33% of the issued Common Shares.
