Mr. Niels G. Stolt-Nielsen, primary insider, Chief Executive Officer 
and Director of Stolt-Nielsen Limited (the "Company"), has today 
transferred 523,570 shares in the Company to The Stonehedge Trust, a 
trust established for the benefit of him and certain members of his 
immediate family. The shares were transferred without payment.
 
Following this transaction, The Stonehedge Trust controls a total of 
523,570 shares in the Company and Niels G. Stolt-Nielsen personally 
owns no shares and holds no options.
