Reference is made to the announcement published on 20 May 2022 by Havyard Group ASA (the "Company") regarding an agreement for the acquisition of the shares in HG Group AS.

Even Matre Ellingsen, chairman and primary insider, has on 29 June 2022 received respectively 2 999 511 and 4 960 847 settlement shares in the Company at an average price of NOK 5,19 per share, through his closely associated companies Neve Holding AS and Neve Eiendom AS.

After the transaction, Neve Holding AS holds 2 999 511 shares and Neve Eiendom AS holds 4 960 847 shares in the Company.

Please see the attached documents for further details of the transactions.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation article 19 and 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
