Reference is made to the stock exchange notice published on 20 March 2024 regarding the completion of a block sale of existing shares in Hafnia Limited (the “Company”) by BW Group Limited (the “Seller”) through an accelerated bookbuilding process (the “Offering”).

The Seller has successfully sold 26,000,000 shares in the Company which is equal to approx. 5.1% of the Company’s issued shares at NOK 73 per share. Following completion of the Offering, the Seller owns 220,106,112 shares in the Company which is equal to approx. 43.14% of the Company’s issued shares.

Castel AS, a company closely related to Mr. Erik Bartnes who is a director of the Company, was allocated 27,397 shares in the Offering at the offering price. Following this allocation, Castel AS owns 1,592,012 shares in the Company which is equal approx. 0.31% of the Company’s issued shares.

Please refer to the attached notifications of trading for further details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation article 19.
