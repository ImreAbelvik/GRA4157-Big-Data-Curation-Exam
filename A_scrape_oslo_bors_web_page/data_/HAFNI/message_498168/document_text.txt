12 March 2020

BW Group Limited, which is controlled by Andreas Sohmen-Pao (Chairman of the Board in Hafnia Limited), has on 12 March 2020 acquired 850,000 shares in Hafnia Limited at a price of NOK 15.60 per share on Oslo Axess.

Following the acquisition, BW Group Limited holds a total of 246,106,112 shares in Hafnia Limited.
