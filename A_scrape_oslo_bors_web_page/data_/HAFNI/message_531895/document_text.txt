HAFNIA LIMITED: Award of shares and share options

30 April 2021

The Board of Directors of Hafnia Limited (the "Company") has approved the award of shares and share options to the senior management of the Company under the Company's bonus and long-term incentive plan.

Mikael Skov, CEO, has been awarded 1,357,632 options and 93,243 shares. After the award of the options and the shares, Mikael Skov holds a total of 4,087,896 share options and 919,711 shares.

Perry van Echtelt, CFO, has been awarded 296,112 share options. After the award of the share options, Perry van Echtelt holds a total of 888,336 share options and 40,285 shares.

The options will vest three years from the award date and expire six years after the award date. The strike price at the award date for the options awarded in 2021 is NOK 19.16. 

Please see further details in the attached document.

About Hafnia:

Hafnia is one of the world's leading oil product tanker owners and operators. The Company provides transportation of oil and oil products to leading national and international oil companies, major chemical companies, as well as trading and utility companies. Hafnia operates a fleet of 184 vessels in pools including newbuilds, of which 101 are owned or chartered-in including six owned LR2s, 35 owned or chartered-in LR1s, 47 owned or chartered-in MRs and 13 owned Handy vessels. 

Hafnia is a global company with offices in Singapore, Copenhagen and Houston and presence in Mumbai. The Company is part of BW Group, an international shipping group that has worked in oil and gas transportation, floating gas infrastructure, environmental technologies and deep-water production for over 80 years.
