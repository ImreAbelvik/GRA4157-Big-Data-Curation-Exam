TIME CRITICAL PETROLEUM RESOURCES AS ("TCPR"), a company 100 % owned by the chairman of Petrolia SE ("PDR"), Berge Gerdt Larsen, has purchased 1,100 shares at NOK 2.30 each.

Following the transaction TCPR holds 1,118 PDR shares, however, through indirect non-controlled interests, Mr Larsen holds a significant economic interest in Petrolia SE.

In addition, TCPR has a non-revocable option (expiring on 31st July 2018) to purchase all 3.730.486 PDR shares (6.94%) owned by IOS at a strike price of NOK 2.40 per share, and with an option premium of NOK 0.10 per share. Following an eventual exercise of the option, TCPR can vote for a total of 3.731.604 shares (6.94%). Furthermore, following an eventual exercise of the option, IOS will no longer hold any shares in PDR.
