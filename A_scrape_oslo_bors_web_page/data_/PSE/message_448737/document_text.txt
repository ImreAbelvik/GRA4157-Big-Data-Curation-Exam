TIME CRITICAL PETROLEUM RESOURCES AS ("TCPR"), a company 100 % owned by the chairman of Petrolia SE ("PDR"), Berge Gerdt Larsen, has purchased 6,000 shares at NOK 2.90 each. Following the transaction TCPR holds 35,587 (0.07%) PDR shares, however, through indirect non-controlled interests, Mr Larsen holds a significant economic interest in Petrolia SE. In addition, TCPR has a non-revocable option (expiring on 31st July 2018) to purchase 3,730,486 PDR shares (6.94%) at a strike price of NOK 2.40 per share, and with an option premium of NOK 0.10 per share. Following an eventual exercise of the option, TCPR can vote for a total of 3,766,073 shares (7.01%).
