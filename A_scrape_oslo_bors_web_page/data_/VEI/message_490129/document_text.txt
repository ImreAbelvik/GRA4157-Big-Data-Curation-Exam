- Allocation to primary insiders

Reference is made to the announcement published 13 November 2019 by Veidekke
regarding commencement of the subscription period for shares in the employee
share purchase program.

Veidekke hereby announce that the share issue directed towards employees is
successfully completed.

The subscription period expired on 22 November 2019

The subscription price is set to NOK 91,96 per share, corresponding to the
volume-weighted average share price on the Oslo Stock Exchange in the
subscription period with at deduction for a discount of 20%. The shares are
blocked for a period of two years.

A total of 1,257,425 new shares was allocated in the share issue. A total of
2,170 employees were allotted shares.

The new shares are expected to be issued on or about 20 December 2019. Provided
timely payment, delivery of the shares are expected to be made between 21-31
December 2019. Notifications of allocated shares, and the corresponding amount
to be paid, are expected to be communicated on or about 28 November 2019.
Employees having access to investor services through their VPS account manager
will be able to check the number of shares allocated to them when shares are
issued and registered in VPS(the Norwegian CSD)

After the registration of the new shares, the total number of shares in Veidekke
ASA will increase by 1,257,425 shares to 134,962,367 shares.

About Veidekke:

Veidekke is one of Scandinavia's largest construction and property development
companies. The company undertakes all types of building construction and civil
engineering contracts, develops residential projects, maintains roads, and
produces asphalt and aggregates. The company is known for its involvement and
local knowledge. Its annual turnover is approximately NOK 36 billion, and half
of its 8,600 employees own shares in the company. Veidekke is listed on the Oslo
Stock Exchange and has always posted a profit since it was founded in 1936.
