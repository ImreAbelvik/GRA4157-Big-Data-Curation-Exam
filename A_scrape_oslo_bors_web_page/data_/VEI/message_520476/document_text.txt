On 14 December 2020, Mrs. Hanne Rønneberg, a board member and primary insider in
Veidekke ASA, bought 700 Veidekke shares at a price of NOK 107 each. After this
transaction Mrs. Hanne Rønneberg holds 700 shares in Veidekke.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
