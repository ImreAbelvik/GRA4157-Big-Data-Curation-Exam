


1,007 senior executives in Veidekke have received offers to purchase between 50
and 2,000 Veidekke shares each. The senior executives could choose between two
alternatives: Option A with 20 % discount and partial financing (1,000 - 2,000
shares) and option B with 30 % discount and no financing (50 - 2,000 shares).

588 senior executives subscribed for a total of 821,717 shares during the
subscription period from 11 to 20 May.

The offer prices were NOK 84.48 (option A) and NOK 73.92 (option B) after the
20 % / 30 % discount on the market price during the subscription period. The
lock-in period for the shares is 3 years.

The purchases of primary insiders, as well as new holdings, are attached to this
report.
