


On 21 June 2018, Mrs. Ann Christin Andersen, a primary insider in Veidekke ASA,
bought 2 400 Veidekke shares at a price of NOK 83.25 each. After this
transaction Mrs. Ann Christin Andersen holds 5 700 shares in Veidekke.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
