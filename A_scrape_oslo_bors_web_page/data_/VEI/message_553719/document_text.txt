OBOS, a related party to Daniel Kjørberg Siraj, Member of the Board of Veidekke,
has today bought 212 219 shares in Veidekke ASA at a price of NOK 134.0943 per
share.

After the transaction OBOS holds 26 518 316 shares in Veidekke ASA,
corresponding to an ownership share of 19.65%

For further information, please see enclosure at www.newsweb.no.
