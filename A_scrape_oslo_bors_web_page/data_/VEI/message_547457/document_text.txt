Shares purchased by Key employees in 2021

A total of 530 participants in the Company's share program for key employees
have on November 19[th], 2021 subscribed for 474,603 shares.

The purchase price is equal to the volume-weighted average share price on the
Oslo Stock Exchange in the subscription period, with a deduction for a discount.
Discount for Swedish employees is 25 % which gives a purchase price of 94.27 NOK
per share. For the Norwegian and the Danish employee`s the discount is 20%,
which gives a purchase price of 100.55 NOK per share.

The shares are subject to a three-year lock-in period. The shares will be
transferred in December.

A total of 444 participants subscribed 397 783 shares at a price of 100.55 NOK.

A total of 86 participants subscribed 76 820 shares at a price of 94.27 NOK.

From the total above, the primary insiders purchased a total of 45,850 shares in
Veidekke.

The purchases of primary insiders, as well as new holdings, are attached to this
report.
