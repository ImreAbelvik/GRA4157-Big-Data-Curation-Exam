


On 17 November 2015 Stiftelsen Veidekkeansattes aksjekjøp (the Veidekke Employee
Share Trust) purchased 448 Veidekke shares at a price of NOK 104.50 each. The
Trust now holds 294 321 shares.

These shares will be resold to employees in Veidekke in the share purchase offer
in November 2015.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
