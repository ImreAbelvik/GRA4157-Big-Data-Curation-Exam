On 12 February 2021, Mr. Mats Nyström, Executive Vice President of Veidekke,
sold and bought 20 000 Veidekke shares at a price of NOK 110.20 each. The
transaction was completed to transfer 20 000 shares from his share/depot account
to an investment savings account (ISK). After this transaction Mr. Mats Nyström
still holds 20 000 shares in Veidekke.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
