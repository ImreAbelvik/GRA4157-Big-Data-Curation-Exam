Options issued:

In accordance with the AGM 5 May 2021 the Board of Directors of Veidekke ASA
offered 1 044 Veidekke employees a total of 1,349,562 new share options. 539
employees accepted the offer and are now enrolled in the share option program.
Following this enrolment process 979,900 new share options were awarded.

To participate the participants must pay 1 NOK per option.

The strike price for the options awarded on 18 May 2021 is NOK 122.01.

The options will vest over one (25% of the options), two (25% of the options)
and three (50% of the options) years from grant. All unexercised options will
lapse five years after grant.

The options are non-tradable and conditional upon the option holder being
employed by the Company and not having resigned prior to the vesting date.

Shares acquired through the program will have a sales restriction on one year
after exercise.
