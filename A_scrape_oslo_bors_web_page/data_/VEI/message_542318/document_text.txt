OBOS, a related party to Daniel Kjørberg Siraj, Member of the Board of Veidekke,
has today bought 400 000 shares in Veidekke ASA at a price of NOK 112 per share.

After the transaction OBOS holds 25 548 220 shares in Veidekke ASA,
corresponding to an ownership share of 18.93%

For further information, see attached available at www.newsweb.no.
