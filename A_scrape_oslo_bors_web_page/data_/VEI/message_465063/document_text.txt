On 4 December 2018 Stiftelsen Veidekkeansattes aksjekjøp (the Veidekke Employee
Share Trust) purchased 25 000 Veidekke shares at a price of NOK 97,68 each. The
Trust now holds 3 954 shares.

Employees of Veidekke purchased 1 037 579 shares in Veidekke from the Trust in
November 2018, see notification 26 November 2018. These shares will be
transferred from the Trust in December 2018. The balance of Veidekke shares in
the Trust is shown after this transaction.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
