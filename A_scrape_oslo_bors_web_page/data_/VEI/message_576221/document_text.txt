A total of 600 participants in the Company's share programme for key employees
have on November 18[th] 2022 subscribed for 747 111 shares.

The purchase price is equal to the volume-weighted average share price on the
Oslo Stock Exchange in the subscription period, with a deduction for a discount.
Discount for Swedish employees is 25 % which gives a purchase price of 67.64 NOK
per share. For the Norwegian and the Danish employee`s the discount is 20%,
which gives a purchase price of 72.14 NOK per share.

The shares are subject to a three-year lock-in period.

A total of 535 participants purchased 669 311 shares at NOK 72.14 per share.
A total of 65 participants purchased 77 800 shares at a price of NOK 67.64.
