Mr. Egil Haugsdal, Chairman of the board and primary insider in Veidekke ASA,
has today bought 2 200 Veidekke shares at a price of NOK 147 each. After this
transaction Mr. Egil Haugsdal holds 3 200 shares in Veidekke.
