


On 2 June 2016 Stiftelsen Veidekkeansattes aksjekjøp (the Veidekke Employee
Share Trust) purchased 70 000 Veidekke shares at a price of NOK 105.8693 each.
The Trust now holds 26 219 shares.

Employees purchased 821 717 shares in Veidekke from the Trust in May 2016, see
notification 23 May 2016. These shares will be transferred from the Trust in
June 2016. The balance of Veidekke shares in the Trust is shown after this
transaction.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
