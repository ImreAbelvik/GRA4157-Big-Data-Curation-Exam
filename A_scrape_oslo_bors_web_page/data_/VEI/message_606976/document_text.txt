Mrs. Hanne Rønneberg, a board member and primary insider in Veidekke ASA, has
today bought 1 000 Veidekke shares at a price of NOK 104 each. After this
transaction Mrs. Hanne Rønneberg holds 2 700 shares in Veidekke.

For further information, please see enclosure at www.newsweb.no.
