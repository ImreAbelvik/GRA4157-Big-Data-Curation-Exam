On 10 March 2021 Stiftelsen Veidekkeansattes aksjekjøp (the Veidekke Employee
Share Trust) purchased 20 000 Veidekke shares at a price of NOK 121.01 each.

Veidekke employees purchased 1 363 571 shares in Veidekke from the Trust in
February 2021, cf. stock exchange announcement dated 22 February 2021. These
shares will be transferred from the Trust during March 2021.

The Trust's holding following the transaction with Veidekke employees stands at
33 732 shares in Veidekke.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
