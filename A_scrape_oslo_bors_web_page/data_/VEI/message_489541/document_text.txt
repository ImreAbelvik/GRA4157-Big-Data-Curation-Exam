OBOS BBL (“OBOS”) has today bought 100,000 shares in Veidekke ASA (“Veidekke”), at NOK 112.4989 per share.
Following the transaction, OBOS holds 24,430,440 shares in Veidekke, representing 18.27 percent of the outstanding share capital in Veidekke.

OBOS is currently a primary insider and represented on the board of directors in Veidekke by Daniel Kjørberg Siraj, CEO in OBOS.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 first paragraph of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

Oslo, 15. November 2019
