Tocaba AS, a company owned by a related party to Tone Hegland Bachke, board
member and primary insider of Veidekke ASA, has on 14 November 2019 bought 3,000
Veidekke shares at a price of NOK 112,00. After his transaction, the
shareholding for Tocaba AS in Veidekke ASA is 3,000.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
