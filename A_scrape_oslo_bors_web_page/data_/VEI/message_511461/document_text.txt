On 17 August 2020, Mr. Svein Richard Brandtzæg, chair of the board of directors
in Veidekke ASA, bought 500 Veidekke shares at a price of NOK 127.20 each. After
this transaction Mr. Svein Richard Brandtzæg holds 2 300 shares in Veidekke.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
