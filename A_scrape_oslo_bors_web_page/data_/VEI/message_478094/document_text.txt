On 28 May 2019, Mr. Svein Richard Brandtzæg, chair of the board of directors in
Veidekke ASA, bought 1 800 Veidekke shares at a price of NOK 83.90 each. After
this transaction Mr. Svein Richard Brandtzæg holds 1 800 shares in Veidekke.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
