On 10 March 2021, the Board of Directors of DNB ASA decided to offer employees
in DNB ASA shares with a discount of up to 25 % (up to NOK 14,500 per employee).

The shares were allotted today, 18 May 2021, at a gross price of NOK 181.4740
per share.

Please see attachment for further details regarding the primary insiders that
made use of the offer.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
