On 11 April 2019, the Board of Directors of DNB ASA decided to offer employees
in DNB ASA shares with a discount of 20 % (up to NOK 14,500 per employee).

The shares were allotted today, 28 May 2019, at a gross price of NOK 153.2472
per share.
