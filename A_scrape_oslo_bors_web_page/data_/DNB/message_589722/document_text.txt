Alexander Opstad, Group Executive Vice President in DNB Bank ASA, has on 5 May
2022 transferred 12 000 shares in DNB Bank ASA at a share price of NOK 184,70 to
Chaos Capital AS, which is a wholly owned investment company of Opstad. The
shareholding of Alexander Opstad, including close associates, is unchanged at 56
662 shares after the transfer.
