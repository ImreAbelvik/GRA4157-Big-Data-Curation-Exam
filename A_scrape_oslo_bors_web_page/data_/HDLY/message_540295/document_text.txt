On 19 August 2021, Leif Hübert, acquired 100,000 shares in Huddly AS at NOK 9.85 per share. Mertoun Capital AS acquired 85,542 shares in Huddly AS at NOK 9.85 per share.
Leif Hübert controls 97.6% of Mertoun Capital AS. Leif Hübert and Mertoun Capital AS are related parties to Jostein Devold, who represents Mertoun Capital AS on the board of Huddly AS. 
Following the transaction, Jostein Devold and related parties hold 15,431,013 shares in Huddly AS, representing 7.13% of the outstanding share capital.
