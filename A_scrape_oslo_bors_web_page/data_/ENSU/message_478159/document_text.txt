Thin Film Electronics ASA ("Thinfilm") issues this notice on behalf of two board
members.

Jon S. Castor, newly elected board member and primary insider in Thinfilm, today
received   8,000,000 subscription   rights   ("SRs")   under   Thinfilm's  2019
Subscription Rights Incentive Plan.

Following  the transaction, Jon  S. Castor and  close associates hold or control
8,000,000 SRs in Thinfilm.

Kelly S. Doss, newly elected board member and primary insider in Thinfilm, today
received   4,000,000 subscription   rights   ("SRs")   under   Thinfilm's  2019
Subscription Rights Incentive Plan.

Following  the transaction, Kelly  S. Doss and  close associates hold or control
4,000,000 SRs in Thinfilm.

The  exercise price of the  SRs is NOK 0.15 per  share. The vesting schedule for
the  SRs for the two new board members  is 50% on the earlier of 28 May 2020 and
one  day prior to  the 2020 Annual General  Meeting, and additionally 50% on the
earlier of 28 May 2021 and one day prior to the 2021 Annual General Meeting. The
SRs expire on 28 May 2024.
