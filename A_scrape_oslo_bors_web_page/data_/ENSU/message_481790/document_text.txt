Thin Film Electronics ASA ("Thinfilm") issues this notice on behalf of Mallorie
Burak, Chief Financial Officer.

Mallorie Burak, Chief Financial Officer and primary insider in Thinfilm, today
received 9,360,000 subscription rights ("SRs") under Thinfilm's 2019
Subscription Rights Incentive Plan.

Following the transaction, Mallorie Burak and close associates hold or control
9,360,000 SRs in Thinfilm.

The exercise price of the SRs is NOK 0.15 per share. The vesting schedule for
the SRs is 33.3% one year from the Vesting Commencement date, 15 July 2019, and
thereafter 33.3% and 33.4%, respectively, the two following years. The SRs
expire on 28 May 2024.
