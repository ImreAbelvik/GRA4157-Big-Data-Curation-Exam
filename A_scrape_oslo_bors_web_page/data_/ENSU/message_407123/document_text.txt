


Thin Film Electronics ASA ("Thinfilm") issues this notice on behalf of two
employees.

Ole Ronny Thorsnes, incoming CFO and primary insider in Thinfilm who today
received 750,000 subscription rights ("SRs") under Thinfilm's 2016 Subscription
Rights Incentive Plan.

Following the transaction, Ole Ronny Thorsnes and close associates hold or
control 750,000 SRs in Thinfilm.

Tauseef Bashir, EVP Global Sales and primary insider in Thinfilm who today
received 500,000 subscription rights ("SRs") under Thinfilm's 2016 Subscription
Rights Incentive Plan.

Following the transaction, Mr Bashir and close associates hold or control
500,000 SRs in Thinfilm.

The exercise price of the SRs is NOK 5.05 per share. 25 per cent of the SRs vest
at each anniversary from the date of the grant. The SRs expire on 10 May 2021.


11 August 2016
Thin Film Electronics ASA

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
