


On 22 August 2018, the following primary insiders purchased shares in Thin Film
Electronics ASA ("Thinfilm"):

Chairman Morten Opstad purchased 300,000 shares through his wholly owned company
Marc O Polo Norge AS at an average price of NOK 1.11 per share. Following the
transaction, Mr. Opstad and his close associates hold or control 1,986,708
shares and 0 incentive subscription rights in Thinfilm.

Board Member Laura Oliphant purchased 10,000 ADRs (American Depositary Receipts;
corresponding to 100,000 shares) at an average price of USD 1.39 per ADR.
Following the transaction, Ms. Oliphant holds or controls 375,000 shares,
53,000 ADRs and 0 incentive subscription rights in Thinfilm.

Board Member Richard Bernstein purchased 500,000 shares at an average price of
NOK 1.10 per share. Following the transaction, Mr. Bernstein holds or controls
500,000 shares and 0 incentive subscription rights in Thinfilm.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
