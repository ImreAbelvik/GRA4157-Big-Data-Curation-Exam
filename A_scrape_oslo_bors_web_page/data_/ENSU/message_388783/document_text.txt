


Thin Film Electronics ASA ("Thinfilm") issues this notice on behalf of certain
primary insiders in Thinfilm who today received subscription rights ("SRs")
under Thinfilm's 2015 Subscription Rights Incentive Plan.

Davor Sutija, CEO, received 1,250,000 SRs. Following the transaction, Mr. Sutija
and close associates hold or control 2,200,000 shares and 6,500,000 SRs in
Thinfilm.

John Afzelius-Jenevall, CFO, received 500,000 SRs. Following the transaction,
Mr. Afzelius-Jenevall and close associates hold or control 2,150,000 SRs in
Thinfilm.

Christer Karlsson, CTO, received 500,000 SRs. Following the transaction, Mr.
Karlsson and close associates hold or control 215,000 shares and 2,700,000 SRs
in Thinfilm.

Peter Fischer, Chief Product Officer, received 500,000 SRs. Following the
transaction, Mr. Fischer and close associates hold or control 1,450,000 SRs in
Thinfilm.

Kai Leppänen, Chief Commercial Officer, received 500,000 SRs. Following the
transaction, Mr. Leppänen and close associates hold or control 1,700,000 SRs in
Thinfilm.

Henrik Sjöberg, Senior Vice President of Product Management received 500,000
SRs. Following the transaction, Mr. Sjöberg and close associates hold or control
1,650,000 SRs in Thinfilm.

Anders Harnes, Sr. Director Finance & Accounting, received 100,000 SRs.
Following the transaction, Mr. Harnes and close associates hold or control
450,000 SRs in Thinfilm.

The exercise price of the SRs is NOK 3.95 per share. 25 per cent of the SRs vest
at each anniversary from the date of the grant. The SRs expire on 7 May 2020.
