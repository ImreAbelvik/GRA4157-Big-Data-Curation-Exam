Marianne Hagen, board member in Treasure ASA (the company), has today 20 February 2024 bought 1,000 shares in Treasure ASA at an average price of NOK 20.30. Following the transaction, Marianne Hagen owns 1,000 shares in the company. Please refer to the attached notification of transaction for further details. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
