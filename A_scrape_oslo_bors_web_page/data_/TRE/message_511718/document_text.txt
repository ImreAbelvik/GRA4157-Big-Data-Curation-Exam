Primary insider and chairman of Treasure ASA, Thomas Wilhelmsen has today, (in form of a testamentary inheritance), inherited 14.000 shares of Treasure ASA.
 
New number of shares owned and controlled by Mr. Wilhelmsen is 1.165.095 shares or 0,53% of outstanding shares.
 
In addition Mr. Wilhelmsen, through holding companies, is the controlling shareholder of Wilh. Wilhelmsen Holding ASA; the latter owning 160.000.000 shares of Treasure ASA or 73,46% of outstanding shares.
