


Primary insider Sigurd Pettersen, Regional Director in Grieg Seafood Hjaltland
UK Ltd., has redeemed 50,000 synthetic share options in Grieg Seafood ASA at a
redemption rate of NOK 24.03. The settlement is paid in cash. The synthetic
share option program beneficiary is obliged to re-invest 50% of his proceeds
from the program in Grieg Seafood ASA shares. This obligation is enforced until
the beneficiary owns 100% of his gross annual salary. Sigurd Pettersen has after
this 50,000 synthetic share options available in Grieg Seafood ASA.

Sigurd Pettersen is part of Grieg Seafood ASA's synthetic share option program,
and in this connection he has an obligation to hold shares in Grieg Seafood ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
