Grieg Seafood ASA have on 5 December acquired 6 390 Grieg Seafood ASA shares at
NOK 116.15 per share.


3 260 shares have been further sold to Grant Cumming, Managing Director of Grieg
Seafood Shetland Ltd.,
and 3 130 shares have been further sold to Marvin D. Boschman, Managing Director
of Grieg Seafood BC Ltd for NOK 116.15 per share.

They are both part of Grieg Seafood ASA's synthetic share option program, and in
this connection, they have an obligation to hold shares in Grieg Seafood ASA.

Total shareholding after this share purchase and sale for Grieg Seafood ASA is
1 228 424 shares.

Total shareholding after this share purchase for Grant Cumming is 3 425 shares.

Total shareholding after this share purchase for Marvin D. Boschman is 3 295
shares.
