


Andreas Kvame, CEO in Grieg Seafood ASA, has on 23 August 2016 acquired 14,000
Grieg Seafood ASA shares at NOK 50.89 per share. Andreas Kvame's total
shareholding after this share purchase is 29,000 shares.

Knut Utheim, COO in Grieg Seafood ASA, has on 23 August 2016 acquired 8,900
Grieg Seafood ASA shares at NOK 50.25 per share. Knut Utheim's total
shareholding after this share purchase is 12,400 shares.

Both primary insiders are part of Grieg Seafood ASA's synthetic share option
program, and in this connection they have an obligation to hold shares in Grieg
Seafood ASA.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
