


Sigurd Pettersen, regional director in Grieg Seafood Shetland Ltd., has on 25
August 2015 acquired 1,500 Grieg Seafood ASA shares at NOK 25.50 per share.
Total shareholding after this share purchase is 3,300 shares.

Sigurd Pettersen is part of Grieg Seafood ASA's synthetic share option program,
and in this connection he has an obligation to hold shares in Grieg Seafood ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
