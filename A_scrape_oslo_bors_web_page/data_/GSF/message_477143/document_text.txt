Grieg Seafood ASA have on 15 May 2019 acquired 6 887 Grieg Seafood ASA shares at
NOK 106.77 per share.


3 858 shares have been further sold to Grant Cumming, Managing Director of Grieg
Seafood Shetland Ltd., and 3 029 shares have been further sold to Marvin D.
Boschman, Managing Director of Grieg Seafood BC Ltd for NOK 106.77 per share.


They are both part of Grieg Seafood ASA's synthetic share option program, and in
this connection, they have an obligation to hold shares in Grieg Seafood ASA.


Total shareholding after this share purchase and sale for Grieg Seafood ASA is
1 228 424 shares.


Total shareholding after this share purchase for Grant Cumming is 7 283 shares.


Total shareholding after this share purchase for Marvin D. Boschman is 6 324
shares.





This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
