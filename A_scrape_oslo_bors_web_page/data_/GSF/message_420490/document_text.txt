



Andreas Kvame, CEO in Grieg Seafood ASA, has on 17 February 2017 acquired 6,000
Grieg Seafood ASA shares at NOK 72 per share. Andreas Kvame's total shareholding
after this share purchase is 35,000 shares.

Knut Utheim, COO in Grieg Seafood ASA, has on 17 February 2017 acquired 5,800
Grieg Seafood ASA shares at NOK 72 per share. Knut Utheim's total shareholding
after this share purchase is 18,200 shares.

Both primary insiders are part of Grieg Seafood ASA's synthetic share option
program, and in this connection they have an obligation to hold shares in Grieg
Seafood ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
