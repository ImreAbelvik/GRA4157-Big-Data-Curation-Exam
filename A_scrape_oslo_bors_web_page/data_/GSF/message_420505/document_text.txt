


Atle Harald Sandtorv, CFO in Grieg Seafood ASA, has on 17 February 2017 sold
8,868 Grieg Seafood ASA shares at NOK 70.59 per share (volume weighted average
rate of exchange).

Total shareholding after this transaction is 21,793 shares.

Atle Harald Sandtorv is part of Grieg Seafood ASA's synthetic share option
program, and in this connection he has an obligation to hold shares in Grieg
Seafood ASA. Atle Harald Sandtorv holds 111,274 unredeemed options. After the
transaction he still has fulfilled the legal obligation to hold shares according
to the company's synthetic share options program.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
