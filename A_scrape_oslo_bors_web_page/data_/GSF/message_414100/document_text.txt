



Alexander Knudsen, managing director in Grieg Seafood Rogaland AS, has on 18
November 2016 sold 15,000 Grieg Seafood ASA shares at NOK 81.00 per share.

Total shareholding after this transaction is 29,000 shares.

Alexander Knudsen is part of Grieg Seafood ASA's synthetic share option program,
and in this connection he has an obligation to hold shares in Grieg Seafood ASA.
Alexander Knudsen holds 192,379 unredeemed options. After the transaction, he
still has fulfilled the legal obligation to hold shares according to the
company's synthetic share options program.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
