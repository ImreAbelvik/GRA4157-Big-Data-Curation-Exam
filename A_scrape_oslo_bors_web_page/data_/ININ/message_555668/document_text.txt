Øivind Horpestad, chairman of the board and CEO in ELOP AS, has through the company Gimle Invest AS, on March 4th bought a total of 150,000 shares in ELOP AS at an average price of NOK 2.535 pr share. Following the transaction Øivind Horpestad and related parties control 9,016,734 shares in ELOP AS.
