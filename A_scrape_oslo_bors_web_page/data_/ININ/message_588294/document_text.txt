Leif Chr. Salomonsen, Chairman of the Board of Inin Group AS has through the company Gracchus AS, on April 21st, bought a total of 75.000 shares in Inin Group AS at an average price of NOK 3,45 per share. Following the transaction Leif Salomonsen and related parties’ control 272.912 shares in Inin Group.

Kristian Lundkvist, Member of the Board of Inin Group AS, has through the company Middelborg Invest AS, on April 21st, bought a total of 75.000 shares in Inin Group AS at an average price of NOK 3,45 per share. Following the transaction Kristian Lundkvist and related parties’ control 6.306.842 shares in Inin Group AS through Middelborg Invest AS and Zono Invest AS.

Øivind Horpestad, CEO of Inin Group AS, has through the company Gimle Invest AS, on April 21st, bought a total of 50.000 shares in Inin Group AS at an average price of NOK 3,45 per share. Following the transaction Øivind Horpestad and related parties’ control 14.435.736 shares in Inin Group AS.
