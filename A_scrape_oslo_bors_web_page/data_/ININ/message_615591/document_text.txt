Øivind Horpestad, CEO of Inin Group AS, has through the company Gimle Invest AS, on April 10th, bought a total of 130,000 shares in Inin Group AS at an average price of NOK 3.41 per share. Following the transaction Øivind Horpestad and related parties’ control 13,933,164 shares in Inin Group AS.

Kristian Lundkvist, member of the board, has through the company Zono Invest AS, traded according to the attached documents.
