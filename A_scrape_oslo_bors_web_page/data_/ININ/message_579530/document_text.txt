Øivind Horpestad, CEO of Inin Group AS, has through the company Gimle Invest AS, from the 30th of December 2022 to the 2nd of January 2023 bought a total of 197 823 shares in Inin Group AS at an average price of NOK 1.811 per share. Following the transaction Øivind Horpestad and related parties’ control 13,786,709 shares in Inin Group AS.
