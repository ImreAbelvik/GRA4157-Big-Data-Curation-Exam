Gjermund Sogn, CEO of Nordic Infrastructure Group AS, has through the company Sogn Invest AS, on October 21st bought a total of 50,000 shares in ELOP AS at an average price of NOK 2.145 per share. 

Following the transaction Gjermund Sogn and related parties’ control 12,732,467 shares in ELOP AS.
