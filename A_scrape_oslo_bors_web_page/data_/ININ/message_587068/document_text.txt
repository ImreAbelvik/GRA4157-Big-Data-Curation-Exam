Øivind Horpestad, CEO of Inin Group AS, has through the company Gimle Invest AS, on April 3rd, bought a total of 50,000 shares in Inin Group AS at an average price of NOK 2.79 per share. Following the transaction Øivind Horpestad and related parties’ control 14,185,736 shares in Inin Group AS.
