Arne Arthur Molland, Senior Financial Controller at Inin Group AS, has through the company Vikastøl Invest AS, between December 23rd and 28th, bought a total of 50,000 shares in Inin Group AS at an average price of NOK 1.73 per share. 

Following the transaction Arne Arthur Molland and related parties’ control 271,626 shares in Inin Group AS.
