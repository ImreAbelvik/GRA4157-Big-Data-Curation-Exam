Øivind Horpestad, CEO in ELOP AS, has through the company Gimle Invest AS, on September 27th bought a total of 50,000 shares in ELOP AS at an average price of NOK 2.048 pr share. 

Following the transaction Øivind Horpestad and related parties control 12,007,965 shares in ELOP AS.
