Gjermund Sogn, CEO of Nordic Infrastructure Group AS, has through the company Sogn Invest AS, on January 10th bought a total of 10,000 shares in Inin Group AS at an average price of NOK 2.14 per share. Following the transaction Gjermund Sogn and related parties’ control 13,417,538 shares in Inin Group AS.
