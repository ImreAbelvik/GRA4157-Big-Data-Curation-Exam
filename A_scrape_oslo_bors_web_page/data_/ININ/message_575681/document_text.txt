Øivind Horpestad, CEO of Inin Group AS (formerly ELOP AS), has through the company Gimle Invest AS, on November 14th bought a total of 54,029 shares in Inin Group AS at an average price of NOK 1.89 per share. Following the transaction Øivind Horpestad and related parties’ control 13,525,100 shares in Inin Group AS.
