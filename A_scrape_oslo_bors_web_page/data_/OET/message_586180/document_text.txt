GREECE, March 24, 2023 – Okeanis Eco Tankers Corp. (“OET”) (Oslo Børs: OET) has been notified that QVT Financial LP (“QVT”), legal person closely associated with primary insider and member of the Board of Directors of OET, Daniel Gold, on March 23, 2023, sold 14,209 shares at a price of NOK 262.448 per share. Following the transaction, QVT holds 1,683,492 shares in OET. 

Primary Insider notification, pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) article 19, is enclosed. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
