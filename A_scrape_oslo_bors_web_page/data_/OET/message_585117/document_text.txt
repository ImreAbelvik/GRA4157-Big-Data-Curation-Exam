GREECE, March 13, 2023 – Okeanis Eco Tankers Corp. (“OET”) (Oslo Børs: OET) announces that Aristidis Alafouzos, Chief Executive Officer of OET, has today, 13 March 2023, purchased 22,000 shares in OET at a price of NOK 234.202 per share. Following the transaction, Aristidis Alafouzos owns 49,800 shares in OET.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
