On November 20, 2019, a fund managed by Ironsides Partners (“Ironsides”), sold an aggregate 62,049 shares in Okeanis Eco Tankers Corp. (“OET”) at a price of NOK 100.2096 per share.

Ironsides hereafter owns a total of 1,767,951 shares in OET, or 5.40% of the registered share capital. Ironsides’s CIO and founder, Robert Knapp, is a member of the board of directors of OET.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 4-4 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
