On March 27, 2020, a fund managed by Ironsides Partners (“Ironsides”), sold an aggregate 50,000 shares in Okeanis Eco Tankers Corp. (“OET”) at a price of NOK 61.688 per share.

Ironsides hereafter owns a total of 1,548,558 shares in OET, or 4.71% of the registered share capital. Ironsides’s CIO and founder, Robert Knapp, is a member of the board of directors of OET.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
