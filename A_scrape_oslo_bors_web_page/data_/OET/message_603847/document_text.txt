ATHENS, GREECE, November 13, 2023 – Okeanis Eco Tankers Corp. (“OET”) (Oslo Børs: OET) has been notified that on November 13, 2023, VR Capital Group Ltd. (“VRCG”) and VR Global Partners L.P. (“VRGP”), closely associated parties with primary insider and member of the Board of Directors of OET, Joshua Nemser, sold respectively 56,642 shares and 243,358 shares in OET at a price of NOK 290.50 per share. 

Following the transactions, VRCG holds 109,805 shares and VRGP holds 471,767 shares in OET. 

Primary Insider notification, pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) article 19, is enclosed.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
