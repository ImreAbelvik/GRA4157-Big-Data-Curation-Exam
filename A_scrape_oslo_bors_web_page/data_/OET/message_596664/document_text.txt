ATHENS, GREECE, August 12, 2023 – Okeanis Eco Tankers Corp. (“OET”) (Oslo Børs: OET) has been notified that on August 11, 2023, a fund managed by QVT Financial LP (“QVT”), legal person closely associated with primary insider and Member of the Board of Directors, Daniel Gold, sold 56,736 shares in OET at a price of NOK 266.7088 per share. Following the transaction, QVT holds 1,626,756 shares in OET. 

Primary Insider notification, pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) article 19, is enclosed. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
