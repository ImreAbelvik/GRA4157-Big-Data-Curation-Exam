A fund managed by QVT Financial LP (“QVT”) purchased 768 shares in Okeanis Eco Tankers Corp. (“Okeanis”) at a price of NOK 56.4 per share on September 22, 2020 and another 7,131 shares in Okeanis at a price of NOK 56.14 per share on September 23, 2020.

QVT thereafter owns a total of 2,196,185 shares in Okeanis, or 6.68% of the registered share capital. QVT’s CEO and founder, Dan Gold, is a member of the board of directors of Okeanis.
