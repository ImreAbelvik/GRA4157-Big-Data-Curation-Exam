On 14 October 2019, Ironsides Partners LLC and an affiliated entity, Ironsides P Fund LP (together, "Ironsides") sold an aggregate 50,000 shares in Okeanis Eco Tankers Corp. (“OET”) at a price of NOK 100.0 per share.

Ironsides hereafter owns a total of 1,780,000 shares in OET, or 5.69% of the registered share capital. Robert Knapp, CIO and founder of Ironsides, is a member of the board of directors of OET.
