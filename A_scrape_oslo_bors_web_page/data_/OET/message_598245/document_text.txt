ATHENS, GREECE, August 29, 2023 – Okeanis Eco Tankers Corp. (“OET”) (Oslo Børs: OET) has been notified that on August 29, 2023, VR Capital Group Ltd. (“VRCG”) and VR Global Partners L.P. (“VRGP”), closely associated parties with primary insider and member of the Board of Directors of OET, Joshua Nemser, sold respectively 3,987 shares and 17,130 shares in OET at a price of NOK 270.00 per share. 

Following the transactions, VRCG holds 166,447 shares and VRGP holds 715,125 shares in OET. 

Primary Insider notification, pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) article 19, is enclosed. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
