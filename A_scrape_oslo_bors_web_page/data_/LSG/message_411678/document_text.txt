


Lerøy Seafood Group ASA (LSG) has the 20(th) of October 2016 bought 554 019
shares in Havfisk ASA (Havfisk) at an average price of NOK 36.4963 per share.
Ownership after this transaction is 81 750 082 shares in Havfisk, representing
96.58% of the shares and the votes in the Company.

Helge Singelstad and Arne Møgster, both representing Lerøy, are members of the
board of directors of Havfisk ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

Dated: 21 October 2016
