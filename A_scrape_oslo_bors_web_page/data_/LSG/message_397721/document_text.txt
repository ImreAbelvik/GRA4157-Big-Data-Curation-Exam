Austevoll, 18 March 2016

Austevoll Seafood ASA ("Austevoll") has today sold 
2,750,000 shares in Lerøy Seafood Group ASA ("Lerøy") 
at a price of NOK 355 per share. After the 
transaction, Austevoll owns 31,394,281 shares in 
Lerøy, corresponding to approximately 57.52 per cent 
of the share capital in Lerøy.

Austevoll has entered into a customary lock up of the 
remaining of the shares for a period of 90 calendar 
days from closing of the transaction.

Pareto Securities AS acted as Sole Bookrunner for the 
transaction. 

Austevoll is currently a primary insider in Lerøy and 
is represented on the Board of Directors of Lerøy by 
Helge Singelstad, the Chairman of the Board of 
Austevoll, who acts as the Chairman of the Board, Arne 
Møgster, the CEO of Austevoll, who acts as a Board 
member, and Britt Kathrine Drivenes, the CFO of 
Austevoll, who acts as a Board member.  

This information is subject to the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act.
