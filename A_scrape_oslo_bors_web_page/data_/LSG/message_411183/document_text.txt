


Reference is made to the offer document dated 16 September 2016 regarding the
mandatory offer (the "Offer") to acquire all outstanding shares in Havfisk ASA
(the "Company") for an offer price of NOK 36.50 per share made by Lerøy Seafood
Group ASA ("Lerøy"), with an acceptance period from 19 September 2016 to 17
October 2016 at 18:00 CET (subject to extension).

Lerøy has on 13 October 2016 received acceptances for 7,490 shares in the
Company under the Offer. Following this, Lerøy has received acceptances under
the Offer for a total of 3,265,627 shares in the Company.

Including the 57,692,157 shares already held by Lerøy, this will aggregate to
60,957,784 shares in Havfisk, representing 72.01% of the shares and the votes in
the Company.
