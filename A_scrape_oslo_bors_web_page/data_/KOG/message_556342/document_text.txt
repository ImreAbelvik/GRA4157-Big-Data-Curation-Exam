Oslo, 11 March 2022 

Janne Katrine Haugen, closely associated with Hans Petter Blokkum, Executive Vice President HR and Security in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA has on 11 March 2022 purchased 185 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA at an average price of NOK 323.56 per share. Following this transaction Janne Katrine Haugen holds 923 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
