Oslo, 16 May 2022 

Janne Katrine Haugen, closely associated with Hans Petter Blokkum, Executive Vice President HR and Security in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA has on 11 May 2022 purchased 147 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA at an average price of NOK 341.20 per share. Following this transaction Janne Katrine Haugen holds 1 070 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
