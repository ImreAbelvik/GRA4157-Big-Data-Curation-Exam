Harald Aarø, Group Executive Vice President Business Development & CSO has today, Tuesday 30 October, purchased 1 500 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA. The shares were purchased at a price of NOK 136/share. After the purchase, Aarø owns 5 075 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to §5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
