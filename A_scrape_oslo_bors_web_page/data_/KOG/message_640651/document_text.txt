Kongsberg, 6 March 2025

On 6 March 2025, Even Aas, Group Executive President of Public Affairs, Communication and Sustainability, and primary insider of Kongsberg Gruppen ASA, sold 5 000 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA at a price of NOK 1 615.31 per share. Following the transaction, Even Aas owns 26 873 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
