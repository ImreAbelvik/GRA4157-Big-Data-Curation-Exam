According to KONGSBERG's long-term incentive (LTI) program adopted by the Annual General Meeting in 2012, KONGSBERG on 27 March 2020 sold 44 081 KONGSBERG shares at price 152.30 per share to participants in the program. The price is set in accordance with the guidelines for the LTI. There is a three-year lock-in period for the shares. Enclosed is a list of primary insiders that purchased shares through the program. After this transaction, KONGSBERG holds 1 024 698 own shares.
