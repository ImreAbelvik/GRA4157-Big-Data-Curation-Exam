Following approval by Kongsberg Gruppen ASA's  Annual 
General Meeting in 2012, the Board of Directors has 
established a long term incentive program (LTI) in the 
form of a remuneration of 25 percent and 15-20 percent of 
annual base salary for, respectively, the Chief Executive 
Officer and the other members of the corporate management 
team.
The LTI payment is conditional upon KONGSBERG having 
obtained a positive EBIT in the previous year. The 
participants are required to invest the net amount after 
tax in KONGSBERG shares and such shares must be held for 
at least three years.

Enclosed is the list of the following primary insiders 
have on 7 April 2015 acquired KONGSBERG shares under the 
above mentioned program at NOK 136.11 per share.

A total of 17,856 shares have been sold by KONGSBERG to 
the participants in the long term incentive program.

KONGSBERG holds 288,069 own shares after this transaction.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities 
Trading Act.
