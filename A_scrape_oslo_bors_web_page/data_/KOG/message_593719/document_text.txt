Today, 20 June 2023, Kongsberg Gruppen ASA has started the transfer of 691 443 own shares at price NOK 460.24 per share in conjunction with a share program for employees. The shares are purchased in the market from 30th of March 2023 to 15th of June 2023 and the share price corresponds to the average purchase price adjusted for dividends. The participants receive 25 % discount on the purchase price. The discount from market price is subject to taxation. A total of 5 194 employees participated in the program. The maximum number of shares per employee was 174. There is a lock-in period of one year for the shares.
