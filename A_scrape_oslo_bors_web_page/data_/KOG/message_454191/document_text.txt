Today, 20 June 2018, Kongsberg Gruppen ASA has sold 446,868 own shares at price NOK 138.6 per share in conjunction with a share program for all employees. The discount from market price is subject to taxation. The shares are transferred to each employee's VPS account. A total of 2,269 employees participated in the program. The maximum number of shares per employee was 216. There is a lock-in period of one year for the shares. Enclosed is a list of primary insiders that have participated in the program. After this transaction, Kongsberg Gruppen ASA owns 19,869 own shares.
