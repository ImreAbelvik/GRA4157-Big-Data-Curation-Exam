Today, 20 June 2017, Kongsberg Gruppen ASA has sold 550,951 own 
shares at price NOK 103.60 per share in conjunction with a share 
program for all employees.

The discount of NOK 24.40 per share, from market price NOK 128.00, 
is subject to taxation. The shares will be transfered to each 
employee's VPS account approximately 20 June 2017.

A total of 2,132 employees participated in the program. The maximum 
number of shares per employee was 289. There is a lock-in period of 
one year for the shares.

Enclosed is a list of primary insiders that have participated in the 
program.

After this transaction, Kongsberg Gruppen ASA owns 8,961 own shares.
