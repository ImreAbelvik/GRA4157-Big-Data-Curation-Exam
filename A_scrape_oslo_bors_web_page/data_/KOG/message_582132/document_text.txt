Oslo, 13 February 2023 

Bård Brath Ingerø, closely associated with Gyrid Skalleberg Ingerø, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA has on 10 February 2023 purchased 8 800 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA at an average price of NOK 381.69 per share. Following this transaction Bård Brath Ingerø holds 8 800 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
