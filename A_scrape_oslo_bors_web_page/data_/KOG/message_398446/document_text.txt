According to KONGSBERG's long-term incentive program 
adopted by the Annual General Meeting in 2012, 
KONGSBERG on 1 April 2016 sold 16,512 KONGSBERG shares 
at price 128.08 per share to participants in the 
program. For current members of the corporate 
management team there is a three-year lock-in period 
for the shares.

Enclosed is a list of primary insiders that purchased 
shares through the program.

KONGSBERG holds 349,459 own shares after this 
transaction.

This information is subject of the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act.
