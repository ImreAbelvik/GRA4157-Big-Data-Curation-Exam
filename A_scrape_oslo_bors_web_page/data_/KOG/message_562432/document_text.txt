Oslo, 16 May 2022
 
Hans Petter Blokkum, Executive Vice President HR and Security in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA has on 16 May 2022 purchased 585 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA at an average price of NOK 338.77 per share. Following this transaction Hans Petter Blokkum holds 9 647 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
