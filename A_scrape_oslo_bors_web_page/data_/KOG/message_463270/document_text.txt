AGSE Consulting, 50% owned by Anne-Grete Strøm-Erichsen, Member of the board in Kongsberg Gruppen, and 50% owned by her husband Arve Strøm-Erichsen had today, Friday 9 November, purchased 2,000 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA. The shares were purchased at a price of NOK 113.00/share. After the purchase, AGSE Consulting owns 2,000 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
