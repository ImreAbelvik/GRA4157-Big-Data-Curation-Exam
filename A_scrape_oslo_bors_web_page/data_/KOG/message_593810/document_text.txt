20 June 2023, Kongsberg Gruppen ASA transferred own shares at price NOK 460.24 per share in conjunction with the share program for employees as announced 20th of June 2023.  

This notification and attached document provide an overview of related parties of primary insiders who have purchased shares through the program.

Shares in the company have been transferred to the following related parties of primary insiders on 20 June 2022:
Pål Rønning Haugan has purchased 174 shares
Sheila Kristina Hoff has purchased 174 shares

After these transactions the new holdings are:
Pål Haugan 408 shares
Sheila Kristina Hoff 174 shares

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to §5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act and requirements under the EU Market Abuse Regulation.

Kongsberg (OSE-ticker: KOG) is an international, knowledge-based group that supplies high-tech systems and solutions to customers in the energy, merchant, navy and defence and aerospace industries. Kongsberg has more than 12,000 employees in 39 countries and had total revenues of NOK 31.8bn in 2022.
