Pål Rønning Haugan, close associate of Lisa Edvardsen Haugan, President of Kongsberg Maritime, has today on 10 July 2024 sold 408 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA at a price of NOK 955 per share. Following the transaction, Pål Rønning Haugan owns 205 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
