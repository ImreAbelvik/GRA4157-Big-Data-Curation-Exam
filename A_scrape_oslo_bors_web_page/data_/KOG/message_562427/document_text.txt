Oslo, 16 May 2022 

Geir Håøy, CEO and President in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA has on 16 May 2022 purchased 735 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA at an average price of NOK 341.60 per share. Following this transaction Geir Håøy holds 44 075 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
