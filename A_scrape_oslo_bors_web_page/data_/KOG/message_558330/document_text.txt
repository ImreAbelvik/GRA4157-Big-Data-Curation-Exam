Kongsberg Gruppen ASA (KONGSBERG) has today sold a total of 31 710 own shares at a price of NOK 284.79 per share in connection with the long-term incentive program (LTI) for senior management and key employees. The price has been set in accordance with the guidelines in the long-term intensive program and based on the purchase price KONGSBERG achieved in the market, please see stock exchange announcement on 18 February 2022. There is a three-year lock-in period for the shares.
