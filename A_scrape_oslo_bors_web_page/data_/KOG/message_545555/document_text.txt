Oslo, 29 October 2021 

Gry Pedersen Solberg, deputy board member in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA has on 29 October 2021 sold 180 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA at a price of NOK 278.00 per share. Following this transaction Gry Pedersen Solberg holds 455 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
