Morten Henriksen, Board member in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA has on 24th of May 2022 purchased 1 933 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA at an average price of NOK 340.00 per share. Following this transaction Hans Petter Blokkum holds 4 960 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA. 
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
