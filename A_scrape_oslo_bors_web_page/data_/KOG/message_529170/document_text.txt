Kongsberg Gruppen ASA (KONGSBERG) has today sold a total of 46 089 own shares at a price of NOK 179.63 per share in connection with the long-term incentive program (LTI) for senior management and key employees. The price has been set in accordance with the guidelines in the long-term intensive program and based on the purchase price KONGSBERG achieved in the market, please see stock exchange announcement on 5 March 2021. There is a three-year lock-in period for the shares. 

This notification and attached document provide an overview of primary insiders who have purchased shares through the program.

KONGSBERG (OSE-ticker: KOG) is an international, leading global technology corporation delivering mission-critical systems and solutions with extreme performance for customers that operate under extremely challenging conditions.
