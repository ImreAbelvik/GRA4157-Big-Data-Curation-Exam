Kongsberg Gruppen ASA has today sold 583,382 
Kongsberg shares at a price of NOK 115.60 as a 
result of an employee share program. The discount 
of 36.90 NOK per share, based on 152.5 NOK, is 
subject to tax.

After the sale Kongsberg Gruppen ASA owns 15,971 
Kongsberg shares. A total of 2,521 employees have 
been assigned shares. The maximum number of 
shares per employee was 260. The shares will be 
transferred to each employees VPS account around 
19 June 2015.

taking part in the program, showing their 
balance of KONGSBERG shares.

This information is subject of the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
