Mocca Invest AS owned by Eivind Reiten, Board member in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA has on 13th of June 2022 purchased 1 000 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA at an average price of NOK 323.35 per share. Following this transaction Mocca Invest AS holds 3 850 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA. 
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in MAR regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
