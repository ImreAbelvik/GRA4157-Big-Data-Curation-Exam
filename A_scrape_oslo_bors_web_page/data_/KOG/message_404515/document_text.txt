Today, Kongsberg Gruppen ASA has sold 571,896 own 
shares at price NOK 105.20 per share in conjunction 
with a share program for all employees.

The discount of NOK 20.30 per share, from market price 
NOK 125.50, is subject to taxation. The shares will be 
transfered to each employee's VPS account 
approximately 20 June 2016.

A total of 2,244 employees participated in the 
program. The maximum number of shares per employee was 
285. There is a lock-in period of one year for the 
shares.

Enclosed is a list of primary insiders that have 
participated in the program.

After this transaction, Kongsberg Gruppen ASA owns 
1,049 own shares.
