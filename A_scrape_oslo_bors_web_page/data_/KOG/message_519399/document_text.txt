Kongsberg Gruppen's board member, Per Arthur Sørlie, has today, Tuesday 1 December, purchased 2 000 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen ASA. The shares were purchased at a price of NOK 171.80/share. After the purchase, Sørlie owns 3 400 shares in Kongsberg Gruppen. 

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to §5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
