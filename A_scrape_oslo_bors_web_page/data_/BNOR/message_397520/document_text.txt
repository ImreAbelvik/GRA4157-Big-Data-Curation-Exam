Oslo, 17 March 2016: Silje Augustson, Group CEO and member of the Board of
Directors of Norwegian Energy Company ASA ("Noreco"), has on 16 March 2016
purchased 10,000 shares in Noreco at a price of NOK 47 per share. Silje
Augustson owns 10,000 shares in Noreco after the transaction and has the right
to acquire or subscribe up to 100,000 additional shares, in aggregate 110,000
shares, equal to 1.55% of the outstanding number of shares in the company.
