Oslo, 17 March 2016: Riulf Rustad, Chairman of the Board of Directors of
Norwegian Energy Company ASA ("Noreco"), has on 16 March 2016 through his wholly
owned company Ousdal AS purchased 127,770 shares in Noreco at a price of NOK 47
per share. Riulf Rustad (indirectly) owns 157,975 shares in Noreco after the
transaction and has the right to acquire or subscribe up to 100,000 additional
shares, in aggregate 257,975 shares, equal to 3.64% of the outstanding number of
shares in the company.
