Atle Sonesen, Chief Operating Officer of Norwegian Energy Company ASA ("Noreco"
or "the Company"), has on 29 May 2020 purchased 7,000 shares in Noreco at a
price of NOK 140.4281 per share. Atle Sonesen owns 7,000 shares in Noreco after
the transaction and has the right to acquire or subscribe up to 100,000
additional shares in the Company, in aggregate 107,000 shares.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
of 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
