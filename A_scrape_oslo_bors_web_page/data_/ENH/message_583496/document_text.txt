Limassol, Cyprus - 24 February 2023 - Mr Sondre Helsing, Board member, has
through his wholly owned company Hubris Industrier AS today purchased 325,000
shares in Seabird Exploration Plc at a price of NOK 4,54 per share. Following
this transaction Mr Helsing owns 1,208,333 shares in the Company.



DISCLOSURE REGULATION

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the
Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading
Act.
