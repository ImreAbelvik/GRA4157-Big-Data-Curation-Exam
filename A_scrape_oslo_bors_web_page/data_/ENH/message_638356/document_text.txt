Nicosia, Cyprus - 12 February 2025 - Mr Sondre Helsing, Board member, has
through his wholly owned company Hubris Industrier AS on 11 February purchased
63,627 shares in Seabird Exploration Plc at an average price of NOK 6.4195 per
share. Following this transaction Mr. Helsing owns 1,308,333 shares,
representing approximately 1.63% of the outstanding shares in Seabird
Exploration Plc.

DISCLOSURE REGULATION

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the
Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading
Act.
