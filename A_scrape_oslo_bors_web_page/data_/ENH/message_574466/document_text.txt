Mandatory notification of trade primary insiders

Limassol, Cyprus - 01 November 2022 - Reference is made to the stock exchange
notice from Green Energy Group (Seabird Exploration Plc) (the "Company") dated
25 October 2022 regarding completion of a subsequent offering of 2,000,000 new
shares in the Company.

The Company's CFO, Mr Sveinung Alvestad, was allocated 4,843 shares in the
subsequent offering. Following this Mr. Alvestad holds 44,843 shares and 300,000
options in the Company.

 

DISCLOSURE REGULATION

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the
Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading
Act.
