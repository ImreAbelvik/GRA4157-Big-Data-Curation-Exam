9 December 2020, Limassol, Cyprus

The Company's Executive Chairman, Mr Ståle Rodahl has today purchased 100,000
shares in Seabird Exploration Plc at a price of NOK 5.77 per share. Following
this transaction Mr Rodahl owns 675,000 shares and 360,000 options in the
Company.

SeaBird is a global provider of high quality marine seismic operations within
the source vessel and 2D market, as well as in the shallow water 3D market The
Company has a strong focus on Quality, Health, Safety and Environment, combined
with cost efficient services to its customers.
