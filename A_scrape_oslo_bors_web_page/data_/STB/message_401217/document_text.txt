Drew Holding Ltd ("Drew"), a company affiliated with 
Tor Olav Troim, has on 4 May extended a forward 
contract in Storebrand with exposure to 4.950.000 
shares. The new forward contract expires 4 Nov 2016 
and has a forward price of 29.71.

In addition Drew has acquired a forward contract for a 
further 1.000.000 shares. This forward contract 
expires 4 Nov at price 34.56.

Including the shares held directly by Drew, its total 
exposure in Storebrand has increased from 7.500.000 to 
8.500.000 shares following these transactions.

Midelfart Capital AS ("Midelfart") has, on 4 May 2016, 
acquired 300.000 shares in Storebrand at a price of 
NOK34.17 per share.

Midelfart's total holding in Storebrand has increased 
from 2.700.000 to 3.000.000 shares following this 
transaction.
