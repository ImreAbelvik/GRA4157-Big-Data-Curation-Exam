In connection with Storebrand's remuneration program for management where part
of the salary is paid in shares, Camilla Leikvoll, Executive Vice President
Retail, have been awarded 853 shares in Storebrand ASA on 29 November 2023 at
NOK 92.92 per share. After the transaction Camilla Leikvoll owns 12,758 shares
in Storebrand ASA.

Please see further details about the transaction in the attached form.

Storebrand ASA has today sold 12,743 shares at 92.92 per share to leading
employees. After the transaction Storebrand owns 16,686,420 shares, representing
3.58% of Storebrand ASA's share capital.

Lysaker, 29 November 2023
