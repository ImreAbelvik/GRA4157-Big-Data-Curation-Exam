Medistim ASA has transferred 10,000 shares to Medistim CEO Kari Krogstad. It is part of her agreement with the Board of Medistim that she receives 10,000 shares. The agreement has been previously described in the Company's Annual Report. Medistims last traded share price in 2018 was 71.00 so the shares are transferred at this cost. 
Medistim ASA has 158,500 own shares and a after the transfer to Kari Krogstad the company has 148,500 own shares shares. Kari Krogstad has after the latest transfer 122,500 shares in Medistim ASA.
