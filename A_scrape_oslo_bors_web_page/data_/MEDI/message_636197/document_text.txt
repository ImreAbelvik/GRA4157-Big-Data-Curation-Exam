Intertrade Shipping AS has today acquired 30,000 shares in Medistim ASA at a price of NOK 147 per share.
After the acquisition, Intertrade Shipping AS owns 30,000 shares in Medistim ASA.  
Intertrade Shipping AS owns 100% of the subsidiary Fløtemarken AS.Fløtemarken AS already owns 1,285,000 shares in Medistim ASA. In total after today's purchase, Intertrade Shipping AS controls 1,315,000 shares in Medistim ASA.

Intertrade Shipping AS is majority-owned and controlled by Øyvin A. Brøymer, Chairman of the Board of Directors of Medistim ASA. After the purchase Øyvin A. Brøymer holds 7.17% of the shares in Medistim ASA.
