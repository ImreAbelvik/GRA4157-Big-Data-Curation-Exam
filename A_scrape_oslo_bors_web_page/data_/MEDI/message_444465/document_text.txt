The Board of Directors in Medistim ASA decided at the board meeting the 14th of February to transfer 5,000 shares to Medistim CFO Thomas Jakobsen.  Medistims last traded share price the 14 th of February was NOK 79.00 per share so the shares are transferred at this cost. 

Medistim ASA has 163,500 own shares and a after the transfer to Thomas Jakobsen the company has 158,500 own shares. Thomas Jakobsen has after the latest transfer 75,000 shares in Medistim ASA.
