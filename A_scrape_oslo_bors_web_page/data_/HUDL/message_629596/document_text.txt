14.10.2024 16:35:01 CEST | Huddlestock Fintech AS | Mandatory notification of
trade primary insiders

Reference is made to the announcement published today 14 October 2024 by
Huddlestock Fintech AS (the "Company" or "Huddlestock") (ticker: HUDL) regarding
the increase of the Company's share capital through the conversion of NOK
47,681,168.60 in debt owed to Bricknode Holding AB , a close associate of board
members Stefan Willebrand and Erik Hagelin by issuance of 18,338,911 new shares
(the "Conversion Shares") at a price of NOK 2.60 per share.

Following the issuance of the Conversion Shares, Bricknode Holding AB owns
18,338,911 shares in the Company.

Attached are the notifications of the transaction in accordance with the Market
Abuse Regulation Article 19.
