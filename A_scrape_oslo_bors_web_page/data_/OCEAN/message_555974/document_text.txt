Ocean GeoLoop AS: Mandatory notification of trades
 
Certain primary insiders of Ocean GeoLoop AS (the "Company" or "Ocean GeoLoop") has today on 9 March 2022 bought shares in the Company as further described below: 

- AB Investment AS, controlled by Anders Onarheim, Chairman of the Board of Ocean GeoLoop, has today bought 25,000 shares in Ocean GeoLoop at an average price of NOK 33 per share. After the transaction, AB Investment AS holds 3,613,000 shares in Ocean GeoLoop.

- K4 Invest AS, controlled by Ole Rogstad Jørstad, member of the Board of Ocean GeoLoop, has today bought 15,000 shares in Ocean GeoLoop at an average price of NOK 35,2727 per share. After the transaction, K4 Invest AS holds 429,666 shares in Ocean GeoLoop.
