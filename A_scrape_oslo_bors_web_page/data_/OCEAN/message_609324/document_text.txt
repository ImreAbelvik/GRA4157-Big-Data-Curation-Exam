Ocean GeoLoop AS: Mandatory notification of trades 

Certain primary insiders of Ocean GeoLoop AS (the "Company" or "Ocean GeoLoop") has today on 26 January 2024 bought shares in the Company as further described below: 

•	AB Investment AS, controlled by Anders Onarheim, Chairman of the Board of Ocean GeoLoop, has today bought 50,000 shares in Ocean GeoLoop at an average price of NOK 6.70 per share. After the transaction, AB Investment AS holds 3,673,000 shares in Ocean GeoLoop.

•	Kold Invest AS, controlled by Martha Kold Monclair, boardmember of Ocean GeoLoop, has today bought 31,481 shares in Ocean GeoLoop at an average price of NOK 6.70 per share. After the transaction, Kold Invest AS holds 31,481 shares in Ocean GeoLoop.
