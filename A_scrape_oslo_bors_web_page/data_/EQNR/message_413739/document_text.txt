


On behalf of Statoil (OSE: STL, NYSE:STO), DNB has on 15 November 2016 purchased
631,197 shares for use in the group's Share saving plan.

The shares have been acquired at a price of NOK 137.83 per share.

Before distribution to the employees, the Share saving plan has 11,216,000
shares.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
