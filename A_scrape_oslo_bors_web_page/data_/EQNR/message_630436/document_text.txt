A close associate of a primary insider in Equinor ASA (OSE: EQNR, NYSE: EQNR)
has purchased shares in Equinor ASA:

Tocaba AS, a close associate of board member Tone Hegland Bachke, has on 25
October 2024 purchased 2000 shares in Equinor ASA at a price of NOK 276.76 per
share.

Details of the purchase of shares are set forth in the attached notification.

This is information that Equinor ASA is obliged to make public pursuant to
Article 19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and subject to the disclosure
requirements pursuant to Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
