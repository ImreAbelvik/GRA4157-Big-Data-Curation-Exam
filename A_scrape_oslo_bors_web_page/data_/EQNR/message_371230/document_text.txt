


On behalf of Statoil (OSE:STL, NYSE:STO), DNB has on 13 February 2015 purchased
628,251  shares for use in the group's Share saving plan.

The shares have been acquired at a price of NOK 149.56  per share.

Before distribution to the employees, the Share saving plan has 8,154,144
shares.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
