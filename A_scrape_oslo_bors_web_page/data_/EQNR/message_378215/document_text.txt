


On behalf of Statoil (OSE: STL, NYSE:STO), DNB has on 15th of May 2015 purchased
605,625 shares for use in the group's Share saving plan.

The shares have been acquired at a price of NOK 154.68 per share.

Before distribution to the employees, the Share saving plan has 9,023,190
shares.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
