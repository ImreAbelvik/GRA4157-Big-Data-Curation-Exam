


On behalf of Statoil (OSE:STL, NYSE:STO), DNB has on 15 January 2015 purchased
713,771 shares for use in the group's Share saving plan.

The shares have been acquired at a price of NOK 130.63 per share.

Before distribution to the employees, the Share saving plan has 10,869,020
shares.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
