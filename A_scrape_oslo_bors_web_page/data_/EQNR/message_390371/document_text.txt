


The following primary insider in Statoil ASA has sold shares in Statoil ASA:

Lars Christian Bacher, executive vice president in Statoil ASA, has on 27
November 2015 sold 5,000 shares in Statoil ASA at a price of NOK 135.40 per
share, and will after the purchase hold 20,172 shares in Statoil ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
