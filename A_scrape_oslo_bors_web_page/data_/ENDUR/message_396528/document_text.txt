


Eikeland Holding AS, a company under control of Hans Petter Eikeland, CEO of
Bergen Group ASA, has on 2 March 2016 bought 469 024 shares in Bergen Group ASA
at an average price of NOK  0,987 per share.  Total holding of shares in Bergen
Group ASA after the transaction is 469 024 shares.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
