The following primary insiders of Endúr ASA (the "Company") have today, 10 February 2025, executed the following share options, each giving the right to acquire one share in the Company:

o Pål Reiulf Olsen, Chair of the Board of Directors, has exercised 10,000 options at a strike price of NOK 41.25 and 10,000 options at a strike price of NOK 44.88. Following the transaction, Pål Reiulf Olsen and his close associates hold a total of 135,075 shares and 10,000 share options in the Company.

o Jeppe Raaholt, CEO, has exercised 40,000 options at a strike price of NOK 41.25 and 40,000 options at a strike price of NOK 44.88. Following the transaction, Jeppe Raaholt and his close associates hold a total of 300,000 shares and 720,000 share options in the Company.

o Einar Olsen, CFO, has exercised 30,000 options at a strike price of NOK 41.25 and 30,000 options at a strike price of NOK 44.88. Following the transaction, Einar Olsen and his close associates hold a total of 110,000 shares and 480,000 share options in the Company.

The exercised share options with a strike price of NOK 41.25 were granted in 2021 and expires on 1 March 2026. The exercised share options with a strike price of NOK 44.88 were granted in 2023 and expires on 5 June 2027.

See the enclosed forms for further details about the transactions.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 number 3 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

About Endúr ASA
Endúr ASA (OSE: ENDUR) is a leading supplier of construction and maintenance projects and services for marine infrastructure, including facilities for land-based aquaculture, quays, harbours, dams, bridges and other specialised concrete and steel projects. The company and its subsidiaries also offer a wide range of other specialised project and marine services. Endúr ASA is headquartered in Lysaker, Norway. See www.endur.no.
