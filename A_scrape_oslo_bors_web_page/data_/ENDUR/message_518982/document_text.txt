Mons-Ove Hauge, managing director of Endúr ASA’s subsidiary company, Endúr Sjøsterk, has through his wholly owned company, Move AS, bought 240,000 shares in Endúr ASA at a price of NOK 1,100 per share. Following the acquisition, Moves AS owns 240,000 shares in Endúr ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
