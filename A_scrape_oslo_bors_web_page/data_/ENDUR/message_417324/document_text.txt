Reference is made to the announcement distributed 2 
January 2017 regarding the registration of new share 
capital in connection with the completion of the 
private placement (the "Private Placement") in Bergen 
Group ASA (the "Company") which increases the total 
number of issued shares in the Company from 60,622,009 
shares to 79,014,342 shares. The following primary 
insiders of the Company have been allocated shares in 
the Private Placement:  

Brian Chang Holdings Limited has been 
allocated 6,078,000 new shares. Total shareholding 
after the allocation is 26,146,500 shares, equivalent 
to 33.09 % of the total outstanding number of shares 
in the Company after the Private Placement. 

AS Flyfisk has been allocated 4,167,000 new 
shares. Total shareholding after the allocation is 
22,027,570 shares, equivalent to 27.88 % of the total 
outstanding number of shares in the Company after the 
Private Placement.

Eikeland Holding AS, a company controlled by 
CEO Hans Petter Eikeland in Bergen Group ASA, has been 
allocated 4,167,000 new shares. Total shareholding 
after the allocation is 4,636,024 shares, equivalent 
to 5.87 % of the total outstanding number of shares in 
the Company after the Private Placement.

Køhlergruppen AS has been allocated 1,839,000 
new shares. Total shareholding after theallocation is 
7,901,721 shares, equivalent to 10.00 % of the total 
outstanding number of shares in the Company after the 
Private Placement.

FJ Holding AS, a company controlled by CEO 
Frode Johansson in Bergen Group Services AS, has been 
allocated 833,000 new shares. Total shareholding after 
the allocation is 833,000 shares.

Eagle AS, a company controlled by CFO Nils 
Hoff in Bergen Group ASA, has been allocated 417,000 
new shares. Total shareholding after the allocation is 
417,000 shares.

Roger Toftegaard, VP Defence in Bergen Group 
Services AS, has been allocated 333,500 new shares. 
Total shareholding after the allocation is 333,000 
shares.

Asbjørn Grini, VP Energy & Industry in Bergen 
Group Services AS, has been allocated 292,000 new 
shares. Total shareholding after the allocation is 
342,000 shares.

Jonny Arefjord, VP Maritime in Bergen Group 
Services AS, has been allocated 125,000 new shares. 
Total shareholding after the allocation is 125,500 
shares.

Øyvind Risnes, SVP Corporate Functions & 
Communications in Bergen Group ASA, has been allocated 
83,000 new shares. Total shareholding after the 
allocation is 117,000 shares.

Silje Bergmann, finance manager in Bergen 
Group Services AS, has been allocated 58,333 new 
shares. Total shareholding after the allocation, 
including shares owned by related parties, is 98,165 
shares.

All the above subscriptions and allocations are made 
at a price of NOK 1.20 per share. The share price and 
the abovementioned allocations are made pursuant to 
the resolution made at the extraordinary general 
meeting held on 17 November 2016. A prospectus for the 
listing of the new shares on Oslo Børs will be 
prepared. The new shares are registered on a separate 
ISIN number in the VPS and will not be listed on Oslo 
Børs until the prospectus has been approved and 
published.

This information is subject to the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section
4-3 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
