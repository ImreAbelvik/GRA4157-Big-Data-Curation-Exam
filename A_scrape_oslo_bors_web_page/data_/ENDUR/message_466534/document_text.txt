CEO Nils Hoff has today announced that he call an option to subscribe for 333,333 shares in Bergen Group ASA (the company) with a subscription prize of NOK 1 per share. The call is in accordance with the option program for senior executives resolved upon at the company's annual general meeting in June 2018. After the shares have been issued, Nils Hoff will, including related parties, hold a total of 1,750,333 shares in Bergen Group ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
