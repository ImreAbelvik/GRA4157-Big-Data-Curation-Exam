Artec Holding AS, a company closely associated with Bjørn Finnøy, board member of Endúr ASA (the "Company"), has on 9 March 2023 sold 642,921 shares in the Company at a price of NOK 40.50 per share. Following the transaction, Artec Holding AS holds 8,598,313 shares, equal to approximately 26.42% of the total number of shares and votes in the Company.  

Jörn Ryberg Holding AB, a company closely associated with board member Jörn Ryberg, has on 9 March 2023 sold 134,575 shares in the Company at a price of NOK 40.50 per share. Following the transaction, Jörn Ryberg Holding AB holds 1,799,789 shares, equal to approximately 5.53% of the total number of shares and votes in the Company.

Both transactions are part of a block sale to one investor. See the enclosed forms for further details about the transactions.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 number 3 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

ABOUT ENDÚR

Endúr ASA (OSE: ENDUR) is a leading supplier of construction and maintenance projects and services for marine infrastructure, including facilities for land-based aquaculture, quays, harbours, dams, bridges and other specialised concrete and steel projects. The company and its subsidiaries also offer a wide range of other specialised project and marine services. Endúr ASA is headquartered in Lysaker, Bærum, Norway. See www.endur.no.
