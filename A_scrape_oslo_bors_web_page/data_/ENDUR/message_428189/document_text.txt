Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement 
disclosed on 22 May 2017 regarding Board resolution in 
Bergen Group ASA of executing a private placement in 
connection with an option exercise, notified by Eikeland 
Holding AS and Eagle AS.

Eikeland Holding AS, controlled by CEO Hans Petter Eikeland 
of Bergen Group ASA, has now completed the purchase of 1 
million new shares at a subscription price of NOK 1 per 
share in accordance with the option agreement. New 
shareholding in Bergen Group ASA after the acquisition is 5 
636 024 shares. Hans Petter Eikeland owns or controls no 
other shares in Bergen Group ASA other than the above-
mentioned shares owned by Eikeland Holding AS.  

Eagle AS, controlled by CFO Nils Ingemund Hoff in Bergen 
Group ASA, has now completed the purchase of 1 million new 
shares at a subscription price of NOK 1 per share, in 
accordance with the option agreement. New shareholding in 
Bergen Group ASA after the purchase is 1 417 000 shares. 
Nils Ingemund Hoff owns or controls no other shares in 
Bergen Group ASA other than the above-mentioned shares owned 
by Eagle AS.  

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to section
4-3 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
