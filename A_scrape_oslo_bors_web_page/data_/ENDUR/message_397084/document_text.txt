


Asbjørn Grini, head of Energy & Industry in Bergen Group Services AS, has on 10
March 2016 bought 50 000 shares in Bergen Group ASA at an average price of NOK
1.30 per share.  Total holding of shares in Bergen Group ASA after the
transaction is 50 000 shares.

Øyvind Risnes, SVP Corporate Functions & Communications in Bergen Group ASA, has
on 10 March 2016 bought 30 000 shares in Bergen Group ASA at an average price of
NOK 1.35 per share.  Total holding of shares in Bergen Group ASA after the
transaction is 34 000 shares.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
