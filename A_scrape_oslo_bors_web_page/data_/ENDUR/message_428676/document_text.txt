Reference is made to the announcement distributed 1 June 
2017 concerning the registration of new share capital as a 
result of the completed private placement in Bergen Group 
ASA (the "Company"). The following primary insiders have 
purchased shares in connection with the private placement:  

AS Flyfisk has purchased 370,370 new shares. Total 
shareholding after the purchase is 22,397,940 shares, 
equivalent to 23.84 % of the total outstanding number of 
shares in the Company after the private placement.

Eikeland Holding AS, a company controlled by CEO 
Hans Petter Eikeland in Bergen Group ASA, has purchased 
187,000 new shares. Total shareholding after the purchase is 
5,823,024 shares, equivalent to 6.20 % of the total 
outstanding number of shares in the Company after the 
private placement.
