Poca Invest AS, a company closely associated with Pål Reiulf Olsen, Chairman of the Board of Directors in Endúr ASA, has today purchased 12,000 shares in Endúr ASA at a price per share of NOK 42. Following this purchase, Pål Reiulf Olsen and his affiliated companies hold a total of 95,363 shares and 20,000 options in Endúr ASA.

Skjæggestad Invest AS, a company closely associated with Pål Skjæggestad, Board Member in Endúr ASA, has today purchased 14,000 shares in Endúr ASA at a price per share of NOK 42. Prior to this transaction, Pål Skjæggestad and his affiliates did not hold any shares or options in Endúr ASA.

Råbjørn AS, a company closely associated with Jeppe Raaholt, CEO in Endúr ASA, has today purchased 3,000 shares in Endúr ASA at a price per share of NOK 42. Following this purchase, Jeppe Raaholt and his affiliates hold a total of 209,685 shares and 80,000 options in Endúr ASA.

Red Devil Holding AS, a company closely associated with Einar Olsen, CFO in Endúr ASA, has today purchased 3,000 shares in Endúr ASA at a price per share of NOK 42. Following this purchase, Einar Olsen and his affiliates hold a total of 32,843 shares and 60,000 options in Endúr ASA.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 number 3 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

About Endúr ASA
Endúr ASA (OSE: ENDUR) is a leading supplier of construction and maintenance
projects and services for marine infrastructure, including facilities for
land-based aquaculture, quays, harbours, dams, bridges and other specialised
concrete and steel projects. The company and its subsidiaries also offer a wide
range of other specialised project and marine services. Endúr ASA is
headquartered in Bergen, Norway. See www.endur.no.
