Asbjørn Grini, Head of the Energy & Industry Department of 
Bergen Group Services AS, has transferred his ownership of 
375,000 shares in Bergen Group ASA to VBJ Holding AS (BRN 
919349131), a company 100% owned by Asbjørn Grini. The 
transfer has taken place at a price of NOK 1.60 per share, 
corresponding to the closing price at Oslo Børs as of 7 
August 2017. Following the transaction, Asbjørn Grini owns a 
total of 375,000 shares in Bergen Group ASA through related 
parties.
