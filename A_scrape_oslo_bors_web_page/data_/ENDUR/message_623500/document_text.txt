Einar Olsen, CFO and primary insider of Endúr ASA (the "Company"), has through his wholly owned company Red Devil Holding AS on 11 July 2024 acquired 4,500 shares in the Company at a price of NOK 55.50 per share. Following the transaction, Einar Olsen and his close associate hold 44,500 shares and 90,000 share options in the Company.

Pål Reiulf Olsen, chair of the board of directors and primary insider of the Company, has through his wholly owned company Poca Invest AS on 11 July 2024 acquired 9,000 shares in the Company at a price of NOK 55.50 per share. Following the transaction, Pål Reiulf Olsen and his close associate hold 107,248 shares and 30,000 share options in the Company. 

See the enclosed forms for further details about the transactions.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 number 3 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

About Endúr ASA
Endúr ASA (OSE: ENDUR) is a leading supplier of construction and maintenance projects and services for marine infrastructure, including facilities for land-based aquaculture, quays, harbours, dams, bridges and other specialised concrete and steel projects. The company and its subsidiaries also offer a wide range of other specialised project and marine services. Endúr ASA is headquartered in Lysaker, Norway. See www.endur.no.
