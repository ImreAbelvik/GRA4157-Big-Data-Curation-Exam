Øyvind Risnes, SVP in Bergen Group ASA and Managing Director in Bergen Group Sjøsterk AS, has today announced that he call an option to subscribe for 111,111 shares in Bergen Group ASA (the company) with a subscription prize of NOK 1 per share. The call is in accordance with the option program for senior executives resolved upon at the company's annual general meeting in June 2018. After the shares have been issued, Øyvind Risnes will hold a total of 228,111 shares in Bergen Group ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
