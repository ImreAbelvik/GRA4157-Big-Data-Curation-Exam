A related party of primary insider Silje Bergmann, 
Controller Group Accounting in Bergen Group ASA, has today 3 
April 2017 been allocated 20 000 shares in the Subsequent 
Offering finalized 31 March 2017, at an price of NOK 1.20 
per share, and according to the allocation principles set 
out in the prospectus dated 16 March 2017. 

Following this transaction, the related party to Silje 
Bergmann owns 59 132 shares in Bergen Group ASA and in sum 
Silje Bergman and related parties have in total 118 165 
shares in Bergen Group ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading 
Act.
