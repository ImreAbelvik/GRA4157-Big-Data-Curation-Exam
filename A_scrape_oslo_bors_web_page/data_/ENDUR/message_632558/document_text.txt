•	Pål Reiulf Olsen, Chair of the Board of Directors, has, through his wholly owned company Poca Invest AS, acquired 7,827 shares in the Company. Following the transaction, Pål Reiulf Olsen and his close associates hold a total of 115,075 shares and 30,000 share options in the Company.

•	Pål Skjæggestad, Deputy Board Member, has, through his wholly owned company Skjæggestad Invest AS, acquired 7,827 shares in the Company. Following the transaction, Pål Skjæggestad and his close associates hold a total of 26,712 shares in the Company.

•	Jostein Devold, Board Member, has acquired 5,000 shares in the Company. Following the transaction, Jostein Devold and his close associates hold a total of 5,000 shares in the Company.

•	Kristine Landmark, Board Member, has acquired 5,000 shares in the Company. Following the transaction, Kristine Landmark and her close associates hold a total of 30,885 shares in the Company.

•	Jeppe Raaholt, CEO, has, through his wholly owned company Råbjørn AS, acquired 5,430 shares in the Company. Following the transaction, Jeppe Raaholt and his close associates hold a total of 220,000 shares and 120,000 share options in the Company.

About Endúr ASA
Endúr ASA (OSE: ENDUR) is a leading supplier of construction and maintenance projects and services for marine infrastructure, including facilities for land-based aquaculture, quays, harbours, dams, bridges and other specialised concrete and steel projects. The company and its subsidiaries also offer a wide range of other specialised project and marine services. Endúr ASA is headquartered in Lysaker, Norway. See www.endur.no.
