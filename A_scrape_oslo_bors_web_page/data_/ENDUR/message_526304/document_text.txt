Øivind O. Horpestad, chairman of the board of Endúr ASA, has through his wholly owned company Gimle Invest AS, on February 26th bought 100,000 shares in Endúr ASA at a price of NOK 1,175 per share. Following the purchase, Gimle Invest AS owns 12,306,513 shares in Endúr ASA.
 
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
