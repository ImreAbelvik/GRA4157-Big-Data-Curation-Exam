Hans Martin Nakkim, Board Member in Induct AS, has purchased 40,000 shares in
Induct at an average price of NOK 8.75 per share, through Fiona Holding Ltd, a
company owned 100% by Hans Martin Nakkim.

After the transaction, Fiona Holding Ltd holds 250,000 shares in Induct AS, and
100,000 options.
