DELIVERY OF PRIVATE PLACEMENT SHARES AND PRE-COMMITMENT FEE SHARES 

Reference is made to the announcement by Induct AS ("Induct" or the "Company") on 24 April 2025 regarding the private placement of new shares in the Company with gross proceeds in the amount of approximately NOK 14.5 million through the placement of 4,816,586 new shares (the "New Shares") at a subscription price of NOK 3.00 per share (the "Offer Price") (the "Private Placement") and the issue of 481,655 new shares as pre-commitment fee (the "Pre-commitment Shares"). 

In order to facilitate a quicker settlement and delivery of the New Shares and the Pre-commitment Shares, the board has resolved the consummation of the Private Placement by the issuance of the New Shares and the Pre-commitment shares pursuant to an authorisation granted by the annual general meeting 14 June 2024. 

ISSUE OF SHARES TO EMPLOYEES AND SUPPLIER 

In connection with the Private Placement, the Company has decided to settle debt to two employees (the "Employees") and a supplier (the "Supplier") by issuance of new shares and settle the Company's claim on share contribution by way of set off against the Company's debt to the Employees and Supplier. The Supplier has subscribed for 83 333 new shares at the Offer Price, and one of the Employees has subscribed for 60 000 new shares at the Offer Price. 

Further, with regard to the second Employee, reference is made to the stock exchange notice published by the Company on 31 December 2024 regarding CTO Ignacio Orteu's (the "CTO") conversion of sign-on bonus into 50,000 new shares in the Company (the "Bonus Shares"). The Company has now resolved to issue the said shares. The CTO has subscribed for the Bonus Shares at a subscription price of NOK 2.20 in accordance with his employment agreement with the Company. 

ISSUE OF SHARES TO CLOSE ASSOCIATE OF PRIMARY INSIDER 

In 2023, NPP Capital AS ("NPP"), a close associate of Alf Martin Johansen and part of the executive management team, transferred 20,000 shares on behalf of the Company to a new employee. The transfer was made to settle the Company's obligation to issue 20,000 sign-on shares at a subscription price of NOK 6 per share. Following this transfer, NPP assumed the employee's entitlement to be issued 20,000 shares at the said subscription price. In connection with the Private Placement, the board has resolved to issue 20,000 shares to NPP at a subscription price of NOK 6 per share. to settle the Company's obligation to NPP. 

Primary insider notification pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation Article 19 is attached.

Each share represents one vote at the Company's general meeting.
