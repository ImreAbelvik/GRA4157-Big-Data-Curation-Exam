About Induct

Based on eight years of collaboration with over 250 organizations, Induct offers
innovation communities, delivered as "Software as a Service," that enable
organizations to create, manage, track and measure the innovation process from
idea creation through to final implementation and impact reporting. Induct
enables its customers to connect with each other in larger networks to share
best practices, while deploying and monetizing initiatives - all within a
secure, collaborative, and access-controlled cloud-based network. Induct is
listed on the Merkur Market list on the Oslo Stock Exchange with the ticker
INDUCT-ME.
