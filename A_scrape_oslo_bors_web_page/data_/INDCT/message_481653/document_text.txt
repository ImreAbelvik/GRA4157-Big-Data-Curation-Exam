Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement by Induct AS ("Induct" or
the "Company") on 28 June 2019 regarding completion of ordinary general meeting
approving a Preferential Rights Issuee with gross proceeds of up to NOK 1.9
million through issuance of new shares at a subscription price of NOK 5 per
share (the "Preferential Rights Issue"). The investors in the Preferential
Rights Issue will also receive 1 warrant per share subscribed for and allocated
in the Preferential Rights Issue.

The application period in the Preferential Rights Issue ended on 18 July 2019 at
16.30 CET. At the end of the application period, the Company had received
applications of 380,000 shares amounting to a total of NOK 1.9 million in the
Preferential Rights Issue.

The Company's board of directors (the "Board") has now approved the allocation
of shares and warrants in the Preferential Rights Issue, which implies that the
Company will issue 380,000 new shares and 380,000 new warrants related to the
Preferential Rights Issue.

About Induct

Based on ten years of collaboration with more than 250 organizations, Induct
offers collaborative communities, delivered as "Software as a Service," that
enable organizations to digitalize and manage business processes. This also
include create, manage, track and measure the innovation process from idea
creation through to final implementation and impact reporting. Induct enables
its customers to connect with each other in larger networks to share best
practices, while deploying and monetizing initiatives - all within a secure,
collaborative, and access-controlled cloud-based network.

Induct is listed on the Merkur Market list on the Oslo Stock Exchange with the
ticker INDUCT-ME.
