Public Property Invest ASA, Martin Mæland, has on 16 August 2024 purchased a
total of 50,000 shares in Public Property Invest ASA. In addition, Martin

Please see the attached forms of notification and public disclosure for
details of the transactions.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of
Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of
the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
