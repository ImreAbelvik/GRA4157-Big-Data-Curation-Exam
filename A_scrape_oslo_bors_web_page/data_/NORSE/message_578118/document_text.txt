Arendal, 14 December 2022: Norse Atlantic ASA ("Norse") refers to the attached
notification form regarding a transaction in Norse's shares by a primary
insider. Bosel AS, a company controlled by Ben Boiling, CFO of Norse, has
purchased 22,000 shares at an average price of NOK 2.05 per share. Following the
transaction, Bosel AS owns a total of 107,000 shares in Norse Atlantic ASA.

About Norse:

Norse Norse Atlantic Airways is a new airline that offers affordable fares on
long -haul flights, primarily between Europe and the United States. The Company
was founded by CEO and major shareholder  Bjørn Tore Larsen in March 2021. Norse
has a fleet of 15 modern, fuel-efficient and more environmentally friendly
Boeing 787 Dreamliners that will serve destinations including New York, Los
Angeles, Fort Lauderdale, Orlando, Oslo, London, Berlin and Paris. The company's
first flight took off from Oslo to New York on June 14, 2022.
