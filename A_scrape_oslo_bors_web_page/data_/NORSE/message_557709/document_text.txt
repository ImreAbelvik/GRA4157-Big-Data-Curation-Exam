Arendal, 28 March 2022: Norse Atlantic ASA (the "Company") refers to the
attached notification form regarding public disclosure of a transaction in the
Company's shares by a close associate of a primary insider.

Audun Pettersen, a close associate of Chief Culture Officer Kristin Berthelsen,
has purchased 16,000 shares at a price of NOK 12.80 per share.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU
596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act § 5-12.
This stock exchange release was published by Ben Boiling, CFO, Norse Atlantic
ASA, on 28 March 2022 at 07:00 CET.
