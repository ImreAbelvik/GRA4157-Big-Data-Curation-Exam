Arendal, 14 June 2022: Norse Atlantic ASA (the "Company") refers to the attached
notification form regarding a transaction in the Company's shares by a primary
insider. B T Larsen & Co Ltd, a company controlled by Bjørn Tore Larsen, CEO of
the Company, has purchased 121,000 shares at an average price of NOK 12.88 per
share. Following the transaction, B T Larsen & Co Ltd owns a total of 9,958,311
shares in Norse Atlantic ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU
596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act § 5-12.
This stock exchange release was published by Ben Boiling, CFO, Norse Atlantic
ASA, on 14 June 2022 at 07:00 CET.
