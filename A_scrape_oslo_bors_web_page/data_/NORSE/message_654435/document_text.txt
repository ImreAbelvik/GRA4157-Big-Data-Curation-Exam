Arendal, 1 September 2025: Felix Fürst, board member and primary insider of
Norse Atlantic ASA (the "Company"), has today, 1 September 2025,  acquired
100,000 shares in the Company at a price of NOK 8,7853 per share. Following the
transaction, Felix Fürst owns 750,000 shares in the Company equal to
approximately 0.5% of the company's share capital.

Please see the attached notification form for further details about the
transaction.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in articles 19 of the
EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading
Act.
