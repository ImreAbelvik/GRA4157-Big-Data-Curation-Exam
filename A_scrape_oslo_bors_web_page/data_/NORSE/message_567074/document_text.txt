Arendal, 15 July 2022: Norse Atlantic ASA ("Norse") refers to the attached
notification form regarding a transaction in Norse's shares by a primary
insider. Bosel AS, a company controlled by Ben Boiling, CFO of Norse, has
purchased 5,000 shares at an average price of NOK 11.90 per share. Following the
transaction, Bosel AS owns a total of 45,000 shares in Norse Atlantic ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU
596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act § 5-12.
This stock exchange release was published by Ben Boiling, CFO, Norse Atlantic
ASA, on 15 July 2022 at 07:00 CET.
