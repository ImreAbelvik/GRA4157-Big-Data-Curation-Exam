22 May 2024. Verdane Capital VIII K/S (“Verdane”) has today sold 5,500,000 shares in Napatech A/S (the “Company” or "Napatech"), representing approx. 5.52 per cent of the share capital and voting rights in the Company, at a price per share of NOK 32 for a total transaction size of NOK 176 million (the “Placing”). The shares were sold through a process managed by SpareBank 1 Markets AS (the “Manager”). 

Prior to the Placing, Verdane holds 15,213,618 shares in Napatech, representing approx. 15.27 per cent of Napatech's share capital and voting rights. After the Placing, Verdane holds 9,713,618 shares in Napatech, representing approx. 9.75 per cent of the share capital and voting rights in Napatech. Verdane has thereby fell below the 10% and 15% thresholds pursuant to section 38 of the Danish Capital Markets Act.

Verdane is a closely associated legal person of chairman Christian Jebsen. Please see the transaction notification form for the transaction from Verdane attached to this release.

In connection with the Placing, Verdane has entered into a 90-day lock-up agreement with the Manager.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to MAR (regulation (EU) No. 596/2014 on market abuse) as implemented in Norway in accordance with section 3-1 of the Securities Trading Act and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act, and section 38 of the Danish Capital Markets Act.
