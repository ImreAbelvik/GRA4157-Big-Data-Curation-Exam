The board of directors of Northern Ocean Ltd. has approved a bonus for Arne Jacobsen (CEO), in the amount of USD 225,000 in the form of 331,728 shares in the company valued at the closing price on 31 January 2025. The shares have a lock up period of one year. Please see the attached PDMR-form for further details of the transaction. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation article 19.
