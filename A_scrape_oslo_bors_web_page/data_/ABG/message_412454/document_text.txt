With reference to our press releases of 3 March 2015 
and 8 March 2016 a resigned partner purchased on 31 
October 2016 105,000 ASC-shares from ABG Sundal 
Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") as settlement of forward 
contracts that were previously enter into. The shares 
were sold at an average price of NOK 4.0462 per share. 
The previously agreed prices of NOK 4.41 and NOK 5.28 
per share have been adjusted for dividends, interest 
adjustment and earlier settlement of the forward 
contracts. 

In addition ABGSC purchased on 31 October 2016 52,500 
ASC-shares from the resigned partner. The purchase 
price is NOK 0.23 per share, reflecting the 
restrictions imposed on partner shares.

ABGSC owns 3,446,790 treasury shares following the 
transactions above. ABGSC has forward contracts with 
partners purchasing a total of 28,782,967 shares with 
settlement in 2017 - 2019. ABGSC has authorization to 
re-purchase its shares in the market or to issue new 
shares.
