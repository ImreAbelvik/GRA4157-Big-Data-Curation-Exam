With reference to our press release of 6 September 
2013 a resigned partner purchased yesterday 50,000 
ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA (ASC) shares from the 
company as settlement of a forward contract that was 
previously entered into. The shares were sold at a 
price of NOK 2.14 per share. The previously agreed 
price of NOK 3.76 per share has been adjusted for 
dividends, interest changes and earlier payment of 
the forward contract.

In addition the company purchased yesterday 25,000 
ASC shares from the resigned partner. The purchase 
price is NOK 0.23 per share, reflecting the 
restrictions imposed on partner shares.
 
ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA owns 9,127,925 
treasury shares following the transactions above. 
The company has forward contracts with partners 
purchasing a total of 36,512,038 shares with 
settlement in 2016 - 2018.
