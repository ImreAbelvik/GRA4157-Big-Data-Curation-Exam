ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") announced on 26 February 2018 an offer to buy back up to an aggregate of 14,500,000 shares in ABGSC at an offer price of NOK 6.35 per share in cash. The offer was valid until today. ABGSC purchased the 14,833,241 ABGSC shares from the shareholders in the offering. All shareholders, incl. partners, who tendered shares in the offering sold all shares tendered.

In addition ABGSC has purchased 175,000 shares from a resigned partner at a price of NOK 5.68 per share, reflecting the restrictions imposed on partner shares.

With reference to our previously press releases ABGSC sold yesterday 3,042,900 shares to partners as settlement of forward contracts that were previously entered into. The shares were sold at an average price of NOK 2.7316 per share. The previously announced prices have been adjusted for dividends, interest changes and earlier settlement of the forward contract.

ABGSC owns 20,301,439 treasury shares following the transactions above. The company has forward contracts with partners purchasing a total of 43,605,200 shares with settlement in 2018-2023.
