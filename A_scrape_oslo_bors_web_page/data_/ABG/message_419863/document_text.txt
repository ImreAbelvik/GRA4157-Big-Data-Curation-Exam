With reference to our press release of 5 March 2014 a 
resigned partner purchased on 10 February 2017 100,000 
ASC-shares from ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA 
("ABGSC") as settlement of a forward contract that was 
previously enter into. The shares were sold at a price 
of NOK 2.58 per share. The previously agreed price of 
NOK 4.79 per share has been adjusted for dividends, 
interest adjustments and earlier settlement of the 
forward contract. 

ABGSC owns 9,411,790 treasury shares following the 
transaction above. ABGSC has forward contracts with 
partners purchasing a total of 29,342,967 shares with 
settlement in 2017 - 2019. ABGSC has authorization to 
re-purchase its shares in the market or to issue new 
shares.
