ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") purchased on 25 July 2016 275,000 ABGSC 
shares ("ASC") at an average price of NOK 5.1128 per share.

With reference to our press releases of 6 March 2013, 5 March 2014 and 3 March 2015 
ABGSC sold on 25 July 2016 275,000 ASC-shares to a partner as settlement of forward 
contracts previously entered into. The shares were sold at an average price of NOK 2.2145 
per share. The previously agreed prices of NOK 4.13, NOK 4.79 and NOK 4.41 has been 
adjusted for dividends, interest adjustments and earlier settlement of the forward contracts.

ABGSC owns 6,398,890 treasury shares following the transactions above. The company has 
forward contracts with partners purchasing a total of 30,430,067 shares with settlement in 
2017 - 2019. ABGSC has authorization to re-purchase its shares in the market or to issue 
new shares.
