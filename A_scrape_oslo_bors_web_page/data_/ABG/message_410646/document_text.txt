ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") on 3 October 
2016 sold 500,000 ASC-shares to a partner on a forward 
contract with settlement 1 June 2019 at NOK 4.56 per 
share. The price is reflecting the restrictions 
imposed on partner shares.

In addition and with reference to our press releases 
of 24 September 2014 and 3 March 2015 two resigned 
partners purchased on 3 October 2016 850,000 ASC-
shares from ABGSC as settlement of forward contracts 
that were previously enter into. The shares were sold 
at an average price of NOK 2.9959 per share. The 
previously agreed prices of NOK 4.96 and NOK 4.41 per 
share have been adjusted for dividends, interest 
adjustment and earlier settlement of the forward 
contracts. 

In addition the company purchased 3 October 2016 
75,000 ASC-shares from a resigned partner. The 
purchase price is NOK 0.23 per share, reflecting the 
restrictions imposed on partner shares.

ABGSC owns 3,455,490 treasury shares following the 
transactions above. ABGSC has forward contracts with 
partners purchasing a total of 29,111,667 shares with 
settlement in 2017 - 2019. ABGSC has authorization to 
re-purchase its shares in the market or to issue new 
shares.
