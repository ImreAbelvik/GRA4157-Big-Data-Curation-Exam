ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA (OSE: ASC) today 
announced a placement of 3,626,400 shares from 
partners of the firm to investors, representing 3.8% 
of the free partners shares available for sale. The 
shares were sold at a price of NOK 6.65 per share. 

In addition, partners have settled 5,889,500 shares on 
forward contracts. ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA has 
sold the shares to the partners at an average price of 
NOK 1.7715 per share. The previously agreed prices of 
NOK 3.34, NOK 3.45, NOK 4.13 and NOK 10.66 per share 
(see press releases of 24 October 2006, 18 October 
2011, 8 August 2012 and 6 March 2013) have been 
adjusted for dividends, earlier settlement of the 
contract or extension of the contract. Following the 
transactions ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA owns 
3,043,425 treasury shares. The company has forward 
contracts with partners purchasing a total of 
30,382,538 shares with settlement in 2016 - 2018.  

Four insiders has sold shares in the transaction:

Knut Brundtland has through the company Giotto AS sold 
1,317,400 shares at NOK 6.65 per share. In addition, 
Giotto AS has settled a forward contract of 4,200,000 
shares at an adjusted price of NOK 1.26 per share. The 
previously agreed price of NOK 3.34 per share has been 
adjusted for dividends and extension of the forward 
contract. Following the transactions Knut Brundtland 
owns, including the company Giotto AS, 3,900,000 fully 
paid shares plus 100,000 shares on a forward contract.
 
Per-Ove Breivold has through the 100% owned company 
Vibepob AS sold 100,000 shares at NOK 6.65 per share. 
Following the transaction Per-Ove Breivold, incl. 100% 
owned companies, owns 1,300,000 fully paid shares plus 
100,000 shares on a forward contract.
  
Roar Hatling has through the 100% owned company Mafica 
Finans AS sold 75,000 shares at NOK 6.65 per share. 
Following the transaction Roar Hatling incl. a 100% 
owned company, owns 225,000 fully paid shares plus 
125,000 shares on forward contracts.

Espen Wahl has sold 100,000 shares at NOK 6.65 per 
share. In addition, Wahl has settled a forward 
contract of 100,000 shares at an adjusted price of NOK 
2.04 per share. The previously agreed price of NOK 
4.13 per share has been adjusted for dividends and 
earlier settlement of the forward contract. Following 
the transactions Espen Wahl owns 220,733 fully paid 
shares.    

Following the placement partners of ABG Sundal Collier 
will hold approx. 28% of the fully diluted number of 
shares in the company.
