With reference to our press release of 3 November 2014 
a resigned partner purchased on 1 February 2017 50,000 
ASC-shares from ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA 
("ABGSC") as settlement of a forward contract that was 
previously enter into. The shares were sold at a price 
of NOK 2.88 per share. The previously agreed price of 
NOK 4.62 per share has been adjusted for dividends, 
interest adjustments and earlier settlement of the 
forward contract. 

In addition ABGSC purchased on 1 February 2017 25,000 
ASC-shares from the resigned partner. The purchase 
price is NOK 0.23 per share, reflecting the 
restrictions imposed on partner shares.

ABGSC owns 9,511,790 treasury shares following the 
transactions above. ABGSC has forward contracts with 
partners purchasing a total of 29,442,967 shares with 
settlement in 2017 - 2019. ABGSC has authorization to 
re-purchase its shares in the market or to issue new 
shares.
