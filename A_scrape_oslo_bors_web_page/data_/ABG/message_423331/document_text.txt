ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") announced on 10 
March 2016 an offer to buy back up to an aggregate of 
13,000,000 shares in ABGSC at an offer price of NOK 5.60 per 
share in cash. The offer was valid until yesterday. ABGSC 
purchased the 13,000,000 ABGSC shares from the shareholders 
in the offering.

With reference to our previously press releases ABGSC sold 
yesterday 1,090,764 shares to partners as settlement of 
forward contracts that were previously entered into. The 
shares were sold at an average price of NOK 2.944 per share. 
The previously announced prices have been adjusted for 
dividends, interest changes and earlier settlement of the 
forward contract.

ABGSC owns 17,688,026 treasury shares following the 
transactions above. The company has forward contracts with 
partners purchasing a total of 39,608,503 shares with 
settlement in 2017-2022.
