With reference to our press release of 16 December 
2014 an ex-partner purchased on 4 May 2016 100,000 ABG 
Sundal Collier Holding ASA (ASC) shares from the 
company as settlement of a forward contract that was 
previously entered into. The shares were sold at a 
price of NOK 2.60 per share. The previously agreed 
price of NOK 4.38 per share has been adjusted for 
dividends, interest adjustments and earlier settlement 
of the forward contract.

ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA owns 2,699,425 treasury 
shares following the transaction above. The company 
has forward contracts with partners purchasing a total 
of 36,809,138 shares with settlement in 2016 - 2019. 
ABGSC has authorization to re-purchase its shares in 
the market or to issue new shares.
