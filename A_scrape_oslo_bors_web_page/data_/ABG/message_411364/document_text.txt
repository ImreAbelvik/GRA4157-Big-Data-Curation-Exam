With reference to our press release of 7 March 2007 a 
resigned partner purchased on 17 October 2016 223,700 
ASC-shares from ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA 
("ABGSC") as settlement of a forward contract that was 
previously enter into. The shares were sold at a price 
of NOK 7.09 per share. The previously agreed price of 
NOK 14.07 per share has been adjusted for dividends, 
interest adjustments and extension of the forward 
contract.  

ABGSC owns 3,499,290 treasury shares following the 
transaction above. ABGSC has forward contracts with 
partners purchasing a total of 28,887,967 shares with 
settlement in 2017 - 2019. ABGSC has authorization to 
re-purchase its shares in the market or to issue new 
shares.
