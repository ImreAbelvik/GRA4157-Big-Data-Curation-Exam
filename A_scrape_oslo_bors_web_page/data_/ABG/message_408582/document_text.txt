With reference to our press release of 3 March 2015 a 
partner purchased on 31 August 2016 25,000 ASC-shares 
from ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") as 
settlement of a forward contract that was previously 
entered into. The shares were sold at a price of NOK 
2.69 per share. The previously agreed price of NOK 
4.41 per share has been adjusted for dividends, 
interest adjustment and earlier settlement of the 
forward contract. 

ABGSC owns 5,853,890 treasury shares following the 
transaction above. ABGSC has forward contracts with 
partners purchasing a total of 29,885,067 shares with 
settlement in 2017 - 2019. ABGSC has authorization to 
re-purchase its shares in the market or to issue new 
shares.
