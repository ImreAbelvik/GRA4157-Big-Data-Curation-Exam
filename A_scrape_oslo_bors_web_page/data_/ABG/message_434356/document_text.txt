
With reference to our press release of 3 March 2015 ABG 
Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") on 7 September 2017 
sold 300,000 ASC-shares to Hans Øyvind Haukeli as settlement 
of a forward contract that was previously enter into. The 
shares were sold at a price of NOK 2.24 per share. The 
previously agreed price of NOK 4.41 per share has been 
adjusted for dividends, interest adjustments and earlier 
settlement of the forward contract. 

Hans Øyvind Haukeli owns 2,300,000 shares following the 
transaction above. His total holding incl. forward contracts 
is unchanged.

ABGSC owns 10,481,298 treasury shares following the 
transaction above. ABGSC has forward contracts with partners 
purchasing a total of 34,468,700 shares with settlement in 
2018 - 2022. ABGSC has authorization to re-purchase its 
shares in the market or to issue new shares.
