With reference to our press release of 6 March 2013 
a partner purchased yesterday 100,000 ABG Sundal 
Collier Holding ASA (ASC) shares from the company. 
The shares were sold at a price of NOK 2.99 per 
share. The previously agreed price of NOK 4.13 per 
share has been adjusted for dividend and earlier 
payment of the forward contract.

In addition the company purchased yesterday 25,000 
ASC shares from a resigned partner. The purchase 
price is NOK 0.23 per share, reflecting the 
restrictions imposed on partner shares.
 
ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA owns 3,601,393 
treasury shares following the transactions above. 
The company has forward contracts with partners 
purchasing a total of 43,587,638 shares with 
settlement in 2015 - 2017.
