With reference to our press releases of 7 March 2012 
and 3 June 2013, a partner purchased 570,000 ABG 
Sundal Collier Holding ASA (ASC) shares from the 
company yesterday in settlement of forward contracts 
that were previously entered into. The shares were 
sold at an average price of NOK 3.3125 per share. 
The previously agreed prices of NOK 4.22 and NOK 
4.92 per share have been adjusted for dividends, 
interest adjustments and earlier payment of the 
forward contract.

ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA owns 1,933,193 
treasury shares following the transaction above. The 
company has forward contracts with partners 
purchasing a total of 50,679,438 shares with 
settlement in 2015 - 2018, plus agreements with 
partners to issue 5,867,100 new shares after the AGM 
to be held at 29 April 2015.
