With reference to our press releases of 7 March 2007 
and 11 March 2009 two partners purchased 295,000 ABG 
Sundal Collier Holding ASA (ASC) shares from the 
company yesterday in settlement of a forward 
contract that was previously entered into. The 
shares were sold at an average price of NOK 2.0263 
per share. The previously agreed prices of NOK 14.07 
and NOK 3.84 have been adjusted for dividends, 
interest adjustments and extension of the forward 
contracts.

ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA owns 1,638,193 
treasury shares following the transaction above. The 
company has forward contracts with partners 
purchasing a total of 50,384,438 shares with 
settlement in 2015 - 2018, plus agreements with 
partners to issue 5,867,100 new shares after the AGM 
to be held today.
