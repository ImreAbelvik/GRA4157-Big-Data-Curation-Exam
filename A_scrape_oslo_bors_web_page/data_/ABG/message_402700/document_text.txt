ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA (OSE: ASC) yesterday 
did a placement of 5,831,964 shares from partners of 
the firm to investors and the CEO. The shares were 
sold at a price of NOK 5.40 per share. Following the 
placement partners of ABG Sundal Collier will hold 
approx. 28% of the fully diluted number of shares in 
the company.  

With reference to our previously press releases ASC 
sold yesterday 1,902,600 shares to partners as 
settlement of forward contracts that were previously 
entered into. The shares were sold at an average price 
of NOK 2.333 per share. The previously announced 
prices have been adjusted for dividends, interest 
changes and earlier settlement of the forward contract.
 
ASC owns 8,862,261 treasury shares following the 
transactions above. The company has forward contracts 
with partners purchasing a total of 34,074,838 shares 
with settlement in 2016-2019.
