On 9 March 2017 ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") 
sold 12,450,000 own shares on forward contracts with 
settlement on 1 June 2022. The shares were sold to new and 
existing partners as part of the annual share incentive 
program for partners. The shares were sold at a forward 
price of NOK 5.17 per share, the price is reflecting the 
restrictions imposed on such shares. 

ABGSC owns 5,872,490 treasury shares following the 
transaction above. ABGSC has forward contracts with partners 
purchasing a total of 40,792,967 shares with settlement in 
2017 - 2022. ABGSC has authorization to re-purchase its 
shares in the market or to issue new shares.  

The following partners who hold executive positions in the 
firm ("insiders") participated in the transaction:

Per Flostrand purchased 400,000 shares on a forward contract 
with settlement 1 June 2022. Flostrand owns 165,000 shares 
plus 1,100,000 shares on forward contracts following the 
transaction.

Tarjei Unneland purchased 350,000 shares on a forward

shares plus 850,000 shares on forward contracts following 
the transaction.

Christer Linde purchased 100,000 shares a forward contract

John Olaisen purchased 100,000 shares on a forward contract

transaction.
