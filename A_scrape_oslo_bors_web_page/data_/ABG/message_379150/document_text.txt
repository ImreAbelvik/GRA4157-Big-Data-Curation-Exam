ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA (OSE: ASC) today 
announced a placement of 4,137,287 shares from 
partners of the firm to institutional investors. The 
shares were sold at a price of NOK 5.55 per share. 
Following the placement partners of ABG Sundal 
Collier will hold approx. 30% of the fully diluted 
number of shares in the company.  

With reference to our previously press releases ASC 
sold yesterday 1,632,300 shares to partners as 
settlement of forward contracts that were previously 
entered into. The shares were sold at an average 
price of NOK 2.65 per share. The previously 
announced prices have been adjusted for dividends, 
interest changes and earlier settlement of the 
forward contract.
 
ASC owns 13,292,525 treasury shares following the 
transactions above. The company has forward 
contracts with partners purchasing a total of 
40,101,638 shares with settlement in 2015-2018.
