ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") sold on 15 February 
2017 2,714,300 own shares to specially identified staff who 
according to local regulations have to purchase shares as 
part of their variable compensation. The shares were sold at 
a price of NOK 4.69 per share, the price is reflecting the 
restrictions imposed on such shares. 

ABGSC owns 6,747,490 treasury shares following the 
transaction above. ABGSC has forward contracts with partners 
purchasing a total of 29,342,967 shares with settlement in 
2017 - 2019. ABGSC has authorization to re-purchase its 
shares in the market or to issue new shares.
