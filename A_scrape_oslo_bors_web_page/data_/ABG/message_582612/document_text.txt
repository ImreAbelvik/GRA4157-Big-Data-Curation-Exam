Jan Petter Collier has on 15 February 2023 sold 740,767 ABG-shares to Sanden Equity AS at a price of NOK 6.70 per share. Sanden Equity AS is a closely related company to Jan Petter Collier. 

Jan Petter Collier, incl. the closely related company Sanden Equity AS, owns 40,538,000 ABG-shares. The total number of shares owned is unchanged after the transaction.
