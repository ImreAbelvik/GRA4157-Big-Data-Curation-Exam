With reference to our press release of 3 March 2015 
ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") will issue 
5,867,100 new shares to partners. The shares will be 
issued at NOK 3.20 per share. In additions and with 
reference to the same press release ABGSC will issue 
355,000 new shares at a price of NOK 3.20 per share. 
The previously agreed prices of NOK 4.20 and NOK 
4.41 per share have been adjusted for dividend and 
earlier settlement of the forward contracts. 

Total proceed for the 6,222,100 new shares issued is 
NOK 19,910,720, of which NOK 1,431,083 is added to 
the share capital and NOK 18,479,637 is added to the 
account for paid in surplus capital.

Following the transactions above the company will 
have a total of 466,167,695 shares outstanding. The 
company owns 1,538,193 treasury shares. The company 
has forward contracts with partners for a total of 
49,929,438 shares with settlement in 2015 - 2018. 
ABGSC has authorization to re-purchase its shares in 
the market or to issue new shares.
