On 23 February 2018 ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") sold 12,606,700 own shares on forward contracts with settlement on 1 June 2023. The shares were sold to new and existing partners as part of the annual share incentive program for partners. The shares were sold at a forward price of NOK 6.00 per share, the price is reflecting the restrictions imposed on such shares. 

ABGSC owns 8,361,098 treasury shares following the transaction above. ABGSC has forward contracts with partners purchasing a total of 46,798,100 shares with settlement in 2018 - 2023. ABGSC has authorization to re-purchase its shares in the market or to issue new shares.
