Knut Brundtland has on 10 May 2022 purchased 102,900 ABG-shares from Giotto AS at a price of NOK 6.59 per share. Giotto AS is a company owned by Knut Brundtland and his family.

Knut Brundtland, incl. the family owned companies Giotto AS and Piero AS, owns 6,583,000 ABG-shares plus 7,500,000 shares on forward contracts following the transaction. His exposure towards ABG shares remains unchanged after the transaction.
