With reference to our press release dated 2 June 
2014, a partner purchased 59,800 ABG Sundal Collier 
Holding ASA (ASC) shares from the company yesterday 
in settlement of forward contracts that were 
previously entered into.

The shares were sold at an average price of NOK 
3.285 per share. The previously agreed prices of NOK 
4.13 and NOK 5.36 per share have been adjusted for 
dividends and early settlement of the forward 
contracts.

ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA owns 3,676,393 
treasury shares following the transaction above. The 
company has agreements with partners who have 
purchased a total of 43,687,638 shares on forward 
contracts for settlement in 2015 - 2017. ABGSC has 
authorization to re-purchase its shares in the 
market or to issue new shares.
