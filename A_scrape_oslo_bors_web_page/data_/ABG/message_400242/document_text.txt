With reference to our press release of 8 March 2016 
ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") will issue 
3,195,000 new shares to partners. The shares will be 
issued at NOK 4.43 per share. In addition and with 
reference to the press release of 3 March 2015 and 30 
May 2014 ABGSC will issue 819,400 new shares at a 
price of NOK 2.67 per share and 565,000 new shares at 
a price of NOK 3.19 per share. The previously agreed 
prices of NOK 5.03, NOK 4.41 and NOK 4.98 per share 
have been adjusted for dividends and earlier 
settlement of the forward contracts. 

Total proceed for the 4,579,400 new shares issued is 
NOK 18,143,998, of which NOK 1,053,262 is added to the 
share capital and NOK 17,090,736 is added to the 
account for paid in surplus capital.

Following the transactions above the company will have 
a total of 470,747,095 shares outstanding. The company 
owns 2,899,425 treasury shares. The company has 
forward contracts with partners for a total of 
36,909,138 shares with settlement in 2016 - 2019. 
ABGSC has authorization to re-purchase its shares in 
the market or to issue new shares.
