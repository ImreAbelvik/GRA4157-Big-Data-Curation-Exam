ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") announced on 
6 May 2016 an offer to buy back up to an aggregate of 
10,097,136 shares in ABGSC at an offer price of NOK 
5.65 per share in cash. The offer was valid until 
yesterday. ABGSC purchased the 10,097,136 ABGSC shares 
from the shareholders in the offering.

With reference to our previously press releases ABGSC 
sold yesterday 2,031,700 shares to partners as 
settlement of forward contracts that were previously 
entered into. The shares were sold at an average price 
of NOK 2.5242 per share. The previously announced 
prices have been adjusted for dividends, interest 
changes and earlier settlement of the forward contract.

ABGSC owns 10,764,861 treasury shares following the 
transactions above. The company has forward contracts 
with partners purchasing a total of 35,977,438 shares 
with settlement in 2016-2019.
