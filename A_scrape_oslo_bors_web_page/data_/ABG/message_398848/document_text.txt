ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") on 7 April 
2016 sold 750,000 ASC-shares to a partner on a forward 
contract with settlement 1 June 2019 at NOK 5.52 per 
share.

ABGSC owns 2,948,425 treasury shares. The company has 
forward contracts with partners purchasing a total of 
38,342,538 shares with settlement in 2016 - 2019, plus 
agreements with partners to issue 3,195,000 new shares 
after the AGM to be held on 26 April 2016. ABGSC has 
authorization to re-purchase its shares in the market 
or to issue new shares.
