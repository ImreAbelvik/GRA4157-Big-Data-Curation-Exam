ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") has today 
sold 600,000 ASC-shares to a partner on a forward 
contract with settlement 1 June 2018 at NOK 5.04 per 
share.

ABGSC owns 13,292,525 treasury shares. The company 
has forward contracts with partners purchasing a 
total of 40,701,638 shares with settlement in 2015 - 
2017. ABGSC has authorization to re-purchase its 
shares in the market or to issue new shares.
