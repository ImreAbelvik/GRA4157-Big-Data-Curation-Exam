ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") purchased on 
15 July 2016 300,000 ABGSC shares (ASC) at an average 
price of NOK 5.216 per share.

With reference to our press release of 3 March 2015 
ABGSC sold on 15 July 2016 300,000 ASC-shares to a 
partner as settlement of a forward contract that was 
previously entered into. The shares were sold at a 
price of NOK 2.68 per share. The previously agreed 
price of NOK 4.41 per share has been adjusted for 
dividends, interest adjustments and earlier settlement 
of the forward contract.

ABGSC owns 6,398,890 treasury shares following the 
transaction above. The company has forward contracts 
with partners purchasing a total of 30,705,067 shares 
with settlement in 2017 - 2019. ABGSC has 
authorization to re-purchase its shares in the market 
or to issue new shares.
