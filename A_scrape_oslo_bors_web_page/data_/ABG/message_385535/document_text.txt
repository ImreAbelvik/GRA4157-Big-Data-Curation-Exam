With reference to our press releases of 5 March 2014 
and 3 March 2015 a resigned partner purchased today 
90,000 ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA (ASC) shares 
from the company as settlement of a forward contract 
that was previously entered into. The shares were 
sold at an average price of NOK 3.15 per share. The 
previously agreed prices of NOK 4.79 and NOK 4.41 
per share have been adjusted for dividends, interest 
changes and earlier payment of the forward contract.

In addition the company purchased today 45,000 ASC 
shares from the resigned partner. The purchase price 
is NOK 0.23 per share, reflecting the restrictions 
imposed on partner shares.
 
ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA owns 8,932,925 
treasury shares following the transactions above. 
The company has forward contracts with partners 
purchasing a total of 36,272,038 shares with 
settlement in 2016 - 2018.
