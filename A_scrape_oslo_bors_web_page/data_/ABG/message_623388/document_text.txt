Knut Brundtland, incl. the family-owned companies Giotto AS and Piero AS, has today sold 4.083,000 ABG-shares at a price of NOK 6.30 per share. 

Following the transaction, the family-owned company Giotto AS, owns 7,500,000 ABG-shares plus 2,500,000 shares on a forward contract. 

For further information, see attachment available at www.newsweb.no.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements set out in the Market Abuse Regulation EU 596/2014 Article 19 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act Section 5-12.
