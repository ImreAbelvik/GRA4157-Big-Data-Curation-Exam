ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") on 27 April 
2016 sold 100,000 ASC-shares to a partner at NOK 4.83 
per share. The price is reflecting the restrictions 
imposed on partner shares.

transaction. The company has forward contracts with 
partners purchasing a total of 36,909,138 shares with 
settlement in 2016 - 2019. ABGSC has authorization to 
re-purchase its shares in the market or to issue new 
shares.
