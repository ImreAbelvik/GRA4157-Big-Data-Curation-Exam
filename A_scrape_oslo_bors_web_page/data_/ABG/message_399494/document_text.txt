With reference to our press release of 6 March 2013 an 
ex-partner purchased yesterday 49,000 ABG Sundal 
Collier Holding ASA (ASC) shares from the company as 
settlement of a forward contract that was previously 
entered into. The shares were sold at a price of NOK 
2.06 per share. The previously agreed price of NOK 
4.13 per share has been adjusted for dividends, 
interest adjustements and earlier settlement of the 
forward contract.

ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA owns 2,899,425 treasury 
shares following the transaction above. The company 
has forward contracts with partners purchasing a total 
of 38,293,538 shares with settlement in 2016 - 2019, 
plus agreements with partners to issue 3,195,000 new 
shares after the AGM to be held in 26 April 2016. 
ABGSC has authorization to re-purchase its shares in 
the market or to issue new shares.
