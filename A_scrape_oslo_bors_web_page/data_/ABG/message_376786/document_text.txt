With reference to our press releases of 7 March 2007 
a partner purchased yesterday 100,000 ABG Sundal 
Collier Holding ASA (ASC) shares from the company in 
settlement of a forward contract that was previously 
entered into. The shares were sold at a price of NOK 
8.45 per share. The previously agreed price of NOK 
14.07 has been adjusted for dividends, interest 
adjustments and extension of the forward contracts.

ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA owns 1,538,193 
treasury shares following the transaction above. The 
company has forward contracts with partners 
purchasing a total of 49,929,438 shares with 
settlement in 2015 - 2018, plus agreements with 
partners to issue 6,222,100 new shares after the AGM 
which was held yesterday.
