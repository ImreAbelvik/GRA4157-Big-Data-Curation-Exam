ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") on 1 
September 2016 sold 700,000 ASC-shares to a partner on 
a forward contract with settlement 1 September 2019 at 
NOK 4.60 per share. The price is reflecting the 
restrictions imposed on partner shares.

In addition and with reference to our press release of 
3 March 2015 a partner purchased on 1 September 2016 
150,000 ASC-shares from ABGSC as settlement of a 
forward contract that was previously entered into. The 
shares were sold at a price of NOK 2.69 per share. The 
previously agreed price of NOK 4.41 per share has been 
adjusted for dividends, interest adjustment and 
earlier settlement of the forward contract. 

ABGSC owns 5,703,890 treasury shares. The company has 
forward contracts with partners purchasing a total of 
30,435,067 shares with settlement in 2017 - 2019. 
ABGSC has authorization to re-purchase its shares in 
the market or to issue new shares.
