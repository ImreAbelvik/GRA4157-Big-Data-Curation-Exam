ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA ("ABGSC") announced 8 
May 2015 an offer to buy back up to an aggregate of 
20,582,358 shares in ABGSC at an offer price of NOK 
5.55 per share in cash. The offer was valid until 
yesterday. ABGSC increased the buy back and has in 
total purchased 21,582,132 ABGSC shares from the 
shareholders in the offering.

With reference to our previously press releases 
ABGSC sold yesterday 7,995,500 shares to partners as 
settlement of forward contracts that were previously 
entered into. The shares were sold at an average 
price of NOK 2.1871 per share. The previously 
announced prices have been adjusted for dividends, 
interest changes and earlier settlement of the 
forward contract.

ABGSC owns 14,924,825 treasury shares following the 
transactions above. The company has forward 
contracts with partners purchasing a total of 
41,733,938 shares with settlement in 2015-2018.
