With reference to our press releases of 5 March 2014 
and 3 March 2015 a partner purchased on 1 July 2016 
27,500 ASC-shares from ABG Sundal Collier Holding ASA 
("ABGSC") as settlement of forward contracts that were 
previously entered into. The shares were sold at an 
average price of NOK 2.6682 per share. The previously 
agreed prices of NOK 4.79 and NOK 4.41 per share have 
been adjusted for dividends, interest adjustments and 
earlier settlement of the forward contracts.

ABGSC owns 5,792,490 treasury shares following the 
transaction above. ABGSC has forward contracts with 
partners purchasing a total of 31,005,067 shares with 
settlement in 2017 - 2019. ABGSC has authorization to 
re-purchase its shares in the market or to issue new 
shares.
