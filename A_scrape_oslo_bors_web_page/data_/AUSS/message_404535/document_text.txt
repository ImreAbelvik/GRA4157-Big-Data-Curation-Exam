On 20 June 2016 Lerkehaug AS, a company owned by 
Austevoll Seafood ASA's CFO Britt Kathrine Drivenes, 
sold 75,000 shares in Austevoll Seafood ASA at a price 
of NOK 71.2127 per share. Subsequent to this 
transaction Lerkehaug AS holds 50,367 shares in 
Austevoll Seafood ASA.


This information is subject to the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act.
