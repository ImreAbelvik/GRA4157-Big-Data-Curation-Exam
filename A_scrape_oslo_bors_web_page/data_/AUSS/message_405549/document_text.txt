On 7 July 2016 the extraordinary General Meeting of 
shareholders in Br. Birkeland AS, a company controlled 
by Austevoll Seafood ASA, resolved to distribute 
dividends to its shareholders in the form of 1,722,223 
shares in Austevoll Seafood ASA. Of these dividend 
shares, Austevoll Seafood ASA has acquired 893,300 
shares at NOK 72.25 per share. Subsequent to this 
transaction Austevoll Seafood ASA holds 893,300 own 
shares.



Board member Helge Møgster owns shares in Br. 
Birkeland AS, through his wholly owned company 
Møgsterbas AS. Møgsterbas acquires 61,469 shares in 
Austevoll Seafood ASA through this distribution. 
Subsequent to this transaction Møgsterbas AS holds 
61,469 shares in Austevoll Seafood ASA.



This information is subject to the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act.
