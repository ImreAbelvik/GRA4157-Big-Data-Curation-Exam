On 22 June 2016 Fagerbø AS, a company owned by 
Austevoll Seafood ASA's Group Chief Accountant Liv 
Bente Storebø, 
sold 12,735 shares in Austevoll Seafood ASA at a price 
of NOK 71.00 per share. Subsequent to this 
transaction Fagerbø AS holds 10,000 shares in 
Austevoll Seafood ASA. 
The proceeds of sale will be used to repayment of debt.


This information is subject to the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act.
