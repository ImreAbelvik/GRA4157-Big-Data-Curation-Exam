


Mr. John Arild Ertvaag, Board member of Scana Industrier ASA, has on 26(th)
August 2015 acquired 300,000 shares in Scana Industrier at an average price of
NOK 0.7831. The shares are acquired through his company CAMAR AS.

Following this transaction CAMAR AS owns 21 831 121 shares in Scana Industrier
ASA.
