Bergen, 17 January 2024: Primary Insider Baste Tveito, Executive Vice President Operations in Scana ASA, has 17 January 2024 through his related party Mebas AS acquired 264,053 shares in Scana ASA at an average price of NOK 2.13 per share. Following this transaction, Baste Tveito owns 654,059 shares in Scana ASA.

Scana in brief
Scana is a listed industrial owner company in the ocean industries creating value through active ownership in market-leading portfolio companies. Scana is headquartered in Bergen and has around 500 employees. www.scana.no
