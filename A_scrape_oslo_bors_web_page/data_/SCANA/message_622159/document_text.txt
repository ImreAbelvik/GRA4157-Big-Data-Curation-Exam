Bergen, 21 June 2024: Primary Insider Sverre Morten Blix, Board Member of Scana ASA, has 21 June 2024 through his related party Spiralen Holding AS acquired 852,631 shares in Scana ASA at an average price of NOK 2.0929 per share. Following this transaction, Sverre Morten Blix owns 9,424,059 shares in Scana ASA.

Scana in brief
