


Mr. John Arild Ertvaag, Board member of Scana Industrier ASA, has on 25(th)
August 2015 acquired 300,000 shares in Scana Industrier at an average price of
NOK 0.739. The shares are acquired through his company CAMAR AS.

Following this transaction CAMAR AS owns 21 531 121 shares in Scana Industrier
ASA which gives an ownership of 20.03%.

For further information please contact:
Mr. Kjetil Flesjå, CFO Scana Industrier ASA, Mobile: +47 900 41 213

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
