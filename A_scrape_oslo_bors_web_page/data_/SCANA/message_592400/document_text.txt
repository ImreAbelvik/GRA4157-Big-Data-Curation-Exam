Bergen 3 June 2023: Primary Insider Baste Tveito, Executive Vice President Operations in Scana ASA, has 2 June 2023 through his related party Mebas AS acquired 390,006 shares in Scana ASA at an average price of NOK 1.93 per share. 
Following this transaction, Baste Tveito owns 390,006 shares in Scana ASA.
