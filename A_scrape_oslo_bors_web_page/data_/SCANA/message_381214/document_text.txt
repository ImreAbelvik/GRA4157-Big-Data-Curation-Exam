


Reference is made to the ongoing rights issue in Scana industrier ASA (the
"Company"). Primary insiders in the Company have conducted the following
transactions on 29June:

Kjetil Flesjå, CFO, have subscribed for 1 000 000 new shares. Based on the
subscription on the basis of granted subscription rights, Kjetil Flesjå will
hold minimum 930 315 new shares in the Company following the completion of the
share issue.

Inge Kvalvik, Special Advisor, have subscribed for 780 062 new shares through
his company Inge Kvalvik AS. Based on the subscription on the basis of granted
subscription rights, Inge Kvalvik will hold 838 659 new shares in the Company
following the completion of the share issue through his company Inge Kvalvik AS.

Torunn Hognestad, Administration Secretary, have subscribed for 35 157 new
shares. Based on the subscription on the basis of granted subscription rights,
Torunn Hognestad will hold 37 798 new shares in the Company following the
completion of the share issue.

Terje Egeland, Commercial Manager, have subscribed for 2 000 000 new shares
through his company Miglia AS. Based on the subscription on the basis of granted
subscription rights, Terje Egeland will hold 1 574 356 new shares in the Company
following the completion of the share issue through his company Miglia AS.

All shares have been subscribed for at a subscription price of NOK 0.10 per
share. Subscribers are entitled to increase their subscription prior to the end
of the subscription period.

Allocation of any over-subscription by primary insiders will be resolved on or
about 30 June 2015, and will subsequently be announced.
