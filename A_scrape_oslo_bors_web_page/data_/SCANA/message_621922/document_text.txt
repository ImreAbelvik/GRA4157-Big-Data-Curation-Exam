Bergen, 19 June 2024: Primary Insider Baste Tveito, COO in Scana ASA, has 19 June 2024 through his related party Mebas AS acquired 245,000 shares in Scana ASA at an average price of NOK 2.045 per share. Following this transaction, Baste Tveito owns 899,059 shares in Scana ASA.

Scana in brief
Scana is a listed industrial owner company in the ocean industries creating value through active ownership in market-leading portfolio companies. Scana is headquartered in Bergen and has above 500 employees. www.scana.no
