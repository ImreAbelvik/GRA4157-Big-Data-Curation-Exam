


Mr. John Arild Ertvaag, Board member of Scana Industrier ASA, has on 27(th)
August 2015 acquired 22,582 shares in Scana Industrier at an average price of
NOK 0.8. The shares are acquired through his company CAMAR AS.

Following this transaction CAMAR AS owns 21 853 703 shares in Scana Industrier
ASA.
