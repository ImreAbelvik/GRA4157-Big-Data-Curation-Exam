


CAMAR AS has on 12January 2016 acquired 40 000 shares in Scana Industrier ASA at
an average price of NOK 0.7575.

Following this transaction CAMAR AS owns 23 090 287 shares in Scana Industrier
ASA.

CAMAR is considered as a primary insider because CAMAR is represented in the
Board of Directors in Scana Industrier ASA by Sindre Ertvaag.
