


Reference is made to the ongoing rights issue in Scana industrier ASA (the
"Company"). Primary insiders in the Company have conducted the following
transactions on 29June:

John Arild Ertvaag, member of the Board, has subscribed for 170 000 000 new
shares through his company Camar AS. Based on the subscription on the basis of
granted subscription rights, John Arild Ertvaag will hold minimum 176 201 971
new shares in the Company following the completion of the share issue through
his company Camar AS.

Sindre Ertvaag, primary insider, has subscribed for 195 837 new shares through
his company Sincap AS. Based on the subscription on the basis of granted
subscription rights, Sindre Ertvaag will hold 210 548 new shares in the Company
following the completion of the share issue through his company Sincap AS.

Bjørn Torkildsen, chairman of the Board, has subscribed for 1 730 602 new shares
through his company Invenius AS. Based on the subscription on the basis of
granted subscription rights, Bjørn Torkildsen will hold 1 860 602 new shares in
the Company following the completion of the share issue through his company
Invenius AS.

Per Ravnestad, member of the Board, has subscribed for 60 000 000 new shares
through the company IOS AS. Based on the subscription on the basis of granted
subscription rights, Per Ravnestad will hold 62 389 361 new shares in the
Company following the completion of the share issue through the companies Panda
AS and International Oilfield Services AS.

Martha Kold Bakkevig, member of the Board, has subscribed for 145 024 new shares
through the company Kold Invest AS. Based on the subscription on the basis of
granted subscription rights, Martha Kold Bakkevig will hold 155 918 new shares
in the Company following the completion of the share issue through the company
Kold Invest AS.

All shares have been subscribed for at a subscription price of NOK 0.10 per
share. Subscribers are entitled to increase their subscription prior to the end
of the subscription period.

Allocation of any over-subscription by primary insiders will be resolved on or
about 30 June 2015, and will subsequently be announced.
