


Mr. John Arild Ertvaag, Board member of Scana Industrier ASA, has on 31 August
2015 acquired 137,320 shares in Scana Industrier at an average price of NOK
0.795. The shares are acquired through his company CAMAR AS.

Following this transaction CAMAR AS owns 22 191 023 shares in Scana Industrier
ASA.
