Norsk Innovasjonskapital AS ("NIK I") and Norsk Innovasjonskapital II AS ("NIK II") have today, 15 March 2023 sold 126,838 and 88,495 shares in Nordic Technology Group AS (the "Company"), respectively. NIK I and II are closely associated to certain members of the Company's board of directors, including the Chair, Henrik Christensen, and to the Company's CEO, Rune Rinnan.

Please refer to the attached notification forms for further information.

This information is subject to the disclosure pursuant to article 19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation, as implemented in the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
