


Wiborg Kapitalforvaltning AB, a company controlled by Ragnhild Wiborg,
chairperson of the board of directors of EAM Solar ASA, has subscribed for
1,000 new shares in the rights issue of EAM Solar ASA. Allocation of new shares
in the rights issue is expected to take place on or about 29 August 2017, and
the number of shares allocated to Wiborg Kapitalforvaltning AB will only be
known at that time. The rights issue is being carried out at a subscription
price of NOK 24 per share.


For more information or questions, please contact:


Viktor E Jakobsen, CEO, phone +47 9161 1009, viktor@eam.no


About EAM Solar: EAM Solar is a utility company that acquires and operates solar
power plants. The Company currently owns solar PV power plants in Italy. The
plants are operating under long-term sales contracts.


This disclosure is made pursuant to section 4-2 of the Securities Trading Act.
