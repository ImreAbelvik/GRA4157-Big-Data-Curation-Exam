



28 June 2017



Wiborg  Kapitalforvaltning AB which is  indirectly controlled by Ragnhild Wiborg
(chair  of the board of  directors of EAM Solar  ASA), has today acquired 3,000
shares  at a price  of NOK 43.40 per  share in EAM  Solar ASA ("EAM"). After the
acquisition  Ragnhild Wiborg and  her associated companies  hold 3,000 shares in
EAM, representing 0.06 % of the shares and votes in EAM.



For more information or questions, please contact:



Viktor E Jakobsen, CEO, phone +47 9161 1009, viktor@eam.no



About EAM Solar: EAM Solar is a utility company that acquires and operates solar
power  plants. The Company  currently owns solar  PV power plants  in Italy. The
plants are operating under long-term sales contracts.



This disclosure is made pursuant to section 4-2 of the Securities Trading Act.
