3 September 2019

AS Brdr Michaelsen which is indirectly controlled by Christian Hagemann (Chief Operating Officer of EAM Solar ASA), has today acquired 5,000 shares at a price of NOK 18.50 per share in EAM Solar ASA ("EAM"). After the acquisition Christian Hagemann and his associated companies hold 10,000 shares in EAM, representing 0.15 % of the shares and votes in EAM. 

This disclosure is made pursuant to section 4-2 of the Securities Trading Act.

Viktor E Jakobsen, CEO, phone +47 9161 1009, viktor@eam.no 

About EAM Solar ASA: EAM Solar ASA is a utility company that acquires and operates solar power plants. The Company currently owns solar PV power plants in Italy. The plants are operating under long-term sales contracts. In addition, the Company's business is lawsuits in relation to solar power plants.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
