Carol Bell, member of the board of directors in Bonheur ASA, has on 26 October
2020 purchased 1,200 shares in Bonheur ASA at an average price of NOK 200.8 per
share. Following this transaction, Carol Bell owns 1,200 shares in Bonheur ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to sections
5-12 and 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
