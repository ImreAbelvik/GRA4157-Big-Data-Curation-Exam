2020 purchased 765 shares in Bonheur ASA at an average price of NOK 234.69 per
share. Following this transaction, Bente Hagem owns 765 shares in Bonheur ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to sections
5-12 and 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
