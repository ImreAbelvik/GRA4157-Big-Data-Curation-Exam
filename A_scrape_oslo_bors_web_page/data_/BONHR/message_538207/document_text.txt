Bente Hagem, member of the board of directors in Bonheur ASA, has on 15 July
2021 purchased 740 shares in Bonheur ASA at a price of NOK 271 per share.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to sections
5-12 and 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
