Nick Emery, member of the board of directors in Bonheur ASA, has on 9 March
2022 purchased 325 shares in Bonheur ASA at a price of NOK 337.35 per share.
Following this transaction Nick Emery owns 325 shares in Bonheur ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in the EU Market
Abuse Regulation (EU 596/2014 article 19) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act.
