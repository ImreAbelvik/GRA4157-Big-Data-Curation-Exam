Brian Glover, member of the Board and primary insider in Ocean Sun AS has on the 2nd of December 2022, through BKraft Holding AS, bought 11,000 shares in the company at a price of NOK 7.69 per share. Brian Glover owns 100% of the shares in BKraft Holding AS which, following this transaction, holds 400,000 shares in Ocean Sun AS. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation article 19.
 

About Ocean Sun:

By combining the Norwegian maritime expertise and knowledge within photovoltaics, Ocean Sun has developed an innovation that offers a bold solution to our global energy needs. The patented technology is based on solar modules mounted on hydro-elastic membranes and offers cost and performance benefits not seen in any other floating PV system today. With offices in Oslo, Singapore and Shanghai, Ocean Sun aims to become the world's leading technology provider of floating solar.
