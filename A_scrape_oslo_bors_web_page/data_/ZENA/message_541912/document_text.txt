ZENITH ENERGY LTD.

("Zenith" or the "Company")

Granting of Stock Options 

Zenith Energy Ltd. ("Zenith" or the "Company") (LSE: ZEN; OSE: ZENA), the listed international oil & gas production company focused on pursuing African development opportunities, is pleased to announce that it has granted a total of 13,882,232 stock options (the "Options") to certain Directors and employees of the Company in accordance with the Company’s Stock Option Plan. 

The Options will have an exercise price of NOK 0.125 per Option (approximately 
equivalent to £0.01). The Options are fully vested and have the duration of five years from the date of granting.
