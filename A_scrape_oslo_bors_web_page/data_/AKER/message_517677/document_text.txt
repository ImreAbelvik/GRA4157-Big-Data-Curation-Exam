Normann Eidsvig Røkke, son of Kjell Inge Røkke, has today acquired 57 shares in
Aker ASA at a price of NOK 444.28 per share. The shares were acquired on Oslo
Stock Exchange. Following the transaction, Normann Eidsvig Røkke holds a total
of 57 shares in Aker ASA.
