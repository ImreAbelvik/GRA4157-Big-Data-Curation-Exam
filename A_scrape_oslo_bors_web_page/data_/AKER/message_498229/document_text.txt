March 13, 2020 - Finn Berg Jacobsen, deputy chairman of Aker ASA, today
purchased 1,000 shares in Aker ASA at a price of NOK 238.60 per share.

The shares were acquired on the Oslo Stock Exchange. Following the transaction,
Jacobsen holds a total of 6,159 shares in Aker ASA, through his company FBJ
Consulting AS.
