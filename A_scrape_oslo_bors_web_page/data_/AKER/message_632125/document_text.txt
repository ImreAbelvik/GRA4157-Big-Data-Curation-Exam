Aker ASA ("Aker") has today carried out its employee share purchase program for
the year. Participants in the share purchase program were offered a discount of
20 per cent on the closing share price as of 13 November 2024. Hence, each
participant paid NOK 443.20 per share. All shares will be locked in for a period
of three years from delivery of the shares, during which the employees will not
be able to sell the shares.

in Aker after the acquisition will be 11,400 shares.
