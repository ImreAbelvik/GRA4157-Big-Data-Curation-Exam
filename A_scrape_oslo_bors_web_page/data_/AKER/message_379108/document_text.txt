


Reference is made to the issue of up to 2 310 189 new shares in Aker ASA (the
"Company") in connection with the dividend payment (the "Dividend Issue"). The
subscription price in the Dividend Issue is NOK 156.58 per share.

Additionally, the Company has carried out an employee share purchase programme.
The subscription period for the programme ran from 21-27 May 2015. Employees in
the Company were offered to buy Aker ASA shares at a 20 per cent discount on the
closing price of the share on 27 May 2015. Hence, each participant paid NOK
128.80 per share. All shares will be locked for a period of three years from the
date of delivery, during which employees will not be able to sell their shares.
