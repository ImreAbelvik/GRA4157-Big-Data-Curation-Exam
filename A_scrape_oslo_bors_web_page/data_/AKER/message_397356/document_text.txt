


As part of the existing bonus program for employees in Aker ASA (the "Company"),
the following primary insiders have received bonus shares. The shares have a
lock-in period of three years. Upon allocation, the value of the shares was set
to NOK 129.84. This corresponds to the three-day average, volume-weighted price
of the Company share starting Thursday 10 March 2016, to which a 20 per cent
discount was applied.
