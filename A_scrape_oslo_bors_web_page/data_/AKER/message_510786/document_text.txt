Martin Bech Holte, Investment Director at Aker ASA, has acquired 10,000 shares
in Aker ASA at a price of NOK 329.60 per share.

The shares were offered at a discount of 20 per cent on the closing share price
6 August 2020 of NOK 412. All shares will be locked up for a period of three
years from delivery of the shares, during which Mr. Bech Holte will not be able
to sell the shares. Following the transaction, Mr. Bech Holte holds a total of
10,000 shares in Aker ASA, and Aker ASA holds 49,101 own shares.
