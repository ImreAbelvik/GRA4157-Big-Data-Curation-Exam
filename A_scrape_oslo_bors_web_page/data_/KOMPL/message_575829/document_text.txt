Sandefjord/Oslo, 16 November 2022: Reference is made to the stock exchange
notice by Komplett ASA (the "Company") on 15 November 2022 regarding the
successfully placed private placement of 67,800,000 new shares in the Company
(the "Private Placement").

The following primary insiders in the Company were allocated shares in the
Private Placement:

* Jo Lunder (Chair of the Board of Directors) has, through his wholly owned
company Cigalep AS, been allocated 146,445 shares in the Private Placement;

* Lars Thoresen (Board Member) has, through his wholly owned company LT Invest
AS, been allocated 146,445 shares in the Private Placement;

* Lars Olav Olaussen (CEO) has, through his wholly owned company R og L Invest
AS, been allocated 16,949 shares in the Private Placement;

* Krister Pedersen (CFO) has, through his wholly owned company Crit AS, been
allocated 67,797 shares in the Private Placement;

* Jon Martin Klafstad (Managing Director Komplett) has, through his wholly owned
company AS Master Trading, been allocated 33,898 shares in the Private
Placement;

* Anders Torell (Managing Director Webhallen) has, through his wholly owned
company Grand Mosse Konsult AB, been allocated 16,949 shares in the Private
Placement;

* Trine-Lise Jensen (Head of Group SC/IT Program) has, through here wholly owned
company TLJ Invest AS, been allocated 13,559 shares in the Private Placement;
and

* Kristin Hødal Torgersen (Chief HR Officer) has been allocated 10,169 shares in
the Private Placement.

Primary insider notifications pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation article 19
are attached.
