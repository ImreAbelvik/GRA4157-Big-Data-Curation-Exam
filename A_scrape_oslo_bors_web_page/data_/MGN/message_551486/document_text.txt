Gisle Grønlie, EVP Legal & M&A of Magnora ASA, has purchased 21500 shares in
Magnora ASA ("Magnora") today 17 January 2021 at an average price of NOK 23 per
share.

Following this acquisition, Mr. Grønlie controls 34500 shares in Magnora ASA,
which equals 0.06 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.

Gisle Grønlie also holds 25,000 share options in Magnora ASA.

DISCLOSURE REGULATION

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

ABOUT MAGNORA ASA

Magnora ASA (OSE: MGN) has a legacy royalty business that is re-invested in
renewable energy development projects and companies. Magnora's portfolio of
renewable companies consists of Evolar AB, Helios Nordic Energy AB, Kustvind AB,
Magnora Offshore Wind AS, Vindr Group AS, and a South African SPV. The company
is listed on the main list on Oslo Stock Exchange under the ticker MGN.
