Hilde Ådland, Director on the Magnora ASA board, has purchased 5,500 shares in Magnora ASA (“Magnora”) today March 12th, 2020 at an average price of NOK 8.50 per share.

Following this acquisition, Hilde Ådland controls 5,500 shares in Magnora ASA, which equals 0.01 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company. 

Hilde Ådland also holds 50,000 share options in Magnora ASA.
