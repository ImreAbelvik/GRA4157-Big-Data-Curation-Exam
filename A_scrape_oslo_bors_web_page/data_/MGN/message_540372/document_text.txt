Bjørn Gisle Grønlie, EVP Legal and M&A of Magnora ASA, has purchased 13,000
shares in Magnora ASA ("Magnora") today 20 August 2021 at an average price of
NOK 15.50 per share.

Following this acquisition, Mr. Grønlie controls 13,000 shares in Magnora ASA,
which equals 0.02 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.

Mr. Grønlie also holds 25,000 share options in Magnora ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section

ABOUT MAGNORA ASA

Magnora ASA (OSE: MGN) is a profitable renewable energy development company
looking for green investment opportunities. Magnora's portfolio of renewable
companies consists of Evolar AB, Helios Nordic Energy AB, Kustvind AB, Magnora
Offshore Wind AS, Vindr Group AS, and a South African SPV. The company is listed
on the main list on Oslo Stock Exchange under the ticker MGN.
