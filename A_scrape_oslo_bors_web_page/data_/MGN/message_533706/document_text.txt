Haakon Alfstad, EVP Projects of Magnora ASA, has purchased 5,000 shares in Magnora ASA (“Magnora”) today 20 May 2021 at an average price of NOK 18.80 per share.

Following this acquisition, Mr. Alfstad controls 47,755 shares in Magnora ASA, which equals 0.08 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company. 

Haakon Alfstad also holds 200,000 share options in Magnora ASA.
