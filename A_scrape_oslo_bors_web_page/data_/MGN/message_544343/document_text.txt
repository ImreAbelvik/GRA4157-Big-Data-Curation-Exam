Hilde Ådland, Director on the Magnora ASA board, has purchased 4,500 shares in
Magnora ASA ("Magnora") today 14 October 2021 at an average price of NOK 16.983
per share.

Following this acquisition, Hilde Ådland controls 13,700 shares in Magnora ASA,
which equals 0.02 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.

Hilde Ådland also holds 55,000 share options in Magnora ASA.

ABOUT MAGNORA ASA

Magnora ASA (OSE: MGN) has a legacy royalty business that is re-invested in
renewable energy development projects and companies. Magnora's portfolio of
renewable companies consists of Evolar AB, Helios Nordic Energy AB, Kustvind AB,
Magnora Offshore Wind AS, Vindr Group AS, and a South African SPV. The company
is listed on the main list on Oslo Stock Exchange under the ticker MGN.
