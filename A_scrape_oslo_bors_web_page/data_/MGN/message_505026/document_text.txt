Bård Olsen, CFO of Magnora ASA, has purchased 2,000 shares in Magnora ASA (“Magnora”) today May 8th, 2020 at an average price of NOK 7.90 per share.

Following this acquisition, Bård Olsen controls 18,000 shares in Magnora ASA, which equals 0.03 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company. 

Bård Olsen also holds 25,000 share options in Magnora ASA.
