Ballista AS (“Ballista”), a company directly controlled by Erik Sneve has settled a forward agreement underlying 480,700 shares in Magnora ASA (“Magnora”) expiring June 19th, 2020 and subsequently entered into a new forward agreement underlying the same number of shares 480,700. New expiry date is September 18th, 2020 and new forward price is NOK 7.7973. Ballista does not hold other shares or rights to shares in Magnora.

Mr. Sneve also holds 450,000 share options in Magnora ASA.
