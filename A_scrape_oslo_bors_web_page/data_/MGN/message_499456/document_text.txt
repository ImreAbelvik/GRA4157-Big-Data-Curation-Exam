As notified to the market on March 23, 2020, Erik Sneve, CEO of Magnora ASA, purchased 10,000 shares in Magnora ASA (“Magnora”) on March 23rd, 2020 at NOK 7.525 per share.

Erik Sneve’s total ownership after this trade directly and through affiliated ownership through close family and other accounts is 544,919 shares, which equals 1.04 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company. 

The initial notification did not disclose that Mr. Sneve also holds 400,000 share options in Magnora ASA.
