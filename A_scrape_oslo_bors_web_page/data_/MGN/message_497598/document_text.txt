Bård Olsen, CFO of Magnora ASA, has purchased 6,000 shares in Magnora ASA (“Magnora”) today March 9th, 2020 at an average price of NOK 11.30 per share.

Following this acquisition, Bård Olsen controls 16,000 shares in Magnora ASA, which equals 0.03 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company. 

Bård Olsen does not hold other shares or rights to shares in Magnora ASA.
