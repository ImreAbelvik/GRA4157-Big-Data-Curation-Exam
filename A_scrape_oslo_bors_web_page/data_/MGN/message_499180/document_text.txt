Erik Sneve, CEO of Magnora ASA, has purchased 10,000 shares in Magnora ASA (“Magnora”) today March 23rd, 2020 at NOK 7.525 per share.

Erik Sneve’s total ownership after this trade directly and through affiliated ownership through close family and other accounts is 544,919 shares, which equals 1.04 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company. 

Erik Sneve does not hold other shares or rights to shares in Magnora ASA.
