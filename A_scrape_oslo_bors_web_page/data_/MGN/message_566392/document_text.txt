Bård Olsen, CFO of Magnora ASA, has purchased 24,000 shares in Magnora ASA
("Magnora") today 4 July 2022 at an average price of NOK 18.2068 per share.

Following this acquisition, Bård Olsen controls 50,000 shares in Magnora ASA,
which equals 0.09 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.

Mr. Olsen also holds 100,000 share options in Magnora ASA.

ABOUT MAGNORA ASA

Magnora ASA (OSE: MGN) has a legacy royalty business that is re-invested in
renewable energy development projects and companies. Magnora's portfolio of
renewable companies consists of Evolar AB, Helios Nordic Energy AB, Kustvind AB,
Magnora Offshore Wind AS, Magnora South Africa, and AGV. The company is listed
on the main list on Oslo Stock Exchange under the ticker MGN.
