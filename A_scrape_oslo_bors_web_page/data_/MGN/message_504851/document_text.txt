Theis Hanang Pedersen, Head of Business Development & Strategy of Magnora ASA, has purchased 3,800 shares in Magnora ASA (“Magnora”) today May 7th, 2020 at an average price of NOK 7.90 per share.

Following this acquisition, Mr. Pedersen controls 3,800 shares in Magnora ASA, which equals 0.01 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company. 

Theis Hanang Pedersen also holds 25,000 share options in Magnora ASA.
