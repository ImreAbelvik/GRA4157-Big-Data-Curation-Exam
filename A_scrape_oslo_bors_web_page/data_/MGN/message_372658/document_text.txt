BLMF Holding AS, which is controlled by Lars 
Ødeskaug, COO of Sevan Marine ASA, has March 3, 
2015, bought 20,000 shares in Sevan Marine ASA for 
an average price NOK 20.00 per share.

Following this acquisition, Lars Ødeskaug controls 
220,000 shares in Sevan Marine ASA. 

The information in this announcement is subject to 
the disclosure requirements of the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act section 5-12 and/or the 
Continuing Obligations of Oslo Børs.
