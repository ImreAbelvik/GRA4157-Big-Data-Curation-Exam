Haakon Alfstad, Investor Director of Magnora ASA, has purchased 6,450 shares in Magnora ASA (“Magnora”) today June 19th, 2020 at an average price of NOK 7.7185 per share.

Following this acquisition, Mr. Alfstad controls 19,055 shares in Magnora ASA, which equals 0.04 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company. 

Haakon Alfstad also holds 50,000 share options in Magnora ASA.
