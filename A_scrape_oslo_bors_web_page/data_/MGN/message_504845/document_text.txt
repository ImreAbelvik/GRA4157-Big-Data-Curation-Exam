Haakon Alfstad, Investor Director of Magnora ASA board, has purchased 2,000 shares in Magnora ASA (“Magnora”) today May 7th, 2020 at an average price of NOK 7.90 per share.

Following this acquisition, Mr. Alfstad controls 12,605 shares in Magnora ASA, which equals 0.02 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company. 

Haakon Alfstad also holds 50,000 share options in Magnora ASA.
