Torstein Sannes, Chairman of Magnora ASA, has purchased 26,950 shares in Magnora ASA (“Magnora”) today March 10th, 2020 at an average price of NOK 9.50 per share.

Following this acquisition, Torstein Sannes controls 26,950 shares in Magnora ASA, which equals 0.05 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company. 

Mr. Sannes also holds 400,000 share options in Magnora ASA.

Furthermore, John Hamilton, Director on the Magnora ASA board, has purchased 13,950 shares in Magnora ASA (“Magnora”) today March 10th, 2020 at an average price of NOK 9.50 per share.

Following this acquisition, John Hamilton controls 13,950 shares in Magnora ASA, which equals 0.03 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company. 

Mr. Hamilton also holds 50,000 share options in Magnora ASA.
