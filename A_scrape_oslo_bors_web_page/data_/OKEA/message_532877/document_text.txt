Primary insider in OKEA ASA has bought shares in OKEA

Knut Gjertsen has 11.5.2021 purchased 1,000 shares in OKEA at an average price
of NOK 13.30. The shares have been purchased in connection with OKEA's approved
annual incentive scheme. Knut Gjertsen holds after this transaction a total of
132,000 shares in OKEA.
