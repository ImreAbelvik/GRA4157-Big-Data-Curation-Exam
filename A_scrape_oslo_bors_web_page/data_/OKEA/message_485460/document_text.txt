OKEA - purchase of shares under company incentive program

Reference is made to the announcement on 13 September 2019 concerning a purchase
program for shares under OKEA ASA's incentive programs. On 17 September 2019,
75,000 shares have been acquired under the program, at an average price of NOK
20.5536 per share. The lowest and highest price paid per share was NOK 19.952
and NOK 21,000 per share, respectively.  In total, 150 000 shares have now been
acquired under the program. For further details, including list of relevant
primary insiders, see the announcement of 13 September 2019.
