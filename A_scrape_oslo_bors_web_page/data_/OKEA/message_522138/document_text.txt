CEO in OKEA, Erik Haugane, has 06.01.21 purchased 10 000 shares in OKEA at an average price of NOK 11,50. 
The shares have been purchased in connection with OKEA’s annual incentive programme. Erik Haugane holds after this transaction a total of 161 000 shares in OKEA. Haugane also holds 67% in Kørven AS that holds 1 120 344 shares in OKEA.
