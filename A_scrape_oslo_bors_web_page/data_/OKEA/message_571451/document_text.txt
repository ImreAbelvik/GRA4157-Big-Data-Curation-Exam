(Trondheim, 16 September 2022) OKEA ASA (OSE: OKEA): BCPR Pte. Ltd (a closely
related party to Mike Fischer, board member in OKEA and Chaiwat Kovavisarach,
chairman of the board of directors in OKEA), has today sold a total of 6,465
shares at an average price of NOK 40.18 pr share. Following the sale, BCPR Pte.
Ltd holds 47,218,098 shares in OKEA.

Copy of notification is attached hereto.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to
Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act.
