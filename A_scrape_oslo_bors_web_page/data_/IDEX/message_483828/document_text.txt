Stephen A. Skaggs, who is a board member of IDEX Biometrics ASA ("IDEX"), has on
26 August 2019 bought 50,000 shares in IDEX in the Oslo Børs market at the
equivalent of USD 0.1985 per share.  Following this transaction, Mr. Skaggs
holds 250,000 shares in IDEX.

Derek D'Antilio, who is CFO of the IDEX group, has on 26 August 2019 bought
50,000 shares in IDEX, in the Oslo Børs market at NOK 1.86 per share.  Following
this transaction, Mr. D'Antilio holds 100,000 shares and 2,000,000 incentive
subscription rights to shares in IDEX.

About IDEX Biometrics
IDEX Biometrics ASA (OSE: IDEX) is the leading provider of fingerprint
identification technologies offering simple, secure and personal authentication
for all. We help people make payments, prove their identity, gain access to
information, unlock devices or gain admittance to buildings with the touch of a
finger. We invent, engineer and commercialize these secure, yet incredibly user-
friendly solutions. Our total addressable market represents a fast-growing
multi-billion-unit opportunity.
