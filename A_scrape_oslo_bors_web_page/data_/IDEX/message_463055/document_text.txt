On 7 November 2018, Sarah Mathews, Sr. Director of HR in IDEX ASA, was granted
30,100 incentive subscription rights under the company's 2018 incentive
subscription rights plan. The exercise price of the subscription rights is NOK
5.12 per share. 25 per cent of the subscription rights vest per year and the
subscription rights expire on 9 May 2023. Following the grant, Ms Mathews holds
or controls 0 (nil) shares and 221,300 subscription rights in IDEX.

About IDEX Biometrics
IDEX Biometrics, also known as IDEX ASA (OSE: IDEX), is the leading provider of
fingerprint identification technologies offering simple, secure and personal
authentication for all. We help people make payments, prove their identity, gain
access to information, unlock devices or gain admittance to buildings with the
touch of a finger. We invent, engineer and commercialize these secure, yet
incredibly user-friendly solutions. Our total addressable market represents a
fast-growing multi-billion-unit opportunity.
