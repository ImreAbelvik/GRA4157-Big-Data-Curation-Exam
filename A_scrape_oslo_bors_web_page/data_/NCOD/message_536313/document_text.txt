Artha Norcod IV A/S, has purchased 800.000 of shares on the 17th of June 2021 from Isfjord Norway AS in a private placement carried out by Sparebank 1 Markets at a price of 92,50 NOK per share.

After the transactions Artha Norcod IV A/S holds 800.000 shares in Norcod AS. 

Jan Severin Sølbæk, CEO of Artha Norcod IV A/S, is a primary insider of Norcod AS.

Through this transaction primary insider Jan Severin Sølbæk has indirectly acquired 13,675 shares in Norcod, through his related company Sølbæk Holding ApS. Sølbæk Holding ApS now controls 138,935 shares in Norcod AS.
