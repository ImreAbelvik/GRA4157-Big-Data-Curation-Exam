Oslo, 3rd December 2015. As a result of the exercise of the 
over-allotment option by ABG Sundal Collier ASA, Gjelsten 
Holding AS has sold 120,691 shares in Kid ASA at a price of 
NOK 31 per share under the over-allotment option granted to 
ABG Sundal Collier. Gjelsten Holding AS is represented on 
the board of Directors of Kid ASA. 

Following the transaction, Gjelsten Holding AS holds 
19,879,309 shares in Kid ASA, equal to 48.9% of the shares 
in the company, based on a total number of outstanding 
shares of 40,645,162 shares.
