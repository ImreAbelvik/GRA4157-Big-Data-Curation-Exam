Rune Magnus Thomassen, employee-elected director, has sold 12 349 shares at a
price of NOK 61.6175 per share. Following this transaction, Thomassen owns
10 567 shares in Selvaag Bolig ASA.

The shares traded were acquired through the company's share savings programme
for employees and have been sold after the expiry of the lock-up period.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
