Management buys shares in Panoro Energy ASA

Oslo, 28 January 2020 - Panoro Energy ASA (the "Company" or "Panoro" with OSE
Ticker: PEN) announces that the following primary insiders have today purchased
shares in the Company:

John Hamilton, Chief Executive Officer: 5,000 shares at NOK 18.5933 each. After
the transaction, Mr. Hamilton owns 268,962 shares in the Company.

Qazi Qadeer, Chief Financial Officer: 3,000 shares at NOK 18.5933 each. After
the transaction, Mr. Qadeer owns 121,877 shares in the Company.



This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 and section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

About Panoro Energy

Panoro Energy ASA is an independent E&P company based in London and listed on
the Oslo Stock Exchange with ticker PEN. The Company holds high quality
production, exploration and development assets in Africa, namely the Dussafu
License offshore southern Gabon, OML 113 offshore western Nigeria and Sfax
Offshore Exploration Permit and Ras El Besh Concession, offshore Tunisia. For
more information, please visit the Company's website at www.panoroenergy.com.
