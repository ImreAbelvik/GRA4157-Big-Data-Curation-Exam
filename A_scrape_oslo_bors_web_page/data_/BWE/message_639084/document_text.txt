Oslo, 18 February 2025: BW Energy Limited (the "Company") has been informed that
Meridian Finance Limited, a close associate to a primary insider in the Company,
has purchased 85,000 bonds in the Company's bond issue BW Energy Limited
24/29 10,00% USD, with ISIN NO0013259663, in the secondary market. Please see
the attached notification form for further details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article
19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act.
