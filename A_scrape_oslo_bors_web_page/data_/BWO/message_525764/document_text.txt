Mandatory notification of trade

Marco Beenen, CEO of BW Offshore, has settled a Total Return Swap ("TRS")
agreement underlying 50,000 shares in BW Offshore Limited ("BWO") expiring 22
February 2021. Mr Beenen has subsequently entered into a new TRS agreement with
exposure to the same number of underlying shares in BWO with expiry date 25
August 2021 and TRS price NOK 37.47 per underlying share. In addition, Mr.
Beenen owns 46,692 shares in BWO, as previously disclosed.

About BW Offshore:
BW Offshore engineers innovative floating production solutions. The Company has
a fleet of 15 FPSOs with potential and ambition to grow. By leveraging four
decades of offshore operations and project execution, the Company creates
tailored energy solutions for evolving markets world-wide. BW Offshore has
around 2,000 employees and is publicly listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange.
