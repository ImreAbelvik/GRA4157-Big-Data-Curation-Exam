As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2018, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 23 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,494,902 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 19 shares. After the transaction, he holds 1,413
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 19 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 172,825 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 8 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,454 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 26 March 2018. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 731 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 800
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 223,313 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 26 March 2018, which was NOK
420.80 per share, corresponding to DKK 327.80 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
