As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2017, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 29 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,493,827 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 20 shares. After the transaction, he holds 682
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 22 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 171,992 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 10 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,091 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 26 January 2017. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 682 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 763
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 266,568 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 26 January 2017, which was
NOK 320.00 per share, corresponding to DKK 266.75 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
