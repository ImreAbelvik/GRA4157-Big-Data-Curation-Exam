As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2018, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 21 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,495,152 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 17 shares. After the transaction, he holds 1,613
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 17 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 173,028 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 7 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,539 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 25 October 2018. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 627 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 689
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 204,183 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 25 October 2018, which was
NOK 463.40 per share, corresponding to DKK 364.33 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
