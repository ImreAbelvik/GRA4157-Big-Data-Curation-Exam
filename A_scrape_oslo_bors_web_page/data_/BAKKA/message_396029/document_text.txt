As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2016, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 30 shares. After the transaction he holds
4,493,078 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 21 shares. After the transaction he holds 363
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 23 shares. After the transaction he
holds 171,381 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 10 shares. After the transaction
she holds directly and indirectly 14,829 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 25 February 2016. In addition
to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 770 shares to
other employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold
854 shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 298,713 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 25 February 2016, which was
NOK 319.90 per share, corresponding to DKK 250.16 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
