As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2019, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 29 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,495,669 shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 20 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 173,417 shares.

Høgni Dahl Jakobsen, CFO, purchased 18 shares. After the transaction, he holds
18 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 8 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,708 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 29 April 2019. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 808 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 883
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 190,726 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 29 April 2019, which was NOK
441.00 per share, corresponding to DKK 339.79 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
