As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2022, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

  · Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 34 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,617,876 shares.
  · Høgni Dahl Jakobsen, CFO, purchased 24 shares. After the transaction, he
holds directly and indirectly 74,945 shares.
  · Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 25 shares. After the transaction,
he holds 185,336 shares.
  · Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 12 shares. After the
transaction, she holds directly and indirectly 16,393 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost and the purchase was completed on
29 Aug 2022. In addition to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F
Bakkafrost sold 2,109 shares to other employees as part of the share savings
plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 2,201 shares and after the sale, P/F
Bakkafrost holds 48,802 treasury shares. The transaction was based on a share
price of NOK 414.60 per share, corresponding to DKK 294.99 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
