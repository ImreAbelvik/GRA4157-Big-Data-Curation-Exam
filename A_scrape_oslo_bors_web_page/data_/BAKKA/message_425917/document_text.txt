As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2017, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 33 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,493,920 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 24 shares. After the transaction, he holds 749
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 25 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 172,063 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 11 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,123 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 27 April 2017. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 972 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 1,065
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 263,587 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 27 April 2017, which was NOK
287.00 per share, corresponding to DKK 228.65 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
