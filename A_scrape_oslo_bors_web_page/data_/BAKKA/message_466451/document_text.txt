As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2018, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 23 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,495,198 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 19 shares. After the transaction, he holds 1,651
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 19 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 173,066 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 8 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,556 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 20 December 2018. In addition
to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 716 shares to
other employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold
785 shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 202,565 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 20 December 2018, which was
NOK 443.80 per share, corresponding to DKK 334.85 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
