Today 20 May 2025, Bakkafrost has purchased 67,363 Bakkafrost shares to be used
in the Share Savings Plan for employees. The shares were purchased in the market
at a price of NOK 499.6233 per share. After the share purchase and settlement
the Bakkafrost group holds a total of 80,227 shares, whereof Bakkafrost
Processing holds 11,642 shares and Bakkafrost Holding holds 68,585 treasury
shares.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
