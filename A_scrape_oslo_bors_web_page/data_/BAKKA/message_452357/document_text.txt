As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2018, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 22 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,494,946 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 18 shares. After the transaction, he holds 1,450
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 19 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 172,862 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 7 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,469 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 28 May 2018. In addition to the
shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 715 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 781
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 221,753 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 28 May 2018, which was NOK
434.00 per share, corresponding to DKK 339.82 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
