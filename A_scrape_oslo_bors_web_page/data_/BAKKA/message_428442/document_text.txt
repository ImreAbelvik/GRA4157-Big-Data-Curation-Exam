Allocation of Shares

P/F Bakkafrost has as a bonus to the employees for 2016, paid the following
primary insiders shares in P/F Bakkafrost based on the closing price on 29 May
2017:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, was allocated 162 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, was allocated 117 shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, was allocated 123 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member and part of the sales team in Bakkafrost, was
allocated 51 shares.

All full-time employees from 2016, still employed in Bakkafrost, have received
bonus shares with a total value of 2% of paid out salary in 2016. In total,
Bakkafrost has allocated 19,631 shares to its employees, including the shares to
the primary insiders, as a bonus to the employees for 2016.

The transaction was allocated based on the closing share price on 29 May 2017,
which was NOK 303.50 per share, corresponding to DKK 240.07 per share.

Share Savings Plan Transaction

As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2017, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 31 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 23 shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 24 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 11 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 29 May 2017. In addition to the
shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 859 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 948
shares. The transaction was based on the closing share price on 29 May 2017,
which was NOK 303.50 per share, corresponding to DKK 240.07 per share.

After these two transactions, the holding of Bakkafrost shares of the following
primary insiders are:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, holds 4,494,113 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, holds 889 shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, holds 172,210 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member and part of the sales team in Bakkafrost,
holds directly and indirectly 15,185 shares.

In total P/F Bakkafrost allocated 19,631 shares as bonus for employees for 2016
and sold 948 shares as part of the share savings plan. After these two
transactions, P/F Bakkafrost holds 243,008 treasury shares.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
