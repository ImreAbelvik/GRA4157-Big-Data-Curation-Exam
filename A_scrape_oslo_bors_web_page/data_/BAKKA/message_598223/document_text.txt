As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2023, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

  · Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 63 shares. After the transaction, he holds
directly and indirectly 4,640.156 shares.
  · Høgni Dahl Jakobsen, CFO, purchased 44 shares. After the transaction, he
holds directly and indirectly 76,508 shares.
  · Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 44 shares. After the transaction,
he holds 186,625 shares.
  · Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 17 shares. After the
transaction, she holds directly and indirectly 16,748 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost, with a transaction date of 29
August 2023. In addition to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F
Bakkafrost sold 1.693 shares to other employees as part of the share savings
plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 1,861 shares, and after the sale, P/F
Bakkafrost holds 21,522 treasury shares. The transaction was based on the share
price and currency rate at market close on 24 August 2023, which were NOK 522.00
per share and corresponding to DKK 336.17 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
