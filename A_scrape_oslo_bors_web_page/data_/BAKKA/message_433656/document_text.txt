As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2017, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 26 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,494,200 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 19 shares. After the transaction, he holds 951
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 20 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 172,276 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 9 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,215 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 28 August 2017. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 612 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 686
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 240,437 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 28 August 2017, which was
NOK 358.30 per share, corresponding to DKK 287,79 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
