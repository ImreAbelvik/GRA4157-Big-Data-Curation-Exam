As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2017, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 31 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,494,174 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 22 shares. After the transaction, he holds 932
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 23 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 172,256 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 11 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,206 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 24 July 2017. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 862 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 949
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 241,123 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 24 July 2017, which was NOK
304.70 per share, corresponding to DKK 242.27 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
