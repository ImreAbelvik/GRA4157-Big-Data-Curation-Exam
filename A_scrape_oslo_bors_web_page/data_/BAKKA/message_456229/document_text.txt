As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2018, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 20 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,495,092 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 17 shares. After the transaction, he holds 1,563
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 17 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 172,978 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 7 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,518 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 26 July 2018. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 583 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 644
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 206,122 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 26 July 2018, which was NOK
485.60 per share, corresponding to DKK 379.11 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
