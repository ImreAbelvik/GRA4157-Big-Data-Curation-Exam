As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2023, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

  · Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 30 shares. After the transaction, he holds
directly and indirectly 4,640,186 shares.
  · Høgni Dahl Jakobsen, CFO, purchased 20 shares. After the transaction, he
holds directly and indirectly 76,537 shares.
  · Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 21 shares. After the transaction,
he holds 186,646 shares.
  · Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 7 shares. After the
transaction, she holds directly and indirectly 16,755 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost, with a transaction date of 3
October 2023. In addition to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F
Bakkafrost sold 2,016 shares to other employees as part of the share savings
plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 2,094 shares, and after the sale, P/F
Bakkafrost holds 19,428 treasury shares. The transaction was based on the share
price and currency rate at market close on 28 September 2023, which were NOK
534.00 per share and corresponding to DKK 351.85 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor/share-information/share-savings
-plan (http://www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share
-savings-plan)
