As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2023, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

  · Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 77 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,619.593 shares.
  · Høgni Dahl Jakobsen, CFO, purchased 54 shares. After the transaction, he
holds directly and indirectly 76,445 shares.
  · Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 53 shares. After the transaction,
he holds 186,581 shares.
  · Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 21 shares. After the
transaction, she holds directly and indirectly 16,731 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost, and the purchase was completed on
29 June 2023. In addition to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F
Bakkafrost sold 1.708 shares to other employees as part of the share savings
plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 1.913 shares, and after the sale, P/F
Bakkafrost holds 24.841 treasury shares. The transaction was based on a share
price of NOK 645.00 per share, corresponding to DKK 408.35 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
