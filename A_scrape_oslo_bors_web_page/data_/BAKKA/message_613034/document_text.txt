As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2024, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

  · Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 48 shares. After the transaction, he holds
directly and indirectly 4,640,572 shares.
  · Høgni Dahl Jakobsen, CFO, purchased 34 shares. After the transaction, he
holds directly and indirectly 76,899 shares.
  · Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 35 shares. After the transaction,
he holds 186,941 shares.
  · Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 13 shares. After the
transaction, she holds directly and indirectly 16,861 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost, with a transaction date of 5
March 2024. In addition to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F
Bakkafrost sold 1,340 shares to other employees as part of the share savings
plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 1,470 shares, and after the sale, P/F
Bakkafrost holds 1,222 treasury shares. The transaction was based on the share
price and currency rate at market close on 26 February 2024, which were NOK
658.00.00 per share and corresponding to DKK 439.22 per share.
