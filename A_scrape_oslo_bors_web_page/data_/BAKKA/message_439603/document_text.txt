As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2017, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 32 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,494,283 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 26 shares. After the transaction, he holds 1,017
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 26 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 172,344 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 11 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,244 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 27 November 2017. In addition
to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 846 shares to
other employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold
941 shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 237,848 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 27 November 2017, which was
NOK 312.30 per share, corresponding to DKK 238.32 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
