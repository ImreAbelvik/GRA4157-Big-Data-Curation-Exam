As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2016, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 31 shares. After the transaction he holds
4,493,411 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 23 shares. After the transaction he holds 585
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 23 shares. After the transaction he
holds 171,635 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 11 shares. After the transaction
she holds directly and indirectly 14,938 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 25 August 2016. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 660 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 748
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 277,465 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 25 August 2016, which was
NOK 302.30 per share, corresponding to DKK 242.57 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
