As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2022, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

  · Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 22 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,617,731 shares.
  · Høgni Dahl Jakobsen, CFO, purchased 15 shares. After the transaction, he
holds directly and indirectly 74,798 shares.
  · Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 16 shares. After the transaction,
he holds 185,231 shares.
  · Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 6 shares. After the
transaction, she holds directly and indirectly 16,354 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 28 March 2022. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 1,101 shares to
other employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold
1,160 shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 56,839 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on a share price of NOK 596.60 per share, corresponding to
DKK 466.60 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
