As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2020, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

  · Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 25 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,616,602 shares.
  · Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 20 shares. After the transaction,
he holds 184,356 shares.
  · Høgni Dahl Jakobsen, CFO, purchased 16 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 298 shares.
  · Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 7 shares. After the
transaction, she holds directly and indirectly 16,019 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 25 June 2020. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 1,088 shares to
other employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold
1,156 shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 30,492 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 25 June 2020, which was NOK
581.00 per share, corresponding to DKK 398,22 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
