Today 2 April 2024, Bakkafrost Processing, a 100% owned subsidiary of
Bakkafrost, has purchased 50,000 Bakkafrost shares to be used in the Share
Savings Plan for employees. The shares were purchased in the market at a price
of NOK 684.47712 per share. After the share purchase and settlement the
Bakkafrost group holds a total of 51,222 shares, whereof Bakkfrost Processing
holds 50,000 shares and Bakkafrost Holding holds 1,222 treasury shares.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
