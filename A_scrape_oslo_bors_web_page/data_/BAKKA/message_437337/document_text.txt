As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2017, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 25 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,494,251 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 22 shares. After the transaction, he holds 991
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 22 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 172,318 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 9 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,233 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 26 October 2017. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 720 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 798
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 238,789 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 26 October 2017, which was
NOK 367.90 per share, corresponding to DKK 288.65 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
