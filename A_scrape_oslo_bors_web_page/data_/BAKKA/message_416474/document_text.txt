As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2016, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 26 shares. After the transaction he holds
4,493,517 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 19 shares. After the transaction he holds 662
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 20 shares. After the transaction he
holds 171,717 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 9 shares. After the transaction
she holds directly and indirectly 14,974 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 19 December 2016. In addition
to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 600 shares to
other employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold
674 shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 274,575 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 19 December 2016, which was
NOK 347.50 per share, corresponding to DKK 285.51 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
