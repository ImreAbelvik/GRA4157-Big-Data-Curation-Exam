As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2018, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 22 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,494,924 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 19 shares. After the transaction, he holds 1,432
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 18 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 172,843 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 8 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,462 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 25 April 2018. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 712 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 779
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 222,534 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 25 April 2018, which was NOK
444.00 per share, corresponding to DKK 340.77 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
