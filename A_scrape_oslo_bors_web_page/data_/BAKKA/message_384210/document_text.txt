As a part of the share saving plan for employees in 2015, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 80 shares. After the transaction he holds
4,492,863 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 47 shares. After the transaction he holds 226
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 60 shares. After the transaction he
holds 171,216 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 26 shares. After the transaction
she holds directly and indirectly 14,757 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 25 August 2015. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 1,266 to other
employees as part of the share saving plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 1,479
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 304,045 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 25 August 2015, which was
NOK 237.50 per share, corresponding to DKK 188.81 per share.
