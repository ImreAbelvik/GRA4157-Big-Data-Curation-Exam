As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2021, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

  · Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 75 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,617,227 shares.
  · Høgni Dahl Jakobsen, CFO, purchased 48 shares. After the transaction, he
holds directly and indirectly 74,486 shares.
  · Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 60 shares. After the transaction,
he holds 184,840 shares.
  · Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 21 shares. After the
transaction, she holds directly and indirectly 16,216 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 24 February 2021. In addition
to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 898 shares to
other employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold
1,106 shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 74,272 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on a share price of NOK 722.00 per share, corresponding to
DKK 527.71 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
