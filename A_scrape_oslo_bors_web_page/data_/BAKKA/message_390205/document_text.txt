As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2015, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 42 shares. After the transaction he holds
4,492,976 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 25 shares. After the transaction he holds 294
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 32 shares. After the transaction he
holds 171,303 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 14 shares. After the transaction
she holds directly and indirectly 14,795 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 25 November 2015. In addition
to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 923 shares to
other employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold
1,036 shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 301,316 treasury shares.
The transaction was based on the closing share price on 25 November 2015, which
was NOK 224.00 per share, corresponding to DKK 182.02 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
