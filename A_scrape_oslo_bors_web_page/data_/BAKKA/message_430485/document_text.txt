As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2017, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 30 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,494,143 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 21 shares. After the transaction, he holds 910
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 23 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 172,233 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 10 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,195 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 26 June 2017. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 852 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 936
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 242,072 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 26 June 2017, which was NOK
326.00 per share, corresponding to DKK 255.75 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
