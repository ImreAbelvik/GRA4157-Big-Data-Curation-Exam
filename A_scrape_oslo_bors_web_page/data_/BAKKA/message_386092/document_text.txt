As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2015, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 37 shares. After the transaction he holds
4,492,900 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 23 shares. After the transaction he holds 249
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 29 shares. After the transaction he
holds 171,245 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 13 shares. After the transaction
she holds directly and indirectly 14,770 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 24 September 2015. In addition
to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 781 to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 883
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 303,162 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 24 September 2015, which was
NOK 256.00 per share, corresponding to DKK 201.86 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
