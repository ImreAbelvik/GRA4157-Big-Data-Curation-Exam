As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2018, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 23 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,495,175 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 19 shares. After the transaction, he holds 1,632
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 19 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 173,047 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 9 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,548 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 26 November 2018. In addition
to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 763 shares to
other employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold
833 shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 203,350 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 26 November 2018, which was
NOK 423.60 per share, corresponding to DKK 324.86 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
