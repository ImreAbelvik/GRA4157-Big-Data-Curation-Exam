As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2016, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 33 shares. After the transaction he holds
4,493,140 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 24 shares. After the transaction he holds 408
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 25 shares. After the transaction he
holds 171,429 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 12 shares. After the transaction
she holds directly and indirectly 14,851 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 26 April 2016. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 783 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 877
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 297,092 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 26 April 2016, which was NOK
285.00 per share, corresponding to DKK 229.85 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
