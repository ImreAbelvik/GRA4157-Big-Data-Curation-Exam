As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2017, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 31 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,494,314 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 26 shares. After the transaction, he holds 1,043
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 25 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 172,369 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 11 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,255 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 21 December 2017. In addition
to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 830 shares to
other employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold
923 shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 236,925 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 21 December 2017, which was
NOK 329.90 per share, corresponding to DKK 246.24 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
