As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2019, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

  · Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 21 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,615,963 shares.
  · Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 17 shares. After the transaction,
he holds 183,870 shares.
  · Høgni Dahl Jakobsen, CFO, purchased 13 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 140 shares.
  · Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 6 shares. After the
transaction, she holds directly and indirectly 15,810 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 19 December 2019. In addition
to the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 530 shares to
other employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost
sold 587 shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 892 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 19 December 2019, which was
NOK 636.00 per share, corresponding to DKK 476.24 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
