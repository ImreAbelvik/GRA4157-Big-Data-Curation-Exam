As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2016, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 34 shares. After the transaction he holds
4,493,048 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 25 shares. After the transaction he holds 342
shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 26 shares. After the transaction he
holds 171,358 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 11 shares. After the transaction
she holds directly and indirectly 14,819 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 25 January 2016. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 781 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 877
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 299,567 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 25 January 2016, which was
NOK 279.00 per share, corresponding to DKK 220.13 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
