Allocation of Shares

P/F Bakkafrost has as a bonus to the employees for 2014, paid the following
primary insiders shares in P/F Bakkafrost based on the closing price on 12th May
2015:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, was allocated 202 shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, was allocated 177 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, was allocated 11 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member and part of the sales team in Bakkafrost, was
allocated 70 shares.

All full-time employees from 2014, still employed in Bakkafrost, have received
bonus shares with a total value of 2% of paid out salary in 2014. In total
Bakkafrost has allocated 22,107 shares to its employees including the shares to
the primary insiders as a bonus to the employees for 2014.

The transaction was allocated based on the closing share price on 12th May 2015,
which was NOK 187.50 per share, corresponding to DKK 166.69 per share.

Share Saving Plan Transaction

As a part of the share saving plan for employees in 2015, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 90 shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 69 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, purchased 54 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member and part of the sales team in Bakkafrost,
purchased 30 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost. In addition to the shares sold to
the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 2,137 shares to other employees as
part of the share saving plan. The transaction was based on the closing share
price on 12th May 2015, which was NOK 187.50 per share, corresponding to DKK
166.69 per share.

After these two transaction, the holding of Bakkafrost shares of the following
primary insiders are:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, holds 4,492,701 shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, holds 171,092 shares.

Gunnar Nielsen, CFO, holds 129 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member and part of the sales team in Bakkafrost,
holds 14,703 shares.

In total P/F Bakkafrost allocated 22,107 shares as bonus for employees for 2014
and sold 2,380 shares as part of the share saving plan. After these two
transactions, P/F Bakkafrost holds 307,813 treasury shares.
