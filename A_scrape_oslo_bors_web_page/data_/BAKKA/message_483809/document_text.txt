As a part of the share savings plan for employees in 2019, the following primary
insiders have purchased Bakkafrost shares:

Regin Jacobsen, CEO, purchased 25 shares. After the transaction, he holds
4,495,871 shares.

Odd Eliasen, Managing Director, purchased 16 shares. After the transaction, he
holds 173,575 shares.

Høgni Dahl Jakobsen, CFO, purchased 15 shares. After the transaction, he holds
82 shares.

Annika Frederiksberg, Board Member, purchased 7 shares. After the transaction,
she holds directly and indirectly 15,785 shares.

The shares were purchased from P/F Bakkafrost on 26 August 2019. In addition to
the shares sold to the primary insiders, P/F Bakkafrost sold 507 shares to other
employees as part of the share savings plan. In total P/F Bakkafrost sold 570
shares and after the sale P/F Bakkafrost holds 173,464 treasury shares. The
transaction was based on the closing share price on 26 August 2019, which was
NOK 539.50 per share, corresponding to DKK 403.87 per share.

For further information about Bakkafrost's share savings plan, see
www.bakkafrost.com/en/investor-relations/share-information/share-savings-plan
