About SpareBank 1 Østlandet: 
SpareBank 1 Østlandet is the fourth largest Norwegian savings bank, measured by total adjusted assets. The Bank has approximately 334,000 customers spread mainly across its four counties of operation: Hedmark, Oppland, Akershus and Oslo. The Bank's head office is in Hamar (Hedmark county). The Bank offers a wide range of products within loans, deposits, insurance, pensions, payment services, estate agency, accounting services, leasing and financing.
