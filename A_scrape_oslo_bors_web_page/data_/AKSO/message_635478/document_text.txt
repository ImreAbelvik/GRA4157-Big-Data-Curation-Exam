December 20, 2024 - Aker Solutions ASA ("Aker Solutions") has today allocated
shares to primary insiders participating in the company's employee share
purchase program for 2024.

Reference is made to the stock exchange release published earlier today,
December 20, 2024, concerning the employee share purchase program and share
allocation. For the December 2024 allocation, shares were sold at a price per
share, excluding applicable discount, of NOK 30.4451 which equals the volume
-weighted average share price of Aker Solutions on Euronext Oslo Børs from and
including December 13, 2024, to and including December 19, 2024.

Under the December 2024 allocation, a total of 492 shares were allocated to
Sturla Magnus, Executive Vice President, Newbuild. Following the allocation,
Magnus holds 224 579 shares in Aker Solutions.

Under the December 2024 allocation, a total of 492 shares were allocated to
Hilde Karlsen, Employee Elected Director. Following the allocation, Karlsen,
together with related parties, hold 29 849 shares in Aker Solutions.

Under the December 2024 allocation, a total of 492 shares were allocated to Rolf
Arne Grønning, Deputy Employee Elected Director. Following the allocation,
Grønning holds 30 027 shares in Aker Solutions.

Please see the attached notification for persons discharging managerial
responsibilities in Aker Solutions in accordance with Regulation EU 596/2014
(MAR) article 19.
