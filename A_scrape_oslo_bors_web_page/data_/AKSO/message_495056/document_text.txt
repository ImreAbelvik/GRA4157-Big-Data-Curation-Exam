February 11, 2020 - Atle Teigland, board member of Aker Solutions ASA, today
purchased 7,000 shares in Aker Solutions at a price of NOK 13.95 per share.

The shares were acquired on the Oslo Stock Exchange. Following the transaction,
Teigland holds a total of 15,751 shares in the company.
