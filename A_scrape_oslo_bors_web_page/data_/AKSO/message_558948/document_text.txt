April 8, 2022 - Aker Holding AS ("Aker"), a subsidiary of Aker ASA, has
purchased 29,860,405 shares in Aker Solutions ASA, at a price of NOK 27.10 per
share.

Following the transaction, Aker holds 193,950,894 (39,41%) shares in Aker
Solutions ASA.

Please see the attached notification in accordance with Regulation EU 596/2014
(MAR) article 19 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
