June 20, 2022 - Aker Solutions ASA ("Aker Solutions") has today allocated shares
to a primary insider participating in the company's employee share purchase
program for 2022.

Reference is made to the stock exchange release published earlier today, June
20, 2022, concerning the employee share purchase program and share allocation.
For the June 2022 allocation, shares were sold at a price per share, excluding
applicable discount, of NOK 30.9883 which equals the volume-weighted average
price of the Aker Solutions share on Euronext Oslo Børs over the period from and
including June 13, 2022 to and including June 17, 2022.

Under the June 2022 allocation, a total of 484 shares were allocated to Sturla
Magnus, Executive Vice President, Topside and Facilities. Following the
allocation, Magnus holds 175,899 shares in Aker Solutions.

Please see the attached notification for person discharging managerial
responsibilities in Aker Solutions in accordance with Regulation EU 596/2014
(MAR) article 19.
