January 29, 2025 - Aker Solutions ASA ("Aker Solutions") has today allocated
matching shares to primary insiders participating in the company's employee
share purchase program and manager share purchase program for 2021.

Reference is made to the stock exchange release published earlier today, January
29, 2025, concerning the employee and manager share purchase programs and
matching share allocations. For the matching share allocations, shares were sold
at a price per share of NOK 31.9172 which equals the volume-weighted average
share price of Aker Solutions on Euronext Oslo Børs from and including January
22, 2025, to and including January 28, 2025.

Under the matching shares allocation, a total of 18,781 shares were allocated to
Sturla Magnus, Executive Vice President, Newbuild. Following the allocation,
Magnus holds 243,360 shares in Aker Solutions.

Under the matching share allocation, a total of 19,714 shares were allocated to
Idar Eikrem, Chief Financial Officer. Following the allocation, Eikrem, together
with related parties, hold 375,315 shares in Aker Solutions.

Under the matching share allocation, a total of 40,282 shares were allocated to
Kjetel Digre, Chief Executive Officer. Following the allocation, Digre holds
206,809 shares in Aker Solutions.

Under the matching share allocation, a total of 12,442 shares were allocated to
Henrik Inadomi, Executive Vice President, New Energies. Following the
allocation, Inadomi holds 354,739 shares in Aker Solutions.

Under the matching share allocation, a total of 16,193 shares were allocated to
Kjetil Kristiansen, Executive Vice President, People and Transformation.
Following the allocation, Kristiansen holds 49,393 shares in Aker Solutions.

Under the matching share allocation, a total of 2,169 shares were allocated to
Hilde Karlsen, employee elected director. Following the allocation, Karlsen,
together with related parties, hold 32,018 shares in Aker Solutions.

Under the matching share allocation, a total of 3,254 shares were allocated to
Olav Høidalen, Senior Vice President, Group Controlling. Following the
allocation, Høidalen holds 32,275 shares in Aker Solutions.

Please see the attached notification for persons discharging managerial
responsibilities in Aker Solutions in accordance with Regulation EU 596/2014
(MAR) article 19.
