June 28, 2021 - Aker Solutions ASA ("Aker Solutions") has today sold shares to
primary insiders participating in the company's employee share purchase program
for 2021.

Reference is made to the stock exchange
releases (https://www.akersolutions.com/news/news-archive/2021/aker-solutions
-asa-buy-back-of-own-shares-for-employee-share-purchase-program/) of May 27,
2021 and June 28, 2021, concerning the employee share purchase program and share
allocation. Shares were sold at a price per share, excluding any applicable
discount, of NOK 16.0742. This equals the volume-weighted average price of the
Aker Solutions share on Euronext Oslo Børs over the period from and including
June 21, 2021 to and including June 25, 2021.

Shares were sold to primary insiders of Aker Solutions, as follows:

  · Kjetel Digre, Chief Executive Officer, has purchased 78,386 shares.
Following the transaction, Digre holds 78,386 shares in Aker Solutions
  · Idar Eikrem, Chief Financial Officer, has purchased 31,727 shares. Following
the transaction, Eikrem and his private investment company, Omara Holding AS,
hold a total of 232,294 shares in Aker Solutions
  · Sturla Magnus, Executive Vice President, Topside & Facilities, has purchased
31,105 shares. Following the transaction, Magnus holds 111,604 shares in Aker
Solutions
  · Kenneth Simonsen, acting Executive Vice President, Renewables, has purchased
10,575 shares. Following the transaction, Simonsen holds 88,215 shares in Aker
Solutions
  · Marte Mogstad, Executive Vice President, Engineering, has purchased 4,976
shares. Following the transaction, Mogstad holds 5,373 shares in Aker Solutions
  · Linda Litlekalsøy Aase, Executive Vice President, Electrification,
Maintenance and Modifications, has purchased 1,244 shares. Following the
transaction, Aase holds 39,795 shares in Aker Solutions
  · Kjetil Kristiansen, Executive Vice President, People and Organization, has
purchased 26,128 shares. Following the transaction, Kristiansen holds 26,128
shares in Aker Solutions
  · Marianne Hagen, Executive Vice President, Sustainability and Communications,
has purchased 18,663 shares. Following the transaction, Hagen holds 18,663
shares in Aker Solutions
  · Henrik Inadomi, General Counsel, has purchased 12,442 shares. Following the
transaction, Inadomi holds 270,366 shares in Aker Solutions
  · Olav Høidalen, Senior Vice President, Group Controlling, has purchased 1,244
shares. Following the transaction, Høidalen holds 8,057 shares in Aker Solutions
  · Hilde Karlsen, employee-elected director of the board, has purchased 829
shares. Following the transaction, Karlsen together with closely associated

  · Line Småge Breidablikk, employee-elected director of the board, has

  · Rune Rafdal, employee-elected director of the board, has purchased 622

  · Sigurd Maraas, deputy employee-elected director of the board, has purchased
