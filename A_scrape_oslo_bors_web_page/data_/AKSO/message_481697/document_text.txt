July 19, 2019 - Hilde Karlsen, board member of Aker Solutions ASA, today
purchased 2,800 shares in Aker Solutions at a price of NOK 28.45 per share.
The shares were acquired on the Oslo Stock Exchange. Following the transaction,
Karlsen and her husband hold a total of 8,163 shares in the company.
