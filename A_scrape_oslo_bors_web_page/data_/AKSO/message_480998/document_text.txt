July 5, 2019 - Luis Araujo, chief executive officer of Aker Solutions, today
purchased 25,000 shares in Aker Solutions ASA at a price of NOK 33.83 per share.
The shares were acquired on the Oslo Stock Exchange. Following the transaction,
Araujo holds a total of 101,898 shares in the company.
