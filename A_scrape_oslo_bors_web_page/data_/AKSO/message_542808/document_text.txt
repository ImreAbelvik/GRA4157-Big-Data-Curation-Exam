September 20, 2021 - Aker Solutions ASA ("Aker Solutions") has today sold shares
to primary insiders participating in the company's employee share purchase
program for 2021.

Reference is made to the stock exchange releases of June 28, 2021 and September
20, 2021 concerning the employee share purchase program and share allocation.
For the September 2021 allocation, shares were sold at a price per share
(excluding any applicable discount) of NOK 17.6103 which equals the volume
-weighted average price of the Aker Solutions share on Euronext Oslo Børs over
the period from and including September 13, 2021 to and including September 17,
2021.

Shares were sold to primary insiders of Aker Solutions, as follows:

  · Kjetel Digre, Chief Executive Officer, has purchased 1,135 shares. Following
the transaction, Mr. Digre holds 79,521 shares in Aker Solutions
  · Idar Eikrem, Chief Financial Officer, has purchased 1,135 shares. Following
the transaction, Mr. Eikrem and his private investment company, Omara Holding
AS, hold a total of 233,429 shares in Aker Solutions
  · Sturla Magnus, Executive Vice President, Topside and Facilities, has
purchased 1,135 shares. Following the transaction, Mr. Magnus holds 112,739
shares in Aker Solutions
  · Marte Mogstad, Executive Vice President, Engineering, has purchased 1,135
shares. Following the transaction, Mrs. Mogstad holds 6,508 shares in Aker
Solutions
  · Kjetil Kristiansen, Executive Vice President, People and Organization, has
purchased 1,135 shares. Following the transaction, Mr. Kristiansen holds 27,263
shares in Aker Solutions
  · Marianne Hagen, Executive Vice President, Sustainability, HSSE and
Communications, has purchased 1,135 shares. Following the transaction, Mrs.
Hagen holds 19,798 shares in Aker Solutions

Please see the attached notifications for persons discharging managerial
responsibilities in Aker Solutions in accordance with Regulation EU 596/2014
(MAR) article 19.
