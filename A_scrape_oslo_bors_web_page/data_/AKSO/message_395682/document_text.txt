


February 22, 2016 - Aker Solutions ASA today bought 70,000 own shares at an
average price of NOK 24.0644 per share.

The shares were acquired on the Oslo stock exchange as part of the company's
share purchase program for employees. This gives Aker Solutions a new holding of
447,311 own shares.
