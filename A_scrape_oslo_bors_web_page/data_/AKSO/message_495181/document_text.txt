February 12, 2020 - The following primary insiders of Aker Solutions ASA today
purchased shares in Aker Solutions.

Hilde Karlsen, board member of Aker Solutions ASA, purchased 7,000 shares in
Aker Solutions at a price of NOK 14.53 per share. Following the transaction,
Karlsen and her husband hold a total of 17,925 shares in the company.

Birgit Aagaard-Svendsen, board member of Aker Solutions ASA, purchased 25,000
shares in Aker Solutions at a price of NOK 14.45 per share. Following the
transaction, Aagaard-Svendsen holds a total of 50,000 shares in the company.

The shares were acquired on the Oslo Stock Exchange.
