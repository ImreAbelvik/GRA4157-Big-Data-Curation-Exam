June 6, 2023-Nina Amble, a person closely associated to Jan Arve Haugan, member
of the Board of Directors of Aker Solutions ASA, today sold shares in Aker
Solutions ASA on the Oslo Stock Exchange.

Amble sold 22 912 shares at a price of NOK 36.56 per share. Following the
transaction, Amble does not hold any shares in Aker Solutions.

Please see the attached notification for more details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to
Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities
Act section 5-12.
