May 31, 2022 - Elisabeth H. Tørstad, board member of Aker Solutions ASA, on May
30, 2022, purchased 1,000 shares in Aker Solutions ASA at a price of NOK 36.419
per share.

The shares were acquired on the Oslo Stock Exchange. Following the transaction,
Tørstad holds a total of 2,000 shares of the company.

Please see the attached notification for more details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to
Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities
Act section 5-12.
