December 2, 2024 - Aker Solutions ASA ("Aker Solutions") has today allocated
shares to primary insiders participating in the company's manager share purchase
program for 2024.

Reference is made to the stock exchange release published earlier today,
December 2, 2024, concerning the manager share purchase program and share
allocation. Aker Solutions sold shares to program participants at a price per
share of NOK 30.422, which equals the volume-weighted average share price of
Aker Solutions on Euronext Oslo Børs from and including November 25, 2024, to
and including November 29, 2024. The program was launched by Aker Solutions on
November 7, 2024. The subscription period started on the same day and ended on
November 22, 2024.

Kjetel Digre, Chief Executive Officer, acquired 32,870 shares. Following the
allocation, Digre holds 166,527 shares in Aker Solutions.

Idar Eikrem, Chief Financial Officer, acquired 16,435 shares through his
privately owned company, Omara Holding AS. Following the allocation, Eikrem,
together with his privately owned company, Omara Holding AS, hold 355,601 shares
in Aker Solutions.

Jo Kjetil Krabbe, Executive Vice President, Power Solutions, acquired 16,435
shares through his privately owned company, One Management AS. Following the
allocation, Krabbe, together with his privately owned company One Management AS,
hold 26,130 shares in Aker Solutions.

Olav Høidalen, Senior Vice President, Group Controlling, acquired 6,574 shares.
Following the allocation, Høidalen holds 29,021 shares in Aker Solutions.

Paal Eikeseth, Executive Vice President, Life Cycle, acquired 6,574 shares.
Following the allocation, Eikeseth, together with related parties, hold 7,866
shares in Aker Solutions.

Kjetil Kristiansen, Executive Vice President, People and Transformation,
acquired 3,287 shares. Following the allocation, Kristiansen holds 33,200 shares
in Aker Solutions.

Henrik Makoto Inadomi, Executive Vice President, New Energies, acquired 11,504
shares. Following the allocation, Inadomi holds 342,297 shares in Aker
Solutions.

Sturla Magnus, Executive Vice President, New Build, acquired 16,435 shares.
Following the allocation, Magnus, holds 224,087 shares in Aker Solutions.

Please see the attached notification for persons discharging managerial
responsibilities in Aker Solutions in accordance with Regulation EU 596/2014
(MAR) article 19.
