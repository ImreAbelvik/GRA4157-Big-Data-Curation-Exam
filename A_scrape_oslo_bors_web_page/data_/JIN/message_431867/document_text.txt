Shipping and Transportation Limited (the 'Company') in the Oslo Stock 
Exchange at an average price of NOK8.1645 per share. 

Mr. Siu Fai NG has also been allocated new shares regarding the rights 
issue (the 'Rights Issue') as announced on 3 July 2017 and the 
allocation of new shares to primary insiders was announced on 25 July 
2017.

following interests in the Company: 
Shareholding as at 27 July 2017 (prior to the above purchase 
transaction): 2,170,561
Shareholding as at 27 July 2017 (following the above purchase 
transaction): 2,244,131
Allocation of new shares on the basis of exercised subscription rights 
and as a result of oversubscription: 2,229,122
Total shareholding (if all allocated new shares in the Rights Issue are 
issued and registered): 4,473,253
