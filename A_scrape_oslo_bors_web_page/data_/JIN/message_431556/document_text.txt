On 18 July 2017, Mr. Siu Fai NG, Chairman of Jinhui Shipping and 
Transportation Limited (the `Company`), has purchased 205,950 
Subscription Rights of the Company in the Oslo Stock Exchange at an 
average price of NOK0.1699 per Subscription Right. 

Prior to the above transaction, Mr. Siu Fai NG and his spouse have the 
following interests in the Company:
Shareholding as at 18 July 2017 (prior to the transaction): 2,148,215
Subscription Rights as at 18 July 2017 (prior to the transaction): 
1,368,772
Total shareholding (if all Subscription Rights are exercised by 
relevant parties): 3,516,987

Following the above transaction, Mr. Siu Fai NG and his spouse have the 
following interests in the Company:
Shareholding as at 18 July 2017 (following the transaction): 2,148,215
Subscription Rights as at 18 July 2017 (following the transaction): 
1,574,722
Total shareholding (if all Subscription Rights are exercised by 
relevant parties): 3,722,937

Details of the Subscription Rights are stated in the release by the 
Company published on 3 July 2017 regarding a rights issue (the "Rights 
Issue"). An updated overview of the total holding of shares will be 
published following the issuance and allocation of the new offer shares.

Mr. Siu Fai NG also has certain indirect interests in the Company 
through his interests in Jinhui Holdings Company Limited and Fairline 
Consultants Limited. Jinhui Holdings Company Limited is the controlling 
shareholder of the Company, which has direct interests in 46,034,800 
shares of the Company, representing approximately 54.77% interests in 
the Company as at date of this notification. Mr. Siu Fai NG is also a 
director of Jinhui Holdings Company Limited. Fairline Consultants 
Limited is the legal and beneficial owner of 64.53% interests of Jinhui 
Holdings Company Limited and has direct interests in 500,000 shares of 
the Company, representing approximately 0.59% interests of the Company 
as at date of this notification.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to 
section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
