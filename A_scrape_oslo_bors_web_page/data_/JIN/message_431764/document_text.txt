Reference is made to the announcement by Jinhui Shipping and 
Transportation Limited (the "Company") made on 3 July 2017 regarding a 
rights issue (the "Rights Issue") raising up to NOK 201,708,816 in 
gross proceeds through the issuance of up to 25,213,602 new shares 
(the "Offer Shares") at a subscription price of NOK 8.00 per Offer 
Share, and the listing of the Offer Shares on the Oslo Stock Exchange. 

On 25 July 2017, the Board of Directors of Jinhui Shipping and 
Transportation Limited has approved the final allocation of 25,213,602 
new shares subscribed for in the Rights Issue based on the allocation 
criteria set out in the Prospectus. 

Below sets out details of allocation in respect of primary insiders:

Mr. NG Siu Fai, director of the Company, and his spouse:
Shareholding as at 25 July 2017: 2,148,215
Allocation of new shares on the basis of exercised subscription rights 
and as a result of oversubscription: 2,229,122
Total shareholding (if all allocated new shares in the Rights Issue are 
issued and registered): 4,377,337

Mr. NG Kam Wah Thomas, director of the Company:
Shareholding as at 25 July 2017: 50,000
Allocation of new shares on the basis of exercised subscription rights 
and as a result of oversubscription: 814,900
Total shareholding (if all allocated new shares in the Rights Issue are 
issued and registered): 864,900

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to 
section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
