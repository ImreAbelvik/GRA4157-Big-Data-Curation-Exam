
MANDATORY NOTIFICATION OF TRADE BY PRIMARY INSIDERS

On 14 July 2017, Mr. Siu Fai NG, Chairman of Jinhui Shipping 
and Transportation Limited (the `Company`), has purchased 
147,412 shares of the Company in the Oslo Stock Exchange at 
an average price of NOK8.2137 per share. He has also 
purchased 195,193 Subscription Rights of the Company in the 
Oslo Stock Exchange at an average price of NOK0.1777 per 
Subscription Right. 

Prior to the above transactions, Mr. Siu Fai NG and his 
spouse have the following interests in the Company:
Shareholding as at 14 July 2017 (prior to the transactions): 
1,922,800
Subscription Rights granted at Record Date: 576,840
Total shareholding (if all Subscription Rights are exercised 
by relevant parties): 2,499,640

Following the above transactions, Mr. Siu Fai NG and his 
spouse have the following interests in the Company.
Shareholding as at 14 July 2017 (following the 
transactions): 2,070,212
Subscription Rights: 772,033
Total shareholding (if all Subscription Rights are exercised 
by relevant parties): 2,842,245

Details of the Subscription Rights are stated in the release 
by the Company published on 3 July 2017 regarding a rights 
issue (the "Rights Issue"). An updated overview of the total 
holding of shares will be published following the issuance 
and allocation of the new offer shares.

Mr. Siu Fai NG also has certain indirect interests in the 
Company through his interests in Jinhui Holdings Company 
Limited and Fairline Consultants Limited. Jinhui Holdings 
Company Limited is the controlling shareholder of the 
Company, which has direct interests in 46,034,800 shares of 
the Company, representing approximately 54.77% interests in 
the Company as at date of this notification. Mr. Siu Fai NG 
is also a director of Jinhui Holdings Company Limited. 
Fairline Consultants Limited is the legal and beneficial 
owner of 64.53% interests of Jinhui Holdings Company Limited 
and has direct interests in 500,000 shares of the Company, 
representing approximately 0.59% interests of the Company as 
at date of this notification.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading 
Act.
