On 4 September 2017, Mr. Siu Fai NG, director of Jinhui Shipping and 
Transportation Limited (the 'Company'), has purchased 68,858 shares of 
the Company in the Oslo Stock Exchange at an average price of NOK9.1233 
per share. Following to the above purchase, Mr. Siu Fai NG and his 
spouse have direct interests in 4,542,111 shares of the Company.

Mr. Siu Fai NG also has certain indirect interests in the Company 
through his interests in Jinhui Holdings Company Limited and Fairline 
Consultants Limited.

Jinhui Holdings Company Limited is the controlling shareholder of the 
Company, which has direct interests in 60,841,240 shares of the 
Company, representing approximately 55.69% interests of the Company.

Fairline Consultants Limited is the legal and beneficial owner of 
64.53% interests of Jinhui Holdings Company Limited and has direct 
interests in 650,000 shares of the Company, representing approximately 
0.59% interests of the Company.

Mr. Siu Fai NG is the director of the Company and Jinhui Holdings 
Company Limited.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to 
section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
