Oslo, Norway 1 July 2024: Alchemia Capital AS, the sole owner of Scatec Innovation AS, has amended its credit facility agreement with Skandinaviska Enskilda Banken AB (publ). As a consequence, the shares in Norsk Titanium AS owned by Scatec Innovation AS are now included in the security package for the said credit facility.

Scatec Innovation AS is a company closely associated with John Andersen, who is the chairman of the Company's board of directors. Please see the attached notification of trade for information regarding the pledging of shares in the Company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article 19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
