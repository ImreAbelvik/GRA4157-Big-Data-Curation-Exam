Oslo, Norway, 21 February 2024: Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement published by Norsk Titanium AS (the "Company") on 6 February 2024 regarding the receipt of subscription rights by certain primary insiders and close associates of primary insiders of the Company in connection with the commencement of the subscription period for the partially underwritten rights issue (the "Rights Issue"). Each subscription right gives the right to subscribe for and be allocated one offer share in the Rights Issue, at the subscription price in the Rights Issue.

Today, the Company has been informed that the following primary insiders of the Company have exercised subscription rights in the Rights Issue as listed below:

•	28,659 subscription rights have been exercised by Carl Johnson, Chief Executive Officer and President
•	20,000 subscription rights have been exercised by Ashar Ashary, Chief Financial Officer
•	10,000 subscription rights have been exercised by Gail Balcerzak, Chief Legal and People Officer
•	12,000 subscription rights have been exercised by Nicholas Mayer, Vice President, Commercial 
•	55,289 subscription rights have been exercised by Khazeem Adesokan, Vice President, Quality
•	16,162 subscription rights have been exercised by Anne Lene Gullen Bråten, Director Finance

Please see the attached notifications of trade for information regarding the primary insiders' exercise of subscription rights in the Rights Issue.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article 19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
