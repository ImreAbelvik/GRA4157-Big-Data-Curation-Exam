Geveran Trading Co. Limited ("Geveran"), a company indirectly controlled by
trusts established by Mr. John Fredriksen for the benefit of his immediate
family, has on 12 February 2020 acquired 425,000 shares in Axactor SE
("Axactor") at a purchase price of NOK 16.6315 per share. Following the
acquisition, Geveran will own 57,135,233 shares in Axactor which will constitute
30.82% of the shares and votes in Axactor. The foregoing calculations are based
on 185,395,464 outstanding shares in Axactor. This information is subject to the
disclosure requirements set out in section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
