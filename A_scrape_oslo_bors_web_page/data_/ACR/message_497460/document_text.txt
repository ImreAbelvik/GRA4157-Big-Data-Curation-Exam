Oslo, 6 March 2020

Terje Mjøs Holding AS, a company directly controlled by a member of the Board of Directors of Axactor SE, Terje Mjøs, has on 6 March 2020 purchased 20,000 shares in the Company at an average price of 11.40 per share. After this transaction Mr Terje Mjøs and Terje Mjøs Holding AS controls 60,000 shares in the Company.
