Oslo, 15 March 2024

Karl Todsen Mamelund (Chief Investment Officer in Axactor ASA), has on 14 March
2024 acquired 21,858 shares in the Company at an average price of 4.63 per
share. After this transaction Karl Todsen Mamelund controls 196,858 shares in
the Company.

Please refer to the attached notifications of transactions for further details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation
EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section
5-12.
