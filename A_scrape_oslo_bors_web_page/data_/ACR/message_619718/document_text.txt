Kjersti Høklingen a member of Board of Axactor ASA, has on 24 May 2024 acquired 21,000 shares in the company at an average price of 4,15 per share. After this transaction Kjersti Høklingen controls 21.000 shares in the company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
