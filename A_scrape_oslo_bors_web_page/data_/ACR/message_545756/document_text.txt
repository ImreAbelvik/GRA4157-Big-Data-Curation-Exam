Oslo, 2 November 2021

Terje Mjøs Holding AS, a company directly controlled by a member of the Board of Directors of Axactor SE, Terje Mjøs, has on 2 November 2021 purchased 100,000 shares in the Company at an average price of 7.975 per share. After this transaction Mr Terje Mjøs and Terje Mjøs Holding AS controls 400,000 shares in the Company.
