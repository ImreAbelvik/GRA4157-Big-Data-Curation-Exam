Oslo, 14 June 2024

Johnny Tsolis, CEO in Axactor ASA, has today acquired 60,000 shares in the Company at an average price of 4.20 per share. After this transaction Johnny Tsolis controls 1,770,000 shares in the Company directly or indirectly through a company under his control.

Please refer to the attached notification of transactions for further details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
