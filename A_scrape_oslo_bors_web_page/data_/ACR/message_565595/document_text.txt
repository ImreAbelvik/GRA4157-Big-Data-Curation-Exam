Oslo, 23 June 2022

Johnny Tsolis, CEO of Axactor ASA, has on 22 June 2022 purchased 300,000 call
options in the company share at an average price of NOK 0.96 per call option.
The strike price is NOK 6.00 on 22 June 2023 and will be settled in cash. After
this transaction he controls 300,000 call options and 1,710,000 shares in the
company.

Vibeke Ly, Chief of Staff of Axactor ASA, has on 22 June 2022 purchased 70,000
shares in the company at an average price of NOK 5.37 per share. After this
transaction she controls 203,750 shares in the company.

Nina Mortensen, CFO of Axactor ASA, has on 22 June 2022 purchased 65,000 shares
in the company at an average price of NOK 5.47 per share. After this transaction
she controls 160,000 shares in the company.
