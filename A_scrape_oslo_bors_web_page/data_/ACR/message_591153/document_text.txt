Oslo, 22 May 2023

Arnt Andre Dullum, member of the Axactor ASA Executive Management Team, has on
22 May 2023 acquired 38,000 shares in the Company at an average price of 5.4 per
share. After this transaction Arnt Andre Dullum controls 200,000 shares in the
Company.

Please refer to the attached notification of transaction for further details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation
EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section
5-12.
