Oslo, 17 February 2025

Johnny Tsolis, CEO in Axactor ASA, has on Friday 14th of February acquired 400,000 shares in the Company at an average price of 3,9477 per share. After this transaction Johnny Tsolis controls 2 170 000 shares in the Company directly or indirectly through a company under his control.

Please refer to the attached notification of the transaction for further details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
