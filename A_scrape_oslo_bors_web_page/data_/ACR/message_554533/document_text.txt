Oslo, 22 February 2022

Terje Mjøs Holding AS, a company directly controlled by a member of the Board of Directors of Axactor SE, Terje Mjøs, has on 21 February 2022 purchased 100,000 shares in the Company at an average price of 5.77 per share. After this transaction Mr Terje Mjøs and Terje Mjøs Holding AS controls 500,000 shares in the Company.
