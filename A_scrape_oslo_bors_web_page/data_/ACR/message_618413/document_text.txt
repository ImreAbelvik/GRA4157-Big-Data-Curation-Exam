Terje Mjøs Holding AS, a company controlled by Terje Mjøs (Chair of Board of Axactor ASA), has on 13 May 2024 acquired 44,090 shares in the company at an average price of 4,30 per share. After this transaction Terje Mjøs controls 750.000 shares in the company.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act section 5-12.
