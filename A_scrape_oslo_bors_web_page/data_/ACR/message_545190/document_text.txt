Oslo, 27 October 2021

Terje Mjøs Holding AS, a company directly controlled by a member of the Board of Directors of Axactor SE, Terje Mjøs, has on 27 October 2021 purchased 100,000 shares in the Company at an average price of 8.05 per share. After this transaction Mr Terje Mjøs and Terje Mjøs Holding AS controls 300,000 shares in the Company.
