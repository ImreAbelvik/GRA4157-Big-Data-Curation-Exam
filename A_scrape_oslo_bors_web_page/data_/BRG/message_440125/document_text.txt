


A party related to Åsmund Dybedahl, employee elected member of the board of
Borregaard ASA (ticker: "BRG"), has on 5 December 2017 acquired 2,719 shares in
Borregaard ASA at a price of NOK 73.50 per share. Following this transaction,
Åsmund Dybedahl and related parties own 9,342 shares in Borregaard ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5 - 12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
