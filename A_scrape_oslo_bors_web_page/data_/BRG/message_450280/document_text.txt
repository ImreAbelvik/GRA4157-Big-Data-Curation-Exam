


Terje Andersen, member of the board of Borregaard ASA (ticker: "BRG"), has on 2
May 2018 acquired 800 shares in Borregaard ASA at a price of NOK 84.30 per
share. Following this transaction, Terje Andersen owns 4,371 shares in
Borregaard ASA.

This  information is subject to the  disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5 - 12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
