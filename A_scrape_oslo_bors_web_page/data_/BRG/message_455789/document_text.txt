


Tove Andersen, shareholder-elected member of the board of Borregaard ASA
(ticker: "BRG"), has on 18 July 2018 acquired 4,000 shares in Borregaard ASA at
a price of NOK 75.25 per share. Following this transaction, Tove Andersen owns
4,000 shares in Borregaard ASA.

According  to Borregaard's  Annual meeting  resolution, the board's shareholder-
elected  members shall  use 20% of  their gross  board remuneration  to purchase
shares  in the company until they own shares  equal in value to one year's gross
board remuneration. The shares shall be retained for as long as the board member
serves on the board.
