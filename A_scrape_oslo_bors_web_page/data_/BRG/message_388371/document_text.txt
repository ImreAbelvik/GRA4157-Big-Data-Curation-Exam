


Sveinung Heggen, General Counsel of Borregaard ASA (ticker: "BRG"), has on 2
November 2015 together with related parties acquired 3,000 shares in Borregaard
ASA at a price of NOK 45.60 per share. Following this transaction, Sveinung
Heggen and related parties hold 7,209 shares and 50,000 options in Borregaard
ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
