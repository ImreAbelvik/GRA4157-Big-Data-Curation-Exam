


Kristine Ryssdal, member of the board of Borregaard ASA (ticker: "BRG"), has on
20 July 2017 acquired 1,050 shares in Borregaard ASA at a price of NOK 96.50 per
share. Following this transaction, Kristine Ryssdal owns 2,100 shares in
Borregaard ASA.

This  information is subject to the  disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5 - 12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
