


Jon Erik Reinhardsen, member of the board of Borregaard ASA (ticker: "BRG"), has
on 04 May 2017 acquired 2000 shares in Borregaard ASA at a price of NOK 93,75
per share. Following this transaction, Jon Erik Reinhardsen owns 2000 shares in
Borregaard ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
