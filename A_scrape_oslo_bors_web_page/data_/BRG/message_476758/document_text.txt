Helge Aasen, shareholder-elected member of the board of Borregaard ASA, has on
13 May 2019 acquired 1,600 shares in Borregaard ASA at a price of NOK 90.90 per
share. Following this transaction, Helge Aasen owns 2,900 shares in Borregaard
ASA.

Shareholder-elected  board members shall use  20% of their gross remuneration to
purchase  shares in  the company  until they  own shares  equal in  value to one
year's gross board remuneration. The shares shall be retained for as long as the
board member serves on the board.
