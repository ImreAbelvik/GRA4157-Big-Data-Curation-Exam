


21.10.2016: Borregaard ASA ("Borregaard", OSE ticker: BRG)

On 21 October 2016, in connection with Borregaard's option programme, 25,000
options in Borregaard shares were exercised at a strike price of NOK 15.18 per
share.

After this transaction, the total number of options issued in Borregaard shares
is 980,000. Borregaard owns 343,380 treasury shares representing 0.34% of total
shares outstanding.
