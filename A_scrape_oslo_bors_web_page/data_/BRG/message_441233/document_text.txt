


A party related to Åsmund Dybedahl, employee elected member of the board of
Borregaard ASA (ticker: "BRG"), has on 20 December 2017 acquired 2,553 shares in
Borregaard ASA at a price of NOK 78.38 per share. Following this transaction,
Åsmund Dybedahl and related parties own 11,895 shares in Borregaard ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5 - 12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
