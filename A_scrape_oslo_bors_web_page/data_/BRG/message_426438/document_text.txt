


Kristine Ryssdal, member of the board of Borregaard ASA (ticker: "BRG"), has
on 04 May 2017 acquired 1050 shares in Borregaard ASA at a price of NOK 94,00
per share. Following this transaction, Kristine Ryssdal owns 1050 shares in
Borregaard ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
