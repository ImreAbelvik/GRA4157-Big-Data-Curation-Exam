


Dag Arthur Aasbø, Senior Vice President of HR and Communications of Borregaard
ASA (ticker: "BRG"), has on 2 November 2015 acquired 3,000 shares in Borregaard
ASA at a price of NOK 45.30 per share. Following this transaction, Dag Arthur
Aasbø owns 42,721 shares and 62,500 options in Borregaard ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
