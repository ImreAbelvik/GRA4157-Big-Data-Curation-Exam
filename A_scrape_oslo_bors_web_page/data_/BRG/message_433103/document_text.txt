


Related parties to General Counsel of Borregaard ASA, Sveinung Heggen, have on
15 August 2017 acquired 20 shares in Borregaard ASA (ticker:"BRG") at a price of
NOK 94.75 per share. Following this transaction, Sveinung Heggen and related
parties own 8,535 shares in Borregaard ASA. Sveinung Heggen owns 65,000 options
in Borregaard ASA.

This  information is subject to the  disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5 - 12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
