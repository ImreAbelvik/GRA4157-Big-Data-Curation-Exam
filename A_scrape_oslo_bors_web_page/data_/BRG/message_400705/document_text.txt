


29.04.2016: Borregaard ASA ("Borregaard", OSE ticker: BRG)

On 29 April 2016, Per A. Sørlie, President and CEO, exercised 125,000 Borregaard
stock options at a strike price of NOK 17.65 per share.

In the same transaction, Borregaard repurchased 115,000 own shares from the
primary insider Per A. Sørlie at an average price of NOK 60.55 per share. The
repurchase is conducted in accordance with a proxy given at the Ordinary General
Meeting held on 13 April 2016.

As a result of these transactions, Per A. Sørlie has increased his share
ownership by 10,000 shares to 145,990 shares, and he holds 120,000 options in
Borregaard.

The total number of outstanding Borregaard stock options following these
transactions is 1,153,800. Borregaard owns 448,880 treasury shares representing
0.45% of total shares outstanding.
