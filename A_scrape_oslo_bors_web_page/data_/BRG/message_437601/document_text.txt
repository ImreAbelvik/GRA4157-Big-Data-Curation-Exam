


Jon Erik Reinhardsen, member of the board of Borregaard ASA (ticker: "BRG"), has
on 31 Oktober 2017 acquired 2,000 shares in Borregaard ASA at a price of
NOK 78.49 per share. Following this transaction, Jon Erik Reinhardsen owns 4,000
shares in Borregaard ASA.

This  information is subject to the  disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5 - 12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
