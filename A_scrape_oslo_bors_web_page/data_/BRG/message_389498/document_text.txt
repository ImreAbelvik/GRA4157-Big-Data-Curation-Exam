


16 November 2015: Borregaard ASA ("Borregaard", OSE ticker: BRG)

Reference is made to the stock exchange notices of 26 October and 3 November
regarding the shares to employees program. Today Borregaard transferred in total
267,375 shares to the 363 employees who participated in this year's program.

After this transfer Borregaard owns 493,880 treasury shares.
