


JAAG Consult AS, a closely related party to Jan Anders Oksum, chairman of the
Board of Directors of Borregaard ASA (ticker: "BRG"), has on 28 October 2015
acquired 2 000 shares in Borregaard ASA at a price of NOK 44.25 per share.
Following this transaction, Jan Anders Oksum's shareholding in Borregaard ASA,
including his closely related parties, is 10 000 shares.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
