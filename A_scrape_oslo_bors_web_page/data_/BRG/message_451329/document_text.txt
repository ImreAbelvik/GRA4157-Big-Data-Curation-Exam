



JAAG Consult AS, a closely related party to Jan Anders Oksum, chairman of the
board of directors of Borregaard ASA
(ticker: "BRG"), has on 14 May 2018 acquired 1,500 shares in Borregaard ASA at a
price of NOK 84.60 per share.
Following this transaction, Jan Anders Oksum and related parties own 11,500
shares in Borregaard ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
