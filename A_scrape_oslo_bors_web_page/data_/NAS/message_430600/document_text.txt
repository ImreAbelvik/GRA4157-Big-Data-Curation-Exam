Sale of 4.7 million shares and purchase of a Cash Settled 
Total Return Swap related to Norwegian Finans Holding
 
Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA (NAS) has after close of trading 
today, 28 June 2017, sold 4,700,000 million shares in 
Norwegian Finans Holding ASA (NOFI) under a Cash Settled 
Total Return Swap agreement with Danske Bank as counterparty 
at NOK 76.75 per share.

The expiration date of the Cash Settled Total Return Swap 
agreement is 29th of June 2018.

Following the transaction Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA holds 
voting rights for 32,623,739 shares in NOFI, constituting 
17.5 per cent of the issued share capital, and economic 
interest in 37,323,739 shares, constituting 20 per cent of 
the share capital.
