On Friday 28 April 2017 a total of 9,892 shares was 
purchased on behalf of employees in Norwegian Air Shuttle 
ASA participating in the company's employee share saving 
program. The average purchase price was NOK 245.42 per share.

On behalf of Jan Dahm-Simonsen the company has purchased 18 
shares. The new holding is 41 shares.

On behalf of Edward Thorstad the company has purchased 24 
shares. The new holding is 2,502 shares.

On behalf of Tore Østby the company has purchased 18 shares. 
The new holding is 11,600 shares.

On behalf of Qaisar Abbas Khan the company has purchased 18 
shares. The new holding is 349 shares.
