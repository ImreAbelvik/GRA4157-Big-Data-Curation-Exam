Geveran Trading Co. Ltd has today purchased 509 671 
shares in Norwegian Property at NOK 9.9604 per share. 

Following this, Geveran Trading Co. Ltd now owns 
237 746 502 shares, representing approximately 43.36% 
of the issued shares and voting rights in Norwegian 
Property. 

Cecilie Astrup Fredriksen and Espen D. Westeren are 
board members of Norwegian Property and related 
parties to Geveran Trading Co. Ltd.
