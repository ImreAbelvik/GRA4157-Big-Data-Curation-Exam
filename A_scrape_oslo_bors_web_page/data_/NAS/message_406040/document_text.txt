Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA has on Friday 15 July bought 
21,867 shares in Norwegian Finans Holding ASA (NOFI) 
at an average price of NOK 75.9343 per share. 

Following the transaction, new shareholding for 
Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA is 36,012,808 shares, equal 
to 20 % of the total outstanding shares of Norwegian 
Finans Holding ASA.


Fornebu 18 July 2016

requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the 
Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
