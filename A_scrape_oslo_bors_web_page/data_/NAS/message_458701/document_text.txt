1. On 3 September 2018 NAS employees were granted 380,000 share options, of which 380,000 to primary insiders.

The options were granted on terms resolved by NAS' Annual General Meeting on 8 May 2018:
- The number of options to be granted was limited to 400,000. Each share option corresponds to one share.
- The strike price is NOK 278.00.
- Options may be exercised in 2021 at the earliest, and in 2025 at the latest.
- Any calendar year, each optionee's aggregated gross profit from exercise of options under all share option programs shall not exceed 3 years' gross base salary.
- If an optionee leaves the company, the non-vested options will be forfeited. Outstanding options exercisable at the date of such termination shall be exercisable no later than the first exercise period thereafter.
