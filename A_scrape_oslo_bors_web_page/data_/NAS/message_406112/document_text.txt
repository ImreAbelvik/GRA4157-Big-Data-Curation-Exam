purchased on behalf of employees in Norwegian Air 
Shuttle ASA participating in the company's employee 
share saving program. The average purchase price was 
NOK 295.18 per share.

purchased 25 shares. The new holding is 9,604 shares.

On behalf of Wenche Drevland the company has 
purchased 15 shares. The new holding is 200 shares.

purchased 15 shares. The new holding is 507 shares.

On behalf of Kathinka Brevik Bråten the company has 
purchased 15 shares. The new holding is 235 shares.

On behalf of Edward Thorstad the company has 
purchased 21 shares. The new holding is 2,430 shares.
