Reference is made to the approved fully underwritten rights issue (the "Rights issue") in Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA (the "Company").

Eric Holm, Board Member, has today purchased 262 subscription rights at an average price of NOK 26.68 per subscription right. This is in addition to the subscription announced in the stock exchange announcement on 26 February 2019. Following the issuance of the new shares in the Rights Issue, Eric Holm will own 655 shares in the Company.
