On Friday 21 October a total of 7,574 shares was 
purchased on behalf of employees in Norwegian Air 
Shuttle ASA participating in the company's employee 
share saving program. The average purchase price was 
NOK 299.97 per share.

On behalf of Jan Dahm-Simonsen the company has 
purchased 4 shares. The new holding is 4 shares.

On behalf of Edward Thorstad the company has 
purchased 20 shares. The new holding is 2,450 shares.

On behalf of Tore Østby the company has 
purchased 15 shares. The new holding is 10,564 shares.

On behalf of Qaisar Abbas Khan the company has 
purchased 15 shares. The new holding is 305 shares.
