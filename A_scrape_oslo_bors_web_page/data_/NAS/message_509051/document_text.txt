Grant of shares in Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA ("NAS" or the "Company"). 

On 30 June 2020, NAS’ shareholder elected board members were granted 882,352 restricted shares, of which 882,352 to primary insiders. 

The underlying shares have been provided by transfer of the Company’s holding of treasury shares. Subsequent to the transaction, NAS holds 9,048 own shares. 

The shares were granted on terms resolved by the Company’s Annual General Meeting on 30 June 2020: 

• The Chair of the Board shall receive an annual fee of NOK 550,000 in shares in the Company
• The Deputy Chair shall receive an annual fee of NOK 350,000 in shares in the Company 
• Other directors elected by the shareholders shall receive an annual fee of NOK 300,000 in shares in the Company 

The number of shares has been determined based on the share price close on 30 June 2020 of NOK 2.72 per share.
