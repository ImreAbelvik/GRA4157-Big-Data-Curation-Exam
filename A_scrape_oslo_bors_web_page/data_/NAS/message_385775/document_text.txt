Exercise of employee share options in company 
Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA ("the Company")

1.	Participants in the Company's share option 
program have on September 18th 2015 exercised a 
total number of 161,500 options at a strike price of 
NOK 231.20. 

Following the exercise, the Board of Directors, 
pursuant to authorisation granted by the Company's 
Annual General Meeting, has decided to increase the 
Company's share capital by NOK 16,150, by issuing 
161,500 new shares, of par value NOK 0.10 Subsequent 
to the transaction the Company's share capital is 
NOK 3,559,813.90 divided into 35,598,139 shares. 
 
2. Options exercised by primary insiders September 
18th 2015:


Frode Berg has exercised 10,000 options in the 
Company, corresponding to 10,000 shares at the 
strike price of NOK 231.20

Frode Foss has exercised 34,000 options in the 
Company, corresponding to 34,000 shares at the 
strike price of NOK 231.20

Bjørn Kjos has exercised 60,000 options in the 
Company, corresponding to 60,000 shares at the 
strike price of NOK 231.20

Asgeir Nyseth has exercised 45,000 options in the 
Company, corresponding to 45,000 shares at the 
strike price of NOK 231.20


3. Transfer of rights 

On September 18th 2015, the primary insiders 
mentioned below transferred their rights to receive 
shares resulting from the exercise of options to a 
third party. The sales amount will be finally 
determined on the basis of the price subsequently 
obtained by the third party when selling the shares 
in the market. Still, primary insiders cannot be 
held liable for losses incurred by the third party 
if the shares are later sold below the strike price. 
Final compensation for the transferred rights will 
be reported to Oslo Børs when known.
 
Frode Berg has transferred 10,000 rights.

Frode Foss has transferred 34,000 rights.

Bjørn Kjos has transferred 60,000 rights.

Asgeir Nyseth has transferred 45,000 rights.


 
4. New holding

After exercising options, Frode Berg holds no 
unexercised options in the Company. After completion 
of the abovementioned transactions, the primary 
insider holds no shares in the Company.

After exercising options, Frode Foss holds no 
unexercised options in the Company. After completion 
of the abovementioned transactions, the primary 
insider holds 35,000 shares in the Company.

After exercising options, Bjørn Kjos holds no 
unexercised options in the Company. After completion 
of the abovementioned transactions, the primary 
insider holds 8,795,873 shares in the Company.

After exercising options, Asgeir Nyseth holds no 
unexercised options in the Company. After completion 
of the abovementioned transactions, the primary 
insider holds 13,242 shares in the Company.
