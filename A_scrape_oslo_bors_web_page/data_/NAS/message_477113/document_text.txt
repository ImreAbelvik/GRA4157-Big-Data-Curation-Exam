Grant of shares in Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA ("NAS" or the "Company").

1. On 15 May 2019, NAS’ shareholder elected board members were granted 78,125 restricted shares, of which 78,125 to primary insiders.

The underlying shares have been provided by transfer of the Company’s holding of treasury shares. Subsequent to the transaction, NAS holds 1,400 own shares.

The shares were granted on terms resolved by the Company’s Annual General Meeting on 7 May 2019:

• The Chair of the Board shall receive an annual fee of NOK 500,000 in shares in the Company, in addition to a sign-on fee of NOK 1,500,000 in shares in the Company
• The Deputy Chair shall receive an annual fee of NOK 300,000 in shares in the Company
• Other directors elected by the shareholders shall receive an annual fee of NOK 275,000 in shares in the Company

The number of shares has been determined based on the share price close on 7 May 2019 of NOK 40 per share.
