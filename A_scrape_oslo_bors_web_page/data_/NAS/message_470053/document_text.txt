Reference is made to the stock exchange notice from HBK Holding AS (“HBK”) yesterday regarding the sale of subscription rights in the rights issue of Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA (the “Company”). HBK is a company controlled by Bjørn Kjos, Chief Executive Officer of Company, Bjørn Halvor Kise, Chairman of the Board of the Company, and their respective families.
 
The rights issue of the Company was approved by the extraordinary general meeting earlier today. HBK will consequently sell 10,413,849 subscription rights under the agreement to sell subscription rights announced yesterday. The price per subscription right is NOK 15.01. The buyers of the subscription rights have committed to subscribe for the subscription rights that they acquire in the rights issue.
 
HBK currently owns 11,204,809 shares in the Company, representing 24.66% of the shares of the Company.
