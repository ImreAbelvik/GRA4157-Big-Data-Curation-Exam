Grant of employee share options in Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA ("NAS").

1. On 29 May 2019 NAS employees were granted 1,200,000 share options, of which 1,050,000 to primary insiders.

The options were granted on terms resolved by NAS' Annual General Meeting on 7 May 2019:
• The number of options to be granted was limited to 1,200,000. Each share option corresponds to one share.
• The strike price is NOK 43.00.
• Options may be exercised in 2022 at the earliest, and in 2026 at the latest.
• Any calendar year, each optionee’s aggregated gross profit from exercise of options under all share option programs shall not exceed 3 years’ gross base salary.
• If an optionee leaves the company, the non-vested options will be forfeited. Outstanding options exercisable at the date of such termination shall be exercisable no later than the first exercise period thereafter.
