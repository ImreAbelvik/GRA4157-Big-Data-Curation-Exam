Primary insider Tore Østby has today bought 1,000 
shares in Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA through his 100% 
owned company Arken Invest AS at a price of NOK 274.50 
per share. Following the transaction, new shareholding 
for Tore Østby incl. related parties is 8,521 shares.
