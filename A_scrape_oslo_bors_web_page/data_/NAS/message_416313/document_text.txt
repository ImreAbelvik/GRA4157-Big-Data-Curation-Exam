Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA - flagge- og 
primærinnsidermelding

HBK Invest AS har etter børsslutt 16. desember 2016 
besluttet overføring av 8 795 873 aksjer i Norwegian 
Air Shuttle ASA, tilsvarende 24,60 % av samtlige 
aksjer og stemmeretter i selskapet, til sitt heleide 
datterselskap, HBK Holding AS. Aksjene vil bli 
overført i form av tingsinnskudd mot utstedelse til 
HBK Invest AS av aksjer i HBK Holding AS, og 
innskuddet vil skje til aksjenes bokført verdi i HBK 
Invest AS, ca. NOK 27,87 per aksje. Etter innskuddet 
vil HBK Invest AS ikke lenger eie aksjer i Norwegian 
Air Shuttle ASA, mens HBK Holding AS vil ha overtatt 
eierskapet til 8 795 873 aksjer i Norwegian Air 
Shuttle ASA, tilsvarende 24,60 % av samtlige aksjer og 
stemmeretter i selskapet. 

HBK Invest AS er eid med 84,13 % av Bjørn Kjos, 
administrerende direktør i Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA, 
hvorav 76,5 % direkte av Bjørn Kjos og 7,63 % via 
Bøgaset AS som er heleid av Bjørn Kjos. Videre er 8,22 
% av aksjene i HBK Invest AS eid av Bjørn H. Kise, 
styreleder i Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA. De resterende 
7,63 % av aksjene i HBK Invest AS er eid av Read Group 
AS, som er heleid av Tore Kjos. HBK Invest AS er 
representert i Norwegian Air Shuttle ASAs styre. 

Denne meldingen er gitt i henhold til 
verdipapirhandelloven § 4-2 og § 4-3.

Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA - disclosure of transfer of 
large shareholding and primary insider notification 

HBK Invest AS has after close of trading on 16 
December 2016 resolved to transfer 8 795 873 shares in 
Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA, equal to 24.60% of the 
outstanding shares and voting rights in the company, 
to its wholly owned subsidiary, HBK Holding AS. The 
shares will be transferred in the form of contribution 
in kind against issue of shares in HBK Holding AS to 
HBK Invest AS, and the contribution will take place at 
the shares' book value in HBK Invest AS, approximately 
NOK 27.87 per share. After the transfer, HBK Invest AS 
will own zero shares in Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA, 
while HBK Holding AS will have taken over ownership to 
8 795 873 shares in Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA, equal 
to 24.60% of the outstanding shares and voting rights 
in the company. 

HBK Invest AS is owned with 84.13% by Bjørn Kjos, the 
CEO of Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA, of which 76.5% are 
owned by Bjørn Kjos directly and 7.63% through Bøgaset 
AS which is wholly owned by Bjørn Kjos. Another 8.22% 
of the shares in HBK Invest AS are owned by Bjørn H. 
Kise, the chairman of Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA. The 
remaining 7.63% of the shares in HBK Invest AS are 
owned by Read Group AS which is wholly owned by Tore 
Kjos. HBK Invest AS is represented at the board of 
directors of Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA.

This notification is given pursuant to the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act sections 4-2 and 4 3.
