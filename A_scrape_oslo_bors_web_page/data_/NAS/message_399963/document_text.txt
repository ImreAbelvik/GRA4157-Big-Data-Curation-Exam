purchased on behalf of employees in Norwegian Air

share saving program. The average purchase price was 
NOK 358.8 per share.

On behalf of Wenche Drevland the company has 
purchased 12 shares. The new holding is 185 shares.

purchased 12 shares. The new holding is 492 shares.

On behalf of Kathinka Brevik Bråten the company has 
purchased 12 shares. The new holding is 276 shares.

purchased 10 shares. The new holding is 2,409 shares.
