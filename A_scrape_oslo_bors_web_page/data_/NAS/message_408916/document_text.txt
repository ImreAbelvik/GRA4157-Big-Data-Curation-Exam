Jan Dahm-Simonsen joined Norwegian 1 September 2016 as 
new Chief Human Resources Officer for Norwegian. As 
part of the company's incentive program Jan Dahm-
Simonsen has been granted 20,000 options in Norwegian 
Air Shuttle ASA. 

The options have an exercise price 10 % above the 
weighted average price the last ten trading days as of 
6 September 2016, which is equal to NOK 340.00. 
Following the grant Mr. Dahm-Simonsen holds 20,000 
options and no shares in Norwegian.

The options are a part of the incentive program 
announced 14 July 2016. A total of 625,000 share 
options have been granted to management and key 
employees in the Company as described in the stock 
market announcement 14 July.
