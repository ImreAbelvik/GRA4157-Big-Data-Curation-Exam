The Board of Directors has granted a total of 625,000 
share options to the management and to key personnel 
in Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA. 

The following primary insiders have been granted 
options: 

Chief Executive Officer Bjørn Kjos has been granted 
100,000 options. Following the grant Mr. Kjos holds 
100,000 options and 7,443,315 shares in Norwegian Air 
Shuttle ASA. 

Chief Financial Officer Frode E. Foss has been granted 
100,000 options. Following the grant Mr. Foss holds 
100,000 options and 35,000 shares in Norwegian Air 
Shuttle ASA. 

Chief Operating Officer Asgeir Nyseth has been granted 
100,000 options. Following the grant Mr. Nyseth holds 
100,000 options and 12,342 shares in Norwegian Air 
Shuttle ASA.

Chief Corporate Communications Officer Anne-Sissel 
Skånvik has been granted 50,000 options. Following the 
grant Ms. Skånvik holds 50,000 options and no shares 
in Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA.

Chief Commercial Officer Thomas Ramdahl has been 
granted 25,000 options. Following the grant Mr. 
Ramdahl holds 25,000 options and no shares in 
Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA.

Chief Legal Officer Frode Berg has been granted 25,000 
options. Following the grant Mr. Berg holds 25,000 
options and no shares in Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA. 

Chief Information Officer Dag Skage has been granted 
15,000 options. Following the grant Mr. Skage holds 
15,000 options and no shares in Norwegian Air Shuttle 
ASA. 

CEO Norwegian Air International Tore Jenssen has been 
granted 25,000 options. Following the grant Mr. 
Jenssen holds 25,000 options and no shares in 
Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA.

Chief Customer Officer Edward Thorstad has been 
granted 20,000 options. Following the grant Mr. 
Thorstad holds 20,000 options and 2,409 shares in 
Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA.

Vice President Investor Relations Tore Østby has been 
granted 15,000 options. Following the grant Mr. Østby 
holds 15,000 options and 10,533 shares in Norwegian 
Air Shuttle ASA.


A total of 625,000 share options have been granted to 
employees in the Company. The options have an exercise 
price 10 % above the weighted average price the 10 
last trading days as of July 13th 2016, which is equal 
to NOK 321.00. 

The options granted may be exercised two years after 
the grant. The exercise window is six months. The 
potential gain on the options has a maximum cap of 
three times basic salary per year minus employer 
contribution tax.
