HBK Holding AS (“HBK”), a company controlled by Bjørn Kjos, Chief Executive Officer of Norwegian Air Shuttle ASA (the "Company"), Bjørn Halvor Kise, Chairman of the Board of the Company, and their respective families, has entered into agreements to sell subscription rights it will receive in the Company's contemplated rights issue that it will not use to subscribe for in the rights issue. The buyers consist of Norwegian family offices and international institutional investors.

HBK has agreed to sell the subscription rights at a price of 70% of the theoretical value of the subscription rights at the time of pricing of the rights issue, expected on or about 18 February 2019. The buyers have committed to a total investment amount of NOK 500 million including the purchase of subscription rights and subscription in the rights issue. The number of subscription rights to be sold will be determined based on the subscription price and number of new shares to be issued in the rights issue.

The buyers of the subscription rights have committed to subscribe for the subscription rights that they acquire in the rights issue.

The agreement is subject to the extraordinary general meeting on 19 February 2019 approving the contemplated rights issue.

HBK currently owns 11,204,809 shares in the Company, representing 24.66% of the shares of the Company.
