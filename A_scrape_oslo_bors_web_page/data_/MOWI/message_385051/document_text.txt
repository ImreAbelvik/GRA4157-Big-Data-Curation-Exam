Geveran Trading Co Ltd, which is indirectly
controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen
for the benefit of his immediate family, has on 7 
September 2015 extended TRS agreements relating to 
3.000.000 shares in Marine Harvest ASA. The new 
expiration of the TRS agreements is December 7th 
2015. The exercise price on the agreements is NOK 
96.6611 per share. Geveran Trading's affiliated 
ownership in Marine Harvest ASA is following this 
transaction unchanged 117.351.603 shares, 
constituting 26,07 percent of the issued share 
capital.
