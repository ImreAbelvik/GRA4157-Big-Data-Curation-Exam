


On 11 September 2015 Geveran Trading Co Ltd (Geveran), which is indirectly
controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the benefit of his
immediate family, acquired 3 million shares in Marine Harvest pursuant under the
forward contract announced on 23 December 2013.

On 11 September 2015 Framar AS (Framar) (previously named Profond Holding AS), a
company owned by Marine Harvest's Chairman Ole-Eirik Lerøy, acquired 3 million
shares in Marine Harvest from Geveran under the forward contract announced on
23 December 2013.

On 11 September 2015 Framar sold 2.5 million shares in Marine Harvest at a price
of NOK 96.86 per share.

Subsequent to these transactions Ole-Eirik Lerøy and his affiliates hold
3,222,000 shares in Marine Harvest corresponding to 0.72% of the issued share
capital.

Geveran's net exposure to Marine Harvest share is unchanged. Geveran holds
117,351,603 shares representing 26.07% of the issued share capital of Marine
Harvest. In addition Geveran hold a TRS agreement with an exposure to 3 million
shares in Marine Harvest.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
4-1 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
