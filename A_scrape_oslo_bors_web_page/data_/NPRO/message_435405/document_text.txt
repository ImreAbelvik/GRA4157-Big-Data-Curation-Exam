Geveran Trading Co Limited ("Geveran"), which is indirectly 
controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the 
benefit of his immediate family, has on 25 September 2017 
purchased 2,000,000 shares in Norwegian Property ASA 
("Norwegian Property") at a price of NOK 9.84 per share. As 
a result, Geveran's ownership in Norwegian Property has 
increased to 317,969,937 shares, constituting 57.98 % of the 
shares and votes in the company. Geveran does not hold other 
rights to shares in Norwegian Property.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements 
set out in section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading 
Act.
