(Oslo, 15 January 2015): Reference is made to the mandatory 
offer document (the "Mandatory Offer") dated 17 December 
2014 made by Geveran Trading Co Ltd ("Geveran" or 
the "Offeror") to acquire all issued and outstanding shares 
in Norwegian Property ASA ("Norwegian Property"), with 
acceptance period from 18 December 2014 to and including 
16:30 CET on 15 January 2015 (subject to extension). 

On 14 January 2015, Geveran received acceptance under the 
Mandatory Offer for 225,578 shares, corresponding to 0.04 
per cent of the share capital and voting rights in 
Norwegian Property. Following this, Geveran has received 
acceptances for a total of 779,088 shares and votes in 
Norwegian Property. Together with the 205,910,242 shares 
already owned by Geveran, the Offeror now owns and holds 
rights to a total of 206,689,330 shares, representing 
approximately 37.69 per cent of the issued shares and 
voting rights in Norwegian Property. 

Arctic Securities AS is engaged as financial advisor and 
receiving agent, and Advokatfirmaet BA-HR is acting as 
legal advisor to Geveran in connection with the Mandatory 
Offer.

Geveran is represented on the Board of Directors of 
Norwegian Property. The Offeror does not own any other 
rights to acquire shares in Norwegian Property.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements 
of sections 4-3 and 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities 
Trading Act.
