Geveran Trading Co. Ltd has on 16 June 2015 purchased 
5 836 235 shares in Norwegian Property at NOK 9.53 
per share.

Following this, Geveran Trading Co. Ltd now owns 261 
239 304 shares, representing approximately 47.6% of 
the issued shares and voting rights in Norwegian 
Property.

Cecilie Astrup Fredriksen and Espen D. Westeren are 
board members of Norwegian Property and related 
parties to Geveran Trading Co. Ltd.
