Geveran Trading Co Limited ("Geveran"), which is indirectly controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the benefit of his immediate family, has on 16th March 2020 purchased 29,000 shares in Norwegian Property ASA ("Norwegian Property") at a price of NOK 10.5868 per share. As a result, Geveran's ownership in Norwegian Property has increased to 398,216,710 shares, constituting 79.663% of the shares and votes in the company. 

The foregoing calculation is based on 499,875,596 outstanding shares in Norwegian Property.

Geveran does not hold other rights to shares in Norwegian Property. This information is subject to the disclosure requirements set out in section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
