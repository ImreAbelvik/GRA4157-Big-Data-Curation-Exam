Geveran Trading Co. Ltd, which is indirectly 
controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen 
for the benefit of his immediate family, has on 3 June 
2016 extended TRS agreements relating to 28.777.466 
shares in Norwegian Property. The new expiration of 
the TRS agreements is September 5th, 2016. The 
exercise price on the agreements is NOK 9.1769 per 
share. Geveran Trading Co. Ltd's affiliated ownership 
in Norwegian Property is following this transaction 
unchanged 264,724,869 shares, constituting 48.27 
percent of the issued share capital. In addition, 
Geveran Trading hold TRS agreements with an underlying 
Exposure to 28,777,466 shares in Norwegian Property. 
Cecilie Astrup Fredriksen is board member of Norwegian 
Property and related party to Geveran Trading Co. Ltd.
