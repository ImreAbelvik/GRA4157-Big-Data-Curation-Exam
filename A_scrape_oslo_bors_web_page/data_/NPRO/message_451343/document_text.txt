Geveran Trading Co Limited ("Geveran"), which is indirectly controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the benefit of his immediate family, has on 14th May 2018 purchased 2,696,937 shares in Norwegian Property ASA ("Norwegian Property") at a price of NOK 10.53 per share. As a result, Geveran's ownership in Norwegian Property has increased to 320,666,874 shares, constituting 58.47% of the shares and votes in the company. Geveran does not hold other rights to shares in Norwegian Property. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements set out in section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
