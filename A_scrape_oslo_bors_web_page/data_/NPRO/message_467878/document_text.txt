Carucel Holding AS, which is 100% owned by Carl Erik Krefting, has on January
22, 2019 extended a TRS agreement relating to 4.326.842 shares in Norwegian
Property. Carl Erik Krefting is a board member of Norwegian Property and a
related party to Carucel Holding AS. The new termination date of the TRS
agreement is January  24, 2020. The excercise price on the agreement is NOK
10.800 per share. Carucel Holding AS' ownership in Norwegian Property is
following this transaction unchanged at 0 shares. In addition Carucel Holding
AS holds a TRS Agreement with an underlying exposure to 4.326.842 shares in
Norwegian Property.
