Geveran Trading Co Limited ("Geveran"), which is indirectly controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the benefit of his immediate family, has on 31st October 2018 purchased 1,057,723 shares in Norwegian Property ASA ("Norwegian Property") at a price of NOK 10.336 per share. As a result, Geveran's ownership in Norwegian Property has increased to 326,468,339 shares, constituting 59.53% of the shares and votes in the company. Geveran does not hold other rights to shares in Norwegian Property. This information is subject to the disclosure requirements set out in section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
