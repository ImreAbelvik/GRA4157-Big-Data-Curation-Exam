Geveran trading Co. Ltd, which is indirectly 
controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen
for the benefit of his immediate family, has on 7 
December 2015 extended TRS agreements relating to 
28.777.466 shares in Norwegian Property.
The new expiration of the TRS agreements is June 
3rd, 2016.

The exercise price on the agreements is NOK 9.5955 
per share.

Geveran Trading Co. Ltd's affiliated ownership in 
Norwegian Property is following this transaction
unchanged 264,724,869 shares, constituting 48.27 
percent of the issued share capital.
In addition, Geveran trading hold TRS agreements with 
an underlying Exposure to 28,777,466 shares in 
Norwegian Property.

Cecilie Astrup Fredriksen is board member of Norwegian 
Property and related party to Geveran Trading Co. Ltd.
