Svein Hov Skjelle has today purchased 35 000 shares at NOK 9.735 per share.

Following this trade Svein Hov Skjelle has 101 594 shares. Svein Hov Skjelle is
CEO in Norwegian Property.
Norwegian Property is a focused and fully integrated office property company
with 39 properties located in the Oslo area and Stavanger in Norway. The
portfolio, which has a total fair value of NOK 16.5 billion, is characterized by
central location and attractive premises high quality tenants. The group's
properties consist largely of office premises, associated warehousing and car
parking, as well as retail and catering space. The company has identified four
value drivers for long-term value creation; Marketing & letting, Property
management, Property development and Transactions & finance.
Norwegian Property is listed on Oslo Stock Exchange with the ticker NPRO.
