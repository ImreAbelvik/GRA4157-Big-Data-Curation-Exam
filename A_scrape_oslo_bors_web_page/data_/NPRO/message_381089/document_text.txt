Geveran Trading Co. Ltd has on 25 June 2015 entered 
into TRS agreements with exposure to 28.777.466 
shares in Norwegian Property.
The expiration date of the TRS agreements is 7th 
September 2015 and the exercise price in the 
agreement is NOK 9,7469 per share.

Following this, Geveran Trading Co. Ltd owns 263 
724 869 shares, representing approximately 48.09% of 
the issued shares and voting rights in Norwegian 
Property.

In addition Geveran Trading now holds TRS agreements
with an underlying exposure to 28.777.466 shares in
Norwegian Property.

Cecilie Astrup Fredriksen and Espen D. Westeren are 
board members of Norwegian Property and related 
parties to Geveran Trading Co. Ltd.
