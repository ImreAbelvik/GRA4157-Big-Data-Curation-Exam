Geveran Trading Co Limited ("Geveran"), which is indirectly controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the benefit of his immediate family, has on 7th February 2019 purchased 1,812,668 shares in Norwegian Property ASA ("Norwegian Property") at a price of NOK 10.80 per share. As a result, Geveran's ownership in Norwegian Property has increased to 330,047,372 shares, constituting 60.18% of the shares and votes in the company. Geveran does not hold other rights to shares in Norwegian Property. This information is subject to the disclosure requirements set out in section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
