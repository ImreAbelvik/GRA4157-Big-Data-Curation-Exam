Geveran Trading Co. Ltd has on 11 June 2015 purchased
3 050 265 shares in Norwegian Property at NOK 9.90 
per share.

Following this, Geveran Trading Co. Ltd now owns 255
403 069 shares, representing approximately 46.6% of 
the issued shares and voting rights in Norwegian 
Property.

Cecilie Astrup Fredriksen and Espen D. Westeren are 
board members of Norwegian Property and related 
parties to Geveran Trading Co. Ltd.
