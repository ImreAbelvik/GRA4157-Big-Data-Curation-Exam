Geveran Trading Co. Ltd has today purchased 1 700 000 
shares in Norwegian Property at NOK 10.00 per share.
 
Following this, Geveran Trading Co. Ltd now owns 239 
446 502 shares, representing approximately 43.7% of 
the issued shares and voting rights in Norwegian 
Property.
 
Cecilie Astrup Fredriksen and Espen D. Westeren are 
board members of Norwegian Property and related 
parties to Geveran Trading Co. Ltd.
