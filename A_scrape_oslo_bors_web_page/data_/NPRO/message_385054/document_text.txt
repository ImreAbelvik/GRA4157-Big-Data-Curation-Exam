
Geveran trading Co. Ltd, which is indirectly 
controlled by 
trusts established by John Fredriksen for the benefit 
of his immediate family, has on 7 September 2015 
extended TRS 
agreements relating to 28.777.466 shares in Norwegian 
Property.
The new expiration of the TRS agreements is December 
7th, 2015.
The exercise price on the agreements is NOK 9.4549 
per share.

Geveran Trading Co. Ltd's affiliated ownership in 
Norwegian Property
is following this transaction unchanged 264,724,869 
shares, 
constituting 48.27 percent of the issued share 
capital.
In addition, Geveran trading hold TRS agreements with 
an underlying
Exposure to 28,777,466 shares in Norwegian Property.

Cecilie Astrup Fredriksen and Espen D. Westeren are 
board members of Norwegian Property and related 
parties to Geveran Trading Co. Ltd.
