Geveran Trading Co. Ltd has on 20 May 2015 purchased 
2 458 653 shares in Norwegian Property at NOK 9.99 
per share.
 
Following this, Geveran Trading Co. Ltd now owns 248 
855 143 shares, representing approximately 45.3% of 
the issued shares and voting rights in Norwegian 
Property.
 
Cecilie Astrup Fredriksen and Espen D. Westeren are 
board members of Norwegian Property and related 
parties to Geveran Trading Co. Ltd.
