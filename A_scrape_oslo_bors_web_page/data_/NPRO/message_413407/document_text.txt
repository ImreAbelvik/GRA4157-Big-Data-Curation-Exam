Geveran Trading Co Ltd ("Geveran"), which is 
indirectly controlled by trusts established by John 
Fredriksen for the benefit of his immediate family, 
has on 10 November 2016 acquired 1,500,000 shares in 
Norwegian Property ASA ("Norwegian Property") at a 
price of NOK 10.15 per share. Following this 
transaction, Geveran holds 314,418,411 shares in 
Norwegian Property, representing approximately 57.33 
per cent of the issued shares and voting rights in 
Norwegian Property. 

Geveran is represented on the Board of Directors of 
Norwegian Property. Geveran does not own any other 
rights to acquire shares in Norwegian Property. 

This information is subject of the disclosure 
requirements of section 4-2 of the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act.
