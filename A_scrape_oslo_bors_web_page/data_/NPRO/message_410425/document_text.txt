Reference is made to the mandatory offer document 
(the "Mandatory Offer") dated 21 September 2016 made 
by Geveran Trading Co Ltd ("Geveran" or the "Offeror") 
to acquire all issued and outstanding shares in 
Norwegian Property ASA ("Norwegian Property"), with 
acceptance period from 22 September 2016 to and 
including 20 October 2016 at 16:30 (CET) (subject to 
extension).

On 29 September 2016, Geveran received acceptance 
under the Mandatory Offer for 9,367 shares, 
corresponding to 0.002 per cent of the share capital 
and voting rights in Norwegian Property. Following 
this, Geveran has received acceptances for a total of 
17,267 shares and votes in Norwegian Property. 
Together with the 293,502,335 shares already owned by 
Geveran, the Offeror now owns and holds rights to a 
total of 293,519,602 shares, representing 
approximately 53.52 per cent of the issued shares and 
voting rights in Norwegian Property. 

Geveran is represented on the Board of Directors of 
Norwegian Property. The Offeror does not own any other 
rights to acquire shares in Norwegian Property. 

Arctic Securities AS is engaged as financial advisor 
and receiving agent, and Advokatfirmaet BA-HR DA is 
acting as legal advisor to Geveran in connection with 
the Mandatory Offer.

This information is subject of the disclosure 
requirements of section 4-2 of the Norwegian 
Securities Trading Act.
