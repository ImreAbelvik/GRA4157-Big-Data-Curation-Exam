Geveran Trading Co Limited ("Geveran"), which is indirectly controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the benefit of his immediate family, has on 22nd May 2020 purchased 300,259 shares in Norwegian Property ASA ("Norwegian Property") at a price of NOK 11.0999 per share. As a result, Geveran's ownership in Norwegian Property has increased to 399,155,839 shares, constituting 79.85% of the shares and votes in the company. 

The foregoing calculation is based on 499,875,596 outstanding shares in Norwegian Property.

Geveran does not hold other rights to shares in Norwegian Property. This information is subject to the disclosure requirements set out in section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
