Geveran Trading Co. Ltd has on 22 May 2015 purchased 
1 194 710 shares in Norwegian Property at NOK 10.00 
per share.

Following this, Geveran Trading Co. Ltd now owns  251 
052 804 shares, representing approximately 45.8% of 
the issued shares and voting rights in Norwegian 
Property.

Cecilie Astrup Fredriksen and Espen D. Westeren are 
board members of Norwegian Property and related 
parties to Geveran Trading Co. Ltd.
