Oslo, 28 May 2021, Håkan Englund, Deputy Board Member and primary insider in Ultimovacs ASA, has today bought 5,000 shares in the company at an average price of NOK 77.6808  per share. Following this transaction, Håkan Englund through JDS Invest AB holds 73.650  shares in Ultimovacs ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
