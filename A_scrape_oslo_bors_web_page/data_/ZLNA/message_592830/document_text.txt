Oslo, 08 June 2023, Board member Ketil Fjerdingen and primary insider in
Ultimovacs ASA, has today through Langøya Invest AS bought 7,000 shares in the
company at a price of NOK 79.5159 per share. Following this transaction, Ketil
Fjerdingen through Langøya Invest AS holds 1,396,006 shares in Ultimovacs ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
