Oslo, 21 November 2023, a closely related party of Carlos de Sousa, Chief
Executive Officer and primary insider in Ultimovacs ASA, has today bought 500
shares in the company at an average price of NOK 114.00 per share. Following
this transaction, Carlos de Sousa and closely related parties hold 22,056 shares
and 425,535 share options in Ultimovacs ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the
Market Abuse Regulation article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
