Oslo, 25 May 2021, Carlos de Sousa, Chief Executive Officer and primary insider in Ultimovacs ASA, has today bought 500 shares in the company at a price of NOK 79.00 per share, and a closely related party has today bought 650 shares in the company at a price of NOK 79.00 per share. Following this transaction, Carlos de Sousa and closely related parties hold 11,556 shares and 416,035 share options in Ultimovacs ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
