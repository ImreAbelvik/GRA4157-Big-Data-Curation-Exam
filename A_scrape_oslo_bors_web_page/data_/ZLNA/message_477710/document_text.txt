Ultimovacs ASA – Applications by primary insiders

Oslo, 23 May 2019: Reference is made to the announcement on 21 May 2019, whereby Ultimovacs ASA ("Ultimovacs" or the "Company") announced the commencement of the application period for the initial public offering of its shares (the "IPO") at a price per share of NOK 31.25 in connection with a listing of the Company on the Oslo Stock Exchange.

The following primary insiders or related parties of such have submitted applications for shares in the IPO:

CEO Øyvind Kongstun Arnesen, through Vitmed AS, has submitted an application for 3,200 shares in the IPO, corresponding to a total application amount of NOK 100,000.

CFO Hans Vassgård Eid, through Snøtind AS, has submitted an application for 3,200 shares in the IPO, corresponding to a total application amount of NOK 100,000.

Finance Manager Joachim Midttun has submitted an application for 3,200 shares in the IPO, corresponding to a total application amount of NOK 100,000.

Managing Director in Ultimovacs AB Gunilla Ekström has submitted an application for 1,600 shares in the IPO, corresponding to a total application amount of NOK 50,000.

Board member Ketil Fjerdingen, through Langøya Invest AS, has submitted an application for 320,000 shares in the IPO, corresponding to a total application amount of NOK 10,000,000.

Board member Eva Dugstad has submitted an application for 6,400 shares in the IPO, corresponding to a total application amount of NOK 200,000.

Board member Henrik Schussler has submitted an application for 19,200 shares in the IPO, corresponding to a total application amount of NOK 600,000.

Board member Leiv Askvig, through Basen Kapital AS, has submitted an application for 32,000 shares in the IPO, corresponding to a total application amount of NOK 1,000,000.

Solveig Gjelsten, a related party of the controlling shareholder Gjelsten Holding AS, which is represented on the Board of Directors of the Company, has submitted an application for 32,000 shares in the IPO, corresponding to a total application amount of NOK 1,000,000.

Board member Kari Grønås has submitted an application for 5,040 shares in the IPO, corresponding to a total application amount of NOK 157,500.

ABG Sundal Collier and DNB Markets (jointly the “Managers”), are acting as Joint Global Coordinators and Joint Bookrunners in the IPO. Advokatfirmaet Schjødt AS is acting as legal counsel to the Company. Advokatfirmaet Wiersholm AS is acting as legal counsel to the Managers.
