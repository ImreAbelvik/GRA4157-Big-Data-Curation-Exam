Oslo, 15 January 2021, Board member Ketil Fjerdingen and primary insider in Ultimovacs ASA, has today through Langøya Invest AS bought 7,000 shares in the company at a price of NOK 75.54 per share. Following this transaction, Ketil Fjerdingen through Langøya Invest AS holds 1,349,006 shares in Ultimovacs ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
