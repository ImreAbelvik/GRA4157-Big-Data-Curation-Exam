Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement published by SATS ASA
("SATS" or the "Company", OSE ticker "SATS") earlier today, on January 11,
2024, regarding the result of the share investment program for certain senior
executives and certain other key employees (the "Share Investment Program").

Certain primary insiders of the Company have applied for, and been allocated,
shares in the Share Investment Program. Parts of the investment made by one of
the primary insiders in the Share Investment Program will be debt financed
through a loan to be issued by the Company, subject to inter alia approval by
the general meeting expected to be held on or about 25 April 2024. See details
below and in the attached forms.

Karolina Gutke (Country Manager Sweden) has been allocated 111,423 shares in
the Company in the Share Investment Program.

Aleksi Virkkunen (Country Manager Finland) has been allocated 45,618 shares in
the Company in the Share Investment Program and has committed to pledge the
same number of shares in the Company.

The transactions are notifiable pursuant to Article 19 of the Market Abuse
Regulation.

The disclosures set out in this announcement are subject to the disclosure
requirement pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
