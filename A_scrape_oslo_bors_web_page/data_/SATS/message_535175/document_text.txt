Country Manager Sweden and Denmark and primary insider Linda-Li Cederroth has through her wholly owned company MM&I Holding AB disposed 8 000 shares in SATS ASA (the "Company") at a price of NOK 24.00. Following this transaction, Cederroth holds 35 320 shares in SATS ASA, in addition to the shares held by her in SATS Management Invest AS, which owns 8 651 488 shares in the Company, corresponding to approximately 5.0% of the shares in the Company.

SATS refers to the attached document for further details of the transaction.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation article 19.
