Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement published by SATS ASA
(the "Company", OSE ticker "SATS") earlier today, on 14 February 2025,
regarding the result of the share investment program for employees of the
Company and its subsidiaries and members of the Company's board of directors
(the "Share Investment Program").

Certain primary insiders of the Company have applied for, and been allocated,
shares in the Share Investment Program. Parts of the investment made by 14 of
the primary insiders in the Share Investment Program will be debt financed
through a loan to be issued by the Company, subject to inter alia approval by
the annual general meeting of the Company expected to be held on or about 28
April 2025. Furthermore, the allocation of shares to members of the board of
directors is also subject to approval by said annual general meeting. See
details below and in the attached forms.
 
- Mira Investment AS, a close associate of Sondre Gravir (CEO), has been
allocated 128,514 shares in the Company in the Share Investment Program.
Following this transaction, Gravir holds 1,326,237 shares in SATS ASA.

allocated 299,866 shares in the Company in the Share Investment Program.
Following this transaction, Elde holds 789,570 shares in SATS ASA.

will be liquidated within short, and certain primary insiders are reinvesting
their holdings in SATS ASA through their participation in the Share Investment
Program.
