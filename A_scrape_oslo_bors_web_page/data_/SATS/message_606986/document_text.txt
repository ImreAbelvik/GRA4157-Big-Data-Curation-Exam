The CEO of SATS ASA (the "Company"), Sondre Gravir, has reorganized his
indirect holding of shares in the Company by transferring certain shares
between his wholly-owned companies, Gravir Holding AS and Gravir Invest AS.
There has been no change in Gravir's indirect shareholding in the Company as a
result of the transactions.

Please see the enclosed transaction notification for further details of the
transactions.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article
19 of the Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act.
