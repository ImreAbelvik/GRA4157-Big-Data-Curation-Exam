Country Manager Sweden and Denmark and primary insider Linda-Li Cederroth has through her wholly owned company MM&I Holding AB disposed 12 000 shares in SATS ASA (the "Company") at a price of NOK 24.00. Following this transaction, Cederroth holds 43 320 shares in SATS ASA, in addition to the shares held by her in SATS Management Invest AS, which owns 8 651 488 shares in the Company, corresponding to approximately 5.0% of the shares in the Company.

SATS refers to the attached documents for further details of the transactions.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation article 19.
