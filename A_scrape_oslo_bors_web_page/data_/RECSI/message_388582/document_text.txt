


Tore Torvund, President and CEO of REC Silicon ASA ("the Company"), has on
November 4, 2015, bought 200,000 shares in the Company at NOK 1.588 per share.
Mr. Torvund is a primary insider of REC Silicon.

After the transaction Tore Torvund has a total holding of 731,486 shares in REC
Silicon ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
