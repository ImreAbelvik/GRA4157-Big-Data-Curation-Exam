Kagra Gruppen AS, a company controlled by Jens 
Ulltveit-Moe, has on 6 November bought 1.500.000 
shares in REC Silicon ASA (REC) at NOK 1.6128 per 
share. Mr. Ulltveit-Moe is Chairman of the Board of 
Directors of REC.

After the transaction Jens Ulltveit-Moe and associated 
companies have a total holding of 543.840.870 shares 
(including 53.861.032 shares on forward contracts) - 
equivalent to 23.50 % - in REC Silicon ASA.
