EK Kapital AS, a company controlled by Espen Klitzing, 
has on 8 February bought 250.000 shares in REC Silicon 
ASA (REC) at NOK 0.8248 per share. Mr. Klitzing is a 
member of the Board of Directors of REC.

After the transaction Espen Klitzing and associated 
companies have a total holding of 450.000 shares in 
REC Silicon ASA.
