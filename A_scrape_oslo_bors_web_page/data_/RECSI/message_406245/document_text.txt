


Tore Torvund, CEO of REC Silicon ASA ("the Company") has on July 21, 2016 bought
200,000 shares in the Company at NOK 1.528 per share.

Mr. Torvund is a primary insider of REC Silicon.

After the transaction, Tore Torvund has a total holding of 931,486 shares in REC
Silicon ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
