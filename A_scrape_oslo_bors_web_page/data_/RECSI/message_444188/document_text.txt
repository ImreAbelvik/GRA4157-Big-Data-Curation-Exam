


Francine Sullivan, Vice President Business Development of REC Silicon ASA ("the
Company") has on February 13, 2018 bought 32,000 shares in the Company at USD
0.148 per share.

Ms. Sullivan is a primary insider of REC Silicon.

After the transaction, Francine Sullivan has a total holding of 117,000 shares
in REC Silicon ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
