Kagra Gruppen AS, a company controlled by Jens 
Ulltveit-Moe, has on 8 November bought 25.000.000 
shares in REC Silicon ASA (REC) at NOK 0.8028 per 
share. Mr. Ulltveit-Moe is Chairman of the Board of 
Directors of REC.

After the transaction Jens Ulltveit-Moe and associated 
companies have a total holding of 622.701.902 shares 
(including 53.861.032 shares on forward contracts) - 
equivalent to 24.48 % - in REC Silicon ASA.
