


Tore Torvund (President and CEO), has on May 7, 2015 bought 100,000 shares in
REC Silicon ASA (REC) at NOK 1.86 per share. Mr. Torvund is a a primary insider
of the company.

After the transaction Tore Torvund has a total holding of 531,486 shares in REC
Silicon ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
