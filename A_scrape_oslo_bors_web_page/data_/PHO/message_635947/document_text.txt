Oslo, 5 January 2025: Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement made
by Photocure ASA ("Photocure" or the "Company") on 19 December 2024 of the
launch of Photocure's program to repurchase up to 300,000 of its own shares for
a total maximum amount of NOK 30 million during the period from 19 December
2024, until no later than 31 March 2025, pursuant to an agreement with DNB
Markets.
