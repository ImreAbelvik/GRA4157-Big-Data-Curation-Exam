Oslo, Norway, 6 December 2021: Reference is made to the stock exchange release
by Photocure ASA ("Photocure" or the "Company") published on 3 December 2021
regarding exercise of share options.

The price for option shares sold by the primary insider, Susanne Strauss, VP and
General Manager of Europe, has been determined at a price of NOK 94.2502 per
share.

The primary insider having exercised options has used the net proceeds from the
sale of option shares to purchase 1,392 shares in Photocure at a price per share
of NOK 94.2502.

Primary insider notification pursuant to the market abuse regulation article 19
is attached.
