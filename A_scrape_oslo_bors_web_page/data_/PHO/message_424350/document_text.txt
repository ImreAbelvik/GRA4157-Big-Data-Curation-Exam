


On 6 April 2017 Kjetil Hestdal, President and CEO of Photocure ASA, purchased
5 000 Photocure shares at a price of NOK 28.00 each.

Kjetil Hestdal now holds 133 873 shares and 92 500 options in Photocure.
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
