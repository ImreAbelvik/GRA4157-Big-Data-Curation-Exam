Oslo, Norway, 16 January 2025: Reference is made to the stock exchange
announcement made by Photocure ASA ("Photocure" or the "Company") on 19 December
2024 of the launch of Photocure's program to repurchase up to 300,000 of its own
shares for a total maximum amount of NOK 30 million during the period from 19
December 2024, until no later than 31 March 2025, pursuant to an agreement with
DNB Markets.

For the period from and including 19 December 2024, through 14 January 2025, the
Company purchased a total of 300,000 shares at an average price of NOK 60.41per
