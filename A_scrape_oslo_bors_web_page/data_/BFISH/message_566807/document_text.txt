About Biofish: 
Biofish is a land-based producer of smolt – juvenile fish destined for aquaculture facilities. The company’s production takes place in its production facilitiy in Kvam on the Norwegian west coast, using a Recirulating Aquaculture System (RAS), which recycles at least 99% of the water used. Biofish deliver smolt to salmon farms along the Norwegian coast.
