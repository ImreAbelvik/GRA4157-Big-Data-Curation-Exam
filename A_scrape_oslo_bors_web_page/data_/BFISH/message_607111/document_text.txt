Awilco AS has on 19 December 2023 acquired 2.862.177 shares in Biofish Holding AS at a price of NOK 1.39 per share. Awilco AS is a related party of Biofish Holding AS Board member Jens-Julius R. Nygaard. 

Awilco AS controls a total number of 15,281,946 shares after the purchase, representing 17.30% ownership in Biofish Holding AS.
