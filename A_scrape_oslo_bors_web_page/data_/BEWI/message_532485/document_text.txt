Reference is made to the successfully completed private placement of shares in
BEWI ASA, as announced in the stock exchange announcements earlier today, 6 May
2021 (the "Private Placement").

Frøya Invest AS, related party to Christian Bekken, CEO and defined as a person
discharging managerial responsibilities (PDMR) of BEWI ASA, has today been
allocated 2,393,232 shares at NOK 28.30 per share in the Private Placement.

Following the Private Placement, Frøya Invest AS will hold 84,501,058 shares in
BEWI ASA, equal to approximately 54,35 per cent of the outstanding shares
following the issue of the new shares in the Private Placement.

Verdane SKULD 1 AS, related party to Per Rosenmüller Nordlander, board member
and defined as a person discharging managerial responsibilities (PDMR) of BEWI
ASA, has today sold 836,042 shares at NOK 28.30 per share in the Private
Placement.

Verdane ETF III SPV K/S, related party to Per Rosenmüller Nordlander, board
member and defined as a person discharging managerial responsibilities (PDMR) of
BEWI ASA, has today sold 930,742 shares at NOK 28.30 per share in the Private
Placement.

Verdane SKULD 1 AS and Verdane ETF III K/S are both managed by Verdane Capital.
The combined holding of the two funds has been reduced from 22,500,000 shares,
representing 15.16 per cent of the outstanding shares, to 20,733,216,
representing 13.34 per cent of the shares following the issue and sale of the
new shares in the Private Placement. As such, the sale of shares and issuance of
new shares in the Private Placement has led to a crossing of the threshold of 15
per cent, with reference to the Securities Trading Act section 4-2.

About BEWI ASA

BEWI is an international provider of packaging, components, and insulation
solutions. The company's commitment to sustainability is integrated throughout
the value chain, from production of raw materials and end goods, to recycling of
used products. With a vision to protect people and goods for a better every day,
BEWI is leading the change towards a circular economy.

BEWI ASA is listed at the Oslo Børs under ticker BEWI.
