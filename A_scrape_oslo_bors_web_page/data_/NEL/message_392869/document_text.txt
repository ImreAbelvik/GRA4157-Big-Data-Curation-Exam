Simonsen Invest AS, a company controlled by Mr. Bjørn 
Simonsen, Director - Market Development and Public 
Relations of NEL ASA has on 12. January 2016 purchased 
40 000 shares in NEL ASA at NOK 3,56 per share.

After the transaction Bjørn Simonsen and associated 
companies have a holding of 1 705 769 shares in NEL 
ASA, representing 0,25% (based on 680 601 326 
outstanding shares).
