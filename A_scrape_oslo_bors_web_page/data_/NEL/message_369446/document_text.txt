Strata Marine & Offshore AS, a company controlled by 
Mr. Øystein Stray Spetalen, board member of NEL ASA, 
has on 19 January 2015 purchased 164 754 shares at 
NOK 1.32 per share.

After the transaction Øystein Stray Spetalen and 
associated companies have a holding of 75 066 362 
shares in NEL ASA, representing 18.8% (based on 398 
929 104 outstanding shares).
