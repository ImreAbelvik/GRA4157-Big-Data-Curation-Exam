Strata Marine & Offshore AS, Ferncliff Maris AS and AS 
Ferncliff, all companies controlled by board member Øystein 
Stray Spetalen, has on 6 March sold in total 36 367 842 
shares in NEL ASA at NOK 2.55 per share.

After the transactions Øystein Stray Spetalen and associated 
companies have no holding of the company.
