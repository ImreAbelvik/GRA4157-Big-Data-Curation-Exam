Reference is made to the notice from Nel ASA 14 
August 2015.

Strata Marine & Offshore AS, a company controlled by 
Mr. Øystein Stray Spetalen, board member of Nel ASA, 
have been allocated 1 000 000 shares at NOK 2.25 per 
share in the private placement.

After the transactions Øystein Stray Spetalen and 
associated companies have a holding of 74 211 285 
shares in Nel ASA, representing 11.4 % (based on 650 
601 326 outstanding shares).
