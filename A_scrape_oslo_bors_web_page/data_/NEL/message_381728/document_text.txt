Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement 
made earlier today regarding the final result and 
allocation in the subsequent offering in NEL ASA 
(the "Company"). Primary insiders in the Company have 
today been allocated shares in connection with the 
subsequent offering as follows:

ELMO Holding AS, a company controlled by Lars Markus 
Solheim, Erik Evju, Ole Arnt Lindgren and Magne 
Myrhaug, all employees of NEL ASA, have been 
allocated 2,615,385 shares. ELMO Holding AS has 
following this a shareholding of 62,000,000 in the 
Company.

Ann Marie Gohli Russel, member of the Board, has been 
allocated 50,000 shares. Ann Marie Gohli Russel has 
following this a shareholding of 376,923 in the 
Company.

Bjørn Simonsen, Director - Market Development and 
Public Relations, has been allocated 325 000 shares 
through the company Simonsen Invest AS. Bjørn 
Simonsen has following this a shareholding of 
1,665,769 in the Company through Simonsen Invest AS.

All shares were allocated at the subscription price 
in the subsequent offering, being NOK 1.35.

This information is subject to the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the 
Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
