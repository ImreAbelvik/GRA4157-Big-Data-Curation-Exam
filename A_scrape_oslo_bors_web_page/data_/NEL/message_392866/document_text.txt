Ludens AS, a company controlled by Mr. Jon Andrè 
Løkke, CEO of NEL ASA, has on 12. January 2016 
purchased 142 000 shares in NEL ASA at NOK 3,53 per 
share.

After the transaction Jon Andrè Løkke and associated 
companies have a holding of 142 000 shares in NEL ASA, 
representing 0.02% (based on 680 601 326 outstanding 
shares).
