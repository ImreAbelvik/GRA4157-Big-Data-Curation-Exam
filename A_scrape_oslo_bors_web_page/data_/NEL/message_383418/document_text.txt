Dallas Asset Management AS, a company controlled by 
Mr. Jan Christian Opsahl, board member of Nel ASA, 
have been allocated 500 000 shares at NOK 2.25 per 
share in the private placement.

After the transactions Jan Christian Opsahl and 
associated companies have a holding of 25 016 292 
shares in Nel ASA, representing 3.8 % (based on 650 
601 326 outstanding shares).
