CEO of Olav Thon Eiendomsselskap ASA, Dag Tangevald-Jensen, has on August 23 2018  purchased 1,000 shares in Olav Thon Eiendomsselskap ASA at a price of NOK 149.60 per share. 

After the purchase, Tangevald-Jensen holds 2,300 shares in Olav Thon Eiendomsselskap ASA. This information is subject of the disclosure requirements to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading act.
