Luxembourg - 3 October 2022 - Subsea 7 S.A. (Oslo Børs: SUBC, ADR: SUBCY)
announces mandatory notification of the following share transaction by a primary
insider.

Siem Industries S.A., which is represented on the board of Subsea 7 S.A. by
Kristian Siem, purchased 380,539 shares in Subsea 7 S.A. (OSE symbol SUBC), at a
price of NOK86.50 per share, on 30 September 2022. Following this transaction,
the aggregate number of shares held by Siem Industries S.A. in Subsea 7 S.A. is
70,329,916.
