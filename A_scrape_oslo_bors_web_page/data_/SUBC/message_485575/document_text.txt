Luxembourg  - 19 September  2019 - Subsea  7 S.A. (Oslo  Børs: SUBC, ADR: SUBCY)
(the  "Company") in  accordance with  the Oslo  Børs rules,  today announces the
details  of awards under its 2018 Long-Term Incentive Plan (the "Plan") approved
by the Board on 18 September 2019.

The  Company will  make awards  of 1,387,000 performance  shares, subject to the
Plan's performance conditions, on 19 September 2019.
