Oslo, 30 June 2020:

The NEXT executive CTO/COO Dan Cronin have subscribed for 369,000 shares in the Company at a subscription price of NOK 2.59 per share. This instead of receiving an awarded bonus payment during late 2020. Following completion of the issuance of the shares, Dan Cronin will hold 422,643 shares in the Company. Dan Cronin also holds in total 281,500 share options.

The board of directors of NEXT Biometrics Group ASA ("NEXT") has issued 369,000 new shares in the Company, each with a par value of NOK 1.00, in accordance with the boards authorisation to issue shares granted by the general meeting. The shares have been issued to Dan Cronin (primary insider) as a settlement of earlier agreed bonus obligations.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements set out in section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
