Eurostores AS, a company wholly owned by NEXT Biometrics Group ASA's ("NEXT") 
CEO Tore Etholm-Idsøe, has exercised a total of 118,000 share options granted in 
2004/2005 at a strike price of NOK 5.08 per share option.

Furthermore, Eurostores AS has extended its previously announced forward contract 
with DNB Bank ASA published 27 January 2016 with 6 months. Pursuant to the 
announced forward contract, Eurostores AS has extended the January 2016 contract at 
a share price of NOK 107.08 and will buy the same 60,000 shares at NOK 108.85 on 28 
February 2017.
  
Following issuance of the new shares acquired in connection with the exercise of share 
options, Tore Etholm-Idsøe together with Eurostores AS will own a total of  528,458 
shares in NEXT. In addition, Tore Etholm-Idsøe and Eurostores AS holds in total 278,000 
share options each giving the right to receive one share. In total, Tore Etholm-Idsøe and 
Eurostores AS control 806,458 shares and share options.

About NEXT Biometrics
Enabled by the Active Thermal Principle, NEXT offers rigid and flexible, high 
performance, low cost fingerprint sensors. The Company is uniquely positioned in 
markets demanding high performance for close to 100% its targeted populations. The 
company competitive advantages are maximized in a range of product formats including 
SmartCards, Key-fobs, Remote Controls, Door systems and high end Notebook and 
Tablets.

NEXT Biometrics Group ASA is a publicly listed company headquartered in Oslo, Norway 
and with sales, support and development subsidiaries in Seattle, Silicon Valley, Taipei, 
Prague and Shanghai.
