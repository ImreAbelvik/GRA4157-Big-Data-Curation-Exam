Reference is made to the annual general meeting held 16 May 2014 in which the general 
meeting of NEXT Biometrics Group ASA ("NEXT Biometrics" or the "Company") 
authorized the board of directors to increase the share capital of the Company in 
connection with the Company's share option program.  

Pursuant to the Company's share option program, a total of 62,100 share options have 
been exercised at an average strike price of NOK 35.16 per share. Consequently, the 
board of directors has resolved to increase the share capital by issuance of 62,100 new 
shares.

Following this share capital increase, the share capital of the Company is NOK 
13,473,515 divided by 13,473,515 shares each with a nominal value of NOK 1.

The board of directors has resolved to grant a total of 74,000 new share options, in 
accordance with the Company's share option program me. Each share option gives the 
right to subscribe for and be allotted one (1) share in the Company. The share options 
vest over a period of two years. The options are exercisable at any point in time after 
vesting up until 31 December 2018.

The following primary insider has been granted share options:
Radek Matyasek (VP of sales EMEA) was granted 10,000 options. After the grant, Radek
Matyasek holds 30,000 options and 1,000 shares.

After this option exercise and grant of new options, the total number of options 
outstanding are 1,351,376 and the remaining amount under the board authorisation is 
NOK 1,200,900.

About NEXT Biometrics
Enabled by its patented Active Thermal Principle, NEXT offers high quality area 
fingerprint sensors at a fraction of the prices of comparable competitors. A wide range 
of product formats including Smartphones, Tablets, PCs, Doors, Time registration 
systems, wearables, Payment terminals, Flas drives, Usb-tokens, Key fobs and many 
more are target.

NEXT Biometrics Group ASA is a publicly listed company headquartered in Oslo, Norway 
and with sales, support and development subsidiaries in Seattle, Silicon Valley, Taipei, 
Prague and Shanghai.
