Greenbridge Investment Partners Ltd("Greenbridge") has today purchased 200,000 shares in Next Biometrics Group ASA at a price of NOK 18.92 per share from Eurostores AS, a company wholly owned by Tore Etholm-Idsøe.

These shares represent approx. 23% of the shares and options in NEXT Biometrics Group directly and indirectly owned by Tore Etholm-Idsøe. 

Greenbridge is represented on the Board of Directors of Next Biometrics Group ASA by board member Emanuel Lang. 
Following this transaction, Greenbridge holds 3,653,614 shares 18.8% in Next Biometrics Group ASA.
