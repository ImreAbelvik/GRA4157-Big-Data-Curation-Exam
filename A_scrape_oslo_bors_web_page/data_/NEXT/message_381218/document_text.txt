Radek Matyasek, VP Sales EMEA and Primary Insider of NEXT Biometrics Group 
ASA has on 26 June 2015 acquired 1,000 shares in the Company at a price of NOK 
39.00 per share. Following this transaction, Radek Matyasek owns 1,000 shares and 
20,000 options in NEXT.
 
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 
of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

About NEXT Biometrics
Enabled by its patented Active Thermal Principle, NEXT offers high quality area 
fingerprint sensor at a fraction of the prices of comparable competitors. A wide 
range of product formats including Smartphones, Tablets, PC's, Doors, Time 
registration systems, Wearables, Payment terminals, Flashdrives, Usb-tokens, Key 
fobs and many more are targeted. 

NEXT BIOMETRICS GROUP ASA is a publicly listed company headquartered in 
Oslo, Norway, with sales, support and development subsidiaries in Seattle, Prague, 
Taipei and Shanghai.
