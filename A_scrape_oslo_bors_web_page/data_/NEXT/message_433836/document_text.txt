Eurostores AS, a company wholly owned by NEXT Biometrics Group ASA's ("NEXT") Chairman of 
the Board Tore Etholm-Idsøe has extended its previously announced forward contract with DNB 
Bank ASA, published originally 27 January 2016, and renewed in August 2016 and March 2017 with 
6 months. 

Pursuant to the announced forward contract, Eurostores AS has extended the March 2017 contract 
at a share price of NOK 110.63 and will buy the same 60,000 shares at NOK 112.26 on 1 March 
2018.  Tore Etholm-Idsøe together with Eurostores AS will own a total of 528,458 shares in NEXT. 
In addition, Tore Etholm-Idsøe and Eurostores AS holds in total 278,000 unconditional share 
options and 25,000 conditional share options each giving the right to receive one share.
