GEORGE TOWN, GRAND CAYMAN, 10 December 2015 - SIEM 
INDUSTRIES INC. announces that its wholly-owned 
subsidiary, Siem Investments Inc., has today acquired 
50,000,000 Subscription Rights for new shares of 
Electromagnetic Geoservices ASA (OSE Symbol: EMGS), a 
publicly-traded Norwegian company listed on the Oslo 
Stock Exchange, in the market at NOK0.01 per 
Subscription Right and will subscribe for 50,000,000 
new shares at the NOK0.25 per share Subscription Price 
under the EMGS Rights Issue.

Following the completion of the EMGS Rights Issue in 
the latter part of December 2015, Siem Investments' 
beneficial ownership in EMGS will be 220,549,724 
shares of issued and outstanding shares, or 16.8%, and 
is comprised of the 22,926,866 shares of EMGS' issued 
and outstanding shares that are currently owned and 
197,622,858 new shares of EMGS that were subscribed 
using Subscription Rights. Siem Investments was 
granted 127,622,858 Subscription Rights at the rate of 
5.56652 Subscription Rights for each owned share of 
EMGS and purchased 70,000,000 Subscription Rights in 
the market.
