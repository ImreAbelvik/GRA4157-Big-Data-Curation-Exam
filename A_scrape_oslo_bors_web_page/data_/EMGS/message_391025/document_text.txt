Laburnum AS a company owned by the chairman of 
Electromagnetic Geoservices ASA, Eystein Eriksrud has 
today subscribed to 1 million shares in the rights 
issue in EMGS ASA at a subscription price of NOK 0,25 
per share.

Before allocation Laburnum AS owns 120,485 shares and 
670,682 subscription rights in EMGS ASA.
