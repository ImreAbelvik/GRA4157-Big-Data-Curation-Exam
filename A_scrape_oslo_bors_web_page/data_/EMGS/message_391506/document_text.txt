


The following primary insiders of Electromagnetic Geoservices ASA (EMGS) have,
on 14 December 2015, exercised Rights to subscribe for shares at NOK 0.25 per
share in the ongoing Rights Issue as follows:

Stig Eide Sivertsen, CEO, has subscribed for 79,044 new shares and has further
applied for subscription (without subscription rights) for additional 3,000,000
new shares. The shares are owned by, and subscribed through, Sivertsens wholly
owned company Theoline AS. The final ownership will be announced after the
allocation of shares.

David Neser, COO/CTO, has subscribed for 367,390 new shares. Prior to this
transaction, Neser held 66,000 shares in EMGS. After completion of the Right
Issue, he will own 433,390 shares and 625,000 options in EMGS.

Charlotte Knudsen, Head of Investor Relations, has subscribed for 75,000 new
shares. Following this transaction and after completion of the Rights
Issue, Knudsen will own 75,000 shares and 25,000 options in EMGS.

Ole Martin Pedersen, Board member, has  subscribed for 94,630 new shares. Prior
to this transaction, Pedersen held 17,000 shares in EMGS. After completion of
the Right Issue, he will own 111,630 shares and 38,000 options in EMGS.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
