


Anette Mellbye, Chief Legal Counsel and primary insider in Electromagnetic
Geoservices ASA (EMGS) has, on 5 July 2016, bought 953 EMGS shares at an average
price of NOK 6.97 per share. This equals the number of shares used by EMGS to
execute the reverse share split completed today to which Mellbye had agreed for
the use of her shares.

After this purchase, Anette Mellbye owns the same relative number of shares as
before the reverse share split. Anette Mellbye owns 3,471 shares and 9,500
options in EMGS.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
