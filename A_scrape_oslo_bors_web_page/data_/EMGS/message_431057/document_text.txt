


This is a correction of the announcement from 07.07.2017 10:28 CET.

The following primary insiders of Electromagnetic Geoservices ASA (EMGS) have,
on 7 July 2017, been allocated the following number of shares at NOK 2.45 per
share in the Rights Issue:

Siem Investments Inc. (Siem) has been allocated 14,025,201 shares. After this
transaction Siem will own 21,869,450 shares, representing 23.9% of the shares in
EMGS

Perestroika AS has been allocated 13,566,990 shares. After this transaction
Perestroika AS will own 20,560,847 shares, representing 22.5% of the shares in
EMGS

RWC European Focus Master Inc. (RWC) has been allocated 13,566,990 shares. After
this transaction RWC will own 18,074,938 shares, representing 19.8% of the
shares in EMGS.

Laburnum AS, a company owned by the chairman Eystein Eriksrud, has been
allocated 150,000 shares. After this transaction Laburnum AS will own 178,013
shares, representing 0.2% of the shares in EMGS.

MKB Invest AS, a company owned by board member Mimi K. Berdal, has been
allocated 26,819 shares. After this transaction MKB Invest AS will own 41,819
shares, representing 0.05% of the shares in EMGS.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
