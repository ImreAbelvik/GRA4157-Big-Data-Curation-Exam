PRIMARY INSIDER NOTIFICATION:

Reference is made to the stock exchange notifications published by
Electromagnetic Geoservices ASA ("EMGS" or the "Company") pertaining to the
Comprehensive Refinancing, including the stock exchange notifications published
on 16 April 2018 and 19 April 2018, and to the stock exchange notification
published by the Company today, 7 May 2018, announcing the completion of the
Rights Issue and the Bond Issue.

As the Bond Issue was not fully subscribed, and in accordance with their
respective obligations under the Underwriting Agreements, additional allocations
under the Bond Issue have been made to the following two primary insiders on 7
May 2018:

1. An additional allocation of 49,060 bonds have been allocated to Siem
Investments Inc. ("Siem Investments"). Following this, a total of 126,800 bonds
(equal to USD 12,680,000) have been allocated to Siem Investments under the Bond
Issue.

2. An additional allocation of 53,700 bonds have been allocated to Perestroika
AS ("Perestroika"). Following this, a total of 126,800 bonds (equal to USD
12,680,000) have been allocated to Perestroika under the Bond Issue.
