



Lodve Berre, Deputy Board member and primary insider in Electromagnetic
Geoservices ASA (EMGS) has, on 10(th) December 2015, used his allocated
subscription rights to subscribe for 11,517 new shares in the ongoing Rights
Issue in EMGS at NOK 0.25 per share. Prior to this transaction, Berre held
2,069 shares in EMGS.

After completion of the Rights Issue, Lodve Berre will own shares 13,586 and
15,500 options in EMGS.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
