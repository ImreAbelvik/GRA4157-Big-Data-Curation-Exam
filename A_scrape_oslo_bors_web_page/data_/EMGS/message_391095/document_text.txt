


RWC Asset Management LLP, as discretionary investment manager of the RWC
European Focus Master Inc. (RWC), has bought 5,000,000 subscription rights in
Electromagnetic Geoservices ASA (EMGS) at an average price of NOK 0.01 per
subscription right.

After the transaction, RWC holds 128,404,788 subscription rights, of which
103,404,788 have been exercised, and holds 22,713,696 of the issued shares in
EMGS.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
