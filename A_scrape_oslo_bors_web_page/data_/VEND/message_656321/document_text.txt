With reference to the message published today regarding the registration of new share capital, Arribatec Group ASA announces the following warrant exercise:

Håkon Reistad Fure, Chairman and primary insider, through Company One AS, has exercised 24 999 994 warrants

Through this warrant exercise Company One AS now holds 59 693 507 shares in Arribatec Group ASA. His new total shareholding represents 9.2 % of the share capital.
