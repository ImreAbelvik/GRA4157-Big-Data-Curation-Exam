Martin Mæland, Chair of the Board of NRC Group ASA, has on 4 June 2025 purchased
120,000 shares in NRC Group ASA at an average price of NOK 6 per share. Prior to
this purchase, he owned 100,000 shares through his wholly owned company, Brimar
AS.

Following the transaction, Martin Mæland holds a total of 220,000 shares in NRC
Group ASA.

Please refer to the attached notification of trading for further details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the
Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the
Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
