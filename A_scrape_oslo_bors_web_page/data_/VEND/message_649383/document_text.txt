17 June 2025: Reference is made to the announcement on 29 April 2025 regarding an unconditional mandatory cash offer (the "Mandatory Offer") made by Electric AS (the "Offeror") to acquire all outstanding shares in Edda Wind ASA (the "Company" or "Edda Wind"), and the announcement on 27 May 2025 regarding approval of the offer document (the "Offer Document") and commencement of the offer period for the Mandatory Offer. 

The following primary insiders and close associates of primary insiders have on 17 June 2025 accepted the Mandatory Offer against a cash consideration of NOK 23.00 per share:

•	Toril Eidesvik, member of the board of the Company, 21,680 shares
•	Zahlahuset AS, a close associate of Toril Eidesvik, member of the board of the Company, 15,000 shares
•	Kold Invest AS, a close associate of Martha Kold Monclair, member of the board of the Company, 27,820 shares
	

Please see attached PDMR forms.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in the EU Regulation 596/2014 (MAR) Article 19 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act Section 5-12.
