The Tinius Trust, through Blommenholm Industrier AS, has on 13 August 2019
acquired 46 647 B-shares in Schibsted ASA (the "Company"), at a price of NOK
229.3474 per share. After this transaction Blommenholm Industrier owns
28,188,589 A-shares and 30,621,205 B-shares in the Company.
The Tinius Trust and Blommenholm Industrier AS is represented in the board of
the Company by Ole Jacob Sunde.

Oslo, 14 August 2019

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
