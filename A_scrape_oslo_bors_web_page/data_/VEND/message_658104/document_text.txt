Øivind Horpestad, CEO of Inin Group AS, has through his private account and trough the company Gimle Invest AS on October 27, bought a total of 130,000 shares in Inin Group AS at an average price of NOK 2.53 per share. Following the transaction Øivind Horpestad and related parties’ control 210 700 shares in Inin Group AS.
