Ljones, 4 June 2025: Odfjell Land AS has on 3 June 2025 acquired in total
1,392,491 shares in Biofish Holding AS at a price of NOK 1,70 per share.
Odfjell Land AS is a related party of primary insider Kjetil Grønskag. After
this transaction, Odfjell Land AS owns 15,361,970 shares and 2,400,000
warrants in Biofish Holding AS.

This information is subject to disclosure under the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act, §5-12. The information was submitted for publication at
2025-06-04 07:30 CEST.
