Oslo, 10th June 2025. Auris AS (Buyer) - a company associated with Henrik Austgulen, board member and primary insider in Baltic Sea Properties AS (BALT) - has acquired 100,000 shares in Baltic Sea Properties AS (BALT) at a price per share of NOK 51.25. 

Auris AS owns upon completion of the transaction, 362,384 shares, equal to 4.17 %, in Baltic Sea Properties AS.
