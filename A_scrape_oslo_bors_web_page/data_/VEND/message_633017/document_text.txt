22.11.2024 15:50:07 CET | Schibsted | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

Today, primary insiders in Schibsted ASA have been granted 3 554* B-shares in
the form of restricted stock units (RSUs) in connection with the long-term
incentive plan (the "Schibsted LTI Plan"). The shares will be transferred to the
employees in three tranches over a 3-year vesting period.



Please see the attached form(s) for notification and public disclosure of
transactions, including net number* of RSUs to primary insiders.



* Net number of B-shares to be received following the vesting date. The net
number of RSUs is based on the participant's current tax rate. The actual number
of shares received at the vesting date may vary, should the participant's margin
tax rate at the vesting date differ. 



Oslo, 22 November 2024

SCHIBSTED ASA
