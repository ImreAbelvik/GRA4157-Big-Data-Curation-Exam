London, UK – May 23, 2025

Cool Company Ltd. (the “Company”; NYSE:CLCO / CLCO.OL) informs that EPS Ventures Ltd., has on May 23, 2025 acquired 100,000 shares in the Company from Langebru AS, at a price of 61.50 NOK per share. Following the transaction, EPS Ventures Ltd. holds a total of 31,354,390 shares in the Company, equivalent to 59.1% of the Company’s shares.

EPS Ventures Ltd. is a close associate of Cyril Ducau, Chair of the Board of the Company, and Joanna Zhou, Director of the Company. Langebru AS is a close associate of Peter Anker, Director of the Company. 

Please see attached notification form in accordance with the Market Abuse Regulation article 19.

This announcement is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act and the requirements under the EU Market Abuse Regulation.
