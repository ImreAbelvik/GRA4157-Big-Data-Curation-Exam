Oslo, 24 June 2025. Reference is made to SoftwareOne Holding AG’s ("SoftwareOne") recommended voluntary share and cash offer for all issued and outstanding shares in Crayon Group Holding ASA ("Crayon" or the "Company"), (the "Offer"), as further set out in the combined offer document and prospectus (the "Prospectus") for the Offer and secondary listing of SoftwareOne's shares on Euronext Oslo Børs, dated 14 March 2025. 

Reference is further made to the announcement published on 10 June 2025 regarding fulfilment of the closing conditions and issuance of the settlement notification under the Offer.

As a part of the settlement and as agreed in the transaction agreement with SoftwareOne, Crayon has resolved to terminate outstanding options and incentive programs, including for members of the senior management, with cash remuneration. The incentive programs for the below primary insiders and their close associates have been terminated and cash-settled: 

-	Gudmundur Adalsteinsson, Chief Sales Officer
-	Brede Huser, Chief Financial Officer
-	Bente Liberg, CHRO
-	Kyle Stephen Mulholland
-	Melissa Mulholland, Chief Executive Officer
-	Jon Birger Syvertsen, Chief Operating Officer
-	Mette Wam, employee representative
-	Jens Rugseth, board member 
-	Lars Hatlen Larhammer, employee representative 
-	Grethe Helene Viksaas, board member
-	Timothy Jay Herland, employee representative 

Please refer to the attached notifications of the transactions for further details.
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act, EU Market Abuse Regulation Article 19 and Euronext Oslo Rule Book II.
