DOF Group ASA ("DOF") has been informed that Svein Harald Øygard, Chair of the
Board, has purchased 43,974 shares in DOF as further specified in the attached
notification form. Following the transaction, Svein Harald Øygard holds,
directly and indirectly through a wholly owned company, 760,000 shares in DOF.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Article
19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act.
