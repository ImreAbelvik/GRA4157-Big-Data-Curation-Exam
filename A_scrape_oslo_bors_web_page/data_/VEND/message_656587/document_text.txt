Yokohama/Bergen, 2 October 2025: Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement published by Proximar Seafood AS (the "Company") on 22 September 2025, regarding the commencement of the subscription period in the fully underwritten rights issue of 150,000,000 new shares in the Company, at a subscription price of NOK 1 per share (the "Rights Issue") and the receipt of subscription rights in the Rights Issue by certain primary insiders, and certain close associates of primary insiders, of the Company.

The Company has been informed that certain primary insiders and certain close associates of primary insiders have subscribed for new shares in the Rights Issue:

- Dharma Rajeswaran, COO of the Company, has subscribed for 60,000 new shares. 
- Viggo Halseth, member of the Board of Directors of the Company, has subscribed for 118,300 new shares.

Please see the attached forms for further details about the transactions.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article 19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
