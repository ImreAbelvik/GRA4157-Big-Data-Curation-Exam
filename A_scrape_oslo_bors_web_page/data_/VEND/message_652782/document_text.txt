On August 13, 2025, employees in Nordic Semiconductor ASA and subsidiaries of Nordic Semiconductor ASA have exercised 1,325,582 restricted share units (“RSUs”) and Performance Shares (“PSUs”) in Nordic Semiconductor ASA.
 
All employee plan 

The vested RSUs and PSUs were granted to all employees on May 6, 2022, July 12, 2023, and March 20, 2024, as part of the long-term incentive program approved by the Annual general meeting held on April 28, 2022 and April 20, 2023. The Board of Directors has authorized the use of treasury shares to settle the vested shares. 

Executive Management Plan 

The vested RSUs were granted to the executive management team May 6, 2022, as part of the long-term incentive program approved by the Annual general meeting held on April 28, 2022. The executive management team was also granted Performance Shares. However, as the executives achieved less than 80% of the set performance targets, no performance shares vested. The Board of Directors has authorized the use of treasury shares to settle the vested shares. 

On August 13, 2025, the primary insiders mentioned below ordered for a portion of the received shares to be immediately sold by a third party in order to cover the tax liability.
 
SVP BU Wi-Fi, Joakim Ferm, has transferred the rights for 1,024 shares. 

EVP Sales and Marketing, Geir Langeland, has transferred the rights for 1,690 shares. 

EVP Supply Chain, Ole Fredrik Morken, has transferred the rights for 1,215 shares. 

SVP Quality, Ola Boström, has transferred the rights for 250 shares. 

SVP People & Culture, Sonja Kusmin, has transferred the rights for 2,202 shares.

SVP Investor Relations, Ståle Ytterdal, has transferred the rights for 1,044 shares. 

Employee elected board member, Anja Dekens, has transferred the rights for 861 shares.

Employee elected board member, Jon Helge Nistad, has transferred the rights for 561 shares.

Employee elected board member, Monika Lie Larsen, has transferred the rights for 613 shares.

On August 13, 2025, employees excluding primary insiders mentioned above, ordered for 698,908 shares to be immediately sold by a third party. In total 1,325,582 treasury shares will be used, of which 708,368 shares will be sold and 617,214 shares will be transferred to employees. The sales amount will be finally determined on the basis of the price subsequently obtained by the third party in the market and will be announced in a separate stock exchange notice when known. All expenses related to the vested RSUs and PSUs have already been recognized in this and previous quarters, and the settlement has no net impact on the operating expenses of the company in the future quarters. 

Subsequent to the transaction, Nordic Semiconductor holds 345,791 treasury shares in addition to 69,520 shares set aside for shareholders of Mobile Semiconductor Inc and 1,003,634 shares held for the founders of Memfault Inc. There will be no share capital increase to cover the exercise of RSUs and PSUs.
