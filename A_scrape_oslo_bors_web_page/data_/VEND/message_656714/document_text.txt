Yokohama/Bergen, 6 October 2025: Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement published by Proximar Seafood AS (the "Company") on 22 September 2025, regarding the commencement of the subscription period in the fully underwritten rights issue of 150,000,000 new shares in the Company, at a subscription price of NOK 1 per share (the "Rights Issue") and the receipt of subscription rights in the Rights Issue by certain primary insiders, and certain close associates of primary insiders, of the Company.

The Company has been informed that a certain close associate of a primary insider has subscribed for new shares in the Rights Issue:

- Loyden AS, a company closely associated to Joachim Nielsen, CEO of the Company, has subscribed for 145,000 new shares. 

Please see the attached form for further details about the transaction.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to article 19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
