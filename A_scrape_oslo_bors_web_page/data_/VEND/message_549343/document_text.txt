Schibsted's employee share saving plan (ESSP) continues in 2022 with saving in
the Schibsted B-share.

Attached forms show primary insiders that have subscribed to the ESSP for 2022.
Primary insiders can only change their savings amount once a year. The purchase
price and the number of shares will be reported when allocations are made.

Please see the attached form(s) for notification and public disclosure of
transactions.

For more information on the employee share saving plan please refer to the
disclosure published 09 April 2014.
