Schibsted has on 25 September 2019 purchased 35,000 own A shares at an average
price of NOK 269.6893 per share and 23,000 own B shares at an average price of
NOK 256,0220 on the Oslo Stock Exchange. After these transactions Schibsted owns
a total of 1,757,267 own A-shares and 660,857 own B-shares.


The transaction is part of the buyback programme that Schibsted announced 16
July 2019, stating an ambition to buy back up to 2% of the Company's shares
during Q3 2019.
