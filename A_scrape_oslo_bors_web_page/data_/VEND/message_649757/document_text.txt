June 20, 2025 - Aker Solutions ASA ("Aker Solutions") has today allocated shares
to primary insiders participating in the company's employee share purchase
program for 2025.

Reference is made to the stock exchange release published earlier today, June
20, 2025, concerning the employee share purchase program and share allocation.
For the June 2025 allocation, shares were sold at a price per share, excluding
applicable discount, of NOK 36.526 which equals the volume-weighted average
share price of Aker Solutions on Euronext Oslo Børs from and including June 13,
2025, to and including June 19, 2025.

Under the June 2025 allocation, a total of 410 shares were allocated to Sturla
Magnus, Executive Vice President, Newbuild. Following the allocation, Magnus
holds 244,239 shares in Aker Solutions.

Under the June 2025 allocation, a total of 410 shares were allocated to Hilde
Karlsen, Employee Elected Director. Following the allocation, Karlsen, together
with related parties, hold 32,897 shares in Aker Solutions.

Under the June 2025 allocation, a total of 410 shares were allocated to Rolf
Arne Grønning, Deputy Employee Elected Director. Following the allocation,
Grønning holds 32,533 shares in Aker Solutions.

Please see the attached notification for persons discharging managerial
responsibilities in Aker Solutions in accordance with Regulation EU 596/2014
(MAR) article 19.
