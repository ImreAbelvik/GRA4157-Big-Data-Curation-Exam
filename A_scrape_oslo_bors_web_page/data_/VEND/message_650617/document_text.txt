
Oslo, Norway, 3 July 2025

Zalaris ASA (“Zalaris” or the “Company”) has on 2 July granted 698,000 share options to employees at a strike price of NOK 90.80 per share. The options vest after 36 months. Each share option corresponds to one share. Please refer to the latest annual report for further details regarding the Company’s share incentive plans.  

The following primary insiders of Zalaris have been allocated share options as part of the grant: 
 
•	Hans-Petter Mellerud, CEO: 100,000 options   
•	Hilde Karlsmyr, CHRO: 50,000 options  
•	Halvor Leirvåg, CTO: 50,000 options  
•	Øyvind Reiten, EVP Commercial & Sales: 50,000 options  
•	Richard Schiørn, EVP Solution & Delivery: 50,000 options 
•	Gunnar Manum, CFO: 50,000 options
