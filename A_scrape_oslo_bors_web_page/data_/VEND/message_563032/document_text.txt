23.5.2022 14:22:03 CEST | Schibsted | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

As part of the Employee Share Saving Plan, primary insiders in Schibsted ASA
have on 23 May 2022 purchased 813 treasury B-shares at a price of NOK 171.10 per
B-share (the price is set according to standard procedure for the programme
during a two day period subsequent to the release of Schibsted's Q1 2022
results, the closing price on 5 May 2022). The transaction is related to the
first enrollment window in the Employee Share Saving Plan for 2022 which closed
in December 2021, and based on savings made during January, February and March
2022.

In addition, primary insiders in Schibsted ASA received 14 treasury B-shares on
23 May 2022. The transaction is related to bonus matching shares given to
employees who enrolled in the Employee Share Saving Plan for 2020.

For more information on the Employee Share Saving Plan please refer to the
disclosure published 9 April 2014.

Please see the attached form(s) for notification and public disclosure of
transactions.

Oslo, 23 May 2022
SCHIBSTED ASA
