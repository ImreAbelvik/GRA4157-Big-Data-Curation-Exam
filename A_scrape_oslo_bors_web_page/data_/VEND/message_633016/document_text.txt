22.11.2024 15:47:14 CET | Schibsted | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

As part of the Employee Share Saving Plan, primary insiders in Schibsted ASA
("Schibsted Marketplaces") have on 22 November 2024 purchased 370 treasury
B-shares at a price of NOK 338.00 per B-share (the price is set according to
standard procedure for the programme during a two day period subsequent to the
release of Schibsted's Q3 2024 results, the closing price on 28 October 2024).
The transaction is related to the second enrollment window in the Employee Share
Saving Plan for 2024 which closed in June 2024, and based on savings made during
July, August and September 2024.



In addition, primary insiders in Schibsted ASA ("Schibsted Marketplaces")
received 335 treasury B-shares on 22 November 2024. The transaction is related
to bonus matching shares given to employees who enrolled in the Employee Share
Saving Plan for 2022.



For more information on the Employee Share Saving Plan please refer to the
disclosure published 9 April 2014.



Please see the attached form(s) for notification and public disclosure of
transactions.
