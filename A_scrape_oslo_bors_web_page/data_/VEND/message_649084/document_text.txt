Reference is made to the stock exchange announcement made by AMSC ASA (under
liquidation) ("AMSC") on 15 May 2025 that payment of the dividend-in-kind in the
form of AMSC's shares in Solstad Maritime ASA ("SOMA") had been completed, and
that for shareholders subject to withholding tax, a portion of their shares had
been withheld and will be sold by AMSC for cash in the market to cover such tax.

AMSC, a legal person closely associated with board member Frank O. Reite, has
today sold 3,198,059 SOMA shares for cash in the market. The price per SOMA
share was NOK 23.00 which implies a total consideration of NOK 73,555,357.
Following the sale AMSC owns 0 shares in SOMA.

The shares in SOMA are linked to the shares in Solstad Offshore ASA, cf. the
Market Abuse Regulation article 19.

accordance with Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Article
19 of the EU Market Abuse Regulation and section 5-12 of the Norwegian
Securities Trading Act.
