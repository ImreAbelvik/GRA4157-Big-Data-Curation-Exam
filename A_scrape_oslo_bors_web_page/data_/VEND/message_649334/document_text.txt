Refence is made to the public notice from Magnora ASA (“Magnora”) on 16 June 2025. Magnora has received an unsolicited bid from a small group of existing shareholders in Magnora for its full shareholding in Hermana Holding ASA (“Hermana”), totalling 4,025,621 shares. The bid was submitted through Skandinaviska Enskilda Banken AS Publ. (“SEB”), and the bid price was NOK 10 per share and accepted by Magnora. To facilitate equal treatment of its shareholders, Magnora agreed with the Bidding Investors to launch an open bookbuilding process for a part of the offering at a fixed price of NOK 10 per share. All shareholders in Magnora as shown in the VPS register at 08:00 on 16 June were invited to buy shares in the offering.

Several primary insiders of Hermana also being shareholders in Magnora have participated and thus on 16 June 2025 purchased shares, all at price NOK 10 per share.

Primary insiders, their position in Hermana, respective acquisition of Hermana shares and resulting ownership are as follows: 

Erik Sneve, Chair of the Board, has purchased 150,000 shares. Following this, he controls 317,692 shares in Hermana Holding ASA, which equals 2.37 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.

Torstein Sanness, Board member, has purchased 20,000 shares. Following this, he controls 109,920 shares in Hermana Holding ASA, which equals 0.82 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.

Hilde Ådland, Board member, has purchased 20,000 shares. Following this, she controls 25,572 shares in Hermana Holding ASA, which equals 0.19 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.

Stein Bjørnstad, CEO, has purchased 10,000 shares. Following this, he controls 29,286 shares in Hermana Holding ASA, which equals 0.22 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.

Bård Olsen, CFO, has purchased 15,000 shares. Following this, he controls 42,714 shares in Hermana Holding ASA, which equals 0.32 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.

Morten Strømgren, SVP, has purchased 10,000 shares. Following this, he controls 18,000 shares in Hermana Holding ASA, which equals 0.13 percent of the issued shares and votes in the company.

About Hermana:
Hermana Holding ASA is a royalty and investment company that provides financing in exchange for revenue streams or ownership stakes. The company has been listed on the main list of the Oslo Stock Exchange since June 2024. Hermana has revenues from FPSO design rights previously owned by Sevan Marine ASA and Magnora ASA. Hermana has low costs and a patient and ambitious approach to capital allocation.
