Oslo, June 23, 2025: Jurs AS, a company owned 100% by Morten Jurs, Board member
and Primary Insider in Navamedic ASA, has today acquired 22.935 Navamedic ASA
shares at an average price of NOK 21,88 per share. Following this transaction,
Morten Jurs holds 22.935 shares in the company. See attachment for further
details about the transaction.

and is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to the Market Abuse
Regulation (MAR) Article 19 no. 3 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act
