3.3.2022 16:40:27 CET | Schibsted | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

Schibsted has granted 39,098 B-shares to primary insiders in Schibsted ASA in
connection with long-term incentive plans. The shares will be transferred to the
employees during the coming days.

The transfers are primarily related to the 3-year Long Term Incentive Plan 2019
(LTIP 2019).

For more information on the long-term incentive plans, see Schibsted ASA's
annual report 2020 under the section "The Board of Directors' Statement of
Executive Compensation".

Please see the attached form(s) for notification and public disclosure of
transactions.


Oslo, 3 March 2022
SCHIBSTED ASA

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the
