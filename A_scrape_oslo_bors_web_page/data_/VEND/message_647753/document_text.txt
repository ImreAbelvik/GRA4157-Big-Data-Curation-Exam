Pexip Holding ASA ("Pexip" or "The Company", Oslo Børs Ticker: PEXIP) has been notified that the following primary insiders has purchased shares in the company:

Åsmund Fodstad, Chief Commercial Officer and primary insider in Pexip has purchased 6,000 shares in Pexip for an average price of NOK 58.40 per share through his fully owned subsidiary Metrix Invest AS. After the transaction, Fodstad will hold 696,440 shares and rights to 266,667 shares.

Further, Emma Fodstad and Alexander Fodstad, both related parties to Fodstad, has purchased 144 and 90 shares at NOK 58.30 per share.

This information is subject to disclosure requirements pursuant to the EU Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) and Section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.

This stock exchange announcement was published by Øystein Hem, Chief Financial Officer, Pexip Holding ASA, on May 28, 2025.
