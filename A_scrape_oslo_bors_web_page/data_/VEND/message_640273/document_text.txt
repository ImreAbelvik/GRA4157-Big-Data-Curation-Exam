Schibsted has granted 39,722 B-shares to primary insiders in Schibsted ASA in
connection with long-term incentive plans. The shares will be transferred to the
employees during the coming days.



The transfers are related to the 3-year plans: Executive Long Term Incentive
Plan 2022 (ELTIP 2022), Schibsted Long Term Incentive Plan 2022 (SLTIP 2022),
Schibsted Long Term Incentive Plan 2023 (SLTIP 2023), Schibsted Long Term
Incentive Plan 2024 (SLTIP 2024) and Executive Incentive Plan 2023 (EIP 2023).
