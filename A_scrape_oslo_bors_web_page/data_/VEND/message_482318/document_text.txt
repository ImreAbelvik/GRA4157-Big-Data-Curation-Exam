The Tinius Trust, through Blommenholm Industrier AS, has on 6 August 2019
acquired 55 392 B-shares in Schibsted ASA (the "Company"), at a price of NOK
227.3712 per share. After this transaction Blommenholm Industrier owns
28,188,589 A-shares and 29,982,377 B-shares in the Company.


The Tinius Trust and Blommenholm Industrier AS is represented in the board of
the Company by Ole Jacob Sunde.

Oslo, 7 August 2019

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
