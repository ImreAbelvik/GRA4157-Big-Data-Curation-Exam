Blommenholm Industrier AS, a company closely related to Ole Jacob Sunde,
Chairman of the Board in Schibsted ASA, has on 29 March 2022 bought A-shares in

transaction made by Blommenholm Industrier AS.

The Tinius Trust, owner of Blommenholm Industrier AS, is represented in the
Board of Schibsted ASA by Ole Jacob Sunde.
