The Tinius Trust, through Blommenholm Industrier AS, has on 8 August 2019
acquired 575 000 B-shares in Schibsted ASA (the "Company"), at a price of NOK
227.70 per share. After this transaction Blommenholm Industrier owns 28,188,589
A-shares and 30,574,007 B-shares in the Company.

The Tinius Trust and Blommenholm Industrier AS is represented in the board of
the Company by Ole Jacob Sunde.

Oslo, 9 August 2019

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
