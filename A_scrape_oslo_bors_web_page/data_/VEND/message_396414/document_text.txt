


Schibsted Media Group has on 1 March 2016 sold 21,068 treasury A-shares to
participants in the Employee Share Saving Plan at a price of NOK 237.60 per A-
share (the closing price 23 February 2016). The shares will be transferred to
the participants in the coming days. The transaction is in relation to the
fourth enrolment window in the Employee Share Saving Plan for 2015.

Shares sold and transferred to primary insider employees are disclosed in the
attachment.

After the transaction Schibsted ASA holds 293,011 treasury A-shares and 507,479
treasury B-shares.

For more information on the employee share saving plan please refer to the
disclosure published 9 April 2014.
