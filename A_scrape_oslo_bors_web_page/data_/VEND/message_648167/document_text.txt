Ljones, 2 June 2025: Odfjell Land AS has on 2 June 2025 acquired in total
504,793 shares in Biofish Holding AS at a price of NOK 1,70 per share. Odfjell
Land AS is a related party of primary insider Kjetil Grønskag. After this
transaction, Odfjell Land AS owns 13,969,479 shares and 2,400,000 warrants in
Biofish Holding AS.

This information is subject to disclosure under the Norwegian Securities
Trading Act, §5-12. The information was submitted for publication at
2025-06-02 17:00 CEST.
