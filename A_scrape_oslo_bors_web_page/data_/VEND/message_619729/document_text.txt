24.5.2024 15:40:45 CEST | Schibsted | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

As part of the Employee Share Saving Plan, primary insiders in Schibsted ASA
have on 24 May 2024 purchased 357 treasury B-shares at a price of NOK 305.80 per
B-share (the price is set according to standard procedure for the programme
during a two day period subsequent to the release of Schibsted's Q1 2024
results, the closing price on 29 April 2024). The transaction is related to the
fourth enrollment window in the Employee Share Saving Plan for 2024 which closed
in December 2023, and based on savings made during January, February and March
2024.



In addition, primary insiders in Schibsted ASA received 275 treasury B-shares on
24 May 2024. The transaction is related to bonus matching shares given to
employees who enrolled in the Employee Share Saving Plan for 2022.



For more information on the Employee Share Saving Plan please refer to the
disclosure published 9 April 2014.



Please see the attached form(s) for notification and public disclosure of
transactions.
