24.5.2024 15:42:50 CEST | Schibsted | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

Schibsted has granted 15,380 B-shares to primary insiders in Schibsted ASA in
connection with long-term incentive plans. The shares will be transferred to the
employees during the coming days.



The transfers are related to the 3-year plan Executive Incentive Plan 2023 (EIP
2023). 



For more information on the EIP Plan, please refer to Schibsted ASA's
Remuneration Report 2023.



Please see the attached form(s) for notification and public disclosure of
transactions.





Oslo, 24 May 2024

SCHIBSTED ASA
