Schibsted has on 18 September 2019 purchased 63,000 own A shares at an average
price of NOK 271.5352 per share and 17,000 own B shares at an average price of
NOK 261.4972 on the Oslo Stock Exchange. After these transactions Schibsted owns
a total of 1,558,267 own A-shares and 559,857 own B-shares.


The transaction is part of the buyback programme that Schibsted announced 16
July 2019, stating an ambition to buy back up to 2% of the Company's shares
during Q3 2019.
