Oslo, Norway, Sept 5, 2025

Lorenz AS controlled by Anton Lorenz Bondesen, CEO and primary insider, has today acquired 5.000 shares in CodeLab Capital AS at an average price of NOK 2,68 per share. Following the transaction Anton Bondesen and related parties hold 3.792.918 shares in the Company, corresponding to 29,316% of the outstanding shares.
