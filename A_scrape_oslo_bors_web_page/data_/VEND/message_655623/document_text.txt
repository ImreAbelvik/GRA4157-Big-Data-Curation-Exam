ACAPITAL ELIMP HOLDCO AS, a person closely associated with primary insider and board member Eirik Westvig Rogstad, has on 17 September 2025 notified of a decision to distribute 11,474,362 shares of Elektroimportøren AS as a liquidation dividend. The price per share is based on the closing price on 17 September 2025, NOK 14.50 per share, corresponding to a total of NOK 166,378,249.
