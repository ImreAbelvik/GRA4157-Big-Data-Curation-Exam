The Board of Directors of Techstep ASA has resolved to grant additional share
options under the company's 2025 share option programme as announced on 4 April
2025. For details regarding the terms and conditions of the 2025 share option
program, please refer to the Techstep ASA stock exchange announcement of 4 April
2025.

Of the total 1,360,000 share options under the 2025 share option program, a
total of 180,000 unallocated share options have now been granted to new members
of management and primary insiders as follows:

  · Claes Widestadh, COO, has been granted 80,000 share options
  · Terje Bjørnsen, CCO, has been granted 100,000 share options

Please see attached forms for primary insider notifications under MAR.
