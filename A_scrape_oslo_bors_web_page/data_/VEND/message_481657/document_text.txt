Stiftelsen Tinius, through Blommenholm Industrier AS, has on 18 July 2019
acquired 90,000 B-shares in Schibsted ASA (the "Company"), at a price of NOK
222.1318 per share. After this transaction Blommenholm Industrier owns
28,188,589 A-shares and 29,653,924 B-shares in the Company.


Stiftelsen Tinius and Blommenholm Industrier AS is represented in the board of
the Company by Ole Jacob Sunde.

                               Oslo, 18 July 2019

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
