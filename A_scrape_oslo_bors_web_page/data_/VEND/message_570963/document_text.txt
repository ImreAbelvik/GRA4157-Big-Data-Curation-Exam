12.9.2022 10:20:29 CEST | Schibsted | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

Schibsted has granted 2,372 B-shares to a primary insider in Schibsted ASA in
connection with long-term incentive plans. The shares will be transferred to the
employee during the coming days.

The transfer is related to the Extraordinary LTI Grant from 2021.

For more information on the LTI Plan, please refer to Schibsted ASA's
Remuneration Report 2021.

Please see the attached form(s) for notification and public disclosure of
transactions.

Oslo, 12 September 2022
SCHIBSTED ASA
