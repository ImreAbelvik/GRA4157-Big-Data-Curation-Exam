ZENITH ENERGY LTD.

Director/ PDMR Shareholding

Zenith Energy Ltd. ("Zenith" or the "Company") (LSE: ZEN; OSE: ZENA), the listed international energy production and development company, is pleased to announce that its Chief Executive Officer & President, Mr. Andrea Cattaneo, has advised the Company that he has purchased a total of 650,000 common shares in the Capital of the Company of no par value ("Common Shares") at a total average price of NOK 0.5086 per Common Share.

Following the purchase, Mr. Cattaneo is directly beneficially interested in a total of 51,001,414 Common Shares in the capital of the Company, representing 10.12 per cent of the total issued and outstanding common share capital of the Company.
