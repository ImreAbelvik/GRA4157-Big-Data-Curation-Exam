The Tinius Trust, through Blommenholm Industrier AS, has on 3 September 2019
acquired 73 000 A-shares in Schibsted ASA (the "Company"), at a price of NOK
269.7920 per share. After this transaction Blommenholm Industrier owns
28,491,241 A-shares and 30,621,205 B-shares in the Company.

The Tinius Trust and Blommenholm Industrier AS is represented in the board of
the Company by Ole Jacob Sunde.

Oslo, 4 September 2019

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section
5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
