Oslo, 3rd of June 2025: Lokotech Group AS (the "Company") has been informed of the following trades by primary insiders (PDMRs) and persons closely related to primary insiders (PCAs) of the Company: 

* Yngve Bolstad Johansen, chairman of the board of directors of the Company, has sold 856,388 shares in the Company to Black Monday Holding AS at an average price per share of NOK 0.336. Black Monday Holding AS is a company owned and controlled by Mr. Bolstad Johansen. 

* Christian Rustad, CTO of the Company, has sold 555,555 shares in the Company to Prozium AS at an average price per share of NOK 0.336. Prozium AS is a company owned and controlled by Mr. Rustad. 

* Ola Stene-Johansen, CEO of the Company, has returned 2,000,000 shares in the Company to Infigent AS. The transaction entails settlement of the shares borrowed by Mr. Stene-Johansen from Infigent AS, as announced in the stock exchange notification published by the Company on the 20th of December 2024. Infigent AS is a company controlled and partially owned by Mr. Stene-Johansen.  

Please see the attached forms for further details.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
