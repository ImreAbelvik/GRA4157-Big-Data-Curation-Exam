3.3.2025 15:31:43 CET | Schibsted | Mandatory notification of trade primary
insiders

As part of the Employee Share Saving Plan, primary insiders in Schibsted ASA
have on 3 March 2025 purchased 401 treasury B-shares at a price of NOK 312.20
per B-share (the price is set according to standard procedure for the programme
during a two day period subsequent to the release of Schibsted's Q4 2024
results, the closing price on 10 February 2025). The transaction is related to
the fourth enrollment window in the Employee Share Saving Plan for 2024 which
closed in September 2024, and based on savings made during October, November and
December 2024.



In addition, primary insiders in Schibsted ASA received 257 treasury B-shares on
3 March 2025. The transaction is related to bonus matching shares given to
employees who enrolled in the Employee Share Saving Plan for 2022.



For more information on the Employee Share Saving Plan please refer to the
disclosure published 9 April 2014.



Please see the attached form(s) for notification and public disclosure of
transactions.
