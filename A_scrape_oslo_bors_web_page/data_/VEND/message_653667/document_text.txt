Oslo, 22 August 2025: Damgård Invest AS, a company controlled by Andreas Enger, CEO of Höegh Autoliners ASA and primary insider, has today sold 408 719 shares in Höegh Autoliners ASA at an average price of NOK 117,0514 per share.

After the transactions, Damgård Invest AS holds 500 000 shares in Höegh Autoliners ASA representing approximately 0,26% of the share capital and votes in Höegh Autoliners ASA.

Please refer to the attached filing for further information. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19 of the Regulation EU 596/2014 (the EU Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
