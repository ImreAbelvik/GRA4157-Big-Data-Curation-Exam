Norda ASA has on 8th of July 2025 received 1,973,013 shares in LOKOTECH GROUP AS at a price of NOK 0.66 per share, after Norda had the shares returned after lending out the shares.  Following this transaction Norda ASA holds 1,972,195 warrants in LOKOTECH GROUP AS and 20,318,819 shares in in LOKOTECH GROUP AS. In addition Norda has lent 75,354 warrants in LOKOTECH GROUP AS.  Christian Åbyholm is the Chairman of Norda ASA and married to Kristin Åbyholm who is a member of the Board and primary insider in LOKOTECH GROUP AS.

Norda ASA will together with Kristin Åbyholm and the Group of companies INSR ASA and Caaby AS following the share transaction hold in total 30,861,960 shares in LOKOTECH GROUP AS, corresponding to approx. 4.9% of the total outstanding shares. In addition the Group of companies holds in total 10,623,859 warrants in LOKOTECH GROUP AS.  In addition Norda has lent 75,354 warrants in LOKOTECH GROUP AS.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Regulation EU 596/2014 (MAR) article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
