Skeie Kappa Invest AS, a company controlled directly by Trym Skeie, the Chairman
of the Board in Nekkar ASA and primary insider, has on 28th of January 2021
purchased 250 000 shares in Nekkar ASA, at NOK 6.9 per share, from Skeie Alpha
Invest AS which is also controlled by Trym Skeie.

After the transaction, the number of shares in Nekkar ASA owned by Trym Skeie,
directly or through his directly controlled company, is 1 428 939.

Indirectly, Trym Skeie holds 23,5% of the voting shares and 23,5% of the capital
in Skeie Technology AS and Skeie Capital Investment AS, which in sum holds 31
475 823 shares in Nekkar ASA.

ABOUT NEKKAR ASA
