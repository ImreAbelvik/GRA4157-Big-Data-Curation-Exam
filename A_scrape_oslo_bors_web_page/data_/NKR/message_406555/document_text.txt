


TTS Group ASA: Mandatory notification of trade
Reference is made to (dated 18.07.16): TTS GROUP ASA - THE ACCEPTANCE PERIOD FOR
THE RECOMMENDED VOLUNTARY OFFER MADE BY PALFINGER MARINE GMBH HAS COMMENCED

Trylleøen AS, controlled by primary insider Trond Bogsnes (Senior Communications
and Marketing Officer) will accept the offer from Palfinger Marine GmbH and will
if the transaction is completed not own stocks or bonds in TTS Group ASA. Number
of shares: 2000.

The acceptance form attached to the voluntary offer document is signed and
delivered.

Bergen 1st August 2016

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
