


Reference is made to the announcement on 23 November 2015 concerning the
acquisition by Skeie Technology AS of in total 13.725.884 shares in TTS Group
ASA from companies owned and controlled by other members of the Skeie-family in
an off-exchange transaction at a price of NOK 3.48 per share (market close on
Friday 22 November). Skeie Technology AS owns 22 655 763 in TTS Group ASA post
this acquisition.

The sellers included Tamafe Holding AS with 2 160 735 shares. This company is
owned and controlled by Trym Skeie, board leader of TTS Group ASA. Tamafe
Holding AS has no longer any shares in TTS Group ASA. Trym Skeie owns 323.140
shares directly and 250.000 shares indirectly through 100% owned Skeie Alpha
Invest AS, corresponding to 0,65 % of the total outstanding shares in TTS Group
ASA. In addition Skeie Alpha Invest AS holds the convertible bonds issued by TTS
Group ASA in the amount of NOK 4 million.

The sellers also included Skeie Consultants AS with 953 033 shares. This company
is owned and controlled by Bjarne Skeie, board member of TTS Group AS. Skeie
Consultants AS owns 300 000 in TTS Group ASA post this sale. Bjarne Skeie,
through Skeie Consultants AS, Skeie Technology AS and Skeie Capital AS owns in
total 31,36 % of the shares in TTS Group ASA, and convertible bonds issued by
TTS Group ASA in the amount of NOK 6 million.

The sellers also included Lesk AS with 5 306 058 shares. The company is owned
and controlled by members of the Skeie-family. Post this sale, Lesk AS, has no
shares in TTS Group ASA.

The sellers also included Stisk AS with 5 306 058 shares. The company is owned
and controlled by members of the Skeie-family. Post this sale, Stisk AS, has no
shares in TTS Group ASA.


This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
