


Anita Kråkenes, employee elected Director of the Board, has on 15 February,
2017 sold 2000 shares in TTS Group ASA at a price of NOK 3,88 per share.

Following the transaction, Anita Kråkenes does not own any shares in TTS Group.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5 -12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
