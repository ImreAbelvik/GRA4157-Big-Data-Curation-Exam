Skeie Kappa Invest AS, a company controlled directly by Trym Skeie, the Chairman
of the Board in Nekkar ASA and primary insider, has on 3 December 2021 purchased

through his directly controlled company, is 1 632 939. Indirectly, Trym Skeie
holds 23,5% of the voting shares and 23,5% of the capital in Skeie Technology AS
and Skeie Capital Investment AS, which in sum holds 31 475 823 shares in Nekkar
ASA.

Rolf-Atle Tomassen, CEO of Syncrolift AS, a subsidiary of Nekkar ASA, and
primary insider, has on 3 December 2021 sold 150,000 shares in the company at a
price per share of NOK 9.06.
