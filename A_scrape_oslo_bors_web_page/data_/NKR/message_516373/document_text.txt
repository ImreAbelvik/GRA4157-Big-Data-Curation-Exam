26.10.2020 17:38:30 CET | Nekkar ASA | Mandatory notification of trade primary

Reference is made to the stock exchange notice by Nekkar ASA (the "Company") on
12 October 2020 regarding the Share program for employees and board members in
the Nekkar Group.

The Company has completed the subscription and the board of directors has
resolved the issuance of 707 051 new shares under the program. The price for
each share will be NOK 4,00 which reflects the volume-weighted 10 days average
trading price in the period up to 23 October 2020, less 30% as the shares will
have a three-year lock-up period.

ABOUT NEKKAR ASA

Nekkar (OSE: NKR) is an industrial technology group offering impact technologies
combined with high-end software solutions. The group combines 50 years' heritage
from the world's number one shiplift company, Syncrolift, with new investments
into sustainable, digitalised technology businesses that aim to unlock growth
within ocean-based industries such as offshore energy and renewables and
aquaculture.
