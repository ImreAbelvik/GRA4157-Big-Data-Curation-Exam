


Reference is made to the announcement made 9 June 2015 on granting of options to
leading employees and to the announcements made 19 June 2016 and 18 July 2016 of
the voluntary offer from Palfinger Marine GmbH for the outstanding shares of TTS
Group ASA.

Out of the 600 000 options granted to leading employees of TTS Group, in total
550 000 options are in force as at 12 August 2016. TTS Group ASA has entered
into agreements with all holders of options that TTS Group will redeem the
550 000 options against a cash consideration equivalent to the difference
between the transaction price per share of NOK 5,60 and the strike price of the
options of NOK 4,75, provided that Palfinger Marine GmbH assumes control over
TTS Group ASA.
