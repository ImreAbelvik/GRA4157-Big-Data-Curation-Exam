Bouvet ASA has on September 23rd 2015 purchased 
160.000 own shares at an average price of NOK 93.00 
per share.

After the transaction Bouvet ASA holds 179.576 own 
shares.  The transaction was carried out as a part 
of the share incentive program for employees and is 
based on authorization given by the company's Annual 
General Meeting 19th of May 2015.
