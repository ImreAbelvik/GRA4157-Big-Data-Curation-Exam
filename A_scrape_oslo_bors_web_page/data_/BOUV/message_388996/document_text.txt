Oslo, November 9th 2015: The employees of Bouvet 
ASA have today acquired a total of 60909 shares in 
Bouvet ASA through the company's employee share 
purchase  program.  76 percent of the employees 
participated in the program. The employees were 
given a 20 percent discount on the share price. The 
purchase price was NOK 75,80 per share. 632 shares 
were purchased by primary insiders of the company.
