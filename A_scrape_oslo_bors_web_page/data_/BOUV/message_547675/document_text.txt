Sverre Hurum, a director of Bouvet ASA, has transferred 30,000 shares at NOK 0.00 per share to Cavarina AS on 23 November 2021. Following this transaction, Hurum holds 3,579,060 shares in Bouvet ASA. 

Cavarina AS, a company closely associated with Hurum, has received 30,000 shares in Bouvet ASA at NOK 0.00 per share on 23 November 2021. As a result, Cavarina AS now holds 150,000 shares in Bouvet ASA. Hurum is a director Cavarina AS. 

Please refer to the attached notification of trading for further details. 

Further information: Trude Hole, CFO, Bouvet ASA, trude.hole@bouvet.no. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in article 19, number 3 of Regulation EU 596/2014 (the Market Abuse Regulation) and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
