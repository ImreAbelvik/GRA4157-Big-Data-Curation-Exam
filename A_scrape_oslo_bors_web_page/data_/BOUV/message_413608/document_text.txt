Oslo, November 14th 2016: The employees of Bouvet ASA have 
today acquired a total of 49206 shares in Bouvet ASA through 
the company's employee share purchase program.  79 percent 
of the employees participated in the program. The employees 
were given a 20 percent discount on the share price. The 
purchase price was NOK 100,80 per share. 531 shares were 
purchased by primary insiders of the company.
