Oslo, November 13th 2017: The employees of Bouvet ASA have today acquired a total of 42636 shares in Bouvet ASA through the company's employee share purchase program.  82 percent of the employees participated in the program. The employees were given a 20 percent discount on the share price. The purchase price was NOK 134,80 per share. Primary insiders of the company purchased 440 shares.
