Oslo, November 19th 2018: The employees of Bouvet ASA have today acquired a total of 72866 shares in Bouvet ASA through the company's employee share purchase program.  82 percent of the employees participated in the program. The employees were given a 20 percent discount on the share price. The purchase price was NOK 169,60 per share. Primary insiders of the company purchased 1050 shares.
