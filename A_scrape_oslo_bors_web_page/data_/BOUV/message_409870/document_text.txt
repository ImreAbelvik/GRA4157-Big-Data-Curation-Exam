Bouvet ASA has on September 21st 2016 purchased 90.000 own 
shares at an average price of NOK 119.50 per share.

After the transaction Bouvet ASA holds 241.317 own shares. The 
transaction was carried out as a part of the share incentive 
program for employees and is based on authorization given by 
the company's Annual General Meeting 12th of May 2016.
