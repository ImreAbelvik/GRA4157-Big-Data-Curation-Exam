Bouvet ASA has on August 25th 2016 purchased 
120.000 own shares at an average price of NOK 119.50 
per share.

After the transaction Bouvet ASA holds 151.317 own 
shares.  The transaction was carried out as a part 
of the share incentive program for employees and is 
based on authorization given by the company's Annual 
General Meeting 12th of May 2016.
