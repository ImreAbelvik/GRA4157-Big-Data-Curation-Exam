Hamilton, Bermuda (March 16, 2020)

Archer Limited ("the Company") has through market purchases executed on March
16, 2020 repurchased 7,125 of the Company's own common stock at an average price
of NOK 1.8597 per share.  After this transaction, the Company held a total of
25,000 treasury shares.

In connection with Archer's long term incentive program 25,000 restricted stock
units ("RSUs") have vested and the same amount of common shares have been
distributed to Archer employees. The Company has used its treasury shares for
settlement.

The following primary insider was amongst the recipients of shares:

Mr. Adam Todd, General Counsel, received 6,250 shares. After this transaction,
Mr. Todd holds 54,416 shares and 143,584 RSUs in Archer Limited.

After these transactions, the total number of options and RSUs outstanding in
Archer Limited is 650,001 and 4,132,364 respectively.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
