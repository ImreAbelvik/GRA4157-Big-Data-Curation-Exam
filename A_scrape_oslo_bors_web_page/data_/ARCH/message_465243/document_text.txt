Hemen Holding Limited, which is indirectly controlled 
by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the 
benefit of his immediate family, has settled Total 
Return Swap (TRS) agreements underlying 8,059,854 
shares in Archer Limited expiring December 6th, 2018 
and subsequently entered into a new TRS agreement 
underlying the same number of shares 8,059,854. New 
expiry date is March 6th, 2019 and new TRS price 
is NOK 5.7304 per share. Hemen Holding Limited's 
affiliated ownership in Archer Limited is following 
this transaction unchanged 12,119,675 shares, constituting
8.22 percent of the issued share capital. Hemen Holding Limited Controls after
this in addition TRS agreements with exposure to totally 8,059,854 shares in
Archer Limited.
