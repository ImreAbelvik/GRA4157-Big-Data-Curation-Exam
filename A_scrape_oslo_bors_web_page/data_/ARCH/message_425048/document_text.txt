Hemen Holding Limited ("Hemen"), which is indirectly 
controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the 
benefit of his immediate family, has today carried out early 
settlement of (i) Total Return Swap (TRS) agreements with 
exposure to 4,914,890 shares in Archer Limited ("Archer") 
with original expiry date 16 May 2017 and exercise price NOK 
10.041 per share, and (ii) TRS agreements with exposure to 
2,395,300 shares in Archer with original expiry date 7 June 
2017 and exercise price NOK 12.9667 per share. The 
settlement has been made based on today's closing price for 
Archer shares on the Oslo Stock Exchange of NOK 12,80 per 
share. At the same time, Hemen has entered into a TRS 
agreement with exposure to 8.059.854 shares in Archer. The 
expiration date of the new TRS agreement is 7 June 2017 and 
the exercise price in the agreement is NOK 12,833 per share.
 
Hemen's affiliated ownership in Archer following this 
transaction is 12,119,675 shares, constituting 8,51 percent 
of the issued shares, based on 142.356.716 shares 
outstanding prior to the repair issue. In addition Hemen 
holds TRS agreements with exposure to 8.059.854 shares in 
Archer as described above.
