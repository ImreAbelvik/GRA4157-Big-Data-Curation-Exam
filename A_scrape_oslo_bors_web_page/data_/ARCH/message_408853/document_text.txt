Hemen Holding Limited, which is indirectly controlled 
by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the 
benefit of his immediate family, has settled Total 
Return Swap (TRS) agreements underlying 2.395.300 
shares in Archer Limited expiring September 5th, 2016 
and subsequently entered into a new TRS agreement 
underlying the same number of shares 2.395.300. New 
expiry date is December 5th, 2016 and new TRS price 
is NOK 4,1517 per share. Hemen Holding Limited's 
affiliated ownership in Archer Limited is following 
this transaction 4.434.565 shares, constituting 7,62 
percent of the issued share capital. Hemen Holding 
Limited controls after this in addition TRS agreements 
with exposure to totally 2.395.300 shares in Archer 
Limited.
