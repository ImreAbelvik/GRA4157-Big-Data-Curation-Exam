Hemen Holding Limited, which is indirectly controlled
by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the
benefit of his immediate family, has settled Total
Return Swap (TRS) agreements underlying 23.953,000
shares in Archer Limited expiring March 2nd, 2015
and subsequently entered into a new TRS agreement
underlying the same number of shares 23.953.000.
New expiry date is June 2nd 2015, and new TRS
price is NOK 2,2845 per share.
 
Hemen Holding Limited's affiliated ownership in
Archer Limited is following this transaction
42.604.658 shares, constituting 7,36 percent of the
issued share capital.
 
Hemen Holding Limited controls after this in addition
TRS agreements with exposure to totally 23.953.000
shares in Archer Limited.
