Hemen Holding Limited ("Hemen"), which is indirectly controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the benefit of his immediate family, has on 6 June 2019 purchased 8,059,854 shares in Archer Limited ("Archer") at a price of NOK 4.15 per share. As a result, Hemen's ownership in Archer has increased to 20,179,529 shares, constituting 13.68 per cent of the shares and votes in the company. Hemen does not hold other rights to shares in Archer. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements set out in sections 4-2 and 4-3 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
