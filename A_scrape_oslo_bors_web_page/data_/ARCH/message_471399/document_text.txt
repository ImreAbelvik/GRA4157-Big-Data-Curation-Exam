Hemen Holding Limited, which is indirectly controlled by trusts established by John Fredriksen for the 
benefit of his immediate family, has settled Total Return Swap (TRS) agreements underlying 8,059,854 shares in Archer Limited expiring March 6th, 2019 and subsequently entered into a new TRS agreement underlying the same number of shares 8,059,854. New expiry date is June 6th, 2019 and new TRS price is NOK 5.4309 per share. Hemen Holding Limited's affiliated ownership in Archer Limited is following this transaction unchanged 12,119,675 shares, constituting 8.22 percent of the issued share capital. Hemen Holding Limited controls after this in addition TRS agreements with exposure to totally 8,059,854 shares in Archer Limited.
