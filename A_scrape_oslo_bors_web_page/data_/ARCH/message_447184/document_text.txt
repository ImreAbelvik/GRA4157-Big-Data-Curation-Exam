


Dag Skindlo, CFO and Director of Archer Limited (ARCHER) has on 21 March 2018
bought 18,000 shares in ARCHER at an average price of NOK 8.40. Following the
transaction Dag Skindlo holds 124,000 shares and 600,000 options in ARCHER.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section
5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
