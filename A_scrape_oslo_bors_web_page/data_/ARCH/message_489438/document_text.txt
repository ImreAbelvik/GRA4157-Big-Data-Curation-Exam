Dag Skindlo, CFO of Archer Limited (ARCHER) has on 14 November 2019 bought
50,000 shares in ARCHER at an average price of NOK 3.26. Following the
transaction Dag Skindlo holds 253,333 shares, 600 000 stock options and 336,667
RSUs in Archer Limited.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
