Oslo, August 20, 2024: Agnvicen AB, a closely related party to primary insider
Tony Brejke, COO of Navamedic ASA, has today sold 50.000 Navamedic ASA shares at
an average price of NOK 30,60 per share. See attachment for further details
about the transaction. Following this transaction, Agnvicen AB holds 757,117
shares in the company.

Rune Wahl, Board member and Primary Insider, has today acquired 50.000 Navamedic
ASA shares at an average price of NOK 30,60 per share. See attachments for
further details about the transaction. Following this transaction, Rune Wahl
holds 50.000 shares in the company.
