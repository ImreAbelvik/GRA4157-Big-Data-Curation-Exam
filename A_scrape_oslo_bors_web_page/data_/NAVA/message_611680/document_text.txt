Åsa Kornfeld and Edmée Steenken, both primary insiders and Board members in
Navamedic ASA, have on 22 February 2024 purchased shares in Navamedic ASA. Åsa
Kornfeld bought 2,679 shares at an average price of NOK 36.80 per share. Edmée
Steenken bought 10,000 shares at an average price of NOK 37.45 per share. See
more details in the attached notification form.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 5
-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
