


Håkan Josephsson, COO in Navamedic ASA has 24 September 2015 acquired 5,000
shares in Navamedic ASA at an average price of NOK 9,00 per share. Following
this transaction Josephsson holds a total of 27 500 shares and 25,000 options
in Navamedic ASA.
