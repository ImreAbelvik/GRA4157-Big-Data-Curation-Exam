Issuance of shares to BW LPG's Management Team

(Singapore, 3 January 2018)

Pursuant to the 2015 Long-Term Incentive Plan (LTIP 2015) for its top management, BW LPG issues shares to its Chief Executive Officer (CEO) - Martin Ackermann, Chief Financial Officer (CFO) - Elaine Ong, and Senior Vice President for Technical and Operations - Pontus Berg. The issuance of shares relates to the Company's share performance under its shares incentive scheme for the year 2015, with deferred shares being issued at the start of 2017 and 2018 respectively. The shares issued on 2 January 2018 are priced at NOK 38.25 and make up the final issuance of shares under LTIP 2015. The LTIP 2015 has been replaced by the 2017 Long-Term Incentive Plan (LTIP 2017), details of which can be found in the following link:

http://www.bwlpg.com/Media/April-2017/BWLPG-MANAGEMENT-SHARE-OPTION-PLAN-MANDATORY-NOTIF

About BW LPG 

BW LPG is the world's leading owner and operator of LPG vessels. BW LPG currently owns and operates 53 Very Large Gas Carriers (VLGC) and Large Gas Carriers (LGC) including two VLGC newbuildings with a total carrying capacity of 4.3 million cbm. With four decades of operating experience in LPG shipping and experienced seafarers and staff, BW LPG offers a flexible and reliable service to customers.

BW LPG is associated with BW Group, one of the world's leading shipping groups. BW Group is involved in oil and gas transportation, floating gas infrastructure, environmental technologies and deep-water production.
