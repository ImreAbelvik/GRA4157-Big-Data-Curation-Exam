


Oslo, 4 March 2015: Yara International ASA Head of Strategy Terje Morten
Tollefsen sold 3,382 Yara shares on Tuesday 3 March at a price of NOK 426.64 per
share.

Following this purchase Terje Morten Tollefsen owns 3,368 Yara shares.
