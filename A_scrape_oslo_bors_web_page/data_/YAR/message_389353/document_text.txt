Oslo (12 November 2015): Yara International ASA has 
today purchased 60,000 own shares in the market, at an 
average price of NOK 403.87. 

The transaction is part of a buy-back program 
authorized by the Annual General meeting held 11 May 
2015. The AGM authorized Yara to buy back up to 5% of 
Yara's shares (13,754,168 shares) before its 2016 AGM. 
Yara has renewed its agreement with the Norwegian 
State according to which the State's shares will be 
redeemed on a pro-rata basis to ensure that the 
State's ownership is unchanged when shares are 
cancelled. 

After this transaction Yara holds 360,000 own shares 
acquired as part of its current buy-back program.
