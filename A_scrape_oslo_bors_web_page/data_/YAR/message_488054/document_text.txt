Drew Holdings Ltd (“Drew”), a Company affiliated with Tor Olav Trøim has on 29th 
October 2019 taken delivery of 1.300.000 shares in Yara held on a 
forward contract with maturity the same date.

At the same time,  Drew Holdings has sold 1.300.000 shares in Yara and
entered into a TRS agreement for the same number of shares. 
Expiry date for the TRS contract is February 12th 2020, and the TRS price is NOK 367.596

After this transaction, Drew’s exposure in Yara is unchanged at 1.300.000 shares.
 
Drew has, since May 2019, been represented on the board of directors of Yara by Mr. Håkon R. Fure.
