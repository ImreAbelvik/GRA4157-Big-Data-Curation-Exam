Aker ASA has on 10 December 2015 transferred its equity holding 
in Ocean Yield ASA to Aker Capital AS, a wholly-owned subsidiary 
of Aker.

As per 10 December 2015, Aker directly held 98 242 575 of the 
issued shares and votes in Ocean Yield ASA, or 72.97 per cent of 
the share capital.

For further information, please refer to Aker's stock exchange 
notice published today.
