Oslo 1 September 2016

Jens Ismar, board member in Ocean Yield ASA (the "Company"), 
has today purchased 10,000 shares in the Company at a price 
of NOK 64,25 per share. Following this transaction, Jens 
Ismar owns a total of 10,000 shares.
 
This information is subject of the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading 
Act.
