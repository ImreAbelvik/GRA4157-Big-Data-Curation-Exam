Oslo, 1 September 2016

The following primary insiders (or their related parties as 
described below) have, as part of the company's incentive 
program, agreed to subscribe for shares in Ocean Yield ASA 
("the Company"):

* Lars Solbakken, CEO, has through his wholly owned company 
Finmarine AS agreed to subscribe for 100,000 new shares in 
Ocean Yield ASA. After this transaction, Lars Solbakken owns 
1,350,880 shares in the Company (all shares are owned 
indirectly through the wholly owned company Finmarine AS).

* Eirik Eide, CFO, has through his wholly owned company 
Kleiver Invest AS agreed to subscribe for 30,000 new shares 
in Ocean Yield ASA. After this transaction, Eirik Eide owns, 
directly or indirectly, 300,051 shares in the Company.

* Marius Magelie, SVP Finance & IR, has through his wholly 
owned company MGM Invest AS agreed to subscribe for 10,000 
new shares in Ocean Yield ASA. After this transaction, Marius 
Magelie owns, directly or indirectly, 95,120 shares in the 
Company.

The subscription of shares is made as part of the Company's 
management incentive program. The subscription price equals 
the price per share in the private placement of new shares, 
completed on 31st August 2016, less a discount of 20%, 
reflecting that the shares have a lock-up period of three 
years. 

A total of 140,000 new shares will be issued, with net cash 
proceeds of approximately NOK 7.1 million. The issued share 
capital post the private placement and this capital increase, 
will amount to NOK 1,483,514,320 divided into 148,351,432 
shares having a par value of NOK 10.00.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading 
Act.
