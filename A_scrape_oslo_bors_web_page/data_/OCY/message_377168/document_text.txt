The following primary insiders (or their related 
parties as described below) have agreed to buy  
shares in Ocean Yield ASA ("the Company") from the 
Company:

* Lars Solbakken, CEO, has (through his wholly owned 
company Finmarine AS) agreed to buy 30 690 new 
shares in Ocean Yield ASA at a price of NOK 45.00 
per share. After this transaction, Lars Solbakken 
owns 1 127 017 shares in the Company (all shares are 
owned indirectly through the wholly owned company 
Finmarine AS).

* Eirik Eide, CFO, has (through his wholly owned 
company Kleiver Invest AS) agreed to buy 9 765 new 
shares in Ocean Yield ASA at a price of NOK 45.00 
per share. After this transaction, Eirik Eide owns, 
directly or indirectly, 216 323 shares in the 
Company.

* Marius Magelie, Vice President, has through his 
wholly owned company MGM Invest AS agreed to buy 4 
185 new shares in Ocean Yield ASA at a price of NOK 
45.00 per share. After this transaction, Marius 
Magelie owns, directly or indirectly, 57 094 shares 
in the Company.

The purchase of shares is made as part of the 
Company's management incentive program. The purchase 
price equals the closing share price of the Ocean 
Yield share on 5th May 2015, less a discount of 20%, 
reflecting that the shares have a lock-up period of 
three years. The share price as of close 5th May 
2015 was NOK 56.25.

An order for the acquisition of up to 150,000 shares 
will be placed in the market with an investment bank 
on 6th of May 2015. These shares will be used in 
settlement of the abovementioned 44 640 shares and 
the remaining shares will be held as treasury shares 
and used towards the management incentive program 
going forward.  

This information is subject of the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of the 
Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
