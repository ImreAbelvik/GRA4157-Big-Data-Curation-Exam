Yield ASA at an average price of NOK 55.5916 per share.

shares in Aker ASA, which again controls 72.97% of the 
outstanding shares in Ocean Yield ASA.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities 
Trading Act.
