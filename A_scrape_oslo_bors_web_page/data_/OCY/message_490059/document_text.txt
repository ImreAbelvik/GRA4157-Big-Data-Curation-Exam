Mr. Tom Henrik Sundby, the spouse of Ms. Anne Christin Døvigen who is a Board
Member of Ocean Yield ASA ("Ocean Yield"), has today acquired 10,000 shares in
Ocean Yield at a price of NOK 46.50 per share. Following this transaction, Mr.
Tom Henrik Sundby owns 25,000 shares and Ms. Anne Christin Døvigen owns 32,400
shares in Ocean Yield.

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4
-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
