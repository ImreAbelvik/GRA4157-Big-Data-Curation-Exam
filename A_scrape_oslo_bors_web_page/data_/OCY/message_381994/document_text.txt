The following primary insiders (or their related parties 
as described below) have, as part of the company's 
incentive program, agreed to buy shares in Ocean Yield ASA 
("the Company") from the Company:

* Lars Solbakken, CEO, has (through his wholly owned 
company Finmarine AS) agreed to buy 28 862 new shares in 
Ocean Yield ASA at a price of NOK 51.80 per share. After 
this transaction, Lars Solbakken owns 1 155 879 shares in 
the Company (all shares are owned indirectly through the 
wholly owned company Finmarine AS).

* Eirik Eide, CFO, has (through his wholly owned company 
Kleiver Invest AS) agreed to buy 9 183 new shares in Ocean 
Yield ASA at a price of NOK 51.80 per share. After this 
transaction, Eirik Eide owns, directly or indirectly, 225 
506 shares in the Company.

* Marius Magelie, Vice President, has through his wholly 
owned company MGM Invest AS agreed to buy 3 935 new shares 
in Ocean Yield ASA at a price of NOK 51.80 per share. 
After this transaction, Marius Magelie owns, directly or 
indirectly, 61 029 shares in the Company.

The purchase of shares is made as part of the Company's 
management incentive program. The purchase price equals 
the closing share price of the Ocean Yield share on 10th 
July 2015, less a discount of 20%, reflecting that the 
shares have a lock-up period of three years. The share 
price as of close 10th July 2015 was NOK 64.75.
Following this transaction, Ocean Yield holds 86 450 
treasury shares, equal to 0.06% of the outstanding shares 
in the Company. 

This information is subject of the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities 
Trading Act.
