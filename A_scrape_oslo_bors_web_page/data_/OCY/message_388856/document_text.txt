The following primary insiders (or their related 
parties as described below) have, as part of the 
company's incentive program, agreed to buy shares in 
Ocean Yield ASA ("the Company") from the Company:

* Lars Solbakken, CEO, has through his wholly owned 
company Finmarine AS agreed to buy 30,001 new shares 
in Ocean Yield ASA at a price of NOK 55.00 per share. 
After this transaction, Lars Solbakken owns 1,185,880 
shares in the Company (all shares are owned indirectly 
through the wholly owned company Finmarine AS).

* Eirik Eide, CFO, has through his wholly owned 
company Kleiver Invest AS agreed to buy 9,545 new 
shares in Ocean Yield ASA at a price of NOK 55.00 per 
share. After this transaction, Eirik Eide owns, 
directly or indirectly, 235,051 shares in the Company.

* Marius Magelie, VP Finance & IR, has through his 
wholly owned company MGM Invest AS agreed to buy 4,091 
new shares in Ocean Yield ASA at a price of NOK 55.00 
per share. After this transaction, Marius Magelie 
owns, directly or indirectly, 65,120 shares in the 
Company.

The purchase of shares is made as part of the 
Company's management incentive program. The purchase 
price equals the closing share price of the Ocean 
Yield share on 3rd November 2015, less a discount of 
20%, reflecting that the shares have a lock-up period 
of three years. The share price as of close 3rd 
November 2015 was NOK 68.75.

Following this transaction, Ocean Yield holds 42,813 
treasury shares, equal to 0.03% of the outstanding 
shares in the Company. This information is subject of 
the disclosure requirements pursuant to section 4-2 of 
the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
