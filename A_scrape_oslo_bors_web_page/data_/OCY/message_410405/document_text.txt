Mr. Kjell Inge Røkke has acquired 100,800 shares in Ocean 
Yield ASA at an average price of NOK 64.66 per share. 

Following this transaction, Mr. Røkke owns directly 280,800 
shares in Ocean Yield ASA. 

Together with his wife, Mr. Røkke controls 68.18% of the 
shares in Aker ASA, which again controls 66.29% of the 
outstanding shares in Ocean Yield ASA.


This information is subject of the disclosure requirements 
pursuant to section 4-2 of the Norwegian Securities Trading 
Act.
