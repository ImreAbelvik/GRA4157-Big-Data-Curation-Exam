On 28th of June 2016, a total number of 250,000 stock options were 
exercised at NOK 2.30 per share according to current employee stock 
option program. For details, see notification of 22nd of August 
2013.

Itera ASA has used 162,000 of its own shares to cover this 
redemption of stock options. After this transaction, Itera ASA has 
962,455 own shares. The remaining 88,000 options were redeemed 
through a cash settlement. 

Following primary insiders have exercised options:

Executive Vice President, Kristian Enger, has exercised 30,000 
options and now holds 177,472 shares and 90,000 options.

Executive Vice President, Jon Erik Høgberg, has exercised 132,000 
options and now holds 476,944 shares and 132,000 options.

Executive Vice President, Niko Nystrøm, has exercised 40,000 
options and now holds 173,736 shares and 70,000 options.

CEO of Itera Aps (Denmark), John Aalling, has exercised 16,000 
options and now holds 24,000 options.
