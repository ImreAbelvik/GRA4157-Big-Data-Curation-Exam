CEO Arne Mjøs has through his company Arne Mjøs Invest AS (AMI) today closed future contracts of 3,350,000 shares in Itera (ITE). Simultaneously, AMI has opened new future contracts for 3,350,000 shares at a price of NOK 9.1907 and expiration date 20 March 2020. New total holding, is 26,181,318 shares (31,9%) of which 3,350,000 shares on future contracts. In addition, Arne Mjøs has 279,920 options.
