AS, sold 625.000 shares in Itera at a price of NOK 6,35 per share, 
and purchased future contracts of 1,800,000 shares at a price price

shares. New total holding is 18,576,998 shares and 3,900,000 shares 
on futures contracts, in total 22,476,998 (27.3%). In addition, he 
has 980,000 options.
