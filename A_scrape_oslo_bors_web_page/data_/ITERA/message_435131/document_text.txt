CEO Arne Mjøs has through his company Arne Mjøs Invest AS today 
prolonged future contracts of 3.900.000 shares in Itera (ITE) at a 
price of NOK 6.550 per share with expiration date on 21 December 2017.  

New total holding is unchanged at 22,976,998 shares (28.0%), of which 
3,900,000 on future contracts. In addition, he has 660,000 options.
