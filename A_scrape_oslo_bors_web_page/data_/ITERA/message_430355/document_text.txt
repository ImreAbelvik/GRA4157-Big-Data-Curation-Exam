Itera ASA has as a part of its new employee share purchase programme 
sold 243,520 shares at a price of NOK 6.53 to a total of 87 
employees in the Group. New total holding is 971,935 shares.

The following purchasers are primary insiders:

CFO Bent Hammer has purchased 3,063 shares. New total holding is 
133,063 shares and 180,000 options. 

EVP Jon Erik Høgberg has purchased 3,063 shares. New total holding 
is 505,007 shares and 302,000 options. 

Communications Director Ane Gjennestad has through her company 
Triceps AS purchased 3,063 shares. New total holding is 53,063 
shares and 96,000 options.

Oddvar Håland, CEO of subsidiary Compendia, has purchased 3,063 
shares. New total holding is 3.063 shares and 136.000 options.

Odd Khalifi, board member, has purchased 3,063 shares. New total 
holding is 13.063 shares. 

EVP Kristian Enger has purchased 3,063 shares. New total holding is 
180.535 shares and 260.000 options.
