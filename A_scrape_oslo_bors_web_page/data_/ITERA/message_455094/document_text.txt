CEO Arne Mjøs has through his company Arne Mjøs Invest AS today closed future contracts of 2,500,000 shares in Itera (ITE) at a price of NOK 9,0795 per share, and sold 700.000 shares in ITE at a price of NOK 9,007 per share. Simultaneously, AMI has opened a new future contract for 3,200,000 shares at a price of NOK 9.123 and expiration date 21 September 2018. New total holding, is unchanged at 25,073,645 shares (31,5%) of which 3,200,000 shares on future contracts. In addition, Arne Mjøs has 479.000 options.
