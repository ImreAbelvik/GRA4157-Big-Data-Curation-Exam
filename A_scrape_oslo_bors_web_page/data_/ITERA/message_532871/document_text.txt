Triceps AS, the investment company for primary insider Ane Gjennestad, has today sold 20,000 shares in Itera ASA to CEO Arne Mjøs' investment company, Arne Mjøs Invest AS, at a price of NOK 13.46 per share.

Following the transaction, Ane Gjennestad personally and through Triceps AS holds 164,019 shares and 41,280 share options, while Arne Mjøs through Arne Mjøs Invest AS holds 26,949,911 shares (32.8%), of which 2,800,000 on futures contracts. In addition, he has 57,960 share options.
