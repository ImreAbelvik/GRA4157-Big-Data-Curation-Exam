CEO Arne Mjøs has through his company Arne Mjøs Invest AS (AMI) today closed future contracts of 2,800,000 shares in Itera (ITE). Simultaneously, AMI has opened new future contracts for 2,800,000 shares at a price of NOK 8.9391 and expiration date 18 June 2021. New total holding is 26,848,214 shares (32.7%) of which 2,800,000 shares on future contracts. In addition, Arne Mjøs has 57,960 options.
