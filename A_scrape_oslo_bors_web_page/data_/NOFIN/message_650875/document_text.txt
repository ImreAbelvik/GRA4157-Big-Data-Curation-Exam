Oslo, 8 July 2025
Reference is made to the approved and fully subscribed rights issue carried out in Nordic Financials ASA, where 2 billion new shares were subscribed for in the company.

Mamalao AS (a close associate of the CEO) acted as guarantor for the shares that remained unpaid as of 3 July 2025. The manager, Norne Securities, continued the collection process through Monday, 7 July. As of Tuesday, 8 July, 6,061,797 shares remained unsettled by subscribers.

Mamalao AS has been allocated the remaining 6,061,797 offer shares at the subscription price. Following the transaction, Mamalao AS holds 29,661,797 shares in Nordic Financials ASA.
