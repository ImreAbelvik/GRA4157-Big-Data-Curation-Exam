14 January 2024 
Halldor Chr. Tjoflaat, chairman of the Aega board, has through his wholly owned company Rybo Nor AS purchased 500 000 shares in Aega ASA at NOK 3 per share. After this transaction Rybo Nor holds 1 078 092 shares in Aega ASA. 

The shares were sold by Mamalao AS, a company closely associated with Halldor Chr. Tjoflaat. After the transaction Mamalao AS holds 1 195 548 shares in Aega ASA.

Please see the attached form for a detailed notification and public disclosure of the transactions. This information is subject to disclosure requirements in regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act $5-12.

About AEGA ASA
