Oslo, 25 June 2025 

Reference is made to the resolution by the annual general meeting of Nordic Financials ASA on 28 May 2025 to issue warrants to Selaco AS and Hardanger Consulting AS as settlement under Consultancy Agreements. 

Halldor Chr.  Tjoflaat, CEO of Nordic Financials ASA, has through Hardanger Consulting AS, a company closely associated with him, subscribed for 80,000,000 warrants. Svend Egil Larsen, CIO of Nordic Financials ASA, has through his wholly owned company Selaco AS, subscribed for 80,000,000 warrants. 

Primary insider notifications pursuant to the Market Abuse Regulation article 19 are attached.
