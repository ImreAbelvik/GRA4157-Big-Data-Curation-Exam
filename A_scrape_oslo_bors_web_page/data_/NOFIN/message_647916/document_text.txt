Oslo, 28 May 2025
Nils Petter Skaset, CEO of Nordic Financials ASA, has through his wholly owned company Brezza AS purchased 23,600,000 shares in Nordic Financials ASA at NOK 0.025 per share. Following this transaction, Brezza AS owns 23,600,000 shares in Nordic Financials ASA.

The shares were sold by RyboNor AS, a company closely associated the chairman of the company, Halldor Chr. Tjoflaat. After the transaction, RyboNor AS holds 23,607,422 shares in Nordic Financials ASA. Please see the attached form for a detailed notification and disclosure of the transactions.
