14 December 2022 

The following transactions and reallocations of shares were conducted by Aega Chariman of the board, Halldor Chr. Tjoflaat, and close associates today. The net effect is that Mr. Tjoflaat increased his exposure towards the Aega stock with 50,000 shares.



Mamalao AS, a company controlled by Halldor Chr. Tjoflaat, acquired 86,643 shares in Aega ASA today. 50,000 of these shares were acquired in the market. Following the transaction Mamalao AS holds 86,643 shares in Aega ASA. 

Mamalao AS also acquired 36,643 “Warrants A” and 36,643 “Warrants B”. After the transaction this represents Mamalaos AS total holding of warrants.

OSG30 AS, a company controlled by primary insider Halldor Chr. Tjoflaat, has today sold 36,643 shares in Aega ASA. Following the transaction OSG30 AS do not own any shares in Aega ASA. 

OSG30 AS also sold 36,643 “Warrants A” and 36,643 “Warrants B”. After the transaction OSG30 AS holds no Warrants.

Rybo NOR AS, a company wholly owned by primary insider Halldor Chr. Tjoflaat, today acquired 250,000 shares in Aega ASA. Following the transaction Rybo NOR AS holds 1,738,735 shares in Aega ASA.

Rybo NOR AS also acquired 100,000 “Warrants A” and 100,000 “Warrants B”. Following the transaction Rybo NOR AS holds 503,730 “Warrants A” and 503,703 “Warrants B”.

Aega Chairman of the Board, Halldor Chr. Tjoflaat, has today sold 250,000 shares in Aega ASA. Following the transaction Mr. Tjoflaat holds no shares in Aega ASA. 

Mr. Tjoflaat also sold 100,000 “Warrants A” and 100,000 “Warrants B”. Following the transaction Mr. Tjoflaat holds no Warrants in Aega ASA.
