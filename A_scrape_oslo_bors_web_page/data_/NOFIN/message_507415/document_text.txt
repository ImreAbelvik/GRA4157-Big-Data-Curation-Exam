Brezza AS, a company controlled by AEGA ASA CEO Nils P.Skaset, has acquired 80 000 shares in Aega ASA. The shares were purchased at NOK 1.24 per share. After this transaction Brezza holds 532 793 AEGA ASA shares.

The shares were purchased from AFT Development AS. 
Chairman in AEGA ASA, Halldor Chr. Tjoflaat, is a minority shareholder (0,33%) in this company. The total number of shares sold by AFT Development was 160 000. After the transaction AFT Development controls 2 090 152 shares in AEGA ASA.
