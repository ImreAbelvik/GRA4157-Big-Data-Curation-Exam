CFO of Zaptec AS Kurt Østrem has today sold 300.000 shares in Zaptec AS at an average price of NOK 57.2659 from his fully owned company Østrem Invest AS. 

Following these transactions, Østrem Invest AS owns 1.000.000 shares in Zaptec AS. In addition, Kurt Østrem  holds 200.000 options which have vested and 100.000 options which vest in October 2022.
