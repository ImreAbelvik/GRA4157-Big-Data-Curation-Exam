Peacock & Stargazer AS, a company jointly owned by board member Christian Rangen and his cohabitant, has in the period from the listing of Zaptec AS (the "Company") on Euronext Growth and 8 January 2021, purchased and sold 3,300 and 3,299 shares in the Company, respectively. The average purchase price has been NOK 24.7 per share and the average sale price has been NOK 33.5 per share. The Company and the chairman was notified of the trades today, 11 January 2021.

Following these transactions, Peacock & Stargazer AS owns 20,001 shares in the Company. In addition, Christian Rangen owns 519,561 shares in the Company personally and through various wholly-owned companies.

About ZAPTEC:
ZAPTEC is a leading developer and seller of charging systems for electric vehicles and related technology for multi family homes, office buildings and large parking facilities. Its chargers have integrated phase-balancing technology and utilise Automatic Power Management software, allowing charging at significantly higher speeds compared to conventional chargers without such technology. The group has 32 employees and is headquartered in Stavanger, Norway.
