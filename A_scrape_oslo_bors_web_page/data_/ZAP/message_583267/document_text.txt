Stavanger, Norway, 22 February 2023: 

CEO of Zaptec ASA ("Zaptec" or the "Company") Peter Bardenfleth-Hansen has on 22 February 2023 purchased 20,000 shares in the company at an average price of NOK 26.99. Following the transaction Peter Bardenfleth-Hansen holds a total of 150,000 shares and 100,000 share options in the Company. 

CFO of Zaptec ASA Kurt Østrem has on 22 February 2023 purchased 10,000 shares in the company at an average price of NOK 26.97 through his fully owned company Østrem Invest AS. Following the transaction, Østrem Invest AS owns 1,010,000 shares in Zaptec ASA. In addition, Kurt Østrem holds 300,000 options in the Company.

Primary insider notification pursuant to the market abuse regulation article 19 is attached.

About Zaptec: 
Zaptec ASA is a technology company within Electric vehicle (EV) charging systems in Europe. The company develops EV charging systems for multi and single-family homes and office buildings. The product portfolio enables large number of charging points at a low cost and includes Zaptec Pro, Zaptec Go, Charge365 payment services, Charging columns, Zaptec Portal, and Zaptec Sense. The company accelerates the electrification of the transport sector to assist European countries in reducing CO2 emissions from light-duty cars.
