Christian Rangen, board member in Zaptec AS (Zap) has on December 21st 2021 sold 100.000 privately held shares in Zaptec AS to Peacock & Stargazer AS to an average price of NOK 56.7829. 

Peacock & Stargazer AS has on December 21st 2021 sold 50.000 shares in Zaptec AS to an average price of NOK 56.7829. 

Peacock & Stargazer AS is an investment company owned 50% of board member of Zaptec AS Christian Rangen.

Following these transactions Christian Rangen own, through related companies, a total of 357.562 shares in Zaptec AS.
