Sandnes, Norway, 18 December 2024: 

The CEO of Zaptec ASA ("Zaptec" or the "Company") Kurt Østrem has on 18 December 2024, purchased 120,000 shares in the Company at an average price of NOK 9.47 through his fully owned company Østrem Invest AS. Following the transaction, Østrem Invest AS owns 1,130,000 shares in Zaptec ASA. In addition, Kurt Østrem holds 300,000 options in the Company. 

The CFO & Deputy CEO of Zaptec ASA Eirik Fjellså Hærem has on 18 December 2024 purchased 100,000 shares in the Company at an average price of NOK 9.47. Following the transaction Eirik Fjellså Hærem holds a total of 100,000 shares in the Company.

Primary insider notification pursuant to the market abuse regulation article 19 is attached.

About Zaptec: Zaptec is a technology company within electric vehicle (EV) charging systems in Europe. The company develops EV charging systems for multi and single-family homes and office buildings. The product portfolio enables many charging points at a low cost and includes Zaptec Pro, Zaptec Go, Zaptec Portal, and Zaptec Sense. The company accelerates the electrification of the transport sector to assist European countries in reducing CO2 emissions from light-duty cars.
