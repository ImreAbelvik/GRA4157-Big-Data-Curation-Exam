Oslo, October 12, 2021

The following primary insiders have purchased shares in Xplora Technologies AS:


- S. Munkhaugen AS, a company owned by primary insider and Board Member of Xplora Technologies AS, Torkil Munkhaugen has today bought 100,000 shares in Xplora Technologies AS at a price of NOK 27.50 per share.  

Following the transaction S. Munkhaugen AS holds 1,814,325 shares in Xplora Technologies AS.

- Clement Invest AS, a company owned by primary insider and CFO of Xplora Technologies AS, Mikael Clement has today bought 10,000 shares in Xplora Technologies AS at a price of NOK 27.6367 per share.  

Following the transaction Clement Invest AS holds 124,850 shares in Xplora Technologies AS.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in Regulation EU 596/2014 article 19 number 3 and the Norwegian Securities Trading Act § 5-12.

About Xplora Technologies AS: Xplora is a platform and services company and an industry leader in the market for children’s smartwatches. Xplora was founded to give children a safe onboarding to the digital life and a better balance between screen time and physical activity. Xplora’s vision is to enable children around the world to experience how their everyday activities can create value and make a positive change to the world. The company is headquartered in Norway with operations in leading European markets.
