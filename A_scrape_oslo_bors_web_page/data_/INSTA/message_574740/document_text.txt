Robert Berg, Chief Executive Officer in Instabank ASA, has on November 3rd, 2022 bought 100,000 shares in Instabank ASA at a price of NOK 1.60 per share. Following this transaction, Robert Berg owns, privately and through his wholly-owned company Sonsinvest AS, a total of 5,708,195 shares in Instabank ASA.
