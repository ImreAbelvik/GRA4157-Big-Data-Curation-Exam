Robert Berg, Chief Executive Officer in Instabank ASA, has on October 29th 2020 bought 280,000 shares in Instabank ASA at a price of NOK 1.20 per share. Following this transaction, Robert Berg owns, privately and through his wholly owned company Sonsinvest AS, a total of 5,497,195 shares in Instabank ASA.

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
