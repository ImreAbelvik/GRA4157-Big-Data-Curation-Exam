Henning Munkvold, Deputy employee representative in the board of Instabank ASA, has on March 23rd 2021 bought 33,283 shares in Instabank ASA at a price of NOK 1.80 per share. Following this transaction, Henning Munkvold owns a total of 504,883 shares in Instabank ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
