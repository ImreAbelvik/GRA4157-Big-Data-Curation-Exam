Robert Berg, Chief Executive Officer of Instabank ASA, has on February 9th, 2023 bought 133,000 shares in Instabank ASA at a price of NOK 1.70 per share. Following this transaction, Robert Berg owns, privately and through his wholly-owned company Sonsinvest AS, a total of 5,841,195 shares in Instabank ASA.
