Eivind Sverdrup, Chief Compliance Officer in Instabank ASA, has on February 23rd 2021 bought 45,000 shares in Instabank ASA at a price of NOK 1.55 per share. Following this transaction, Eivind Sverdrup owns, privately and through his wholly owned company Leirin Holding AS, a total of 4,448,833 shares in Instabank ASA. 

This information is subject to the disclosure requirements pursuant to Section 5-12 the Norwegian Securities Trading Act
