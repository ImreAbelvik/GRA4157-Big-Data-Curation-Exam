On 31 May 2017, Carl Westin Ltd has sold 12,986 shares in Europris ASA at an
average share price of NOK 37.00 per share. Carl Westin Ltd is 100% owned by
Christian W Jansson, member of the board of directors and primary insider in
Europris ASA.

Following this transaction, Carl Westin Ltd will own 243,194 shares in Europris
ASA.
