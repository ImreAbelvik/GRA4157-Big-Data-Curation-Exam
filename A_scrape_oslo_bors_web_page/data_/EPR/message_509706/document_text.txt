On 10 July 2020, Eketoppen Holding AS has sold 76,415 shares in Europris ASA at
an average share price of NOK 48,78 per share. Eketoppen Holding AS is 100 per
cent owned by Jon Boye Borgersen, VP Commercial and primary insider in Europris
ASA.

Following this transaction, Eketoppen Holding AS will own 190,252 shares in
Europris ASA.
