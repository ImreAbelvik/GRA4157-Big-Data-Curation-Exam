Primary insider Ole Ingar Apalnes, member of the 
Board of Directors of Europris AS, has today 
purchased 228 shares in Europris ASA at an share 
price of NOK 43,30 per share.



Following the transaction, Ole Ingar Apalnes will 
own 228 shares in Europris ASA.



This information is subject to the disclosure 
requirements pursuant to section 5-12 of the 
Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
