Wendy Lam, CEO of Capsol Technologies ASA ("Capsol"), has purchased 22,325
shares in Capsol today November 5, 2024, at an average price of NOK 11.89 per
share. The shares were acquired on the Oslo Stock Exchange. Following this
acquisition, Lam has 38,198 shares in Capsol.
