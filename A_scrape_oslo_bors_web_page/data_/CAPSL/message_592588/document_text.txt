Oslo, Norway, 06 June 2023


Ingar Bergh, Chief Financial Officer of Capsol Technologies AS (previously CO2 Capsol AS), has on 6 June 2023 purchased 14,800 shares in Capsol Technologies AS (CAPSL) at an average price of 13.47 NOK per share. Bergh does not own shares in Capsol Technologies AS prior to the transaction. The shareholding corresponds to 0.028% of the Company's shares.
 
The purchase was executed via Octopoda AS, a company 100% controlled by Ingar Bergh.  
 
Further information on the purchase is provided in the attached form of notification.
 
This information is subject to the disclosure requirements in the EU Market Abuse Regulation article 19 and section 5-12 of the Norwegian Securities Trading Act.
