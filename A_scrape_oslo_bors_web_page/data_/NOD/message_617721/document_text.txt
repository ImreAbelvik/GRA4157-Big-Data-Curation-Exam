On May 3, 2024, employees in Nordic Semiconductor ASA and subsidiaries of Nordic Semiconductor ASA have exercised 375,650 restricted share units (“RSUs”) in Nordic Semiconductor ASA.
 
All employee plan

The vested RSUs were granted to all employees on April 28, 2021 and May 6, 2022, as part of the long-term incentive program approved by the Annual general meeting held on April 20, 2021 and April 28, 2022. The Board of Directors has authorized the use of treasury shares to settle the vested shares. 

Executive Management Plan

The vested RSUs were granted to the executive management team April 28, 2021, as part of the long-term incentive program approved by the Annual general meeting held on April 20, 2021. The executive management team was also granted Performance Shares. However, as the executives achieved less than 80% of the set performance targets, no performance shares vested. The Board of Directors has authorized the use of treasury shares to settle the vested shares. 

On May 3, 2024, the primary insiders mentioned below ordered for a portion of the received shares to be immediately sold by a third party in order to cover the tax liability. 

Chief Financial Officer, Pål Elstad, has transferred the rights for 1,528 shares. 

Interim SVP R&D, Joakim Ferm, has transferred the rights for 489 shares. 

EVP Sales and Marketing, Geir Langeland, has transferred the rights for 3,070 shares.

EVP Supply Chain, Ole Fredrik Morken, has transferred the rights for 1,163 shares.

SVP Quality, Ola Boström, has transferred the rights for 1,191 shares. 

EVP People & Communication, Katarina Finneng, has transferred the rights for 1,262 shares. 

EVP Strategy and Product Management, Kjetil Holstad, has transferred the rights for 1,078 shares.
 
SVP Investor Relations, Ståle Ytterdal, has transferred the rights for 1,033 shares. 

Employee representative, Jon Helge Nistad, has transferred the rights for 209 shares.
 
Employee representative, Morten Dammen, has transferred the rights for 554 shares. 

Employee representative, Anja Dekens, has transferred the rights for 138 shares.

On May 3, 2024, employees excluding primary insiders mentioned above, ordered for 191,051 shares to be immediately sold by a third party. In total 375,650 treasury shares will be used, of which 202,766 shares will be sold and 172,884 shares will be transferred to employees. 

The sales amount will be finally determined on the basis of the price subsequently obtained by the third party in the market and will be announced in a separate stock exchange notice when known. 

All expenses related to the vested RSUs have already been recognized in this and previous quarters, and the settlement has no net impact on the operating expenses of the company in the future quarters. 

Subsequent to the transaction, Nordic Semiconductor holds 458,024 treasury shares in addition to 69,520 shares set aside for shareholders of Mobile Semiconductor Inc.

There will be no share capital increase to cover the exercise of RSUs.
