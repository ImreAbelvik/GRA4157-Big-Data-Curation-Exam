The following primary insider acquired shares in 
Nordic Semiconductor ASA:

Asbjørn Sæbø, employee elected Board Member of Nordic 
Semiconductor ASA, has on 20th October 2016 acquired 
500 shares in Nordic Semiconductor ASA at a price of 
NOK 33.40 per share.

After this acquisition, Sæbø holds 10.000 shares and 
19.600 options in Nordic Semiconductor ASA.
