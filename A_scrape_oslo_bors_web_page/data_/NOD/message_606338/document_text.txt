(Oslo, December 12, 2023) Birger K Steen, Chair of the Board of Nordic Semiconductor ASA, has today purchased 10,000 shares in Nordic Semiconductor for an average price of NOK 110.20 per share. Following the purchase, Birger K Steen and affiliated entities own 208 745 shares in Nordic Semiconductor ASA.
