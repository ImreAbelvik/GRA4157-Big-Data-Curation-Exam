NOD: Grant of Restricted Stock Units and Performance Shares 

On April 20th, 2023 The Annual General Meeting of Nordic Semiconductor ASA approved the issue Restricted Stock Units (RSUs) and Performance Shares of an aggregate nominal value of up to 1% of the company’s outstanding share capital. 

In total in this release, 1,002,323 RSUs and Performance Shares have been granted, equivalent to 0.52% of the company’s outstanding share capital. 

As basis for the calculation of RSUs to employees (excluding Executive Management team), a weighted average share price the five trading days after the Annual General Meeting of NOK 112.7 has been used. As basis for the calculation of RSUs and Performance Shares to the Executive management team, the weighted average share price on July 11, 2023 of NOK 129.9 has been used.

All Employee Plan 

Today, on July 12th, 2023 the company has granted 916,527 RSUs to employees under the all-employee program. For employees, the RSUs will vest after two years and the shares will be delivered to the employee at the vesting date against payment of the par value of the share (subject to the employee's continued employment with the Company). The shares may be newly issued shares or existing treasury shares.

Executive Management Plan 

With reference to the Annual general meeting held on April 20th, 2023, members of the Executive Management team have today been granted a total of 42,898 RSUs and 42,898 performance shares in the company. The RSUs will vest after three years and the shares will be delivered to the employee at the vesting date against payment of the par value of the share (subject to the employee's continued employment with the Company). The shares may be newly issued shares or existing treasury shares. The performance shares are issued conditional upon the achievement of a certain set of objectives. The performance shares vest and will be delivered at par value upon the completion of the performance period, which is three years. If the executive achieves at least 80% of the set performance targets, 50% of the shares will be granted, whilst on 120% achievement of targets a maximum two times the shares will be granted. As such, if maximum performance is achieved on the performance shares, a total of 85,796 shares will be delivered to the executive management team. If less than 80% performance is achieved, zero shares will be delivered.
