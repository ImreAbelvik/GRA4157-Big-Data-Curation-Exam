Following the market close on April 23, 2015, 
employees in Nordic Semiconductor ASA have exercised 
265,930 share options in Nordic Semiconductor ASA.
 
The options contracts were granted to employees with 
a strike price of NOK 38.43 and a "cap" of NOK 
150.00 upon cash settlement). 

This options settlement will result in a cash 
payment of NOK 5,603,145 (USD 708,747) to those 
employees who have exercised options.  All expenses 
related to the options exercise have already been 
recognized in previous quarters, and the options 
settlement has no net impact on the operating 
expenses of the company in the current or future 
quarters.

After this transaction, the total number of options 
issued in Nordic Semiconductor ASA shares under
the employee option program is 3,878,740.
 
Among the employees that exercised options the 
following primary insider participated:

Chief Technology Officer Svein-Egil Nielsen has 
exercised 100,000 share options. Following this 
transaction he holds 250,000 options and 15,000 
shares in the Company.
