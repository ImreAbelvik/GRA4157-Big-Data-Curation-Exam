Following the market close on October 21, 2020, employees in Nordic Semiconductor ASA have exercised 562 949 options in Nordic Semiconductor ASA.

The options contracts were granted to employees with a strike price of NOK 35.77 and a "cap" of NOK 107.31 (upon cash settlement).

Upon exercise, the company has the right to settle its option contracts in cash by paying the difference between the share price and the strike price, with a maximum settlement fee equal to the difference between the NOK 107.31 "cap" and the strike price of NOK 35.77 (or NOK 71.54 per option).

Based on the current share price, the Board has authorized the company to exercise its right to settle its options contracts in cash by paying the difference between the NOK 107.31 "cap" and the strike price of NOK 35.77.

This options settlement will result in a cash payment of NOK 40,273,372 (USD 4.378 million) to those employees who have exercised options. All expenses related to the options exercise have already been recognized in previous quarters, and the options settlement has no net impact on the operating expenses of the company in the current or future quarters.
