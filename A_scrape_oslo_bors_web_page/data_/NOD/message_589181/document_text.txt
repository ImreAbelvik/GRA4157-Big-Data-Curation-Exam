On April 28, 2023, employees in Nordic Semiconductor ASA have exercised 97,578 performance shares (“PSUs”) and 409,801 restricted share units (“RSUs”) in Nordic Semiconductor ASA.  

The vested PSUs and RSUs were granted to the executive management team April 29, 2020, as part of the long-term incentive program approved by the Annual general meeting held on April 22, 2020. The performance shares were issued conditional upon the achievement of a certain set of objectives. At the end of the performance period the awarded number of shares have been adjusted to 1.8 times the numbers of shares initially granted. The Board of Directors has authorized the use of treasury shares to settle the vested shares. 

The vested RSUs were granted to all employees on April 29, 2020 and April 28, 2021, as part of the long-term incentive program approved by the Annual general meeting held on April 22, 2020 and April 20, 2021. The Board of Directors has authorized the use of treasury shares to settle the vested shares.
 
On April 28, 2023, the primary insiders mentioned below ordered for a portion of the received shares to be immediately sold by a third party in order to cover the tax liability. 

Chief Executive Officer, Svenn-Tore Larsen, has transferred the rights for 14,870 shares. 

Chief Technology Officer, Svein-Egil Nielsen, has transferred the rights for 10,042 shares. 

EVP People & Communication, Katarina Finneng, has transferred the rights for 7,338 shares.

EVP Product Management, Kjetil Holstad, has transferred the rights for 5,987 shares. 

SVP Quality, Ola Boström, has transferred the rights for 852 shares. 

SVP IR, Ståle Ytterdal, has transferred the rights for 6,180 shares. 

Employee representative, Jon Helge Nistad, has transferred the rights for 149 shares. 

Employee representative, Gro Fykse, has transferred the rights for 297 shares.

Employee representative, Morten Dammen, has transferred the rights for 804 shares.

Employee representative, Anja Dekens, has transferred the rights for 134 shares.

On April 28, 2023, employees excluding primary insiders, ordered for 182,134 shares to be immediately sold by a third party. 

In total 507,379 treasury shares will be used, of which 228,787 shares will be sold and 278,592 shares will be transferred to employees. 

The sales amount will be finally determined on the basis of the price subsequently obtained by the third party in the market and will be announced in a separate stock exchange notice when known. 

All expenses related to the vested options, PSUs and RSUs have already been recognized in this and previous quarters, and the settlement has no net impact on the operating expenses of the company in the future quarters.  
Subsequent to the transaction, Nordic Semiconductor holds 380,420 own shares. There will be no share capital increase to cover the exercise of PSUs and RSUs.
