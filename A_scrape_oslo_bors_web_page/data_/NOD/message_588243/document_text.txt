Reference made to the 2023 Annual General Meeting (AGM) approving that part of the Directors’ remuneration for 2023 should be paid in shares in the Company. As approved by the AGM, the share-based part of the compensation for 2023 is paid after the AGM in 2023, and the number of shares is based on the amount due in NOK (NOK 160,625 for ordinary board members and NOK 462,750 to the chair) adjusted for tax, divided by the share price at closing on the day of the AGM 2023. 

Based on the closing price on April 20th, 2023 of NOK 121.40, a total of 5,871 shares have been transferred to the Board Members.
