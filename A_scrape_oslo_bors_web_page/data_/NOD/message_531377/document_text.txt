On April 20th, 2021 The Annual General Meeting of Nordic Semiconductor ASA approved the issue of up to 600 000 Restricted Stock Units (RSUs) and Performance Shares, equivalent to approximately 0.3% of the company’s outstanding share capital. 

In total in this release, 452 353 RSUs and Performance Shares have been granted, equivalent to 0.23% of the company’s outstanding share capital. 

As basis for the calculation of RSUs and Performance Shares, a weighted average share price the five trading days after the Annual General Meeting of NOK 182.2 has been used.

All Employee Plan 
Today, on April 28th, 2021 the company has granted 386 743 RSUs to employees under the all-employee program. For employees, the RSUs will vest after two years and the shares will be delivered to the employee at the vesting date against payment of the par value of the share (subject to the employee's continued employment with the Company). The shares may be newly issued shares or existing treasury shares.

Executive Management Plan 
With reference to the Annual general meeting held on April 20th, 2021, members of the Executive Management team have today been granted a total of 36 640 RSUs and 28 970 performance shares in the company. The RSUs will vest after three years and the shares will be delivered to the employee at the vesting date against payment of the par value of the share (subject to the employee's continued employment with the Company). The shares may be newly issued shares or existing treasury shares. The performance shares are issued conditional upon the achievement of a certain set of objectives. The performance shares vest and will be delivered at par value upon the completion of the performance period, which is three years. 

If the executive achieves at least 80% of the set performance targets, 50% of the shares will be granted, whilst on 120% achievement of targets a maximum two times the shares will be granted. As such, if maximum performance is achieved on the performance shares, a total of 57 940 shares will be delivered to the executive management team. If less than 80% performance is achieved, zero shares will be delivered.
