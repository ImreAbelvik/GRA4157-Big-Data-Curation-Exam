Following the market close on August 11, 2015, 
employees in Nordic Semiconductor ASA have exercised 
35,200 share options in Nordic Semiconductor ASA. Of 
these, 18,700 share options will be settled in cash. 
 
In addition ABG Sundal Collier has been retained to 
buy 16,500 shares on behalf of Nordic Semiconductor 
ASA to be transferred to employees that chose to 
settle the options by receiving shares. The shares 
were purchased at a price of NOK 52.43.  
 
The options contracts were granted to employees with 
a strike price of NOK 38.43 and a "cap" of NOK 
150.00 upon cash settlement). 

This options settlement will result in a total net 
cash payment of NOK 492,652 (USD 60,000) for Nordic 
Semiconductor ASA.  

All expenses related to the options exercise have 
already been recognized in previous quarters, and 
the options settlement has no net impact on the 
operating expenses of the company in the current or 
future quarters.

After this transaction, the total number of options 
issued in Nordic Semiconductor ASA shares under the 
employee option program is 3,843,540
